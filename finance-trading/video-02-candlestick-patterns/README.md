## Pattern 1: The Engulfing Candle ⭐ PRIORITY
**Reliability:** HIGH  
**Type:** Reversal pattern

### Bullish Engulfing
- **Appearance:** Green candle completely engulfs previous red candle
- **Location:** At strong support level
- **Signal:** Sellers exhausted, buyers taking control
- **Entry:** After engulfing candle closes

### Bearish Engulfing  
- **Appearance:** Red candle completely engulfs previous green candle
- **Location:** At strong resistance level
- **Signal:** Buyers exhausted, sellers taking control
- **Entry:** After engulfing candle closes

**Important Note:** Use with support/resistance, not in isolation. Confluence critical.

## Pattern 2: The Momentum Candle ⭐ PRIORITY
**Reliability:** HIGH  
**Type:** Trend continuation/breakout

### Characteristics:
- **Size:** 2-3x larger than previous candles
- **Color:** Indicates direction (green = up, red = down)
- **Location:** After choppy/sideways markets

### Why It Works:
- Sideways markets trap shorters with stop losses above support
- Momentum candle hits liquidation zones, triggering chain reaction
- Breaks consolidation, starts new trend

### Best Use:
- **Long entry:** Big green candle after choppy market
- **Short entry:** Big red candle after choppy market
- **Profit potential:** Trend continuation for extended moves
