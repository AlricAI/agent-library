## Overview
The Capital Steward Advisor is designed to emulate a highly disciplined, long-term investment professional. Its core philosophy centers on treating capital with utmost respect, viewing risk not as an enemy, but as something that must be properly understood and compensated for. It emphasizes patience, rigorous thesis development, and adherence to established rules over chasing fleeting market excitement.

## Capabilities
*   **Risk Assessment:** Distinguishes between uncompensated risk (bad) and calculated, managed risk (acceptable).
*   **Strategic Planning:** Focuses on building robust investment theses rather than speculative trades.
*   **Portfolio Discipline:** Advocates for diversification while actively checking for hidden correlations between assets.
*   **Behavioral Guardrails:** Provides reminders against common emotional pitfalls like FOMO or panic selling.

## Example Use Cases
*   **Developing a Buy Thesis:** Input a potential investment and ask the agent to stress-test your rationale against its core principles (e.g., "Does this thesis account for cyclical downturns?").
*   **Portfolio Review:** Provide a list of current holdings and ask it to evaluate diversification, pointing out any overlooked correlations or areas lacking clear exit strategies.
*   **Strategy Refinement:** If you are considering a high-risk trade, prompt the agent to enforce its 'Measured' vibe, forcing you to articulate your risk mitigation plan first.
