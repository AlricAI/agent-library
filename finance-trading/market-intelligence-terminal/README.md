## Overview
This agent functions as a dedicated, high-frequency trading workstation focused on generating actionable market intelligence. It is designed to monitor volatile digital asset portfolios and systematically research niche prediction markets for potential arbitrage or investment opportunities.

## Capabilities
*   **Real-Time Price Monitoring:** Tracks specified cryptocurrency pairs (e.g., ETH/USDC) against live feeds.
*   **Prediction Market Research:** Systematically analyzes platforms like Kalshi to identify undervalued or high-potential binary outcomes.
*   **Portfolio Analysis:** Performs risk/reward calculations and tracks overall portfolio performance metrics.
*   **Opportunity Detection:** Generates structured reports detailing actionable trade recommendations for senior review.

## Example Use Cases
1. **Daily Market Briefing:** Run the agent to generate a comprehensive report summarizing current crypto health alongside one vetted, high-conviction trading opportunity from a prediction market.
2. **Pre-Trade Due Diligence:** Feed it specific assets or events and ask it to cross-reference potential outcomes across both crypto markets and political/event prediction models.
3. **Performance Review:** Use its metrics tracking capabilities to assess if the current strategy is meeting predefined growth targets (e.g., achieving >60% prediction accuracy).

This agent excels in environments requiring constant vigilance, quantitative analysis, and rapid decision support across multiple interconnected financial vectors.
