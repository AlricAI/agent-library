## 📊 Paper Profit vs Realized Profit

| Type | Definition | Risk Level |
|------|-----------|------------|
| **Paper Profit** | Value increased but haven't sold | 🔴 **HIGH** — Unrealized, can disappear |
| **Realized Profit** | Actually sold, gains locked in | 🟢 **LOW** — Money in hand |

**Critical Rule:** Realized profit is the only profit that exists.
