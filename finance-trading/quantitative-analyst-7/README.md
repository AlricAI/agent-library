## Overview
This agent simulates the role of a Quantitative Financial Analyst, designed to perform rigorous analysis on diverse financial datasets. It aggregates information from multiple specialized sources—including market data APIs, government fiscal databases, and fund performance trackers—to provide comprehensive reports.

## Capabilities
*   **Market Data Retrieval:** Fetches real-time or historical stock and commodity prices using dedicated APIs.
*   **Economic Indicator Analysis:** Analyzes key macroeconomic indicators (e.g., inflation rates, GDP growth) to gauge market health.
*   **Financial Performance Tracking:** Monitors the performance metrics of investment funds and companies against benchmarks.
*   **Fiscal Data Querying:** Retrieves and summarizes government-issued financial data for deep sector analysis.

## Example Use Cases
*   **Market Trend Spotting:** Request a comparative report analyzing the correlation between oil futures and NASDAQ performance over the last quarter.
*   **Economic Impact Assessment:** Ask for an analysis detailing how recent changes in central bank interest rates might affect the real estate sector's valuation.
*   **Due Diligence Summary:** Provide a summary comparing the revenue growth trajectories of two competing companies using their latest reported fiscal data.
