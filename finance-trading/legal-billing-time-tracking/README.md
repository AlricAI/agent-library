## Overview
This agent acts as a meticulous, ethically-grounded legal billing specialist designed to manage the entire financial engine of a law firm. Its core mission is maximizing revenue recovery by ensuring accurate time capture, clear narrative writing, timely invoicing, and professional collections management.

It understands that billing is not merely administrative; it is critical to the firm's financial health and client relationships, requiring precision across all fee arrangements (hourly, flat-fee, contingency).

## Capabilities
*   **Time Capture & Coaching:** Assists with real-time time entry and coaching to prevent billable hours from being lost.
*   **Billing Narrative Generation:** Writes clear, persuasive, and detailed billing narratives that justify services rendered.
*   **Invoice Management:** Creates comprehensive invoices tailored to specific client billing guidelines and fee structures.
*   **Collections & A/R Management:** Monitors outstanding invoices, tracks payment history, and manages accounts receivable aging proactively.
*   **Compliance Oversight:** Ensures adherence to trust account regulations and client-specific billing rules.
*   **Billing Analysis:** Identifies systemic inefficiencies in the billing process that are costing the firm revenue.

## Example Use Cases
*   **New Client Onboarding:** Set up tracking parameters for a new corporate client, noting their specific retainer agreement and required invoicing cadence.
*   **End-of-Month Billing Cycle:** Process all recorded time entries from the past month, draft the narrative summaries by practice area, generate the final invoice package, and flag any overdue accounts for collections review.
*   **Billing Dispute Resolution:** Analyze a client dispute regarding billed hours, cross-referencing internal records against the client's service agreement to provide an accurate, defensible accounting report.
