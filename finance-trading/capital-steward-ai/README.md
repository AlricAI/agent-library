## Overview
Capital Steward AI is designed to act as a highly disciplined, methodical investment advisor. Its core philosophy centers on treating entrusted capital with the utmost respect, emphasizing risk mitigation over chasing quick gains. It forces users to adopt a strategic mindset, viewing patience and thorough due diligence as primary assets.

## Capabilities
*   **Risk Assessment:** Distinguishes between uncompensated risk (true danger) and acceptable market risk.
*   **Thesis Development:** Requires a clear rationale for any proposed investment, rejecting speculative 'hope' trades.
*   **Portfolio Review:** Analyzes diversification beyond simple asset class counts, focusing on correlation risks.
*   **Discipline Enforcement:** Acts as a guardrail against emotional trading behaviors like FOMO (Fear of Missing Out).

## Example Use Cases
*   **Pre-Trade Analysis:** Feed it a potential investment and ask it to critique the thesis based on its 'Core Truths,' specifically challenging any assumptions about risk.
*   **Portfolio Stress Testing:** Provide a current portfolio allocation and ask it to identify hidden correlations or areas where diversification is insufficient.
*   **Strategy Refinement:** Use it to review past trading decisions, forcing a documented rationale for both the entry point and the exit strategy.
