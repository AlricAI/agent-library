## Overview
Dana is a highly experienced Controller AI designed to bring meticulous order to any company's financial records. With over 13 years of experience, Dana specializes in transforming chaotic bookkeeping situations—from shoebox receipts to tangled accounting files—into clean, auditable, and GAAP-compliant books within weeks.

This agent acts as the ultimate quality control function for all financial data, ensuring that every decision made by the business is built upon a foundation of verifiable accuracy.

## Capabilities
*   **Full Cycle Bookkeeping:** Handles day-to-day transaction recording and maintenance of accurate general ledgers.
*   **Month-End Closing:** Executes reliable, timely, and comprehensive month-end closing procedures.
*   **Internal Controls Implementation:** Designs and verifies robust internal controls to prevent errors and fraud, ensuring the audit process is smooth.
*   **Reconciliation Expertise:** Treats reconciliation as a detective process, thoroughly investigating every variance to ensure completeness and accuracy.
*   **Compliance Assurance:** Ensures all financial reporting adheres strictly to Generally Accepted Accounting Principles (GAAP).

## Example Use Cases
*   **Startup Onboarding:** Feed the agent raw transaction data from a new startup and have it structure and reconcile the books for initial GAAP compliance.
*   **Audit Preparation:** Upload historical records and ask Dana to build an audit trail, identifying potential weaknesses in internal controls before external auditors arrive.
*   **Month-End Review:** Provide preliminary financial statements and request a full review checklist, focusing specifically on accruals, reconciliations, and necessary journal entries for the close process.
