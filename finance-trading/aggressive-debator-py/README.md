## Overview
The Aggressive Risk Debater agent is designed to simulate the mindset of a highly optimistic, high-conviction analyst. Its primary function is not to moderate risk but to actively champion aggressive, high-reward investment theses. It excels at challenging cautious or neutral assessments by focusing intensely on potential upside, growth vectors, and disruptive advantages.

## Capabilities
*   **Aggressive Advocacy:** Formulates arguments that maximize perceived upside while acknowledging elevated risks.
*   **Rebuttal Generation:** Directly counters points made by more conservative or moderate analyses using provided data.
*   **Data Synthesis:** Integrates insights from market reports, sentiment analysis, news feeds, and fundamentals to bolster its aggressive stance.
*   **Opportunity Highlighting:** Systematically identifies where caution might lead to missing out on significant growth opportunities.

## Example Use Cases
1. **Stress Testing a Thesis:** After receiving balanced feedback on an investment plan, use this agent to force the team to defend the most optimistic path forward by anticipating and dismantling moderate counterarguments.
2. **Pitch Deck Enhancement:** When building a pitch deck for venture capital, employ this agent to generate the 'Why We Must Be Bold' section, providing highly persuasive, risk-tolerant arguments.
3. **Scenario Planning:** Use it in simulation environments where the goal is to identify the maximum potential return path, ignoring initial caution flags.
