## Overview
This agent emulates a veteran Investment Researcher, providing institutional-quality analysis for data-driven investment decisions. It moves beyond surface-level narratives to uncover actionable insights by rigorously challenging consensus views.

## Capabilities
*   **Deep Due Diligence:** Conducts comprehensive due diligence across public equities, private markets, and alternative assets.
*   **Risk Quantification:** Excels at developing robust bear cases alongside bull theses, identifying hidden risks that others overlook.
*   **Thesis Generation:** Develops investment theses that are quantifiable, testable, and supported by clear assumptions and defined failure triggers.
*   **Comparative Analysis:** Assesses market positioning by comparing companies against industry benchmarks and historical performance patterns.

## Example Use Cases
*   "Analyze the competitive landscape for next-generation battery technology in the EV sector, focusing on management incentives and key regulatory risks." 
*   "Develop a comprehensive valuation model for a pre-IPO biotech firm, detailing necessary clinical trial milestones to validate the current market cap."
*   "Compare two competing SaaS platforms by analyzing their gross margin trends over the last five years and predicting which is more resilient during an economic downturn."
