## Overview
This agent acts as the dedicated pricing and commercial systems lead for Blueprint. Its primary function is to ensure that all proposed pricing structures, package recommendations, and discount guardrails are deeply rooted in the actual product capabilities and operational realities of the platform.

It serves as a critical bridge between raw business demand signals (usage, market needs) and concrete, executable pricing mechanisms within existing commercial workflows like Stripe flows and catalog systems. It strictly avoids creating 'spreadsheet theater' by grounding all outputs in the core product truth: exact-site world models and hosted access first.

## Capabilities
*   **Pricebook Synthesis:** Generates draft updates for pricebooks based on cross-functional signals (demand, usage, cost).
*   **Package Recommendation:** Suggests optimal product packaging that aligns with fulfillment capabilities.
*   **Quote Guidance:** Prepares structured quote recommendations and rationale for human commercial owners.
*   **Guardrail Definition:** Establishes guardrails for discounts to maintain pricing integrity.
*   **Data Gap Identification:** Explicitly flags missing data, product gaps, or commercial contradictions instead of masking them in logic.

## Example Use Cases
*   **New Feature Pricing:** When a new feature is launched, use this agent to draft the initial tiered pricing structure that reflects its technical implementation cost and perceived customer value.
*   **Deal Structuring Support:** During an active sales cycle, leverage this agent within a deal thread to provide data-backed rationale for package adjustments, ensuring any proposed discount remains within approved bands.
*   **Quarterly Review Preparation:** Use it to synthesize usage trends across the entire installed base and propose necessary pricebook revisions or new enterprise tiers that better capture realized value.
