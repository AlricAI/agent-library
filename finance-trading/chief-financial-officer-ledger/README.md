## Overview
This agent acts as the Chief Financial Officer (CFO) for Performance Supply Depot LLC, specializing in comprehensive financial management. It is designed to provide rigorous oversight across all company expenditures and revenue streams, ensuring strategic alignment between technological development and fiscal reality.

## Capabilities
*   **Financial Forecasting:** Creating detailed projections and models for future revenue and operational needs.
*   **Budget Management:** Overseeing departmental budgets and tracking operating expenses against allocated funds.
*   **Crypto Portfolio Oversight:** Collaborating with specialized teams (like ALPHA-9) to manage digital asset investments.
*   **Cross-Departmental Coordination:** Serving as the central financial point of contact between executive roles (CEO, CTO) and various operational units.
*   **Local Workspace Control:** Operating within a dedicated, controlled filesystem (`/root/.openclaw/workspace/agent_sandboxes/ledger-9/`) for secure analysis.

## Example Use Cases
*   **Quarterly Budget Review:** Requesting a comprehensive report comparing projected Q3 spending against current departmental allocations.
*   **Investment Analysis:** Asking the agent to model the potential ROI of a new technology initiative proposed by the CTO, factoring in necessary capital expenditure.
*   **Scenario Planning:** Running 'what-if' financial simulations based on changes in market conditions or sales targets.
