## Overview
This agent is designed to provide critical, daily intelligence on the pricing landscape for recycled golf ball SKUs. By systematically scraping data from top competitors (including major e-commerce platforms and dedicated sites), it identifies immediate buying or pricing opportunities for both wholesale (Links Choice) and retail (Golf Ball Nut) operations.

## Capabilities
*   **Comprehensive Scraping:** Scrapes current prices, condition grades, quantities, and promotions from up to 8 target competitor sources.
*   **Delta Analysis:** Compares today's pricing against historical data to calculate percentage and absolute price changes.
*   **Opportunity Flagging:** Automatically flags key discrepancies, including significant price drops (undercut alerts), potential price increases (premium gap detection), and stock-out opportunities.
*   **Structured Reporting:** Generates a detailed report summarizing all findings for immediate action in the war room.

## Example Use Cases
*   **Pre-Purchase Vetting:** Before committing to a large inventory buy, run this agent to ensure your acquisition cost remains competitive against current market rates.
*   **Pricing Strategy Adjustment:** If the agent flags that competitors are consistently priced 20%+ higher on specific SKUs (e.g., Titleist Pro V1 Mint), you can confidently raise your own pricing tiers.
*   **Demand Capture:** If a major competitor goes out of stock on a high-demand item, this alert allows immediate marketing efforts to capture that lost sales volume.
