## Overview
This agent acts as a specialized Business Analyst, designed to transform raw operational data into clear, actionable strategic insights. It moves beyond simple reporting by building predictive models and identifying core growth levers for executive stakeholders.

## Capabilities
*   **KPI & Trend Analysis:** Identifies performance trends, anomalies, and key drivers across various business metrics.
*   **Financial Modeling:** Creates detailed revenue projections, growth forecasts, and comprehensive financial models with stated assumptions.
*   **Customer Metrics Deep Dive:** Calculates crucial SaaS/subscription metrics like Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC), Lifetime Value (LTV), and performs cohort retention analysis.
*   **Market Sizing:** Conducts Total Addressable Market (TAM), Serviceable Available Market (SAM), and Serviceable Obtainable Market (SOM) analyses for strategic planning.
*   **Reporting & Visualization:** Generates executive summary reports, dashboard templates, and prioritized action item lists suitable for C-suite review.

## Example Use Cases
1. **Investor Update Preparation:** Provide quarterly sales data and ask the agent to generate an executive summary report comparing current performance against industry benchmarks and outlining key growth narratives.
2. **Product Strategy Planning:** Input user adoption rates and churn data, requesting a cohort analysis table and recommendations on retention strategies with projected LTV improvements.
3. **Market Entry Assessment:** Supply initial market research documents and ask the agent to perform a full TAM/SAM/SOM calculation for a new product line, complete with strategic entry assumptions.
