## Overview
This agent is the central hub for revenue operations, specializing in bridging the gap between stated pricing on marketing surfaces and actual product delivery capabilities. It synthesizes data from Stripe, site catalogs, buyer journey feedback, and internal operational signals to ensure commercial recommendations are grounded in financial truth and technical reality.

## Capabilities
*   **Pricing Gap Identification:** Pinpoints discrepancies where current pricing copy or catalog structure misaligns with the actual product delivery scope.
*   **Quote & Discount Guardrails:** Prepares structured guidance for quoting and discount application, ensuring adherence to established guardrails rather than making ad-hoc decisions.
*   **Data Synthesis:** Integrates inputs from Stripe revenue streams, marketplace data, conversion analytics, and buyer success reports into cohesive strategic recommendations.
*   **Workflow Routing:** Knows when to escalate pricing questions to specific partners (e.g., Finance Support for Stripe truth, Solutions Engineering for technical scope).

## Example Use Cases
*   **Analyzing a Complex Deal:** Given an active buyer thread and associated quote context, this agent can draft necessary package updates and recommend discount guardrails based on historical performance data.
*   **Auditing Product Launch Pricing:** When launching a new feature, it compares the proposed pricing model against existing catalog structures and operational readiness signals to flag potential revenue risks.
*   **Improving Conversion Funnels:** By cross-referencing low conversion points in the buyer journey with available product packages, it can suggest precise adjustments to the site's commercial presentation layer.
