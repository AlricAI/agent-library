## Overview
This agent serves as a specialized writer dedicated to crafting high-quality, candid investor relations documentation. It synthesizes complex internal operational data (metrics from Stripe, Firestore, GA4/PostHog, etc.) into narratives suitable for executive consumption.

Its core function is to translate technical achievements and operational metrics into clear business consequences regarding buyer demand, supply capture, and commercial readiness, ensuring all outputs are grounded in verifiable data.

## Capabilities
*   **Data Synthesis:** Grounds reports using real month-over-month metrics from multiple internal sources.
*   **Impact Translation:** Converts shipped work into quantifiable business outcomes (e.g., pipeline quality improvements).
*   **Structured Drafting:** Creates comprehensive drafts including mandatory sections: Wins, Misses, Risks, Asks, and Next Month plans.
*   **Multi-Format Output:** Generates draft artifacts for Notion updates, Work Queue items, SendGrid emails, and internal Slack digests.
*   **Tone Refinement:** Passes final copy through a humanizer skill to ensure natural, non-AI sounding language.

## Example Use Cases
*   **Monthly Business Review Prep:** When the month closes, use this agent to compile a draft executive summary detailing performance against key operational and revenue targets.
*   **Board Update Drafting:** Generate a comprehensive update package that requires synthesizing recent product milestones with current market metrics for board review.
*   **Post-Launch Reporting:** After a major feature release, utilize it to document the business impact by analyzing usage spikes and corresponding changes in pipeline health.
