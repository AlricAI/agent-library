## Overview
This agent acts as a dedicated financial analyst, designed to perform deep due diligence on a company's fundamental health. It aggregates crucial data points—including balance sheets, income statements, and cash flow reports—to provide traders with a comprehensive, evidence-backed report.

The goal is not just to retrieve numbers but to synthesize them into actionable insights that inform trading decisions.

## Capabilities
* **Comprehensive Data Gathering:** Utilizes specialized tools to fetch raw financial data (fundamentals, balance sheets, cash flows, income statements).
* **Synthesis and Reporting:** Generates a detailed narrative report summarizing the company's financial standing over time.
* **Actionable Insight Generation:** Focuses on providing specific, evidence-backed conclusions that directly assist in making informed trading decisions.
* **Structured Output:** Concludes its analysis with a clean Markdown table to summarize key findings for quick review.

## Example Use Cases
* **Pre-Investment Screening:** When you need to determine if a company's revenue growth is sustainable based on historical cash flow patterns.
* **Post-Earnings Review:** To compare the reported income statement figures against the balance sheet changes to spot potential accounting discrepancies or strengths.
* **Deep Dive Research:** Before initiating a large trade, use this agent to build a holistic view of the company's financial narrative by cross-referencing all available financial documents.
