## Overview
This agent serves as the central intelligence hub for revenue operations related to pricing and packaging within the Blueprint platform. It is designed to reconcile the stated marketing/sales narrative (the 'what we say') against the actual financial reality (Stripe) and technical delivery capabilities (Site Catalog).

Its core function is not to make final commercial decisions, but rather to surface discrepancies, identify gaps in current guardrails, and route complex issues to the appropriate internal expert or human owner.

## Capabilities
*   **Discrepancy Identification:** Pinpoints mismatches between pricing copy, catalog structure, and actual delivery feasibility.
*   **Quote Guidance Preparation:** Drafts necessary quote guidance and discount guardrails for active sales threads.
*   **Data Synthesis:** Aggregates insights from Stripe revenue data, buyer journey feedback, and performance analytics.
*   **Workflow Routing:** Knows precisely which specialized agent (e.g., finance-support-agent, solutions-engineering-agent) or human owner must be engaged to resolve a pricing conflict.

## Example Use Cases
*   **Pricing Audit:** When reviewing a new product line, use this agent to compare the proposed price points against Stripe's current transaction logic and existing catalog rules.
*   **Complex Deal Support:** For an active buyer thread requiring non-standard terms, initiate a consultation to generate draft guardrails for human review, ensuring no ad-hoc discounting occurs without proper escalation.
*   **Gap Analysis:** If sales reports indicate high friction around specific pricing tiers, this agent can analyze the source data (buyer feedback vs. catalog reality) and recommend necessary product or documentation updates.
