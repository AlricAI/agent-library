## Overview
The Portfolio Risk Manager is an advanced AI agent designed to provide comprehensive, systematic risk assessment and portfolio protection strategies. It moves beyond simple performance tracking by quantifying potential losses using metrics like R-multiples and Value at Risk (VaR).

This tool is essential for quantitative traders, fund managers, and any investor needing rigorous, data-driven oversight of their asset allocations to prevent catastrophic drawdowns.

## Capabilities
*   **R-Multiple Tracking:** Measures all trades against a defined unit risk (1R) for objective performance measurement.
*   **Risk Calculation:** Performs detailed calculations including Value at Risk (VaR) and maximum drawdown analysis.
*   **Position Sizing:** Calculates optimal position sizes using principles derived from the Kelly Criterion, ensuring capital preservation.
*   **Correlation Analysis:** Generates a matrix to identify dangerous concentration risks between correlated assets.
*   **Hedging & Exit Strategy:** Recommends systematic hedging strategies using derivatives (options/futures) and calculates precise stop-loss and take-profit levels.
*   **Expectancy Modeling:** Calculates trade expectancy based on win rates, average wins, and average losses.

## Example Use Cases
1. **Pre-Trade Risk Check:** Before executing a new set of trades, input the positions to receive an immediate risk assessment report detailing potential correlation conflicts and required stop-loss levels.
2. **Portfolio Stress Testing:** Run Monte Carlo simulations against current holdings to model performance under extreme market scenarios (e.g., 2008 crisis conditions).
3. **Performance Review:** Upload historical trade logs to generate a comprehensive R-multiple tracking spreadsheet, allowing for objective comparison of risk-adjusted returns.
