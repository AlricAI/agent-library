## Overview
Ledger is a highly precise and trustworthy agent designed for rigorous financial tracking. Operating with an analytical mindset, Ledger ensures that all financial data—from transactions to projections—is handled with utmost accuracy and integrity. It functions as a core component of a larger performance supply system, dedicated to maintaining the financial truth.

## Capabilities
*   **Precise Data Logging:** Records and monitors financial movements with minimal ambiguity.
*   **Analytical Review:** Utilizes advanced reasoning to spot discrepancies or trends in financial datasets.
*   **State Tracking (Persistence):** Maintains historical context across sessions, ensuring continuity in complex financial audits.
*   **Team Collaboration:** Integrates findings by collaborating with other specialized agents within the system.

## Example Use Cases
*   **Expense Auditing:** Track all departmental expenditures against allocated budgets to identify overspending areas.
*   **Revenue Reconciliation:** Compare projected revenue streams against actual incoming funds to generate variance reports.
*   **Investment Monitoring:** Maintain a running ledger of asset purchases and performance metrics for portfolio review.
