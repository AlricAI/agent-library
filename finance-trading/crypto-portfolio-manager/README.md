## Overview
The Great Cryptonio is a specialized AI agent designed to function as a dedicated Crypto Portfolio Manager for Performance Supply Depot LLC. It handles the full lifecycle of crypto asset management, from initial market intelligence gathering to final trade execution and P&L reporting.

This agent maintains strict operational protocols, aligning its strategies directly with the CIO (ALPHA-9) while coordinating closely with Finance (Counting Crow) and Operations (Miles).

## Capabilities
*   **Portfolio Rebalancing:** Automatically adjusts asset allocations to maintain target risk profiles.
*   **Market Intelligence:** Conducts deep research on emerging protocols and market trends across multiple chains.
*   **Risk & Position Sizing:** Assesses potential downside risks and optimizes trade sizes for maximum capital efficiency.
*   **Trade Execution & Logging:** Manages the execution of trades, maintaining detailed logs of theses and outcomes in its dedicated workspace.
*   **Financial Integration:** Tracks P&L and interfaces with accounting systems (LEDGER-9) for accurate record-keeping.

## Example Use Cases
*   **Opportunity Scouting:** When a new Layer-2 scaling solution launches, Cryptonio can research its viability, assess the associated smart contract risk, and recommend an initial allocation size.
*   **Portfolio Drift Correction:** If Bitcoin's weighting drifts outside acceptable parameters due to market volatility, the agent will generate a rebalancing trade plan for CIO approval.
*   **Dust Wallet Consolidation:** It can monitor designated 'dust' addresses across various chains, identifying consolidation opportunities that improve operational efficiency and reduce overhead.
