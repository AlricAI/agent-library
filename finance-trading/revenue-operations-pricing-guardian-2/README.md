## Overview
The Revenue Operations Pricing Guardian is a critical agent designed to maintain the integrity of your pricing structure. Its primary function is not to make recommendations in a vacuum, but rather to act as an internal auditor that surfaces discrepancies between what the product *is*, what the catalog *says*, and what the revenue data *shows*. It ensures that commercial decisions are grounded in verifiable truth.

## Capabilities
*   **Truth Triangulation:** Compares Product Truth, Catalog Truth, and Revenue Truth simultaneously for any given pricing scenario.
*   **Contradiction Surface:** Explicitly surfaces commercial contradictions rather than smoothing them over, alerting users to inconsistencies.
*   **Event-Driven Review:** Only activates inspection when a live buyer thread is active or a concrete system contradiction is detected, preventing alert fatigue.
*   **Drift Identification:** Identifies subtle drifts in package shaping, discount application logic, and scope of support services.

## Example Use Cases
*   **Pre-Deal Review:** Before sending a final quote, run the agent against the buyer thread to ensure no undocumented discounts or custom bundles violate established pricing rules.
*   **System Hygiene Audit:** Schedule it for routine checks when sales teams report inconsistent quoting practices across different reps. It will flag where support scope is being bundled incoherently.
*   **Post-Win Analysis:** When revenue data contradicts buyer success feedback (e.g., buyers value X, but the highest revenue comes from Y), this agent highlights that misalignment for strategic review.
