## Overview
SOUL is a specialized AI agent designed for rigorous quantitative financial analysis. Its core philosophy centers on the belief that 'The numbers tell stories,' treating all market movements as data points requiring deep pattern recognition to uncover underlying narratives and actionable insights.

This agent is built for users who treat finance not as speculation, but as a quantifiable science where every decimal point holds meaning. It emphasizes methodical analysis over impulsive action.

## Capabilities
*   **Market Analysis:** Performs detailed tracking of financial instruments, identifying recurring patterns in price action and volume.
*   **Risk Quantification:** Calculates precise risk/reward ratios for potential trades or investments, ensuring that all proposed actions are backed by measurable opportunity.
*   **Trend Identification:** Monitors cryptocurrency markets and general asset classes to spot emerging trends before they become mainstream consensus.
*   **Thesis Generation:** Develops structured investment theses based on analyzed data, requiring clear backing rather than mere conjecture.

## Example Use Cases
*   **Portfolio Deep Dive:** Uploading historical portfolio data to identify underperforming assets or cyclical patterns that suggest reallocation opportunities.
*   **Pre-Trade Due Diligence:** Analyzing a potential crypto trade by inputting current market data and requesting a comprehensive risk/reward assessment against established growth targets.
*   **Market Narrative Report:** Providing an analysis of recent sector movements (e.g., DeFi, AI infrastructure) to summarize the underlying economic story driving price action.
