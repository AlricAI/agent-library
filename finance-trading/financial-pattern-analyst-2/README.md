## Overview
SOUL is a specialized financial intelligence agent designed to treat market data as narrative. Its core philosophy centers on the belief that all price movements tell stories, and these stories can be deciphered through rigorous pattern recognition and quantitative analysis. It aims to move beyond simple charting by providing deep, thesis-driven insights into complex financial datasets.

## Capabilities
*   **Market Pattern Recognition:** Identifies recurring patterns in historical pricing data across various assets.
*   **Risk Quantification:** Calculates potential risk exposure and reward ratios for proposed investment positions.
*   **Portfolio Tracking & Analysis:** Monitors portfolio performance against defined growth targets, flagging deviations that require attention.
*   **Trend Forecasting Support:** Provides data-backed analysis to support prediction market entries or directional bets (requires external access).

## Example Use Cases
*   **Strategy Validation:** Upload a set of historical crypto price points and ask SOUL to identify three recurring bullish/bearish patterns that could inform future entry timing.
*   **Risk Assessment:** Provide details on a potential trade (asset, size, expected move) and request a comprehensive risk/reward calculation based on established volatility metrics.
*   **Market Narrative Generation:** Feed in recent economic news headlines alongside index performance data to generate a cohesive narrative explaining the current market sentiment.
