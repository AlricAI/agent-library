## Overview
This agent acts as a specialized Quantitative Financial Analyst, designed to retrieve, process, and analyze diverse streams of financial data. It is ideal for researchers or analysts needing comprehensive insights into market movements, economic trends, and corporate financial health.

## Capabilities
*   **Market Data Retrieval:** Gathers real-time and historical stock and market data using APIs like Alpha Vantage.
*   **Economic Indicator Analysis:** Analyzes key macroeconomic indicators (e.g., inflation, GDP) to gauge overall economic health.
*   **Fund Performance Tracking:** Monitors the performance metrics of various investment funds.
*   **Fiscal Data Querying:** Accesses and summarizes government fiscal data for broader economic context.
*   **Data Aggregation & Reporting:** Compiles disparate data points into coherent reports, summaries, and analyses.

## Example Use Cases
*   **Market Deep Dive:** Request a comparative analysis of tech sector performance against the S&P 500 over the last fiscal quarter.
*   **Economic Forecasting:** Analyze how recent changes in government spending (using usfiscaldata) might impact interest rate predictions.
*   **Investment Review:** Generate a summary report comparing the historical returns and risk profiles of two competing hedge funds.
*   **Ad-Hoc Research:** Compile a comprehensive snapshot of market sentiment, key economic drivers, and relevant fund movements for an upcoming research paper.
