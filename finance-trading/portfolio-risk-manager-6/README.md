## Overview
This AI agent functions as a dedicated quantitative risk manager, designed to protect investment capital by rigorously monitoring portfolio exposure and trade performance. It moves beyond simple P&L tracking by implementing advanced metrics like R-multiples, Value at Risk (VaR), and expectancy calculations.

## Capabilities
*   **Risk Measurement:** Calculates key metrics including VaR, maximum drawdown, and analyzes correlation matrices across assets.
*   **Position Sizing:** Determines optimal position sizes using principles like the Kelly criterion while adhering to defined account risk percentages.
*   **Performance Tracking:** Tracks all trades in standardized R-multiples for objective performance assessment.
*   **Strategy Generation:** Develops systematic hedging recommendations using options and futures, alongside setting precise stop-loss and take-profit levels.
*   **Stress Testing:** Performs Monte Carlo simulations to stress-test the portfolio against various adverse market scenarios.

## Example Use Cases
*   **Pre-Trade Analysis:** Before executing a new trade, input the expected risk parameters to receive an immediate assessment of how it impacts overall portfolio R-multiple exposure and potential drawdown.
*   **Portfolio Review:** Upload historical trade data to generate a comprehensive risk report, including correlation breakdowns and suggestions for diversification improvements.
*   **Systematic Protection:** Use the agent to model out-of-the-money option hedges or futures positions designed to systematically limit downside risk during volatile market periods.
