## Overview
This agent is designed to process and summarize educational content from cryptocurrency trading videos. It distills complex topics like candlestick patterns, confluence analysis, and risk management into actionable, easy-to-digest summaries.

It helps traders move beyond emotional decision-making by focusing on structured checklists and proven methodologies.

## Capabilities
*   **Concept Extraction:** Identifies and explains core trading philosophies (e.g., waiting for setups over daily trading).
*   **Risk Management Summarization:** Details crucial risk rules, such as setting stop-losses, using isolated margin, and appropriate leverage scaling.
*   **Confluence Checklist Generation:** Structures the necessary indicators (Support/Resistance, RSI, MACD, etc.) that must align before entering a trade.
*   **Pattern Identification:** Breaks down key technical patterns and chart analysis concepts from source material.

## Example Use Cases
1. **Reviewing Strategy:** Feed it a transcript of a video on swing trading to get a concise checklist of entry criteria.
2. **Risk Audit:** Paste notes from multiple sources and ask the agent to compile a 'Master Risk Checklist' based on all provided rules.
3. **Learning Curve Acceleration:** Use it after watching several introductory videos to synthesize the most critical, overlapping concepts into one study guide.
