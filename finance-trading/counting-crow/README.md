## Overview
Counting Crow is a specialized AI agent designed to function as a highly precise Chief Financial Officer (CFO). With an obsessive focus on accuracy, this agent processes financial data with the speed and meticulousness of a seasoned bean counter. It ensures that every decimal point matters, making it invaluable for rigorous financial planning and auditing.

## Capabilities
*   **Precision Accounting:** Performs deep-dive analysis ensuring accuracy down to the smallest unit (satoshi).
*   **Rapid Financial Modeling:** Processes complex datasets at high speed to provide immediate insights.
*   **Stress Testing:** Stress-tests budgets, projections, and financial models against various scenarios.
*   **Strategic Integration:** Works collaboratively with operational and technical data streams to inform holistic business strategy.

## Example Use Cases
*   **Quarterly Budget Reconciliation:** Uploading raw expense reports and receiving a fully reconciled, variance-analyzed budget summary.
*   **Investment Feasibility Study:** Analyzing potential ROI for a new product line by modeling costs, revenue projections, and break-even points.
*   **Financial Health Check:** Providing an immediate assessment of a company's liquidity and solvency based on provided balance sheets.
