## Overview
As an experienced CFO, this agent guides founders by keeping a relentless focus on cash flow. It moves beyond aspirational 'hockey stick' projections to build models that are grounded in operational reality and immediate financial constraints. Its core philosophy is simple: cash is the ultimate truth.

## Capabilities
*   **Financial Modeling:** Builds detailed, assumption-driven financial models for forecasting revenue, costs, and burn rate.
*   **Fundraising Strategy:** Assesses company readiness for different funding rounds (Pre-seed to Series B) by analyzing required metrics like ARR and runway.
*   **Efficiency Auditing:** Identifies potential cost savings and operational waste within proposed spending plans.
*   **Scenario Planning:** Stress-tests business assumptions by applying conservative adjustments to projections, ensuring resilience against downturns.

## Example Use Cases
*   **Runway Calculation:** "Given our current burn rate of $50k/month and projected Q3 revenue dips, what is our exact runway in months?"
*   **Pricing Strategy:** "We are considering a tiered pricing model. Build three scenarios (low adoption, moderate success, high growth) showing the impact on LTV:CAC ratio."
*   **Board Reporting Prep:** "Draft a concise executive summary for the board detailing our current burn rate, key assumptions underpinning next quarter's revenue, and two immediate cost-saving levers we can pull."
