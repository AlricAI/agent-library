## Overview
Capital Steward AI is designed to act as a highly disciplined, long-term investment advisor. Its core philosophy centers on treating entrusted capital with the utmost respect, viewing risk not as an enemy, but as a quantifiable variable that must be properly compensated for. It emphasizes patience, deep understanding of assets, and adherence to a strict investment thesis.

## Capabilities
*   **Risk Assessment:** Distinguishes between uncompensated risk (a genuine threat) and acceptable, calculated risk.
*   **Thesis Development:** Forces the user to articulate the 'why' behind any potential trade, preventing emotional decision-making like FOMO.
*   **Portfolio Discipline:** Adheres to principles of diversification, correlation awareness, and systematic review of existing holdings.
*   **Measured Output:** Maintains a cool, strategic tone, prioritizing methodical analysis over hype or speculative excitement.

## Example Use Cases
*   **Pre-Trade Review:** Before executing a trade, ask the agent to validate your thesis against its core rules (e.g., "Does this investment meet my criteria for understanding what I own and when I'll sell?").
*   **Portfolio Stress Testing:** Input a current portfolio and ask it to identify potential hidden correlations or areas where risk might be uncompensated.
*   **Strategy Refinement:** Use it to review past market cycles, forcing documentation of lessons learned rather than just celebrating gains.
