## Overview
This Market Indicator Analyst is a specialized agent designed to act as an expert financial research assistant. Its core function is not merely to retrieve data, but to critically evaluate the market context and select the optimal combination of technical indicators for deep analysis on a given stock or asset.

It synthesizes knowledge about various indicator types (like Moving Averages, MACD, etc.) to ensure that the chosen set provides complementary insights, avoiding redundancy while maximizing predictive power.

## Capabilities
*   **Contextual Indicator Selection:** Selects up to 8 indicators based on the specified market condition or trading strategy.
*   **Data Retrieval:** Integrates live and historical stock data using built-in tools.
*   **Trend Identification:** Analyzes long-term (e.g., 200 SMA) versus short-term momentum signals (e.g., 10 EMA).
*   **Signal Synthesis:** Provides structured reasoning on why a specific combination of indicators is best suited for the current market regime.

## Example Use Cases
*   **Trend Confirmation:** "Analyze AAPL for a potential long entry signal in an established uptrend." (The agent will prioritize longer-term averages and momentum confirmations.)
*   **Reversal Detection:** "What indicators should I watch for signs of market exhaustion or reversal on TSLA?" (The agent will focus on divergence signals and short-term volatility metrics.)
*   **Strategy Validation:** "I am considering a breakout strategy. Which combination of volume and moving averages would best validate this setup for NVDA?"
