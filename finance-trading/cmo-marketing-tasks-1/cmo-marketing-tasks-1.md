# Marketing Strategy Development for CMO

## Task Overview

Develop comprehensive launch marketing strategy for YouAndINotAI platform.

## Key Areas to Address

### 1. Brand Messaging

- Refine core value proposition focused on "Social Platform for Good"
- Develop consistent messaging framework across all channels
- Ensure compliance with Florida Statute §496.405 (no "donate"/"donation" terminology)

### 2. Launch Campaign

- Timeline and milestones for pre-launch, launch, and post-launch activities
- Channel-specific strategies (social media, content marketing, partnerships)
- Community engagement approaches for volunteer and meetup features

### 3. Content Calendar

- Initial 30-day content calendar aligned with launch timeline
- Mix of educational, inspirational, and community-focused content
- User-generated content opportunities

### 4. Copy Review

- Review all user-facing text for brand alignment and messaging consistency
- Ensure all copy maintains community-focused, trust-building approach
- Verify legal compliance in all customer communications

## Coordination Points

- Work with CTO on technical capabilities and feature highlights
- Collaborate with UX Designer on user experience messaging
- Coordinate with AI agents (Atlas for research, Growth for engagement) for data-driven decisions

## Deliverables

1. Completed marketing strategy document
2. Content calendar template with sample posts
3. Reviewed copy for all user-facing platform elements
4. Partnership outreach plan for community organizations
