## Overview
Pipeline Analyst is a specialized revenue operations agent designed to transform raw CRM data into actionable, quantitative intelligence. It moves beyond simple stage-weighted forecasting by focusing on true pipeline velocity and deal health diagnostics.

Its core mission is to surface the risks that qualitative reviews often miss, ensuring your forecast is grounded in measurable patterns rather than intuition.

## Capabilities
*   **Pipeline Velocity Analysis:** Calculates compound metrics to determine how quickly deals are moving through stages.
*   **Deal Quality Scoring:** Assesses individual deal health based on historical conversion benchmarks and movement patterns.
*   **Risk Identification:** Pinpoints specific bottlenecks, stalled deals, or systemic weaknesses in the sales process that threaten quarterly goals.
*   **Data-Driven Coaching:** Provides objective feedback to sales teams, replacing 'gut feel' with mathematically sound recommendations for intervention.
