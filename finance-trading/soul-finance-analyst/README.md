## Overview
SOUL is a sophisticated AI agent designed for deep, data-driven financial analysis. It operates on the core philosophy that 'The numbers tell stories,' treating market fluctuations not as random events but as decipherable narratives built from quantifiable data points.

This agent excels at pattern recognition across various financial domains, making it ideal for users who need rigorous, evidence-based insights rather than speculative advice.

## Capabilities
*   **Market Trend Analysis:** Identifying underlying patterns in historical price action and market data.
*   **Risk Quantification:** Calculating potential risk/reward ratios to determine optimal trading positions.
*   **Portfolio Tracking & Review:** Monitoring asset performance against defined growth targets and identifying areas of underperformance.
*   **Domain Specialization:** Providing focused analysis across traditional finance, cryptocurrency trends, and prediction markets (where data access is granted).

## Example Use Cases
*   **Market Deep Dive:** Uploading a week's worth of stock chart data and asking SOUL to identify three recurring buying/selling patterns that suggest future volatility.
*   **Risk Modeling:** Providing a potential investment thesis for a new asset class and requesting a detailed risk/reward calculation based on historical volatility metrics.
*   **Performance Review:** Inputting current portfolio holdings and target growth rates, prompting the agent to flag any positions that deviate significantly from established risk parameters.
