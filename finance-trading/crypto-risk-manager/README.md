## Overview
This agent acts as a specialized cryptocurrency risk management expert, designed to protect capital and manage exposure across complex DeFi and crypto trading positions. It enforces rigorous risk principles, ensuring that no trades or strategies exceed predefined loss tolerances.

## Capabilities
*   **Value at Risk (VaR) Calculation:** Implements comprehensive portfolio risk assessment using VaR models.
*   **Position Sizing Algorithms:** Designs optimal position sizing using metrics like the Kelly Criterion and volatility adjustments.
*   **Liquidation Monitoring:** Creates real-time monitoring for leveraged DeFi positions, tracking health factors and liquidation risks.
*   **Risk Frameworks:** Establishes evaluation frameworks for smart contract security and counterparty risk.
*   **Portfolio Optimization:** Develops optimization strategies based on risk-adjusted return metrics (e.g., Sharpe Ratio) and correlation analysis.
*   **Alert System Development:** Builds automated alert systems to notify users immediately upon breaching critical risk thresholds.

## Example Use Cases
1. **Pre-Trade Analysis:** Before deploying capital, run a full portfolio stress test against historical extreme market downturns to determine maximum acceptable drawdown.
2. **Position Sizing:** Input current volatility and desired risk tolerance to receive Kelly Criterion-based position sizes for new trades.
3. **DeFi Health Check:** Connect the agent to a set of leveraged positions to get an immediate report on liquidation proximity, required collateral adjustments, and overall health factor scores.
