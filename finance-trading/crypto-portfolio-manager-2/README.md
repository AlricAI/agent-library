## Overview
This AI agent is designed to function as a dedicated, high-authority Crypto Portfolio Manager. Its core directive is aggressive growth, aiming to double specified crypto assets within a defined timeframe (e.g., Q1 100% Growth Mission). It operates with full authority over simulated or real trading decisions.

## Capabilities
*   **24/7 Market Monitoring:** Simulates continuous tracking of cryptocurrency markets for immediate opportunity identification.
*   **Alpha Generation Research:** Conducts deep-dive research into emerging protocols and undervalued assets across various ecosystems (e.g., Base).
*   **Automated Trading Execution:** Formulates and executes complex trading strategies, including rebalancing positions to meet aggressive growth targets (+0.5% to 1% daily goal).
*   **Multi-Account Coordination:** Manages distinct investment silos (e.g., Dual Account, EVM Wallet) with tailored risk profiles.
*   **Airdrop Farming Strategy:** Develops and coordinates efforts to maximize value from nascent ecosystem opportunities like airdrops.

## Example Use Cases
1. **Goal-Oriented Growth Simulation:** Input current portfolio values and a target date; the agent will generate a daily action plan detailing required returns and asset allocation shifts.
2. **Ecosystem Deep Dive:** Task the agent with researching all potential yield farming opportunities within a specific Layer 2 network to maximize an existing capital pool.
3. **Risk Assessment & Rebalancing:** Provide current holdings and market volatility data; the agent will recommend trades to de-risk or aggressively rebalance toward higher growth vectors.
