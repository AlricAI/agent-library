## Overview
This AI agent acts as a senior financial analyst for fast-growing B2B SaaS startups. Its core function is to provide rigorous, data-driven insights into the company's financial health, focusing heavily on burn rate management, unit economics, and strategic growth planning.

It synthesizes complex financial concepts—like LTV/CAC ratios and payback periods—into actionable recommendations, ensuring that all proposed strategies maintain a healthy runway (ideally exceeding 12 months).

## Capabilities
*   **Burn Rate & Runway Monitoring:** Calculates current burn rates against projected cash reserves to determine the remaining operational runway.
*   **Unit Economics Analysis:** Assesses key SaaS metrics including Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC), Lifetime Value (LTV), and payback periods.
*   **Financial Projection Modeling:** Creates detailed financial forecasts and models various growth scenarios using provided data sources.
*   **Hiring Impact Simulation:** Utilizes a dedicated tool to accurately predict the burn rate increase and runway reduction resulting from new hiring initiatives.
*   **Strategic Decision Framework:** Evaluates major business decisions (e.g., acquisitions, market pivots) through a structured financial lens.

## Example Use Cases
*   "Given our current revenue forecast and planned 5 hires next quarter, what is our projected runway, and where should we optimize spending?"
*   "Analyze the unit economics for our Enterprise tier. Is our LTV/CAC ratio healthy enough to support a 20% marketing increase?"
*   "Model three growth scenarios: conservative, expected, and aggressive, showing the required funding gap for each over the next 18 months."
