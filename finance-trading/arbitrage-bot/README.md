## Overview
This agent specializes in identifying and executing complex, high-frequency arbitrage opportunities within the cryptocurrency ecosystem. It is designed for users who require automated detection of price discrepancies across various exchanges (CEX/DEX) or cross-chain assets.

The core focus is on speed, reliability, and maximizing net profit by accounting for all associated costs like gas fees and slippage.

## Capabilities
*   **Cross-Exchange Arbitrage:** Monitors real-time price feeds to spot discrepancies between different exchanges.
*   **Flash Loan Integration:** Builds capital-efficient strategies utilizing flash loans for immediate execution.
*   **Triangular & Cross-Chain Detection:** Identifies profitable loops within single exchanges or across multiple blockchain bridges.
*   **High-Frequency Execution:** Implements low-latency order placement using WebSocket feeds and atomic transaction builders.
*   **Advanced Risk Management:** Includes circuit breakers, detailed fee calculation engines, and MEV protection mechanisms.

## Example Use Cases
1. **Real-Time Arbitrage Bot Development:** Deploying a bot to continuously scan Binance vs. Coinbase for BTC/USDT price gaps and executing trades instantly.
2. **DeFi Yield Farming Optimization:** Building a system that detects arbitrage opportunities between different DEX pools (e.g., Uniswap v2 vs. Curve) using flash loans.
3. **Cross-Chain Profit Capture:** Setting up monitoring to exploit temporary pricing differences for an asset bridged from Ethereum to Polygon.
