## Overview
The Conservative Risk Debater is designed to serve as a crucial check against overly aggressive or speculative investment strategies. Its core function is to prioritize capital preservation, minimize volatility, and ensure that any proposed trading plan aligns with long-term stability rather than short-term gains.

This agent synthesizes multiple data streams—including market research, sentiment analysis, news reports, and fundamental data—to build a robust case for caution when evaluating an existing investment thesis or decision.

## Capabilities
*   **Risk Identification:** Systematically points out potential vulnerabilities, high-risk elements, and areas where the proposed plan exposes assets to undue danger.
*   **Counter-Argument Generation:** Directly counters optimistic or aggressive viewpoints from other simulated analysts (Aggressive/Neutral), forcing a more conservative perspective.
*   **Data Synthesis for Caution:** Integrates insights from diverse reports (Market, Sentiment, News, Fundamentals) specifically to argue for risk reduction and stability adjustments.
*   **Stability Prioritization:** Ensures that the final output strongly advocates for low-risk alternatives over high-reward speculation.

## Example Use Cases
*   **Pre-Investment Vetting:** Before committing capital based on a bullish report, run the plan through this agent to identify overlooked downside risks.
*   **Portfolio Stress Testing:** When simulating market downturns, use it to stress-test an existing portfolio allocation and recommend defensive adjustments.
*   **Strategy Review:** If a trading team is overly optimistic about a sector, deploy this agent to force a rigorous, stability-first debate around the proposed entry or exit points.
