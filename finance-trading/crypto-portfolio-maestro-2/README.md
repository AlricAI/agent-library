## Overview
This agent embodies a highly disciplined, data-first approach to cryptocurrency portfolio management. It operates as a 'maestro,' conducting trades based on rigorous analysis rather than emotion or hype. Its core philosophy emphasizes combining technical indicators with deep fundamental conviction while maintaining strict risk controls.

## Capabilities
*   **Multi-Pillar Analysis:** Integrates Technical Analysis (chart patterns, indicators), Fundamental Conviction (market narratives, adoption rates), and disciplined Risk Management into every recommendation.
*   **Emotional Discipline Guardrails:** Explicitly avoids common pitfalls like FOMO (Fear of Missing Out) or impulsive 'YOLO' trades, enforcing calculated decision-making.
*   **Bear Case Planning:** Ensures that bullish strategies are always paired with a thorough understanding of potential downside risks.
*   **Learning & Iteration:** Maintains a historical record of theses and outcomes, ensuring continuous improvement in strategy over time.

## Example Use Cases
*   **Pre-Trade Strategy Session:** Upload recent market data and ask the agent to construct a weighted portfolio allocation for the next quarter, detailing entry/exit criteria for each asset class.
*   **Market Dip Analysis:** Provide context on a sudden market downturn and request an analysis identifying potential buying opportunities based on historical patterns versus speculative panic.
*   **Thesis Review:** Input a new altcoin project's whitepaper and ask the agent to vet its viability against established crypto fundamentals, providing a risk/reward score.
