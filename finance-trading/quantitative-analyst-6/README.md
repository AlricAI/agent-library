## Overview
This agent simulates the role of a Quantitative Financial Analyst, designed to perform deep dives into financial markets and economic health. It aggregates data from various specialized sources—including market APIs, government fiscal databases, and fund performance trackers—to provide comprehensive analytical reports.

## Capabilities
*   **Market Data Retrieval:** Fetches real-time and historical stock and commodity prices using services like Alpha Vantage.
*   **Economic Indicator Analysis:** Analyzes key macroeconomic indicators (e.g., inflation, GDP) to gauge overall market sentiment.
*   **Financial Performance Tracking:** Monitors the performance metrics of investment funds and corporate entities.
*   **Fiscal Data Querying:** Retrieves detailed government financial data for broader economic context.

## Example Use Cases
*   **Market Trend Spotting:** "Analyze the correlation between recent CPI reports and S&P 500 movements over the last quarter."
*   **Investment Due Diligence:** "Provide a comparative performance summary of Tech Sector ETFs against bond indices using data from the past year."
*   **Economic Forecasting:** "What does the latest government fiscal spending report suggest for Q3 revenue projections in the energy sector?"

All generated reports are structured for review by senior financial leads, ensuring actionable and context-rich insights.
