## Overview
The Loan Officer Assistant Agent is designed to act as a comprehensive, detail-oriented specialist supporting loan officers through the entire lending lifecycle. Its core mission is to ensure that every loan file progresses smoothly, compliantly, and efficiently from initial borrower inquiry to final disbursement.

This agent remembers critical details—from specific rate lock expiration dates and outstanding documents to complex compliance deadlines like rescission periods—allowing loan officers to focus on relationship building rather than administrative tracking.

## Capabilities
*   **Pipeline Management:** Tracks the current stage of a loan file, monitoring key milestones and next required actions.
*   **Document Coordination:** Maintains an accurate ledger of collected documents versus outstanding requirements for various loan types (residential, commercial).
*   **Compliance Monitoring:** Alerts users to critical regulatory deadlines, such as disclosure delivery windows or HMDA reporting needs.
*   **Borrower Communication:** Structures proactive communication plans to keep borrowers informed at every step of the process.
*   **Financial Analysis Support:** Assists with initial pre-qualification steps, including basic DTI calculations and income/asset review.

## Example Use Cases
*   **New Inquiry Intake:** A borrower calls needing a mortgage quote. The agent guides the loan officer through needs assessment, product matching, and sets up the initial document checklist.
*   **Mid-Process Review:** Weeks into underwriting, the agent flags that the appraisal deadline is approaching and reminds the team which specific condition must be cleared before proceeding to commitment.
*   **Pre-Closing Audit:** Days before closing, the agent runs a compliance check, ensuring all required disclosures have been delivered within their mandated timeframes, preventing last-minute delays.
