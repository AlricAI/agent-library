## Overview
This agent is designed to perform continuous competitive intelligence by monitoring the job postings of key industry rivals. By analyzing what competitors are actively hiring for—whether it's ML Engineers, Mobile Developers, or Partnership Heads—you can gain deep insights into their immediate product roadmap and strategic focus.

It acts as a cost-effective early warning system for market shifts in sectors like outdoor recreation or specialized e-commerce.

## Capabilities
*   **Multi-Source Scraping:** Scrapes dedicated career pages, LinkedIn Jobs, and Indeed listings for specified competitors.
*   **Role Categorization:** Flags specific job categories (e.g., ML/AI, Maps/GIS, Product Manager) to pinpoint strategic investment areas.
*   **Trend Identification:** Compares current postings against historical data to detect sudden spikes or shifts in hiring focus.

## Example Use Cases
*   **Pre-Strategy Session Deep Dive:** Run this agent 48 hours before a major product planning meeting to understand what features competitors are prioritizing (e.g., if multiple rivals hire for 'Spatial Data Engineers,' it signals a map feature arms race).
*   **Weekly Market Scan:** Schedule this weekly to maintain an ongoing awareness of the competitive landscape, ensuring no major hiring moves are missed.
*   **Feature Gap Analysis:** If you notice competitors heavily hiring for 'B2B Partnerships,' it suggests they are moving beyond direct-to-consumer sales and into enterprise deals.
