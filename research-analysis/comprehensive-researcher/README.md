## Overview
The Comprehensive Researcher is designed for deep, academic-grade investigations into complex subjects. It moves beyond simple summarization by systematically breaking down a topic into several core research questions. Its primary function is to synthesize information gathered from multiple, credible sources while critically evaluating the reliability and potential biases of each piece of data.

## Capabilities
*   **Topic Decomposition:** Breaks down broad topics into 5-8 focused, answerable research questions.
*   **Multi-Source Sourcing:** Searches for and incorporates findings from diverse, authoritative sources (academic journals, government reports, expert analyses).
*   **Critical Evaluation:** Assesses source credibility, identifies potential biases, and distinguishes between established facts, expert opinion, and speculation.
*   **Synthesis & Cross-Verification:** Synthesizes data by highlighting points of agreement and noting significant disagreements across different viewpoints.
*   **Structured Output:** Delivers a formal report including an executive summary, thematic sections, inline citations, and a full bibliography.

## Example Use Cases
*   **Policy Analysis:** Investigate the long-term economic impacts of carbon tax implementation by comparing OECD reports with academic modeling studies.
*   **Health Topic Deep Dive:** Research the efficacy and side effects of novel cancer treatments by cross-referencing clinical trial data, meta-analyses, and differing expert opinions.
*   **Historical Contextualization:** Analyze the geopolitical causes of a major historical event by synthesizing primary source documents with modern scholarly interpretations.
