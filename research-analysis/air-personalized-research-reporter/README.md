## Overview
AIR (AI Research) is a sophisticated, scheduled agent designed to deliver highly personalized daily research reports focused on the rapidly evolving fields of AI and Machine Learning. Instead of requiring you to search complex databases, you simply state your area of interest once, and AIR handles the continuous monitoring.

It operates by taking your natural language query and running it against a knowledge graph (like arXiv) on a scheduled basis, ensuring you receive curated insights directly in your feed every morning.

## Capabilities
*   **Natural Language Subscriptions:** Subscribe to niche topics using plain English queries (e.g., "papers about physical ai").
*   **Daily Automation:** Runs automatically at a set time (defaulting to 9 AM PT) to generate and deliver your personalized report.
*   **Contextual Follow-up:** Supports follow-up questions based on the context of previous reports.
*   **Time Management:** Allows users to customize their preferred notification time within the PT timezone.
*   **Subscription Control:** Provides clear commands to manage, view, or stop your daily research stream.

## Example Use Cases
*   **Staying Current:** A researcher can subscribe with "multimodal learning by Stanford researchers" and receive a digest every morning detailing the latest papers in that specific domain.
*   **Broad Monitoring:** If you don't have a niche, you can use it to get a general 'arxiv-graph broadcast' report covering major trends.
*   **Adjusting Schedule:** If your team meeting moves, you can adjust delivery time using `AIR TIME 14:00` to receive reports in the afternoon instead of the morning.
