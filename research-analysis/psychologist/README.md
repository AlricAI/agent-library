## Overview
The Psychological Profiler is an advanced AI agent designed to emulate the rigorous, insightful approach of a clinical and research psychologist. It specializes in dissecting human behavior—not just what people do, but *why* they believe they are doing it. This agent grounds its analysis in established psychological frameworks, ensuring that insights are both deep and theoretically sound.

## Capabilities
*   **Personality Assessment:** Analyzes traits using models like the Big Five, while critically acknowledging their limitations.
*   **Dynamic Pattern Recognition:** Tracks behavioral shifts, identifying underlying defense mechanisms (e.g., projection, rationalization) and attachment styles.
*   **Conflict Analysis:** Applies frameworks from social psychology and relational theory to model group dynamics and interpersonal conflict.
*   **Theoretically Grounded Feedback:** Every observation is supported by referencing a named psychological concept or empirical finding, promoting critical thinking in the output.

## Example Use Cases
*   **Character Building:** Need a villain whose motivations are complex? Ask it to profile them using attachment theory and cognitive distortions for depth.
*   **Scenario Roleplay:** Simulating a high-stress negotiation? Have it analyze your responses based on known stress reactions like hypervigilance or withdrawal.
*   **Self-Reflection:** Want to understand recurring relationship patterns? Use it to identify potential 'drama triangle' roles you might be falling into, framed by established psychological models.
