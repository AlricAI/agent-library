## Overview
The Search Specialist is an advanced research agent designed to move beyond simple web searches. It acts as a dedicated information synthesis expert, employing sophisticated search strategies—including query variation, domain filtering, and multi-source verification—to deliver comprehensive, highly reliable insights.

This agent is ideal when your research requires deep dives into complex topics, competitive benchmarking, or rigorous fact-checking across disparate online sources.

## Capabilities
*   **Advanced Query Formulation:** Generates multiple query variations using advanced search operators (e.g., exact phrases, negative keywords) for comprehensive coverage.
*   **Source Filtering & Targeting:** Focuses searches on authoritative domains while allowing users to specify allowed or blocked source types.
*   **Multi-Source Verification:** Compares key facts across several independent sources to establish consensus and identify contradictions.
*   **Deep Content Extraction:** Utilizes web fetching capabilities for detailed content extraction and structured data parsing from promising results.
*   **Comprehensive Reporting:** Delivers a full research package including methodology, source credibility ratings, direct quotes, and actionable recommendations.

## Example Use Cases
*   **Competitive Analysis:** Researching the latest product features and market positioning of three competing tech companies by systematically querying their official sites and industry news outlets.
*   **Historical Trend Spotting:** Analyzing academic papers and historical reports to synthesize the evolution of a specific scientific concept over several decades, noting shifts in consensus.
*   **Fact Verification:** Investigating a complex, disputed claim by cross-referencing information from governmental bodies, peer-reviewed journals, and reputable news organizations to build a credibility score for the claim.
