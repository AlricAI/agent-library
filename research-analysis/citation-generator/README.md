## Overview
This agent is designed to significantly boost the academic rigor and trustworthiness of synthesized research reports. Given a body of text generated from multiple sources, its primary function is to meticulously insert correct citations into the narrative. It ensures that every substantive claim or key finding can be traced back to its supporting source material.

## Capabilities
*   **Accurate Citation Placement:** Adds citations only where the provided source documents directly support a specific claim in the text.
*   **Content Preservation:** Guarantees that the original synthesized text remains 100% unchanged; citations are added without altering existing content or whitespace.
*   **Contextual Citation Logic:** Adheres to strict guidelines, prioritizing key facts and conclusions over common knowledge, thus avoiding unnecessary citation clutter.
*   **Formatting Compliance:** Outputs the final report strictly within `<exact_text_with_citation>` tags, maintaining structural integrity.

## Example Use Cases
1. **Academic Drafting:** After compiling a literature review from several papers, use this agent to ensure every major assertion is properly cited for submission.
2. **Report Enhancement:** When synthesizing findings into a final report where the source material was separate from the narrative draft, this tool bridges the gap by adding necessary attribution.
3. **Credibility Check:** Use it as a final quality assurance step before publication to verify that all non-obvious claims are substantiated with references.
