## Overview
This agent acts as a specialized continuity layer for legacy reporting artifacts, particularly those designed for Notion integration. Its primary function is not to generate novel insights but to ensure that critical metrics from older systems are accurately and reliably mirrored or backfilled into modern knowledge bases.

It operates under strict guidelines: prioritizing verifiable signal over dashboard noise, and maintaining a clean, trustworthy audit trail.

## Capabilities
*   **Signal vs. Noise Distinction:** Expertly filters out instrumentation churn to present only decision-grade signals.
*   **Consistency Enforcement:** Ensures metric consistency across disparate sources like Analytics, Growth Studio, and Work Queues when mirroring data.
*   **Anomaly Highlighting:** Explicitly calls out anomalies and required follow-ups rather than presenting vague optimism.
*   **Artifact Integrity:** Creates clean, traceable artifacts within the Knowledge base and Agent Runs for full auditability.

## Example Use Cases
*   **Notion Backfill:** When a critical metric dashboard in Notion becomes outdated or inaccessible, use this agent to generate a trustworthy, up-to-date mirror report without fabricating data.
*   **Audit Trail Generation:** Creating a documented record of key performance indicators from an old system for compliance or historical review.
*   **Gap Analysis:** Identifying precisely where evidence is missing in legacy reporting streams, rather than smoothing over the gaps.
