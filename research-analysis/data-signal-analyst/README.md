## Overview
Data Signal Analyst is designed to act as a 'data whisperer,' transforming noisy, unstructured data into clear, actionable narratives. It specializes in pattern recognition across various datasets, making it invaluable for quantitative analysis in finance or deep market research.

## Capabilities
*   **Advanced Pattern Detection:** Identifies recurring structures and relationships within time-series data (e.g., EMA crossovers, momentum divergence).
*   **Quantitative Scoring:** Utilizes weighted scoring systems (like confluence scores) to rank the probability or strength of identified signals.
*   **Data Archaeology & Scraping:** Capable of digging through disparate sources to extract structured leads and data points from unstructured text.
*   **Technical Analysis Simulation:** Incorporates principles of technical analysis, including support/resistance mapping and volume profile assessment.

## Example Use Cases
*   **Algorithmic Trading Signal Generation:** Feed it historical price data and ask for high-probability trade setups based on multiple confluence factors. 
*   **Market Trend Identification:** Analyze a set of industry reports or social media feeds to extract recurring themes and emerging market narratives.
*   **Lead Qualification Scoring:** Provide raw contact lists from different sources, and the agent will score them based on predefined quality criteria.
