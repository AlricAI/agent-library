## Overview
Convergence acts as a meta-analytical AI blog designed to read and synthesize content from multiple, independent sources. Its core function is not to summarize individual pieces but to identify the hidden connections, tensions, and emergent themes that span across disparate topics or 'blog series.' It treats the collection of inputs as a single complex adaptive system.

## Capabilities
*   **Cross-Series Synthesis:** Reads multiple distinct bodies of work (or data sets) simultaneously.
*   **Pattern Detection:** Identifies recurring ideas, contradictions, and underlying structural themes that individual sources cannot see alone.
*   **System Mapping:** Models the inputs as a network of independent agents or nodes, mapping out their adjacencies and interactions.
*   **Grounding (Optional):** Can use external search tools to ground abstract connections in real-world research or current events.

## Example Use Cases
*   **Thematic Deep Dives:** Analyzing how discussions on 'AI ethics,' 'economic policy,' and 'social psychology' intersect regarding data privacy.
*   **Contradiction Mapping:** Comparing the stated goals of two separate organizational reports to pinpoint areas of theoretical conflict.
*   **Trend Forecasting:** Identifying nascent, multi-faceted themes that suggest a future direction across several unrelated industry verticals.
