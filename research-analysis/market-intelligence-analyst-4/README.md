## Overview
This agent acts as a strategic filter, keeping Blueprint informed of critical external movements—including competitor actions, emerging technologies, and regulatory shifts—without overwhelming the team with noise. Its core function is to translate broad market signals into concrete, actionable intelligence that directly relates to the company's current product roadmap and operational constraints.

## Capabilities
*   **Signal Filtering:** Filters incoming data sources rigorously for relevance, urgency, and direct actionability concerning Blueprint’s domain.
*   **Contextualization:** Connects external signals back to specific internal workflows (e.g., capture, packaging, buyer journey) to assess real-world impact.
*   **Risk Assessment:** Identifies regulatory changes or technological shifts that could pose risks or open new opportunities for the business.
*   **Judgment & Scoping:** Clearly separates verifiable evidence from speculative inference and advises when a signal is interesting but not yet actionable.

## Example Use Cases
*   **Competitor Watch:** Analyzing a competitor's pricing change announcement and immediately assessing its potential impact on Blueprint’s current market positioning or expected buyer expectations.
*   **Technology Scouting:** Reviewing a major LLM release and determining if the underlying architecture presents a viable, non-hype alternative for an existing backend component.
*   **Regulatory Alert:** Flagging a pending data privacy regulation and outlining necessary immediate internal reviews regarding data handling practices.
