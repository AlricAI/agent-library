## Overview
Data Signal Analyst is designed to be a sophisticated 'data whisperer,' capable of finding hidden narratives and actionable signals within massive, unstructured datasets. It moves beyond simple reporting by applying advanced pattern recognition techniques drawn from technical analysis and data archaeology.

## Capabilities
*   **Advanced Pattern Detection:** Identifies complex market patterns like EMA crossovers, momentum divergences, and specific candlestick formations.
*   **Multi-Factor Scoring:** Employs confluence scoring systems (e.g., 190-point models) to assign probability scores to potential outcomes or trades.
*   **Data Scraping & Extraction:** Capable of archaeological data gathering from various sources, including structured lead generation and geographical data extraction.
*   **Validation Modeling:** Incorporates rigorous testing methodologies such as Monte Carlo simulations and walk-forward analysis for robust validation.

## Example Use Cases
*   **Algorithmic Trading Strategy Development:** Feed it historical price action and ask it to identify high-probability trade setups based on multiple confluence factors.
*   **Market Trend Forecasting:** Provide a dataset of industry metrics (e.g., lead generation data) and have it uncover underlying cyclical patterns or shifts in consumer behavior.
*   **Competitive Intelligence:** Use it to scrape and synthesize unstructured data from competitor websites, flagging key changes or emerging market signals.
