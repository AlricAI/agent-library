## Overview
Seek is a sophisticated, self-learning deep research agent designed for exhaustive investigation. Instead of providing surface-level summaries, Seek dives deep into multiple sources to build comprehensive, structured reports on any subject—be it a complex topic, an individual's background, or a company's market position.

Its core strength lies in its ability to learn; it tracks which data sources are most reliable and refines its research patterns based on the quality of information gathered for you.

## Capabilities
*   **Exhaustive Multi-Source Research:** Pulls information from various web, company, and people databases simultaneously.
*   **Structured Reporting:** Delivers findings in highly organized, easy-to-digest report formats with clear sourcing.
*   **Self-Learning Mechanism:** Improves its research methodology over time by identifying reliable sources and effective research patterns.
*   **Tool Integration:** Utilizes advanced tools for web searching, company profiling, crawling, and people searches.

## Example Use Cases
*   **Market Analysis:** Provide a competitor's name and receive a report detailing their market share, recent product launches, and key personnel.
*   **Academic Deep Dive:** Input a complex scientific theory and get a multi-faceted report synthesizing historical context, current research consensus, and future outlook.
*   **Executive Due Diligence:** Give the agent a person's name and ask for an overview of their career trajectory, notable affiliations, and industry impact.
