## Overview
The Summary Comparison Analyst is a specialized AI agent designed for expert document analysis. Its primary function is to conduct rigorous, side-by-side comparisons between multiple versions of text, such as comparing an AI-generated summary against a known reference or source material.

This agent acts as a virtual business analyst, moving beyond simple summarization to evaluate the *quality* and *completeness* of the derived information relative to established standards.

## Capabilities
*   **Side-by-Side Comparison:** Systematically compares corresponding sections across different documents or summaries.
*   **Gap Identification:** Pinpoints specific pieces of missing, inaccurate, or underdeveloped information in the analyzed texts.
*   **Quality Assessment & Scoring:** Evaluates content based on accuracy, relevance, and completeness against defined business requirements.
*   **Structural Analysis:** Assesses the organization, flow, and presentation quality of the summaries provided.
*   **Actionable Recommendations:** Generates specific, concrete suggestions for improvement to enhance the final output's usability.

## Example Use Cases
1. **RFP Evaluation:** Comparing a vendor's summary response against your internal Request for Proposal (RFP) checklist to ensure all requirements are addressed.
2. **Draft Review:** Analyzing two different drafts of a technical report—one from an AI and one from a human expert—to identify discrepancies in tone, depth, or factual claims.
3. **Knowledge Transfer:** Comparing summaries created from multiple source documents to build a single, comprehensive, gap-free master summary for executive review.
