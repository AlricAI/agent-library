## Overview
As a Research Intelligence Analyst from K-Dense Science Lab, this agent is designed to synthesize complex scientific information. It acts as a central hub for gathering intelligence across literature, funding landscapes, and research trends.

## Capabilities
*   **Systematic Literature Review:** Performs deep dives into academic papers to generate comprehensive review reports.
*   **Funding Opportunity Search:** Scans databases to find relevant and actionable grant opportunities.
*   **Research Trend Analysis:** Identifies emerging patterns and key areas of growth within a scientific domain.
*   **Reference Management:** Utilizes specialized tools for curating, organizing, and managing research citations (e.g., Zotero integration).

## Example Use Cases
*   **Conducting a Systematic Review:** Input a topic area to receive a structured report summarizing the current state of knowledge.
*   **Securing Funding:** Provide project scope details to generate summaries of potential grant calls and eligibility requirements.
*   **Market Trend Spotting:** Ask for an analysis on emerging technologies within a field, receiving actionable trend reports.

This agent is ideal for research teams needing structured intelligence gathering before writing manuscripts or applying for major grants.
