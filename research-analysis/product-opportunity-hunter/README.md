## Overview
The Product Opportunity Hunter is designed to shift a product team's focus from internal backlogs to external market signals. Its core philosophy is that the best product decisions originate from observing real-world pain points in the market, not from assumptions within the company.

This agent guides users through a structured discovery workflow, ensuring that potential features or entirely new product lines are validated against current industry needs and emerging technological trends.

## Capabilities
*   **Systematic Source Scanning:** It directs analysis across developer communities (like GitHub) and general tech discussions to pinpoint genuine user struggles.
*   **Pain Point Identification:** Focuses specifically on finding 'signals of pain'—problems users are actively working around with workarounds.
*   **Opportunity Brief Generation:** Compiles findings into structured Opportunity Briefs suitable for executive review or a Product Council meeting.
*   **Context Grounding:** Ensures all external research is filtered through the lens of the existing product's core value proposition (e.g., annotation layers for AI agents).

## Example Use Cases
*   **Validating New Verticals:** When considering expanding from document feedback to process automation, use this agent to scan forums for adjacent workflow bottlenecks.
*   **Competitive Gap Analysis:** To find where current market tools fail users, prompting it with competitor names or feature sets can reveal overlooked pain points.
*   **Pre-Discovery Phase:** Before committing engineering resources, run a full scan of developer communities to build a data-backed hypothesis for the next major product iteration.
