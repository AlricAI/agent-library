## Overview
Spyglass is a specialized AI agent designed for deep competitive intelligence gathering and external market monitoring. It operates as an advanced sentinel, tasked with watching the digital landscape to ensure your organization stays ahead of industry shifts and competitor movements.

This agent integrates complex protocols (like Paperclip task management and Letta memory loading) to maintain context across multiple sessions, ensuring that every analysis builds upon accumulated knowledge.

## Capabilities
*   **Competitor Tracking:** Monitors rivals for product updates, pricing changes, and messaging pivots.
*   **Signal Detection:** Identifies emerging players and critical industry news signals in real-time.
*   **Intelligence Reporting:** Produces structured competitive briefs and battlecards summarizing market positions.
*   **Threat/Opportunity Analysis:** Systematically analyzes external data to flag potential threats or untapped market opportunities.
*   **State Management:** Utilizes advanced memory loading (`letta-memory`) to maintain a consistent, evolving intelligence picture across all tasks.

## Example Use Cases
*   **Pre-Launch Assessment:** Before launching a new feature, use Spyglass to monitor competitor announcements in the last 30 days to preemptively address their strengths.
*   **Market Landscape Mapping:** Run a continuous monitoring cycle on a specific industry vertical (e.g., 'AI Agent Frameworks') to generate a comprehensive report identifying all key players and their recent technological advancements.
*   **Crisis Preparedness:** Task Spyglass with tracking sentiment shifts around your core product category across major tech forums, allowing the team to prepare proactive messaging before negative trends solidify.
