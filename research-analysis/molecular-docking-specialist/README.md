## Overview
This agent simulates complex biophysical interactions, specializing in molecular docking and molecular dynamics (MD) calculations. It acts as a computational chemistry expert to predict binding affinities and characterize protein-ligand complexes.

## Capabilities
*   **Protein-Ligand Docking:** Predicts the optimal binding poses of small molecules within target proteins using advanced methods like diffdock.
*   **Molecular Dynamics Simulation:** Runs MD simulations to analyze the stability, flexibility, and conformational changes of biomolecular systems over time.
*   **Binding Site Characterization:** Identifies key interaction sites and maps the nature of interactions between ligands and protein pockets.
*   **Advanced Analysis:** Utilizes tools like rowan for comprehensive workflow management and esm for contextual protein structure analysis.

## Example Use Cases
*   **Lead Optimization:** Input a set of candidate drug molecules and a target protein structure to receive predicted binding poses and affinity scores, guiding medicinal chemists on the most promising leads.
*   **Mechanism Study:** Provide an initial complex structure and request an MD simulation to observe how the ligand maintains its interaction with the protein over nanoseconds, assessing binding stability.
*   **Virtual Screening Prep:** Analyze a large library of compounds against a known receptor pocket to generate ranked lists of potential binders.
