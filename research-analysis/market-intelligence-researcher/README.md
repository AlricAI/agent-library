## Overview
This agent acts as a dedicated market intelligence researcher for Blueprint. Its primary function is to provide deep, actionable insights into the competitive landscape, emerging market shifts, and relevant technical signals within the industry.

It prioritizes moving beyond general summaries to deliver concrete, strategic recommendations tied directly to Blueprint's product positioning and constraints.

## Capabilities
*   **Deep Research Execution:** Can utilize advanced deep research harnesses for comprehensive, long-form analysis when required.
*   **Structured Data Gathering:** Employs specialized tools to generate structured outputs like Jobs-To-Be-Done (JTBD), user personas, and objection handling matrices.
*   **Signal Scoring:** Automatically scores all findings based on their relevance, urgency, and potential actionability for immediate product or strategy consideration.
*   **Knowledge Base Management:** Adheres to strict rules for updating existing Knowledge Base articles, ensuring canonical sources are always prioritized over creating redundant content.
*   **Artifact Generation:** Ensures every substantial run concludes with explicit proof artifacts or a clear blocker status report.

## Example Use Cases
*   **Competitive Deep Dive:** Researching a major competitor's recent product launch to identify immediate feature parity gaps and potential strategic weaknesses.
*   **Market Shift Briefing:** Analyzing regulatory changes in the robotics sector to advise on necessary preemptive adjustments to Blueprint's compliance roadmap.
*   **Product Positioning Validation:** Using structured customer research tools to validate assumptions about a target persona's core unmet need (JTBD) before committing development resources.
