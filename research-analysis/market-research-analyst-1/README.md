## Overview
This agent functions as a senior Market Research Analyst, leading a collaborative research crew to deliver actionable and deeply validated market intelligence. It combines rigorous analytical expertise with modern research methodologies to build comprehensive pictures of any given industry or market segment.

## Capabilities
*   **Comprehensive Market Analysis:** Conducts thorough investigations into market dynamics, including size estimation, growth rates, and segmentation using diverse public sources.
*   **Key Player Profiling:** Systematically identifies and profiles major market participants, detailing their market share, unique value propositions, and strategic positioning.
*   **Trend Detection:** Analyzes emerging trends, technological disruptions, regulatory shifts, and changing consumer behaviors impacting the target market.
*   **Competitive Intelligence Gathering:** Collects detailed data on competitor strategies, pricing models, product offerings, and distribution channels while adhering to ethical research standards.
*   **Data Triangulation & Validation:** Provides multiple source triangulation for high data reliability, clearly distinguishing between verified facts, industry estimates, and analytical insights.

## Example Use Cases
*   **New Product Launch Strategy:** "Analyze the market opportunity for sustainable packaging solutions in the CPG sector, identifying top 3 competitors and their current pricing gaps." 
*   **Investment Due Diligence:** "Provide a comprehensive market sizing report for decentralized finance (DeFi) platforms in Southeast Asia over the next five years."
*   **Market Entry Assessment:** "What are the key regulatory hurdles and emerging consumer trends we must consider when entering the European electric vehicle battery market?"

When using this agent, expect structured outputs that include raw data metrics, a formal research framework (market definition, size/growth, players, trends), and clear confidence levels for all identified opportunities or threats.
