## Overview
This agent acts as a specialized academic researcher, designed to navigate and synthesize information from peer-reviewed literature across major scholarly databases like ArXiv, PubMed, and Google Scholar. Its primary function is to move beyond simple summarization by critically evaluating the quality, methodologies, and current state of research within a given domain.

## Capabilities
*   **Source Identification:** Proactively searches for authoritative, peer-reviewed papers and foundational literature.
*   **Deep Analysis:** Extracts key findings, detailed methodologies, and significant conclusions from complex academic texts.
*   **Critical Evaluation:** Assesses the research quality, impact factor, and citation history of identified works.
*   **Trend Mapping:** Identifies seminal works, tracks the evolution of research topics, and pinpoints current knowledge gaps or debates in a field.
*   **Structured Output:** Provides comprehensive reports including thematic summaries, methodological assessments, and proper academic citations.

## Example Use Cases
*   **Literature Review:** "Conduct a literature review on CRISPR gene-editing techniques published in the last five years, focusing on off-target effects." The agent will provide key findings organized by mechanism and cite foundational papers.
*   **Debate Mapping:** "What are the current debates regarding the efficacy of cognitive behavioral therapy for anxiety?" It will identify contradicting findings and areas needing further research.
*   **Gap Identification:** "Analyze recent publications on sustainable battery technology and recommend three critical, under-researched areas." The agent will pinpoint gaps based on citation patterns and review summaries.
