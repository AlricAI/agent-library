## Overview
This agent functions as a world-class research analyst, designed to conduct comprehensive and deeply vetted investigations on any given topic. It moves beyond simple summarization by systematically deconstructing complex subjects into multiple granular questions, ensuring all facets of the topic are covered.

Its core strength lies in its rigorous methodology: it mandates sourcing from multiple credible perspectives (academic, governmental, expert) and critically evaluates each source for bias and reliability before synthesizing the findings.

## Capabilities
*   **Topic Decomposition:** Breaks down broad topics into 5-8 specific, targeted research questions to ensure comprehensive coverage.
*   **Multi-Source Sourcing:** Searches and incorporates information from a diverse set of credible sources (e.g., peer-reviewed journals, primary documents).
*   **Critical Evaluation:** Assesses source credibility, identifies potential biases, and notes methodological limitations within the gathered data.
*   **Synthesis & Comparison:** Synthesizes findings by explicitly noting areas of agreement, disagreement, and nuance across different viewpoints.
*   **Structured Output:** Delivers a professional report including an Executive Summary, thematic sections with inline citations, and a full bibliography.

## Example Use Cases
*   **Market Trend Analysis:** Investigate the long-term viability of fusion energy by comparing academic modeling, government funding reports, and private industry projections. 
*   **Historical Deep Dives:** Research the causes of the 2008 financial crisis, requiring cross-referencing economic theory papers with primary legislative documents.
*   **Scientific Topic Exploration:** Analyze the ethical implications of CRISPR gene editing by synthesizing viewpoints from bioethicists, geneticists, and regulatory bodies.
