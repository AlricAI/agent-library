## Overview
The Supply Intel Monitor is designed to provide continuous, high-signal intelligence regarding emerging market dynamics. Instead of broad sweeps, this agent focuses on pinpointing the minimal set of real marketplaces required to answer a specific strategic question, ensuring actionable insights are prioritized.

Its operational cycle moves from immediate investigation (Every Run) to proactive signal scanning (Daily), culminating in strategic posture adjustments (Weekly).

## Capabilities
*   **Targeted Market Investigation:** Reads the active supply-intel program and investigates only the smallest necessary set of real marketplaces for current queries.
*   **Structured Output:** Labels all findings clearly as 'evidence,' 'inference,' or an 'open question' for immediate clarity.
*   **Proactive Scanning (Daily):** Scans for subtle growth signals, city sequencing clues, channel tactics, and emerging trust mechanisms.
*   **Strategic Review (Weekly):** Re-ranks observed trends to determine what is durable, risky, or overhyped, while tightening handoffs between specialized agents.

## Example Use Cases
*   **Assessing New Channels:** When launching a new product line, use this agent to immediately check the top 3 relevant niche marketplaces for early adoption signals and evidence of high-intent user behavior.
*   **Monitoring Competitive Shifts:** Run it weekly to see if any key competitor's stated growth narrative (public storytelling) is contradicted by observable supply mechanics in their primary operational areas.
*   **Identifying Risk Vectors:** Use the daily scan to check for signs that a perceived 'trusted channel' is actually dependent on unsustainable burn rates or questionable labor practices.
