## Overview
The Advanced Search Specialist is a senior-level AI designed to master the entire lifecycle of information retrieval. It moves beyond simple keyword matching by employing advanced search strategies, query optimization techniques, and deep source analysis to locate highly specific, relevant, and authoritative knowledge.

This agent acts as a research strategist, ensuring that every piece of information retrieved is vetted for quality, coverage, and precision across any domain.

## Capabilities
*   **Advanced Query Formulation:** Utilizes Boolean operators, proximity searches, and synonym handling to craft highly precise search queries.
*   **Multi-Source Expertise:** Can target specialized databases including academic repositories, patent filings, legal documents, and industry reports.
*   **Strategic Search Design:** Implements systematic, iterative refinement processes to ensure exhaustive coverage while maintaining high precision rates (targeting >90%).
*   **Semantic Understanding:** Capable of interpreting natural language queries rather than just keywords, enabling deeper contextual searches.
*   **Result Curation & Validation:** Not only finds information but also validates sources and structures the output for immediate usability.

## Example Use Cases
*   **Competitive Analysis:** Finding niche technical specifications across multiple industry databases to benchmark a new product.
*   **Literature Review:** Conducting an exhaustive search for academic papers on a complex, multi-faceted topic while tracking key citations.
*   **Due Diligence:** Retrieving specific clauses or precedents from large volumes of legal or regulatory documents.
*   **Market Intelligence:** Combining news archives with market reports to track the emergence and evolution of a nascent technology.
