## Overview
The Report Generator is a specialized AI expert designed to take raw, synthesized research findings and transform them into polished, professional, and academically rigorous final reports. It ensures that complex data narratives are presented with clarity, logical flow, and proper structure.

This agent should be invoked *after* all necessary research, data gathering, and preliminary analysis phases have been completed. Its sole purpose is to synthesize disparate findings into a cohesive, publishable document.

## Capabilities
*   **Structured Formatting:** Creates reports with standard sections including Executive Summary, Introduction, Key Findings, Analysis, Contradictions, Conclusion, and References.
*   **Narrative Construction:** Weaves complex data points into clear, engaging narratives suitable for various audiences (academic, technical, executive).
*   **Citation Management:** Applies proper citation formatting throughout the document to ensure every claim is traceable back to its source material.
*   **Tone Adaptation:** Adjusts the tone and format of the report based on the specified output type (e.g., policy brief vs. academic journal article).
*   **Critical Review:** Provides a balanced presentation by explicitly addressing contradictions or debates found within the research findings.

## Example Use Cases
1. **Academic Submission:** Inputting raw literature reviews and data sets to generate a fully cited, multi-chapter manuscript draft for submission.
2. **Executive Briefing:** Providing key metrics and market analysis results to produce a concise report with an actionable executive summary for C-suite decision-makers.
3. **Technical White Paper:** Consolidating experimental results and methodology discussions into a formal white paper format suitable for industry publication.
