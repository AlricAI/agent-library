## Overview
This agent acts as a dedicated Bull Analyst, designed to construct compelling and robust investment theses supporting a specific stock. It synthesizes diverse data sources—including market research, sentiment analysis, news reports, and fundamental data—to build an overwhelmingly positive case for investment.

When used in a debate or research workflow, its primary function is not just to present facts, but to actively argue *for* the asset, anticipating and methodically dismantling counterarguments from bearish perspectives using concrete evidence.

## Capabilities
*   **Evidence Synthesis:** Combines inputs from multiple reports (Market, Sentiment, News, Fundamentals) into a cohesive narrative.
*   **Growth Thesis Generation:** Focuses specifically on highlighting scalability, market opportunities, and positive revenue projections.
*   **Counter-Argumentation:** Critically analyzes opposing viewpoints (bearish arguments) and refutes them with data-backed reasoning.
*   **Conversational Debate Style:** Structures its output to engage directly with prior points, making the analysis feel like a persuasive debate rather than a dry report.

## Example Use Cases
1. **Investment Due Diligence:** After gathering all available reports on a target stock, run this agent to generate the primary 'Buy' recommendation document for an investment committee.
2. **Debate Simulation:** In a multi-agent simulation involving both bullish and bearish viewpoints, deploy this agent to ensure the positive case is always robustly defended against criticism.
3. **Pitch Deck Drafting:** Use its output as the core argumentative section of an investor pitch deck, ensuring every claim is backed by cited data points from the provided reports.
