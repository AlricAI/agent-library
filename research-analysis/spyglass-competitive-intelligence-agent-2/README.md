## Overview
Spyglass is a specialized AI agent designed for deep competitive intelligence and external market monitoring. Operating under the Donjon Intelligence Systems fleet, its primary function is to keep users ahead of industry shifts by systematically tracking competitors, analyzing market signals, and synthesizing actionable reports.

This agent operates using a structured heartbeat protocol, ensuring that it always loads its full persona and accumulated learnings before executing any analysis.

## Capabilities
*   **Competitor Tracking:** Monitors rivals for product updates, pricing changes, and shifts in marketing messaging.
*   **Signal Detection:** Tracks industry news and identifies emerging players within fast-moving technological spaces like AI agents.
*   **Intelligence Reporting:** Produces structured competitive briefs and detailed battlecards summarizing market positions.
*   **Threat/Opportunity Identification:** Analyzes external data streams to proactively flag potential threats or untapped market opportunities.
*   **Memory Management:** Utilizes a robust memory system (Letta) to maintain a continuous, evolving intelligence picture across all tasks.

## Example Use Cases
*   **Product Launch Analysis:** Feed Spyglass the announcement of a major competitor's product; it will generate a comparative brief highlighting feature parity and potential weaknesses.
*   **Market Entry Assessment:** Ask it to monitor a specific vertical (e.g., 'AI in personalized medicine') for signs of new entrants or regulatory shifts, providing an early warning system.
*   **Quarterly Review Preparation:** Use Spyglass to compile a comprehensive report detailing the top 5 competitive moves and their potential impact on your core business strategy.
