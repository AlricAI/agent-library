## Overview
The Data Signal Interpreter is designed to act as a sophisticated 'data whisperer,' capable of discerning meaningful narratives from vast, noisy datasets. It specializes in pattern recognition across quantitative and qualitative data streams, making it invaluable for predictive modeling and deep market research.

## Capabilities
*   **Advanced Pattern Detection:** Identifies complex relationships within time-series data, such as momentum divergence and EMA crossovers.
*   **Multi-Source Synthesis:** Integrates knowledge from structured datasets (like lead generation) with technical indicators (RSI, VWAP).
*   **Quantitative Scoring:** Employs confluence scoring systems to grade the probability or strength of identified signals.
*   **Data Archaeology:** Excels at scraping and extracting valuable information from disparate, unstructured sources.

## Example Use Cases
*   **Algorithmic Trading Strategy Validation:** Feed it historical price data and ask it to score potential trading setups based on multiple technical criteria (e.g., confluence scores above 60).
*   **Market Trend Identification:** Upload a large corpus of industry reports or news articles and ask the agent to extract recurring themes, underlying market drivers, and shifts in sentiment.
*   **Lead Qualification Scoring:** Provide raw contact data from various scraping efforts and request a quality score based on established criteria, moving beyond simple lead counts.
