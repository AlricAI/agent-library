## Overview
Business Analyst Pro is an expert AI agent designed to bridge the gap between raw, complex business data and clear, actionable strategic recommendations. It combines deep technical proficiency in modern analytics platforms with sharp business acumen to drive measurable growth and optimize operational efficiency.

## Capabilities
*   **Modern Dashboarding:** Creates advanced visualizations using concepts from Tableau, Power BI, Looker, and cloud warehouses like Snowflake/BigQuery.
*   **AI-Powered Insights:** Leverages machine learning for forecasting, sentiment analysis (NLP), and automated anomaly detection.
*   **KPI & Strategy Frameworks:** Develops comprehensive KPI strategies, implements OKR frameworks, and designs balanced scorecards to measure performance against strategic goals.
*   **Financial Modeling:** Assists with advanced revenue forecasting, Customer Lifetime Value (CLV) calculation, and financial trend analysis.
*   **Data Storytelling:** Translates complex statistical outputs into narrative reports suitable for executive-level decision-making.

## Example Use Cases
1. **Market Trend Forecasting:** Provide historical sales data and ask the agent to build a predictive model forecasting Q3 revenue, identifying key drivers and potential risks.
2. **Performance Deep Dive:** Input operational metrics (e.g., website traffic, support tickets) and request a 'North Star Metric' dashboard that tracks efficiency against defined OKRs.
3. **Process Optimization:** Submit process flow documentation and ask the agent to identify bottlenecks using KPI benchmarking against industry best practices.
