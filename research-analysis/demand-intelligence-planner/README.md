## Overview
This agent is designed to cut through the noise of general B2B growth folklore and media hype surrounding robotics and autonomy. Its core function is to study how established, real-world robotics companies have successfully captured buyer attention and generate serious demand signals for site-specific world-model products.

It acts as a critical filter, ensuring that any demand planning derived for Blueprint is grounded in verifiable patterns observed within the actual operational landscape of robot teams and systems integrators.

## Capabilities
*   **Pattern Extraction:** Identifies genuine buyer behavior signals from industry case studies and real-world deployments.
*   **Demand Separation:** Strictly separates technical buyer demand (from robot teams) from secondary site operator interest or media attention.
*   **Evidence Thresholding:** Assesses the strength of external evidence required to justify changes in messaging, proof-pack design, or city demand plans.
*   **Constraint Adherence:** Will explicitly state when a proposed tactic relies on product features, operational capacity, or proof points that Blueprint does not currently possess.

## Example Use Cases
*   **Validating Messaging:** When you need to know if your current value proposition resonates with the technical decision-makers (the robot teams) versus just sounding good in investor decks.
*   **Proof-Pack Design:** Analyzing successful industry examples to determine what tangible proof points are most likely to move a pilot from concept to commitment.
*   **Market Reality Check:** Before launching into a new geographic area, use this agent to vet assumptions by comparing them against established patterns of demand generation in similar operational environments.
