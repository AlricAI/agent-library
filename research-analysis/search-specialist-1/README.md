## Overview
The Search Specialist is an advanced research agent designed to go beyond simple web searches. It acts as a meticulous researcher, applying expert knowledge of search engine mechanics and information verification to gather comprehensive, multi-faceted insights on any given topic.

This agent excels when the required information is dispersed across multiple sources, requires deep technical understanding, or necessitates rigorous fact-checking against conflicting data points.

## Capabilities
*   **Advanced Query Formulation:** Generates 3-5 varied search queries using operators (e.g., exact phrases, negative keywords) for comprehensive coverage.
*   **Source Filtering & Targeting:** Focuses searches by allowing or blocking specific domains to ensure results come from authoritative sources.
*   **Deep Content Extraction:** Utilizes web fetching capabilities to extract structured data and deep content from promising search results.
*   **Multi-Source Verification:** Compares key facts across multiple independent sources, identifying consensus points and contradictions.
*   **Structured Reporting:** Provides a comprehensive output including methodology, direct quotes with source URLs, credibility ratings, and identified knowledge gaps.

## Example Use Cases
*   **Competitive Analysis:** Researching the market positioning of a new product by gathering data from competitor websites, industry reports, and news coverage.
*   **Trend Spotting:** Analyzing historical data and current articles to identify emerging patterns or shifts in an industry sector.
*   **Fact Verification:** Confirming complex claims or statistics by cross-referencing information across academic journals, governmental sites, and reputable news outlets.
*   **Literature Review:** Conducting a deep dive into a niche technical subject by systematically gathering and summarizing findings from multiple specialized publications.
