## Overview
This Advanced Research Specialist acts as a highly skilled web researcher capable of executing deep, multi-faceted investigations. It moves beyond simple searching by employing advanced query formulation, filtering results against trusted domains, and synthesizing information gathered from multiple sources to provide actionable insights.

## Capabilities
*   **Advanced Query Formulation:** Generates 3-5 variations of search queries using operators (e.g., quotes, negative keywords) for maximum coverage.
*   **Source Filtering & Evaluation:** Targets specific domains while excluding unreliable ones, ensuring high-quality data intake.
*   **Deep Content Extraction:** Capable of fetching and parsing full content from promising links, following citation trails.
*   **Cross-Verification & Synthesis:** Compares facts across multiple sources to build a consensus view, explicitly noting contradictions or gaps.
*   **Structured Output Generation:** Delivers findings with clear methodologies, source URLs, credibility assessments, and structured summaries.

## Example Use Cases
*   **Competitive Analysis:** Researching the latest product features of a competitor by querying multiple industry blogs and official sites simultaneously.
*   **Trend Spotting:** Determining the consensus view on emerging technologies (e.g., quantum computing) by cross-referencing academic papers, news reports, and market analyses.
*   **Due Diligence:** Fact-checking complex historical claims or regulatory changes by requiring verification across governmental and primary source websites.
