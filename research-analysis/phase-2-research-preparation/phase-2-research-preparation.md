# Phase 2 User Research Preparation

## Overview

This document outlines the preparation activities for Phase 2 user research execution, building upon the completed research protocols and leveraging the live safety features now available for testing.

## Research Timeline Alignment

With safety features now live in production (as of April 15, 2026), Phase 2 research can begin immediate validation of these implemented components while preparing to test additional features as they come online.

## Participant Recruitment Preparation

### Recruitment Criteria Refresh

Based on live safety features, refine participant criteria to include:

- Users who have experienced or witnessed safety concerns on social platforms
- Participants with varying levels of comfort with safety features
- Users who have blocked or reported others online previously
- Community members who organize or participate in local events

### Outreach Channel Preparation

- Community organization partnerships for accessibility user recruitment
- Social media community groups for general participant sourcing
- University research boards for student demographic access
- Previous platform users for experience comparison studies

### Incentive Structure Finalization

- Base rate: $75-100/hour for standard sessions
- Accessibility participants: 25-50% premium for additional time/instructions
- Focus group participants: $50-75/hour with group dynamics bonus
- Survey completion: $5-10 flat rate with entry抽奖 for larger prizes

### Testing Environment Setup

- Production environment access for live safety feature testing
- Staging environment for unreleased community features
- Recording setup for session capture (video, screen, audio)

### Session Materials Development

- Pre-session questionnaire templates

## Facility and Technical Preparation

### Remote Testing Setup

- Video conferencing platform configuration
- Screen sharing and recording permissions
- Backup communication channels
- Technical troubleshooting guide distribution

### In-person Testing Locations

- Community center partnerships for local accessibility testing
- University lab reservations for controlled studies

- Transportation and parking arrangement for participants

## Team Preparation and Training

### Moderator Training

- Research protocol familiarization sessions
- Bias recognition and neutral questioning techniques
- Technical issue handling and contingency planning
- Note-taking and observation recording methods

### Observer Briefing

- Ethical considerations and privacy protection
- Silent observation protocols during sessions

- Data recording and storage procedures

### Stakeholder Alignment

- Research objective review with product team

- Feedback integration processes
- Reporting and presentation preparation

## Data Collection and Analysis Preparation

### Tool Setup

- Transcription service engagement for qualitative data
- Spreadsheet templates for quantitative metric tracking
- Database configuration for participant management
- Analysis software licensing and training

### Analysis Framework Development

- Coding scheme for qualitative themes

## Pilot Testing Preparation

### Internal Pilot Sessions

- Team member dry runs of all protocols
- Material refinement based on practice sessions
- Technical flow validation and timing adjustments
- Accessibility accommodation verification

### External Pilot Participants

- Recruiting 2-3 participants for pilot testing
- Protocol validation and refinement cycle
- Feedback collection on session experience
- Final material adjustments before full study launch

## Risk Mitigation Planning

### Participant Safety and Comfort

- Emotional distress recognition and response protocols
- Support resource provision for triggered participants
- Cultural sensitivity training for research team
- Flexible session pacing based on participant comfort

### Data Privacy and Security

- Secure recording storage with encryption
- Anonymization procedures for identifying information
- Limited access authorization protocols
- Compliance with research ethics board requirements

### Technical Contingencies

- Backup testing platform setup
- Alternative communication method preparation
- Recording failure recovery procedures
- Session rescheduling processes for technical issues
