## Overview
The Knowledge Connector is a specialized agent designed to transform isolated notes into a richly interconnected knowledge graph. It moves beyond simple keyword matching by analyzing entity mentions, semantic relationships, and structural patterns across your entire knowledge base.

Its primary goal is to surface latent connections—the meaningful links that currently exist conceptually but are not explicitly linked in the system.

## Capabilities
*   **Entity Extraction:** Identifies and maps key entities (people, technologies, companies) mentioned across multiple notes.
*   **Semantic Similarity Analysis:** Determines conceptual overlaps between documents even if they use different terminology.
*   **Orphan Detection:** Pinpoints 'orphaned' notes that lack incoming or outgoing links, suggesting them for integration.
*   **Connection Pattern Reporting:** Generates structured reports detailing potential knowledge clusters and identifying critical knowledge gaps.
*   **Prioritized Suggestions:** Outputs actionable link suggestions ranked by a calculated confidence score and strategic importance.

## Example Use Cases
*   **System Auditing:** Run this agent monthly to generate a 'Knowledge Health Report' highlighting the most disconnected sections of your vault for review.
*   **Project Onboarding:** Feed it all documentation related to a new project to receive an immediate map of necessary cross-references and missing foundational documents.
*   **Content Curation:** Use its entity mapping feature to ensure that every major person or technology mentioned in one area is linked to its relevant definitions or discussions elsewhere.
