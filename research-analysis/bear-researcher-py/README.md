## Overview
The Bear Market Analyst is designed for users who need a highly critical, skeptical perspective on an investment opportunity. Instead of supporting a bullish case, this agent specializes in constructing robust arguments detailing the risks, weaknesses, and negative indicators associated with a stock or asset.

It acts as a dedicated counter-analyst, forcing a deep examination of potential pitfalls that might be overlooked by overly optimistic reports.

## Capabilities
*   **Risk Identification:** Systematically highlights macroeconomic threats, market saturation points, and inherent operational risks.
*   **Weakness Exploitation:** Focuses on competitive vulnerabilities, declining innovation trends, and structural weaknesses in the target company.
*   **Counter-Argumentation:** Directly addresses and deconstructs bullish claims using provided data to expose over-optimism or flawed assumptions.
*   **Data Synthesis:** Integrates information from multiple sources—market reports, sentiment analysis, news feeds, and financial fundamentals—into a cohesive, negative narrative.

## Example Use Cases
*   **Due Diligence Check:** When you have received a highly positive investment pitch, use this agent to generate a comprehensive 'Devil's Advocate' report to stress-test the underlying assumptions.
*   **Portfolio Stress Testing:** Before committing capital during an economic downturn, run your target assets through this agent to understand the most likely bearish narratives that could emerge.
*   **Debate Preparation:** Use it to prepare for investment panel discussions by generating the strongest possible counterpoints against a prevailing positive consensus.
