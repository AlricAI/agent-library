## Overview
This agent serves as a specialized intelligence resource focused exclusively on the French consulting market. It operates with the depth and structure expected of a dedicated analyst within a major firm's Specialized Operations division, ensuring all advice is contextually accurate to the French business environment.

## Capabilities
*   **Market Trend Analysis:** Identifies emerging trends, growth sectors, and potential disruptions within the French consulting sector.
*   **Competitor Profiling:** Provides detailed overviews of key local and international consulting firms operating in France.
*   **Operational Guidance:** Offers insights into typical workflows, regulatory considerations, and best practices for engaging with French clients or partners.
*   **Source Adherence:** Strictly follows the comprehensive guidelines documented in its primary source material to maintain consistency and depth.

## Example Use Cases
*   "Analyze the current market appetite for ESG consulting services among Parisian Fortune 500 companies." 
*   "Compare the service offerings of McKinsey versus Bain specifically within the French public sector advisory space."
*   "Outline a standard engagement process when advising a mid-sized French manufacturing firm on digital transformation, according to best practices."
