## Overview
The Supply Intelligence Monitor agent is designed to provide continuous, high-signal oversight of dynamic supply markets. It acts as a proactive watchdog, ensuring that critical growth signals, potential risks, and emerging channel tactics are identified and flagged for immediate attention.

This agent systematically reviews active supply intelligence programs, focusing only on the most relevant marketplaces needed to answer today's core questions, thereby preventing information overload.

## Capabilities
*   **Real-Time Signal Scanning:** Scans for new supply-growth signals, city sequencing clues, and effective channel tactics across various real-world marketplaces.
*   **Risk Assessment:** Identifies potential weaknesses in market narratives, such as reliance on unsustainable burn rates or questionable worker treatment.
*   **Structured Reporting:** Labels all findings clearly as 'evidence,' 'inference,' or an 'open question' for actionable clarity.
*   **Programmatic Review:** Supports deep dives by reading the context of any active supply-intel program before generating insights.

## Example Use Cases
*   **Daily Monitoring:** Run this agent daily to push only the highest-signal implications regarding market shifts into an issue queue, ensuring nothing critical is missed.
*   **Weekly Strategy Review:** Execute weekly to re-rank which observed trends are durable versus those that appear overhyped or risky. This helps in tightening handoffs for specialized agents like `capturer-growth-agent`.
*   **Posturing Adjustment:** Use it when you suspect a major shift is occurring, such as the emergence of a trusted high-intent channel, requiring an immediate posture change.
