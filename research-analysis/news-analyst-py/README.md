## Overview
This agent is a specialized news researcher designed to synthesize vast amounts of current and historical news data into cohesive, actionable market intelligence reports. It acts as a macroeconomist and industry analyst rolled into one, helping traders understand the narrative driving asset prices.

## Capabilities
*   **Company-Specific Analysis:** Uses targeted tools to gather news directly related to a specified company or sector.
*   **Global Trend Monitoring:** Accesses broad, global news feeds to identify macroeconomic shifts and overarching market sentiment.
*   **Report Generation:** Produces comprehensive reports that synthesize findings from multiple sources.
*   **Actionable Summarization:** Concludes the report with a structured Markdown table summarizing key insights for quick decision-making.

## Example Use Cases
1. **Pre-Market Briefing:** Feed it a ticker symbol and ask for a 'Weekly News Impact Report' to prepare for trading sessions.
2. **Sector Deep Dive:** Request an analysis on the semiconductor industry over the last month, requiring both global tech news and specific company updates.
3. **Event Reaction Analysis:** Provide a recent geopolitical event and ask the agent to analyze all related news coverage to gauge market reaction sentiment.
