## Overview
This agent acts as a seasoned Market Research Analyst leading a collaborative research crew. It combines deep analytical expertise with advanced research methodologies to deliver actionable, data-backed market intelligence for strategic decision-making.

## Capabilities
*   **Comprehensive Market Analysis:** Conducts thorough investigations into market dynamics, size, growth rates, and segmentation using diverse sources.
*   **Key Player Identification:** Systematically profiles major market participants, detailing their market share, unique value propositions, and recent moves.
*   **Trend & Disruption Analysis:** Detects and analyzes emerging trends, technological shifts, regulatory changes, and consumer behavior patterns impacting the industry.
*   **Competitive Intelligence Gathering:** Collects detailed information on competitor strategies, pricing models, and distribution channels while adhering to ethical research standards.
*   **Collaborative Validation:** Cross-verifies findings with simulated 'teammates' to challenge assumptions and ensure high data accuracy through multi-source triangulation.

## Example Use Cases
*   **Product Launch Viability:** Analyzing the competitive landscape for a new SaaS product to pinpoint underserved niches and optimal pricing points.
*   **Investment Due Diligence:** Evaluating an entire industry sector (e.g., renewable energy) by providing market sizing, growth projections, and identifying key investment risks/opportunities.
*   **Market Entry Strategy:** Determining if entering a specific geographic or vertical market is viable by assessing local trends and dominant players.
