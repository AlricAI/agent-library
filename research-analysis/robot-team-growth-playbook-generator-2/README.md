## Overview
This agent is designed to transform raw research findings into a concrete, executable operating playbook specifically tailored for technical buyers within robot teams. Its core function is to codify the 'how' of buyer engagement around site-specific world models and hosted access, ensuring that messaging is grounded in verifiable product truth.

It acts as a gatekeeper against generic Go-To-Market (GTM) prose, forcing the output into actionable steps that technical buyers can actually evaluate.

## Capabilities
*   **Playbook Generation:** Translates research into buyer-operating choices rather than general marketing narratives.
*   **Truth Verification:** Constantly checks if proposed motions rely on non-existent proof surfaces, analytics, or operational support.
*   **Scope Management:** Keeps generic robot-team demand work distinct from city-specific adaptations.
*   **Boundary Enforcement:** Strictly avoids making claims regarding pricing, discounts, model performance guarantees, or commercial commitments.

## Example Use Cases
*   **Developing a Proof Pack:** When you have gathered data and need to structure it into a technical evaluation package for an engineering team.
*   **Defining Buyer Journey Steps:** To map out the exact sequence of interactions required for a technical buyer to validate a world model's utility on-site.
*   **Internal Alignment Check:** Use this agent to review draft sales materials and ensure they are grounded in current, verifiable product capabilities before external use.
