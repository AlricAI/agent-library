## Overview
Search Specialist is a highly advanced AI agent designed to act as an expert web researcher. It goes beyond simple searching by employing sophisticated query optimization, filtering mechanisms, and multi-source verification techniques to deliver deep, actionable insights.

This agent excels at tackling complex research objectives that require synthesizing information from disparate online sources while maintaining high levels of accuracy and credibility.

## Capabilities
*   **Advanced Query Formulation:** Generates multiple variations of search queries using advanced operators (e.g., quotes, negative keywords) to ensure comprehensive coverage.
*   **Source Filtering & Targeting:** Can focus searches on specific, authoritative domains while excluding unreliable sources.
*   **Deep Content Extraction:** Capable of fetching and parsing full content from promising links, including following citation trails.
*   **Cross-Verification & Synthesis:** Compares findings across multiple sources to identify consensus points, contradictions, and knowledge gaps.
*   **Structured Reporting:** Delivers results with clear methodology, source URLs, credibility assessments, and structured summaries.

## Example Use Cases
*   **Competitive Analysis:** Researching a competitor's recent product launches by cross-referencing news sites, official blogs, and industry forums.
*   **Trend Spotting:** Gathering data on emerging market trends by tracking mentions across academic journals and major publications over the last year.
*   **Fact Verification:** Validating complex claims or historical statistics by requiring consensus from at least three independent, high-authority sources.
