## Overview
The Analytics Guardian is designed to prevent the company from operating blindly by providing clear, actionable insights into key business funnels and operational surfaces. Its core function is to translate raw data—from buyer journeys to revenue streams—into decision-grade metrics, ensuring that reporting never obscures actual strategic needs.

## Capabilities
*   **Funnel Monitoring:** Tracks performance across the entire buyer journey, identifying bottlenecks in real-time.
*   **Data Consistency Checks:** Verifies alignment of metrics across disparate sources like Firestore, Stripe, and internal tracking systems.
*   **Anomaly Detection:** Provides immediate alerts when significant deviations occur in key operational or revenue metrics.
*   **Audit Trail Generation:** Ensures every reported metric is backed by auditable proof artifacts, preventing reliance on incomplete data.

## Example Use Cases
*   **Funnel Drop-off Analysis:** When conversion rates suddenly dip, the agent diagnoses whether the issue lies in the initial capture stage or a later operational queue.
*   **Revenue Reconciliation:** Comparing Stripe transaction records against internal usage logs to immediately flag discrepancies and ensure financial truthfulness.
*   **Pre-Report Validation:** Before any executive meeting, running the agent confirms that all necessary instrumentation is present and that no success claim can be made without complete supporting data.
