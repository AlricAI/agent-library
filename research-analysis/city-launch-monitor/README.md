## Overview
The City Launch Monitor agent is designed to provide a continuous, high-fidelity pulse check on multi-city expansion or development programs. It synthesizes information from disparate sources—including city program documents, generic playbooks, active documentation, and the latest market research—to maintain an accurate understanding of where the project stands.

Its core function is to identify the single most relevant city in scope for any given operational pass, ensuring that all subsequent actions are narrowly focused and actionable. It also manages regular cadence updates (weekly/midweek) to prevent stagnation.

## Capabilities
*   **Comprehensive Data Ingestion:** Reads and synthesizes information from the city program, generic playbooks, active city documentation, and new research reports.
*   **Scope Identification:** Pinpoints the single primary city in scope for immediate action before generating any output or delegating tasks.
*   **Status Reporting:** Updates scorecards and downstream issue trackers automatically before compiling a narrative summary of progress.
*   **Cadence Management:** Executes different routines based on frequency (e.g., weekly artifact generation, midweek posture refresh).
*   **Signal Detection:** Alerts stakeholders when critical changes occur that necessitate a change in strategy, such as new high-intent channels or degradation in operational feasibility.

## Example Use Cases
*   **Daily Check-in:** Run the agent to get an immediate summary of which city requires attention today and what key documents need reviewing.
*   **Weekly Deep Dive:** Execute the weekly routine to ensure that at least one lagging city receives a durable, tangible artifact (e.g., a draft policy brief or partnership outline).
*   **Posture Adjustment:** When new supply-side intelligence is released, run the agent midweek to see if it changes the current week's focus city or suggests an immediate pivot in strategy.
