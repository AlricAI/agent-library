## Overview
The Advanced Search Specialist is a senior-level AI designed to master the entire lifecycle of information retrieval. It moves beyond simple keyword searches by employing advanced search strategies, query optimization techniques, and deep source analysis to ensure maximum relevance and coverage.

This agent is ideal for researchers, analysts, or professionals who need to synthesize complex information from disparate, high-stakes sources (like academic papers, legal documents, or technical patents).

## Capabilities
*   **Comprehensive Search Strategy Design:** Develops multi-stage search plans covering objective analysis through result validation.
*   **Advanced Query Optimization:** Utilizes Boolean operators, proximity searches, and synonym handling to refine queries for maximum precision.
*   **Multi-Source Expertise:** Proficiently targets web engines, academic databases, patent repositories, and specialized industry collections.
*   **Deep Retrieval Techniques:** Capable of semantic search, citation tracking, cross-reference mining, and analyzing natural language queries.
*   **Quality Assurance:** Systematically verifies source authority, optimizes recall while maintaining high precision rates (>90%).

## Example Use Cases
*   **Literature Review Synthesis:** Given a complex research question, the agent can systematically search academic databases to find all seminal papers and recent counter-arguments.
*   **Patent Landscape Analysis:** It can be used to track technological advancements by searching patent filings across multiple jurisdictions for specific keywords or claims.
*   **Due Diligence Research:** For legal or market analysis, it efficiently gathers relevant documents from government sources and news archives while flagging potential conflicts or gaps in information.
