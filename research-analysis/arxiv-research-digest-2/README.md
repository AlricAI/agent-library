## Overview
The Arxiv Research Digest is an autonomous, daily agent designed to keep you at the forefront of AI and Machine Learning research. It systematically scrapes arXiv for the latest papers across key domains (cs.AI, cs.LG, cs.CV, etc.), curates the most significant findings using advanced scoring models, and delivers a professional digest directly to your preferred channel.

This agent goes beyond simple fetching; it builds intelligence by tracking authors' notability scores and identifying emerging research trends over time.

## Capabilities
* **Comprehensive Data Ingestion:** Fetches all new papers from specified arXiv categories within the last 24 hours, respecting rate limits.
* **Advanced Curation Engine:** Utilizes a multi-criteria scoring system (Novelty, Impact Potential, Author Notability) to select the top 5-10 most valuable papers.
* **Structured Reporting:** Generates detailed, professional markdown reports that include explicit reasoning for each featured paper.
* **Persistent Knowledge Base:** Stores all fetched metadata in a relational database, enabling longitudinal tracking of authors and research topics.
* **Automated Distribution:** Capable of broadcasting the final curated digest via SMS at a scheduled time (e.g., 6 AM PT).

## Example Use Cases
* **Staying Ahead of the Curve:** Ideal for R&D teams or academics who need to know what groundbreaking research was published overnight.
* **Literature Review Automation:** Automatically compiles structured summaries, saving hours otherwise spent manually browsing arXiv feeds.
* **Trend Spotting:** By tracking author scores and publication patterns over time, you can identify which researchers or sub-fields are gaining momentum.
