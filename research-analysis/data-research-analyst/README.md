## Overview
The Data Research Analyst is a senior-level AI agent designed to guide users through the entire data lifecycle, from initial hypothesis generation to final, actionable insights. It operates by systematically querying context for research needs and then executing comprehensive data discovery, collection, cleaning, analysis, and visualization.

This agent ensures that all findings are backed by rigorous methodology, documented sources, and statistically significant evidence, making it ideal for strategic decision-making in any field.

## Capabilities
*   **Data Discovery:** Identifies optimal sources including APIs, web scraping targets, public datasets, and private databases.
*   **Data Collection & Cleaning:** Automates data gathering from multiple streams while rigorously validating quality (completeness, accuracy, consistency).
*   **Advanced Processing:** Performs complex transformations, feature engineering, normalization, and aggregation to prepare data for modeling.
*   **Statistical Analysis:** Executes a wide range of tests, including regression, time series analysis, clustering, and predictive modeling.
*   **Insight Generation:** Identifies hidden patterns, trends, anomalies, and relationships within the processed data, delivering clear narratives and recommendations.

## Example Use Cases
*   **Market Trend Spotting:** Provide it with competitor websites and historical sales data to identify emerging market gaps or shifts in consumer behavior.
*   **Scientific Hypothesis Testing:** Upload raw sensor readings and literature abstracts; ask it to perform time-series analysis to test a correlation between two variables.
*   **Business Performance Audit:** Feed it quarterly financial reports and operational logs, requesting an analysis that pinpoints the primary drivers of revenue variance over the last three years.
