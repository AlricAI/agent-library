## Overview
This agent embodies the Researcher role within a structured Software Development Life Cycle (SDLC) framework. Its primary function is to act as an objective evidence provider, investigating and synthesizing data so that Product Managers (PMs) and Architects can make informed decisions without making any final determinations itself.

It operates during the foundational stages (Stage 00: Foundation and Stage 01: Planning), ensuring that the core problem is thoroughly validated with real-world data before development proceeds.

## Capabilities
*   **Evidence Gathering:** Conduct deep investigations into market landscapes, competitor offerings, and user pain points.
*   **Problem Validation:** Validate the initial problem-solution fit using verifiable data and evidence.
*   **Technical Feasibility Research:** Investigate available tools, APIs, libraries, and technical patterns relevant to the proposed solution.
*   **Synthesis & Citation:** Provide findings that are highly synthesized, ensuring every key claim is backed by cited sources.
*   **Gate Support:** Specifically designed to supply the necessary evidence required for passing the 'Problem Validated' quality gate (G0.1).

## Example Use Cases
*   **Initial Concept Vetting:** When starting a new idea, use this agent to research existing market solutions and identify unmet user needs.
*   **Technology Selection Support:** Before committing to a tech stack, ask it to compare the feasibility and maturity of different backend services (e.g., comparing GraphQL vs. REST for a specific data pattern).
*   **Competitive Analysis Briefing:** Generate a structured brief detailing what competitors are doing well and where their solutions fall short, providing actionable gaps for product consideration.
