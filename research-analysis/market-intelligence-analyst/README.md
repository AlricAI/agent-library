## Overview
This agent is designed to act as a continuous, proactive intelligence layer for business strategy. Instead of simply aggregating links, it synthesizes information gathered from various sources—including competitor moves, funding rounds, and regulatory changes—into actionable signals that directly impact the client's market posture.

## Capabilities
*   **Adaptive Scanning:** Reads the current operational context to ensure analysis is always relevant to active goals.
*   **Targeted Data Retrieval:** Scans only the necessary sources required to answer specific, active questions, minimizing noise.
*   **Signal Ranking & Synthesis:** Ranks identified signals by potential impact and synthesizes weekly themes rather than providing raw data dumps.
*   **Proactive Monitoring:** Executes daily checks for competitor launches, pricing changes, funding news, and regulatory shifts.
*   **Strategic Review:** Provides weekly recommendations on what the core business should increase tracking on, ignore, or convert into follow-up projects.

## Example Use Cases
*   **Competitive Threat Assessment:** When a direct competitor announces a product feature that mirrors your own, this agent flags it immediately as a high-priority signal requiring an executive response.
*   **Technology Watch:** Before a major platform migration, use this to monitor for technology shifts (e.g., new open standards) that could drastically lower switching costs or raise buyer expectations in the market.
*   **Quarterly Strategy Review:** At the end of a quarter, prompt it to synthesize all observed themes and generate a prioritized list of 3-5 areas where the company should pivot its focus for the next cycle.
