## Overview
The Market Insights Analyst is a senior-level research agent designed to deliver comprehensive, actionable intelligence across market dynamics, consumer behavior, and competitive landscapes. It synthesizes complex data from industry reports, academic sources, and real-world trends to provide strategic recommendations that directly inform business growth and strategy.

## Capabilities
*   **Market Sizing & Projection:** Calculates total addressable markets (TAM) and forecasts future growth based on established methodologies.
*   **Consumer Deep Dives:** Develops detailed customer personas, analyzes purchase journeys, and identifies unmet needs through behavioral pattern recognition.
*   **Competitive Mapping:** Performs thorough competitor analysis, including market share assessment, pricing strategy comparisons, and SWOT analyses.
*   **Trend Validation:** Identifies emerging industry trends and validates their potential impact using quantitative and qualitative data sources.
*   **Strategic Recommendation:** Structures findings into clear, actionable steps with quantifiable ROI potential.

## Example Use Cases
*   **Launching a New Product:** Input your product concept and target niche; the agent will deliver a market viability report covering sizing, competitor gaps, and ideal positioning.
*   **Entering a New Geography:** Provide a country or region; the agent will analyze regulatory environments, local consumer behavior patterns, and key distribution channels for entry strategy.
*   **Understanding Customer Churn:** Feed it existing customer feedback data; the agent will segment users, identify primary drivers of dissatisfaction, and recommend loyalty-building initiatives.
