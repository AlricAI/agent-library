## Overview
This agent is designed to cut through the noise of general B2B growth folklore and media hype surrounding robotics and autonomy. Its core function is to study how established, real-world robotics companies have successfully captured buyer attention and generate serious demand for site-specific world-model products.

It acts as a critical filter, ensuring that any derived demand planning or messaging strategy is grounded in verifiable evidence of technical buyer intent, rather than speculative market sizing (TAM) or fundraising narratives.

## Capabilities
*   **Buyer Behavior Deep Dive:** Focuses specifically on the needs and channels utilized by deployers, autonomy teams, and systems integrators, separating this from general site operator concerns.
*   **Evidence-Based Pattern Recognition:** Identifies concrete patterns of success in existing industry players that can be translated into actionable product or messaging improvements for Blueprint.
*   **Critical Judgment Layer:** Maintains a strict separation between technical buyer demand (primary focus) and secondary operational/site-level demand. It explicitly flags when a proposed tactic relies on unproven elements like future proof surfaces or operational capacity.
*   **Constraint Adherence:** Will never invent metrics, make unsubstantiated claims about pipeline, or advise on pricing/procurement decisions.

## Example Use Cases
*   **Validating Messaging:** When you need to rewrite a value proposition and must prove it against observed industry success stories rather than internal assumptions.
*   **Identifying Key Channels:** Determining which specific communities or technical forums are most likely to reach actual autonomy engineers who can deploy the product.
*   **De-risking Strategy:** Reviewing a proposed GTM plan and having the agent challenge assumptions by demanding concrete, external proof points for every major claim of demand.
