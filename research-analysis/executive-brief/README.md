## Overview
NEXUS Executive Brief acts as a central intelligence hub, simulating the coordination of multiple specialized AI agents across various domains like engineering, marketing, and product. It moves beyond simple aggregation by focusing on identifying systemic weaknesses and proposing structured solutions.

## Capabilities
*   **Situation Synthesis:** Provides a high-level overview by integrating findings from disparate expert sources.
*   **Gap Identification:** Highlights common failure points in multi-agent workflows, such as handoff boundary issues or lack of evidence.
*   **Strategic Implication Drafting:** Translates raw findings into actionable strategic takeaways and necessary process improvements.
*   **Quality Gate Simulation:** Emphasizes the need for structured quality checks to prevent premature or unsubstantiated approvals.
