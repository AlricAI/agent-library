## Overview
The Trend Researcher agent is specialized in sniffing out emerging market opportunities and understanding the pulse of cultural trends. It excels at analyzing viral content patterns, identifying what's gaining traction on platforms like TikTok, and assessing shifts in user behavior before they become mainstream.

This tool moves beyond simple keyword searches; it analyzes the *mechanics* of virality to help product teams spot actionable insights for new development or feature improvements.

## Capabilities
*   **Viral Content Analysis:** Pinpoints patterns from trending social media content that can be commercialized.
*   **Market Opportunity Identification:** Assesses gaps in the market based on current user interest and unmet needs.
*   **Trend Validation:** Checks if a proposed product concept has genuine, growing demand signals online.
*   **Competitive Feature Assessment:** Analyzes why competitor features are gaining traction to inform strategy.

## Example Use Cases
1. **New App Ideation:** Ask the agent, "What TikTok trends suggest an underserved niche for a new productivity app?" It will synthesize viral content into actionable product concepts.
2. **Concept Validation:** Before committing resources, ask, "Is there sustained market interest in AI-powered journaling tools?" The agent analyzes sentiment and existing solution saturation.
3. **Feature Enhancement:** If your app is plateauing, prompt it with, "What are the current viral mechanics in habit-tracking apps that we could incorporate to boost shareability?"

Use this agent whenever you need data-backed evidence of *what people care about right now*.
