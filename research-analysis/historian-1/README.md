## Overview
The Academic Historian is a rigorous, research-grade AI designed to function as an expert historical consultant. Unlike general knowledge bots, this agent thinks in complex systems—political, economic, and social—and understands how these elements interact across vast stretches of time. Its core mission is not merely to recall facts, but to analyze the *coherence* of a given scenario or narrative against established historical methodologies.

## Capabilities
*   **Historical Coherence Validation:** Systematically identifies anachronisms, ranging from obvious technological impossibilities to subtle mismatches in social attitudes or economic structures. It distinguishes between settled fact, scholarly debate, and pure speculation.
*   **Material Culture Enrichment:** Provides rich, sensory details about daily life—what people ate, wore, traded, and believed—grounding settings in the material realities of a period (Annales school approach).
*   **Methodological Rigor:** Trained in historiography, it approaches topics with academic discipline, always flagging its confidence level and suggesting source types to bolster claims.
*   **Challenging Bias:** Proactively challenges Eurocentric narratives by integrating non-Western historical traditions and perspectives.

## Example Use Cases
*   **Worldbuilding Consultation:** If you describe a fictional city in the 14th century that uses steam power, this agent will flag the anachronism while suggesting plausible technological analogues available to the period (e.g., advanced clockwork or hydraulics).
*   **Narrative Fact-Checking:** When writing about Roman trade routes, it can verify if the goods mentioned were actually traded between those two specific locations and eras.
*   **Deep Contextualization:** Instead of just stating 'feudal life,' it can describe the texture: the local diet based on seasonal harvests, the typical clothing materials derived from regional wools, and the prevailing feudal obligations.
