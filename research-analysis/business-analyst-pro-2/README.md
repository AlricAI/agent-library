## Overview
Business Analyst Pro is an advanced AI agent designed to transform raw business data into clear, actionable strategic insights. It specializes in deep dives into performance metrics, forecasting future growth, and building robust reporting frameworks for executive decision-making.

This tool moves beyond simple data aggregation; it identifies underlying trends, calculates critical financial ratios (like LTV/CAC), and provides prioritized recommendations for optimization.

## Capabilities
*   **KPI & Trend Analysis:** Identifies performance indicators, anomalies, and significant shifts in business metrics.
*   **Financial Modeling:** Creates detailed revenue projections, growth forecasts, and comprehensive cost analyses with stated assumptions.
*   **Customer Metrics Deep Dive:** Performs churn analysis, cohort retention studies, and calculates vital customer lifetime value (LTV) and acquisition costs (CAC).
*   **Market Sizing:** Conducts Total Addressable Market (TAM), Serviceable Available Market (SAM), and Serviceable Obtainable Market (SOM) analyses for strategic planning.
*   **Reporting & Visualization:** Generates executive summary reports, dashboard templates, and data pipeline suggestions (including SQL queries).

## Example Use Cases
1. **Quarterly Review:** Upload sales figures and ask the agent to generate an executive summary comparing Q3 performance against industry benchmarks, highlighting the top 3 areas for immediate revenue improvement.
2. **Product Launch Planning:** Provide initial user adoption data and request a full growth projection model over three years, including sensitivity analysis based on marketing spend changes.
3. **Churn Investigation:** Feed in customer subscription data and ask for a cohort retention table to pinpoint when and why customers are leaving, along with suggested retention strategies.
