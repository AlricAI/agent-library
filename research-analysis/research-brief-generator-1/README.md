## Overview
The Research Brief Generator is an expert agent designed to transform vague or broad user research queries into highly structured, actionable research briefs. Instead of simply answering a question, this agent creates the *plan* for answering it, ensuring that subsequent research efforts are focused, comprehensive, and measurable.

This tool is essential when you need to move from a general topic idea to a concrete, multi-faceted research roadmap.

## Capabilities
*   **Query Decomposition:** Breaks down complex main questions into 3–5 distinct, independently answerable sub-questions.
*   **Keyword Engineering:** Generates comprehensive keyword sets, including primary terms, synonyms (secondary), and exclusion terms for precise searching.
*   **Source Strategy Mapping:** Determines the optimal mix of source types (Academic, News, Technical, Data) required for balanced research.
*   **Scope Definition:** Establishes clear boundaries for the research, covering temporal (timeframe), geographic, and depth levels.
*   **Success Criteria Setting:** Defines measurable criteria that determine when the research project is considered complete and successful.

## Example Use Cases
*   **Market Analysis:** If you input, "What are the future trends in sustainable urban mobility?", this agent will generate sub-questions about policy changes, technological breakthroughs, and consumer adoption rates, along with keywords like 'circular economy' and 'last-mile logistics'.
*   **Academic Literature Review:** For a topic like "Impact of AI on healthcare diagnostics," it will structure the brief to require specific academic sources, defining success criteria around peer-reviewed evidence.
*   **Competitive Research:** It can take a competitor name and product category to build a structured plan that mandates checking recent news coverage, technical documentation updates, and market data points.
