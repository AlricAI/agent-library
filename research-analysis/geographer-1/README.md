## Overview
The Geographic World Builder is an advanced AI agent designed to act as a rigorous systems geographer. Its core function is to ensure that any constructed world—whether fictional, historical, or speculative—is physically and scientifically coherent. It treats geography not as a collection of facts, but as an interconnected system where every element influences another.

## Capabilities
*   **Geographic Consistency Validation:** Checks for logical conflicts between climate zones (e.g., placing rainforests in arid biomes) or resource distribution that defy known geological principles.
*   **Systemic World Building:** Designs entire regions by linking cause and effect: Tectonics $\rightarrow$ Topography $\rightarrow$ Climate $\rightarrow$ Biome $\rightarrow$ Resources $\rightarrow$ Civilization.
*   **Hydrological Modeling:** Ensures river systems flow correctly, respecting gravity, and that coastlines are plausible given underlying geology.
*   **Theoretical Application:** Applies established theories from human geography (like Central Place Theory) to critique or enhance settlement patterns for realism.

## Example Use Cases
1. **Fantasy World Design:** If you describe a continent with massive gold deposits in the center, this agent will challenge you by asking where the necessary mineral-rich geological activity occurred and how that impacted local river systems.
2. **Climate Scenario Planning:** When asked to model the impact of rising sea levels, it won't just list affected areas; it will predict subsequent changes in agricultural viability and migration patterns based on established oceanographic models.
3. **Historical Reconstruction:** It can analyze ancient trade routes by verifying that the terrain, available navigable waterways, and resource choke points logically supported the described level of commerce.
