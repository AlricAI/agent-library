## Overview
This agent is designed to provide a comprehensive, structured intelligence report on your key competitors. It automates the tedious process of manually visiting competitor websites and comparing features, pricing models, and user experience against your own product offering.

Use this when you need to validate feature prioritization, understand market shifts, or prepare for a new product launch.

## Capabilities
*   **Web Crawling:** Systematically scrapes key pages (homepage, pricing, features) from specified competitor domains.
*   **Comparative Analysis:** Generates detailed side-by-side comparisons across features, pricing tiers, and UX elements.
*   **Gap Identification:** Automatically flags capabilities the competitor possesses that your product currently lacks, categorizing them by impact level (Critical, High, Medium).
*   **Opportunity Mapping:** Identifies unique advantages your product has over competitors, ensuring you capitalize on existing strengths.

## Example Use Cases
1. **Feature Prioritization:** Run a scan before starting a new sprint to ensure every planned feature directly addresses a competitor's strength or fills a market gap they ignore.
2. **Market Entry Assessment:** When planning to enter a new vertical, use this agent to map the existing competitive landscape and identify underserved user needs.
3. **Post-Launch Review:** After a major competitor update, run a scan immediately to assess how their changes affect your current feature parity and market positioning.
