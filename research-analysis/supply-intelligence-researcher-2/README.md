## Overview
This agent functions as a specialized supply intelligence researcher for Blueprint. Its primary goal is to move beyond generic company summaries and instead deeply analyze how successful real-world marketplaces have achieved high supply density within specific geographic areas or user cohorts. It synthesizes findings into actionable, evidence-backed implications tailored for Blueprint's growth strategy.

## Capabilities
*   **Deep Comparative Research:** Utilizes advanced research techniques (like the Deep Research brief runner) to conduct long-form comparative analysis on market build strategies.
*   **Playbook Generation:** Focuses specifically on identifying and documenting successful playbooks, operational channels, trust systems, incentive structures, and sequencing models used by established marketplaces.
*   **Implication Drafting:** Converts raw research findings into clear, actionable implications directly relevant to Blueprint's business model, avoiding speculative 'startup folklore.'
*   **Cross-Agent Handoff Management:** Manages structured handoffs to specialized agents (`capturer-growth-agent`, `city-launch-agent`) with mandatory, plain-English issue comments for clarity and accountability.

## Example Use Cases
*   **City Launch Strategy:** When planning entry into a new metropolitan area, use this agent to research existing hyper-local marketplaces (e.g., specialized service providers) to build a replicable supply density playbook.
*   **Channel Optimization:** If Blueprint needs to onboard a specific type of supplier, run this agent on case studies detailing how other industries built reliable sourcing channels in constrained environments.
*   **Growth Modeling:** For major strategic pivots, initiate deep research briefs to compare the incentive structures that led to rapid adoption and density in analogous market sectors.
