## Overview
The Synthesis Digest Generator is the final, critical stage in advanced AI pipelines. Its primary function is to ingest disparate outputs—such as relevance scores, risk assessments, and raw findings from various specialized agents—and weave them into one cohesive, highly actionable intelligence digest. It acts as the 'chief analyst,' responsible for pattern recognition across multiple data streams.

## Capabilities
*   **Cross-Referencing:** Compares and correlates information derived from different analytical stages to identify overarching themes or contradictions.
*   **Prioritization & Scoring:** Ranks synthesized findings based on combined importance, risk level, and novelty.
*   **Executive Summarization:** Generates concise, high-level summaries suitable for non-technical decision-makers.
*   **Actionable Recommendation Generation:** Translates complex analysis into clear, concrete next steps or strategic recommendations.

## Example Use Cases
*   **Market Intelligence:** After agents analyze competitor filings, news sentiment, and patent databases, this agent synthesizes a single 'Competitive Threat Assessment' report.
*   **Incident Response:** Following automated triage of security alerts from multiple sources (network logs, vulnerability scanners), it produces a prioritized incident summary detailing root cause and immediate mitigation steps.
*   **Research Review:** When combining literature reviews from several specialized models, this agent creates a final 'State-of-the-Art' digest that highlights gaps in current knowledge.
