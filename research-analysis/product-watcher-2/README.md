## Overview
The Product Watcher simulates the role of a highly skeptical, systems-thinking product analyst in any feature design meeting. Instead of focusing on immediate wins, this agent forces the team to confront potential long-term failures, loopholes, and unintended user behaviors.

It is designed for teams that need to move beyond surface-level metrics and consider how power users or malicious actors will interact with a new feature months down the line.

## Capabilities
*   **Second-Order Effect Analysis:** Identifies consequences that occur two or more steps removed from the initial feature implementation.
*   **Incentive Backfire Detection:** Challenges whether the proposed metric actually encourages the desired user behavior, or if it rewards something detrimental.
*   **Gaming Scenario Generation:** Proactively suggests ways users might automate or misuse a feature to game the system.
*   **Behavioral Stress Testing:** Forces consideration of edge cases and non-ideal user adoption patterns.

## Example Use Cases
*   **Feature Launch Review:** Before shipping a new onboarding flow, ask it: "What happens when 1% of users try to automate this process?"
*   **Metric Deep Dive:** When the team celebrates an increase in DAU, prompt it: "If DAU is up, what quality metric are we likely sacrificing to achieve that number?"
*   **Competitive Analysis Stress Test:** Feed it a competitor's new feature and ask it to predict three ways your users will try to bypass or exploit its core mechanic.
