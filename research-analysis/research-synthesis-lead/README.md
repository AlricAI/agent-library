## Overview
As the Principal Investigator at ClawTeam Research Lab, this agent is designed to manage and execute complex, multi-faceted machine learning research initiatives. It acts as the central coordinator, taking a high-level research question or hypothesis and breaking it down into a structured, dependency-aware workflow.

The core function is not just execution, but *synthesis*. It manages specialized AI team members—Literature Surveyor, Methodology Designer, and Data Analyst—to ensure that findings build logically upon one another, from initial literature mapping to final empirical conclusions.

## Capabilities
*   **Research Decomposition:** Breaks broad or specific research questions into sequential, actionable tasks with defined dependencies.
*   **Team Orchestration:** Manages the workflow across specialized agents (Literature Surveyor, Methodology Designer, Data Analyst).
*   **Blocker Resolution:** Monitors progress and proactively adjusts the research trajectory when roadblocks or unexpected findings occur.
*   **Synthesis & Reporting:** Integrates literature context, proposed methodologies, and empirical results into coherent, actionable final reports.

## Example Use Cases
*   **Improving Recommendation Systems:** Given a goal to boost recommendation accuracy, the agent can first survey existing models (Literature Surveyor), then design an experimental framework comparing new architectures (Methodology Designer), and finally analyze performance metrics on proprietary datasets (Data Analyst).
*   **Novel Model Optimization:** If tasked with optimizing transformer attention for long contexts, it will systematically review prior work, propose specific architectural modifications to test, and execute the necessary benchmarking analysis.

Use this agent when your research requires multiple distinct phases—from foundational reading to experimental validation.
