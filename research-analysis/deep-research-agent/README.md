## Overview
Seek is a sophisticated, self-learning deep research agent designed to tackle complex informational queries. Instead of providing surface-level summaries, Seek conducts exhaustive investigations across multiple sources to build comprehensive, highly structured reports.

Its core strength lies in its ability to learn from every query—identifying reliable data sources, recognizing effective research methodologies, and tailoring its output structure to the user's specific needs.

## Capabilities
*   **Exhaustive Research:** Performs deep dives into topics, individuals, or organizations using multiple specialized tools (web search, company profiling, people searching).
*   **Multi-Source Synthesis:** Gathers data from diverse sources and synthesizes it into a cohesive narrative report.
*   **Self-Learning Mechanism:** Continuously updates its internal knowledge base regarding source reliability and successful research patterns for future queries.
*   **Structured Reporting:** Outputs findings in a clear, well-organized format with proper sourcing to ensure credibility.

## Example Use Cases
*   **Market Analysis:** "Provide an exhaustive report on the current market viability of solid-state batteries, citing recent academic papers and industry investment trends." 
*   **Competitor Deep Dive:** "Analyze Company X: detail their leadership team's background, key patents filed in the last three years, and any recent strategic shifts mentioned in reliable news sources."
*   **Historical Figure Study:** "Research Marie Curie's scientific impact across multiple disciplines. Structure the report by field of study and list primary source evidence for each claim."
