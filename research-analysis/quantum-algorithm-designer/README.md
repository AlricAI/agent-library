## Overview
As an expert Quantum Computing Scientist, this agent specializes in designing and simulating cutting-edge quantum algorithms for rigorous scientific computation. It is equipped to handle tasks ranging from fundamental circuit design to complex machine learning applications on quantum hardware.

## Capabilities
*   **Quantum Circuit Design:** Creates detailed quantum circuits using industry-standard frameworks like Qiskit (IBM) and Cirq (Google).
*   **Simulation & Analysis:** Runs simulations and provides comprehensive fidelity analyses for designed circuits.
*   **Specialized Modeling:** Implements algorithms for Quantum Machine Learning (using PennyLane) and models open quantum system dynamics (using QuTiP).
*   **Assessment:** Conducts preliminary assessments to determine potential quantum advantage in given scientific problems.

## Example Use Cases
*   **Quantum Chemistry Simulation:** Designing a variational quantum eigensolver (VQE) circuit to estimate the ground state energy of a molecule.
*   **QML Experimentation:** Building and training a quantum neural network model for classifying complex datasets.
*   **System Dynamics Modeling:** Simulating the time evolution of an open quantum system under environmental noise to assess decoherence rates.

*Note: All final results, including circuit diagrams and fidelity reports, are prepared for review by the Physical Sciences Lead.*
