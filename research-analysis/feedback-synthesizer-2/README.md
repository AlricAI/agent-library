## Overview
The Product Feedback Synthesizer is an expert agent designed to revolutionize how product teams process user input. It specializes in collecting, analyzing, and synthesizing feedback gathered from numerous channels—including support tickets, surveys, reviews, and interviews—to distill complex qualitative data into clear, actionable, and quantifiable product insights.

This agent moves beyond simple sentiment scoring; it builds a comprehensive understanding of the 'Voice of Customer' to guide strategic decision-making.

## Capabilities
*   **Multi-Channel Collection:** Ingests feedback from surveys, support tickets, social media, and direct interviews.
*   **Advanced Sentiment & Emotion Analysis:** Performs deep NLP processing to detect underlying emotions, calculate satisfaction scores, and identify emerging trends.
*   **Thematic Categorization:** Automatically groups disparate pieces of feedback into core themes, assigns priority levels (e.g., High Impact/Low Effort), and assesses overall business impact.
*   **User Journey Mapping & Pain Point Identification:** Maps user narratives against product workflows to pinpoint critical friction points.
*   **Prioritization Framework Application:** Ranks feature requests using established methodologies like RICE or MoSCoW, providing data-backed recommendations for the roadmap.
*   **Executive Reporting:** Generates structured dashboards and reports suitable for cross-functional stakeholders (Engineering, Marketing, Leadership).

## Example Use Cases
1. **Roadmap Prioritization:** Feed it 500 recent support tickets and ask it to generate a prioritized list of the top 3 features that will reduce churn risk, citing supporting evidence from the data.
2. **Feature Gap Analysis:** Provide competitor review data alongside your own user feedback to identify underserved market needs or critical feature gaps.
3. **CSAT Improvement Strategy:** Upload NPS survey results and qualitative comments; ask the agent to diagnose the root cause of low scores and propose three immediate, measurable improvements.
