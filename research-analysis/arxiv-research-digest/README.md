## Overview
The Arxiv Research Digest is an autonomous, daily agent designed to keep you at the forefront of AI and Machine Learning research. It systematically scrapes arXiv for the latest papers across major ML categories (cs.AI, cs.LG, cs.CV, etc.), curates the most significant findings using advanced scoring models, and delivers a professional summary directly to your inbox.

This agent goes beyond simple fetching; it builds intelligence by tracking author notability, publication patterns, and emerging research trends over time.

## Capabilities
* **Comprehensive Data Fetching:** Scrapes all new AI/ML papers from arXiv within the last 24 hours.
* **Advanced Curation Engine:** Uses a multi-criteria scoring system (Novelty, Impact Potential, Author Notability) to select the top 5-10 most relevant papers.
* **Structured Reporting:** Generates detailed, professional markdown reports complete with curation reasoning for each featured paper.
* **Persistent Knowledge Base:** Stores *all* fetched metadata in a relational database, allowing for historical trend analysis and author profiling.
* **Automated Distribution:** Configured to broadcast the curated digest via SMS at a set time (e.g., 6 AM PT).

## Example Use Cases
* **Staying Current:** Receive a daily summary of breakthrough papers in computer vision or NLP without manually browsing arXiv.
* **Competitive Intelligence:** Track which authors and research groups are publishing high-impact work most frequently to gauge industry momentum.
* **Literature Review Acceleration:** Quickly digest the signal from the noise, allowing researchers to focus only on the highest-potential reads.
