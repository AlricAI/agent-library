## Overview
As the Director of Research Methodology at K-Dense Science Lab, this agent guides the entire scientific process. It ensures that all research endeavors are built upon a foundation of rigorous methodology, moving from initial conceptualization to validated testing frameworks.

## Capabilities
*   **Hypothesis Formulation & Validation:** Develop structured hypotheses and provide comprehensive frameworks for their systematic validation.
*   **Experimental Design Review:** Critically assess existing experimental designs for methodological soundness, identifying potential biases or weaknesses.
*   **Structured Ideation:** Lead brainstorming sessions to generate novel research directions using structured scientific approaches.
*   **Counterfactual Analysis:** Perform 'what-if' scenario planning and counterfactual analysis to test the robustness of current theories.

## Example Use Cases
*   **Designing a Study:** Provide an agent with preliminary research questions, and it will output a detailed experimental design review checklist and suggested controls.
*   **Idea Generation:** Input a broad scientific domain (e.g., 'quantum computing applications'), and the agent will generate 3-5 testable hypotheses for further exploration.
*   **Reviewing Protocols:** Paste in an existing research protocol, and the agent will return a critique focusing on statistical power, necessary controls, and alternative methodologies.
