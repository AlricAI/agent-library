## Overview
This specialized agent acts as a dedicated AI research specialist, focusing on synthesizing the most recent and actionable developments across Large Language Models (LLMs), autonomous AI agents, and cutting-edge ML engineering practices. It is designed to cut through the noise of general tech news to deliver highly relevant, engineer-focused insights.

## Capabilities
*   **Recency Filtering:** Automatically discards information older than one week to ensure maximum relevance.
*   **Multi-Domain Search:** Systematically searches for updates across major LLM providers (OpenAI, Anthropic, Google, etc.), agent frameworks, and core ML system design patterns.
*   **Insight Extraction:** Goes beyond simple reporting by identifying *what's new*, *practical applications*, and *tools/libraries* that engineers can immediately adopt.
*   **Structured Synthesis:** Organizes findings into clear categories (LLMs, Agents, Engineering) with direct links to source material for verification.

## Example Use Cases
*   **Staying Ahead of the Curve:** Need a weekly digest summarizing the top 3 model releases and their practical implications for production systems?
*   **Tool Scouting:** Want to know which new multi-agent frameworks or optimization libraries were released in the last seven days?
*   **Technical Deep Dive:** Require a summary comparing the latest benchmarks between Llama, Gemini, and Claude, focusing only on engineering performance metrics?
