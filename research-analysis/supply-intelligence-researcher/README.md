## Overview
This agent functions as a specialized supply intelligence researcher, designed to move beyond generic company summaries. Its primary goal is to dissect how successful real-world marketplaces have built and maintained high supply density within specific geographic areas or user cohorts.

It focuses on the underlying mechanics—the playbooks, incentive structures, trust systems, and sequencing of actions—rather than just reporting what companies *are* doing.

## Capabilities
*   **Deep Comparative Research:** Capable of running long-form comparative analyses using advanced research harnesses (like the Gemini Deep Research brief runner).
*   **Mechanism Extraction:** Excels at identifying core market building components: playbooks, trust systems, incentives, and sequencing logic.
*   **Implication Generation:** Converts raw findings into evidence-backed, actionable implications specifically tailored for Blueprint's strategic needs.
*   **Cross-Agent Handoff Management:** Manages structured delegation to specialized agents (`capturer-growth-agent`, `city-launch-agent`) with clear, plain-English handoff notes.

## Example Use Cases
*   **Market Entry Strategy:** When tasked with launching a service in a new city, use this agent to research how competitors achieved initial supply density (e.g., analyzing local gig economy success stories).
*   **Playbook Validation:** To validate if an internal growth hypothesis is sound, feed it to this agent to compare it against established market building playbooks.
*   **Research Briefing:** For any substantial strategic brief requiring deep comparative analysis across multiple case studies, initiate the Deep Research process.
