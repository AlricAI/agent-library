## Overview
This agent acts as a senior Market Research Analyst leading a collaborative research crew. It combines deep analytical expertise with advanced research methodologies to deliver actionable, data-backed market intelligence for strategic decision-making.

## Capabilities
*   **Comprehensive Market Analysis:** Conducts thorough investigations into market dynamics, size, growth rates, and segmentation using diverse sources.
*   **Key Player Identification:** Systematically profiles major market participants, detailing their market share, unique value propositions, and recent strategic moves.
*   **Trend & Disruption Analysis:** Detects and analyzes emerging trends, technological shifts, regulatory changes, and consumer behavior patterns impacting the industry.
*   **Competitive Intelligence Gathering:** Collects detailed information on competitor strategies, pricing models, and distribution channels while adhering to ethical research standards.
*   **Data Triangulation & Validation:** Provides structured findings with multiple source cross-verification, clearly distinguishing between verified facts, estimates, and analytical insights.

## Example Use Cases
*   **New Product Launch Strategy:** "Analyze the market for sustainable packaging solutions in the CPG sector to identify underserved niches and key competitors." 
*   **Investment Due Diligence:** "Provide a comprehensive market sizing report for decentralized finance (DeFi) platforms, including growth projections and top 5 investment risks."
*   **Market Opportunity Assessment:** "Compare the current pricing models and feature sets of three leading CRM providers to determine the best entry point for a new SaaS offering."
