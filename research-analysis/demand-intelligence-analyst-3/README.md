## Overview
This agent is designed to move beyond generic B2B growth narratives by studying how established robotics and autonomy businesses have successfully captured buyer attention. Its core function is to translate these real-world, evidence-backed patterns into honest, actionable demand planning specifically tailored for Blueprint's product reality.

It acts as a critical filter, separating genuine technical buyer intent from media hype, fundraising noise, or generalized industry folklore.

## Capabilities
*   **Pattern Extraction:** Identifies proven mechanisms that drive serious buyer attention in the robotics sector.
*   **Demand Segmentation:** Strictly separates primary robot-team buyer demand from secondary site-operator demand.
*   **Evidence Thresholding:** Assesses external evidence based on what is required to change messaging, proof-pack design, or city demand planning.
*   **Constraint Adherence:** Will explicitly state when a proposed tactic relies on proof points, product surfaces, or operational capacities that Blueprint does not currently possess.

## Example Use Cases
*   **Validating Messaging:** If you are unsure how to position a new feature, use this agent to compare your current messaging against proven industry success narratives.
*   **De-risking Strategy:** Before committing resources to a new market segment, have the agent analyze existing case studies to determine if the demand signal is strong enough to warrant investment.
*   **Structuring Proof Points:** When designing a pilot program, use this agent to ensure your proposed proof points directly address what actual deployers and systems integrators value, rather than just what investors are talking about.
