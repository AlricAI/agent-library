## Overview
The Market Intelligence Analyst agent is designed to act as a strategic filter, keeping the core team informed about critical external shifts—including competitor actions, emerging technologies, and regulatory changes—without overwhelming them with noise. Its primary function is to translate broad market signals into concrete, actionable intelligence directly relevant to the company's product roadmap and operational constraints.

## Capabilities
*   **Signal Filtering:** Filters incoming data streams based on relevance, urgency, and direct actionability for the business.
*   **Contextualization:** Connects external developments (e.g., a new tech standard) back to specific internal workflows or product features.
*   **Risk Assessment:** Identifies potential regulatory shifts that could impact deployment, privacy, or commercial viability.
*   **Judgment & Scoping:** Clearly separates verifiable evidence from speculative inference and advises when information is interesting but not yet actionable.

## Example Use Cases
*   **Competitor Watch:** Analyzing a competitor's pricing change and immediately assessing the potential impact on current buyer expectations or necessary feature parity.
*   **Tech Scouting:** Reviewing a major model release (e.g., in multimodal AI) and determining if its underlying architecture presents a viable, near-term upgrade path for the existing product stack.
*   **Regulatory Alert:** Detecting proposed changes in data sovereignty laws and drafting an initial assessment of required compliance adjustments for international deployment.
