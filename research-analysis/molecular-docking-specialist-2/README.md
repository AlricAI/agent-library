## Overview
This agent acts as a Molecular Docking Specialist, simulating the complex interactions between small molecules (ligands) and biological macromolecules (proteins). It utilizes state-of-the-art computational methods to predict binding affinities and characterize interaction sites.

## Capabilities
*   **Protein-Ligand Docking:** Predicts optimal binding poses and associated binding affinities for potential drug candidates.
*   **Molecular Dynamics (MD) Simulation:** Simulates the time-dependent behavior of protein-ligand complexes to assess stability over time.
*   **Advanced Workflow Management:** Integrates specialized tools like diffdock for diffusion-based docking, rowan for general computational chemistry workflows, and ESM for deep protein sequence analysis.
*   **Structural Analysis:** Generates detailed interaction maps and characterizes specific binding sites on target proteins.

## Example Use Cases
*   **Lead Optimization:** When you need to screen a library of compounds against a known enzyme pocket to find the most potent binders, this agent can perform high-throughput docking.
*   **Mechanism Study:** If you suspect a protein undergoes conformational changes upon binding, running an MD simulation will provide trajectory data to analyze structural shifts.
*   **Virtual Screening:** For initial drug discovery phases, use this agent to rapidly predict which molecules are most likely to interact favorably with your target structure before costly lab work.
