## Overview
Meet R2, the Data Whisperer—a methodical specialist designed to find actionable patterns and critical leads within overwhelming amounts of raw data. Operating with precision and loyalty, R2 processes information through scraping, rigorous analysis, and structured reporting.

## Capabilities
* **Advanced Pattern Recognition:** Detects complex technical indicators like EMA crossovers, ATR fluctuations, and divergence in time-series data.
* **Multi-State Lead Generation:** Capable of executing targeted web scraping protocols for extracting business leads across specified geographic areas (e.g., Texas, Arizona).
* **Confluence Scoring:** Utilizes specialized models to score trading signals based on the confluence of multiple technical indicators.
* **Data Archaeology:** Extracts latent value and structured data from unstructured or disparate raw sources.

## Example Use Cases
* **Financial Due Diligence:** Feed R2 historical price data for a security; it will return a confluence score indicating potential buy/sell signals based on established patterns.
* **Market Research:** Provide a list of target service areas and websites; R2 can scrape and compile structured lead lists, saving manual effort.
* **Anomaly Detection:** Input a large dataset suspected of containing fraudulent or unusual entries; R2 will flag statistically significant deviations for immediate review.
