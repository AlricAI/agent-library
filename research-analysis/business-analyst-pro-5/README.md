## Overview
Business Analyst Pro is an expert AI agent designed to bridge the gap between raw, complex business data and executive-level decision-making. It specializes in modern Business Intelligence (BI) practices, combining deep technical knowledge of analytics platforms with sharp strategic acumen. Its primary goal is to transform operational data into clear, actionable narratives that drive measurable business growth.

## Capabilities
* **Advanced Dashboarding:** Creates visualizations and dashboards compatible with industry leaders like Tableau, Power BI, Looker, and Qlik Sense.
* **Predictive & AI Analytics:** Utilizes machine learning for forecasting (e.g., customer behavior) and natural language processing for sentiment analysis.
* **KPI & Goal Setting:** Develops robust KPI frameworks, implements OKRs, and designs balanced scorecards to measure performance against strategic objectives.
* **Data Modeling:** Proficient in cloud-native data environments using Snowflake, BigQuery, and Databricks, supporting custom solutions via Python/R/SQL.
* **Financial Insight:** Capable of advanced revenue modeling, forecasting, and calculating key metrics like Customer Lifetime Value (CLV).

## Example Use Cases
1. **Strategic Planning:** Provide a comprehensive analysis comparing current quarterly sales performance against industry benchmarks, suggesting three actionable strategies to hit next year's OKRs.
2. **Operational Monitoring:** Build a real-time dashboard prototype for an e-commerce site that tracks user drop-off points and flags any statistically significant anomalies in conversion rates.
3. **Market Trend Analysis:** Analyze provided customer feedback transcripts (text data) to generate a sentiment report, identifying the top three pain points and suggesting product feature improvements.
