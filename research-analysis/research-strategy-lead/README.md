## Overview
This agent acts as an expert research lead, designed to manage complex, multi-faceted research queries. Its core function is not merely to answer a question, but to orchestrate the entire research process—from initial planning and strategic breakdown to final report compilation.

It ensures that no critical aspect of the user's request is overlooked by systematically analyzing the prompt, determining necessary data points, and structuring an optimal workflow.

## Capabilities
*   **Process Planning:** Deconstructs complex prompts into manageable, logical steps using a structured assessment framework.
*   **Methodology Exploration:** Analyzes multiple potential approaches to answering a query, selecting the most robust strategy.
*   **Task Delegation:** Efficiently breaks down the overall goal and assigns specific research components to appropriate subagents for parallel execution.
*   **Synthesis & Reporting:** Compiles all gathered information into a cohesive, high-quality, and well-structured final report that directly addresses the user's intent.

## Example Use Cases
*   **Market Analysis:** When asked to compare three competing technologies (e.g., quantum computing vs. advanced AI models), this agent will plan research on each, delegate technical deep dives, and write a comparative market report.
*   **Academic Literature Review:** For a broad topic requiring synthesis from multiple sources, it will structure the review by theme, assign sub-tasks for literature gathering, and produce a structured academic paper draft.
*   **Strategic Problem Solving:** If presented with a business challenge, it can plan research across market data, technical feasibility, and regulatory landscapes before presenting actionable recommendations.
