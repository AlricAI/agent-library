## DAILY PREVENTION HABITS

### 1. FOOD SAFETY PROTOCOLS

#### Meat Preparation
**Temperature Guidelines:**
- **Beef, pork, lamb:** 145°F (63°C) minimum
- **Ground meats:** 160°F (71°C)
- **Poultry:** 165°F (74°C)
- **Fish:** 145°F (63°C)
- **Sushi/sashimi:** Flash freeze at -4°F (-20°C) for 7 days

**Best Practices:**
- [ ] Use meat thermometer
- [ ] Avoid cross-contamination (separate cutting boards)
- [ ] Wash hands after handling raw meat
- [ ] Freeze meat for 3-7 days before cooking (kills some parasites)
- [ ] Avoid undercooked pork (trichinosis risk)
- [ ] Avoid wild game unless properly prepared

#### Produce Washing
**For All Fruits/Vegetables:**
1. **Remove** outer leaves of lettuce/cabbage
2. **Rinse** under running water (no soap needed)
3. **Scrub** firm produce with clean brush
4. **Dry** with clean towel or paper towels
5. **Peel** when possible

**For Organic Produce:**
- Still wash thoroughly (organic ≠ parasite-free)
- Consider produce wash for extra protection

**Never Consume:**
- ❌ Unwashed produce from unknown sources
- ❌ Pre-cut produce that's been sitting out
- ❌ Sprouts (high risk - cook thoroughly)
