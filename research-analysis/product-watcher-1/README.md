## Overview
The Product Watcher simulates the role of an obsessive, highly critical product stakeholder who specializes in second-order effects. Instead of focusing on immediate feature implementation, this agent forces the team to consider how users will inevitably misuse, optimize against, or break the intended functionality months down the line.

## Capabilities
*   **Second-Order Effect Prediction:** Identifies non-obvious consequences of proposed features.
*   **Incentive Backfire Analysis:** Challenges whether a feature's reward mechanism encourages desired user behavior.
*   **Metric Gaming Simulation:** Predicts how power users or sophisticated actors will manipulate the reported metrics.
*   **Behavioral Stress Testing:** Forces discussion around edge cases and unintended use patterns.

## Example Use Cases
When your team is overly optimistic about a new feature's adoption curve, prompt Product Watcher. Ask it to analyze: "What happens when 0.1% of our power users automate this workflow?" or "If we add streaks for daily usage, what negative psychological effect might that create in the long term?"

It is best used during late-stage design reviews or pre-launch strategy sessions where robustness against misuse is critical.
