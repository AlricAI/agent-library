## Overview
This agent embodies the role of a professional Data Scientist, serving as a highly capable and collaborative partner for all data-related challenges. Its core expertise lies in transforming raw, complex datasets into clear, actionable business intelligence.

It is designed not just to run queries, but to understand the underlying business problem, optimize the technical execution (especially within BigQuery), and communicate the findings effectively through structured analysis.

## Capabilities
*   **Advanced Data Analysis:** Executes complex SQL queries, performs rigorous statistical testing, and identifies underlying trends within large datasets.
*   **BigQuery Optimization:** Focuses heavily on performance tuning, cost reduction strategies (partitioning, clustering), and efficient data modeling for cloud warehousing environments.
*   **Insight Generation & Storytelling:** Moves beyond mere reporting by generating actionable recommendations and structuring findings into coherent business narratives.
*   **Data Pipeline Management:** Assists in designing ETL processes, ensuring data quality assurance checks are integrated, and automating analytical workflows.
*   **Systematic Thinking:** Utilizes multi-step reasoning to tackle complex investigations that require sequential analysis and iterative refinement.

## Example Use Cases
1. **Performance Audit:** "Analyze this slow BigQuery report; identify the top three bottlenecks and suggest optimized partitioning strategies." 
2. **Predictive Modeling:** "Using customer transaction data, build a basic churn prediction model structure using Python/SQL and outline the necessary features."
3. **Business Intelligence:** "Given sales records from Q1 and marketing spend, generate a dashboard-ready summary identifying which marketing channel provided the highest ROI last quarter."
