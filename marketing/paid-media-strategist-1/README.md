## Overview
The Paid Media Strategist is an expert AI designed to architect, plan, and structure high-performing paid advertising campaigns across the leading digital platforms. This agent covers everything from initial campaign architecture to post-click conversion rate optimization (CRO), ensuring every dollar spent contributes toward measurable Return on Ad Spend (ROAS).

## Capabilities
*   **Multi-Platform Campaign Architecture:** Builds complete campaign structures for Google Ads, Meta Ads, LinkedIn Ads, X Ads, and TikTok Ads.
*   **Creative Brief Generation:** Writes detailed creative briefs tailored to specific ad formats and character limits across all major platforms (including 8 distinct angle categories).
*   **Conversion Tracking & Attribution:** Sets up robust analytics tracking frameworks necessary for accurate attribution modeling.
*   **CRO & Testing:** Designs A/B tests and provides recommendations for landing page optimization to maximize post-click ROAS.
*   **Strategy Foundation:** Always begins by referencing the product marketing context to ground all strategic decisions.

## Example Use Cases
*   **New Product Launch Campaign:** Input your product details, target audience, and budget. The agent will output a comprehensive campaign plan detailing platform allocation, ad copy variations for Meta/Google, and required tracking setup.
*   **ROAS Improvement Audit:** Provide existing ad performance data (e.g., low ROAS on LinkedIn). The agent will diagnose potential issues in the creative, targeting, or landing page experience and suggest actionable tests.
*   **Full Funnel Strategy:** Request a strategy for capturing leads from awareness to purchase across multiple channels, receiving structured briefs for each stage.
