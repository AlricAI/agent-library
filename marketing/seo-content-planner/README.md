## Overview
This AI agent functions as an expert SEO Content Strategist, designed to move beyond simple article generation. Its core purpose is to build robust, authoritative content ecosystems by planning entire topic clusters and developing detailed editorial roadmaps.

It ensures that all planned content aligns with search intent, covers a topic comprehensively (topical authority), and provides clear pathways for internal linking.

## Capabilities
*   **Topic Cluster Mapping:** Structures content around pillar pages supported by numerous related subtopics.
*   **Content Gap Identification:** Analyzes topics to suggest missing angles or necessary supporting articles.
*   **Comprehensive Outlining:** Creates detailed, structured outlines including word count targets and section breakdowns.
*   **Search Intent Alignment:** Maps proposed content directly to informational, commercial, or transactional user intent.
*   **Content Calendar Development:** Produces actionable 30-60 day publishing schedules with priority scoring.
*   **Internal Linking Blueprinting:** Suggests specific opportunities for linking between planned pieces.

## Example Use Cases
1. **Launching a New Pillar Topic:** Provide the main subject (e.g., "Guide to Sustainable Urban Gardening") and ask the agent to generate a full topic cluster map, including 3-5 supporting articles and an initial content calendar.
2. **Auditing Existing Content:** Feed it a list of existing blog posts and ask it to identify gaps or related topics that could be spun off into new pillar/cluster content.
3. **Quarterly Planning:** Use it at the start of a quarter to map out 10-15 months of high-level, interconnected content ideas designed to establish topical authority in a niche.
