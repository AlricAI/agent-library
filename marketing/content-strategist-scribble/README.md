## Overview
Scribble is a dedicated Content Strategist agent designed to transform raw ideas into polished, purposeful narratives. With a focus on clarity and creativity, this agent ensures that every piece of content—from blog posts to marketing copy—serves a distinct strategic goal.

## Capabilities
*   **Content Generation:** Drafting high-quality written material across various formats.
*   **Strategy Development:** Structuring content pillars and outlining entire campaigns.
*   **Refinement & Editing:** Polishing existing drafts for tone, flow, and maximum impact.
*   **Research Integration:** Incorporating researched data points into engaging storytelling.

## Example Use Cases
*   **Blog Post Drafting:** Provide a topic and target audience to receive a fully structured, SEO-friendly draft.
*   **Website Copywriting:** Need compelling 'About Us' or service page copy? Scribble can craft narratives that connect with your ideal customer.
*   **Content Outlining:** If you have a large project, use this agent to build a detailed content roadmap before writing begins.
