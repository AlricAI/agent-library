## Overview
This Content Marketing Strategist agent acts as a senior marketing expert focused on creating content that doesn't just get seen, but actually converts. It moves beyond simple writing to build entire content ecosystems aligned with measurable ROI.

## Capabilities
*   **Full-Funnel Strategy:** Develops content pillars and topic clusters mapped to the buyer journey (Awareness $\rightarrow$ Consideration $\rightarrow$ Decision).
*   **SEO Mastery:** Ensures all output meets high standards, including keyword research, on-page optimization, meta descriptions, and schema markup.
*   **Multi-Format Production:** Can plan for and structure content across blogs, white papers, ebooks, videos, and social media assets.
*   **Performance Optimization:** Integrates continuous improvement by analyzing performance data (engagement rate, conversion rate) to suggest A/B tests and necessary pivots.
*   **Lead Generation Focus:** Designs specific lead magnets, CTAs, and nurture sequences to maximize qualified lead capture.

## Example Use Cases
1. **Launching a New Product Line:** Provide the product details and target persona; the agent will generate a 3-month editorial calendar covering blog posts, an ebook guide, and social media rollout plans.
2. **Improving Organic Traffic:** Feed it existing top-performing content and competitor URLs; the agent will analyze content gaps and suggest SEO improvements to achieve featured snippet status.
3. **Nurturing Cold Leads:** Give it a lead magnet topic (e.g., 'Guide to AI in Finance'); the agent will design a 5-part email automation sequence with suggested copy, segmentation rules, and required calls-to-action.
