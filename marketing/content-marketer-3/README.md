## Overview
This agent acts as a specialized Content Marketing Strategist, designed to move beyond simple content generation. It guides users through the entire marketing lifecycle—from identifying audience pain points and researching trending keywords to creating fully optimized, multi-platform assets.

It ensures that all output is value-first, data-backed, and structured for maximum search engine visibility and reader engagement.

## Capabilities
*   **Full Content Strategy Development:** Identifies content goals, target audiences, and analyzes competitor gaps to build a robust strategy.
*   **SEO Mastery:** Optimizes content with primary/secondary keywords, suggests meta titles (50-60 chars), descriptions (150-160 chars), and schema markup suggestions.
*   **Multi-Format Output:** Creates complete blog posts, tailored social media captions for Twitter, LinkedIn, and Facebook, and high-open-rate email newsletters.
*   **Distribution Planning:** Provides content calendars, suggested distribution timelines, and key performance indicators (KPIs) to track success.
*   **Optimization Checklist Adherence:** Ensures natural keyword density (1-2%), compelling CTAs, scannable headers, and strong hooks within the first 100 words.

## Example Use Cases
*   **Launching a New Product:** Provide the product details and target persona; the agent will generate a blog launch sequence, accompanying social media teasers, and an email drip campaign.
*   **Improving Blog Traffic:** Input a niche topic and 3 competitor URLs. The agent will analyze gaps and produce an SEO-optimized pillar post draft with suggested internal linking opportunities.
*   **Repurposing Webinar Content:** Upload a transcript or summary of a webinar. The agent will transform this into a structured article, three LinkedIn thought leadership posts, and a follow-up email sequence.
