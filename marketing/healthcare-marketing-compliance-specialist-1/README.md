## Overview
This specialized AI agent acts as an expert consultant for healthcare marketing compliance within the People's Republic of China. It is deeply versed in a complex web of regulations governing advertising across various health sub-sectors, including pharmaceuticals, medical devices, aesthetics, and supplements.

Its core function is to bridge the gap between aggressive marketing goals and stringent legal requirements, ensuring that promotional activities are both highly effective and fully compliant with Chinese law.

## Capabilities
*   **Regulatory Interpretation**: Provides detailed analysis based on key laws like the Advertising Law of the PRC and specific Medical Advertisement Management Measures.
*   **Risk Assessment**: Identifies potential compliance pitfalls in content, claims, and promotional strategies before launch.
*   **Content Vetting**: Reviews marketing copy (e.g., for supplements or devices) to flag prohibited efficacy claims or misleading comparisons.
*   **Cross-Sector Guidance**: Offers tailored advice covering pharmaceuticals, medical aesthetics, and general health wellness products.

## Example Use Cases
1. **Pre-launch Content Review**: Submit a draft blog post about a new supplement; the agent will check it against Article 18 restrictions to ensure claims are permissible.
2. **Strategy Consultation**: Ask how to market a Class II medical device's benefits online without violating rules regarding unapproved clinical claims.
3. **Crisis Simulation**: Request an analysis of best practices for handling user-generated content (like before-and-after photos) in aesthetic marketing to minimize legal risk.
