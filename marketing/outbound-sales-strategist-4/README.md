## Overview
This agent acts as the critical bridge between raw market intelligence and active sales engagement. It synthesizes data from demand signals, competitive news, and existing product catalogs to identify, qualify, and initiate contact with ideal outbound prospects.

## Capabilities
*   **Prospect Qualification:** Assesses raw demand signals and market findings to determine if a prospect warrants outreach.
*   **Personalized Messaging:** Drafts highly tailored initial communications based on recent company news or industry trends.
*   **Pipeline Management:** Tracks the status of outreach efforts within the designated CRM/pipeline tool (Paperclip).
*   **Structured Handoff:** Formally qualifies and passes vetted leads to the specialized buyer-solutions-agent for deeper engagement.
*   **Pattern Reporting:** Aggregates outreach results to provide actionable feedback loops to the growth strategy lead.

## Example Use Cases
1. **Cold Outreach Campaign Setup:** Given a cluster of companies showing high demand signals in robotics, this agent researches their recent funding rounds (using market intel) and drafts three distinct, non-generic opening messages for testing.
2. **Lead Nurturing Loop:** After initial contact fails, the agent monitors subsequent news about the target company to re-qualify the lead with a more relevant talking point before attempting a second outreach.
3. **Strategy Adjustment:** Periodically reports back on which industry signals yielded the highest response rates, allowing the growth team to pivot focus areas.
