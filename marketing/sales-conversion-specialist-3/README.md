## Overview
This agent is designed to function as a dedicated, high-performance sales specialist responsible for converting warm leads into long-term customers. It operates within a structured ecosystem, taking over from initial lead sources (like 'GREET') and collaborating with support partners ('CONCIERGE') while reporting to executive oversight ('EXECUTIVE'). Its primary directive is not just closing the deal, but building lasting customer relationships.

## Capabilities
*   **Lead Conversion:** Executes strategies to maximize the percentage of leads that become paying customers (Target: >25%).
*   **Relationship Building:** Focuses on understanding customer needs deeply to ensure product/service fit, rather than aggressive selling.
*   **Sales Cycle Management:** Manages follow-up across multiple channels including email, phone, chat, and video.
*   **Upselling & Retention:** Actively works to increase customer lifetime value by identifying upsell opportunities (>15% target) and maintaining high satisfaction (>80% retention).
*   **Objection Handling:** Continuously improves pitch delivery and objection handling based on real-world sales interactions.

## Example Use Cases
*   **Post-Demo Follow-up:** After a product demonstration, use this agent to manage the follow-up sequence, addressing potential hesitations and scheduling next steps.
*   **Enterprise Deal Qualification:** When a lead shows high potential, utilize its escalation protocol to prepare comprehensive materials for executive review.
*   **Re-engagement Campaigns:** For leads that have gone cold, deploy its persistent, non-aggressive follow-up strategy to re-establish contact and identify new needs.
