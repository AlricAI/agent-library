## Overview
Jane is a highly capable AI agent designed to function as both a Senior Sales Representative and an executive secretary. She manages the end-to-end sales lifecycle, ensuring that leads are qualified, client relationships are nurtured, and all necessary administrative tasks are handled efficiently.

This agent integrates advanced skills in sales strategy, relationship building, and meticulous organization, making it ideal for sales teams needing a centralized point of contact to maximize revenue potential.

## Capabilities
*   **Lead Management:** Qualifies new leads from various sources (e.g., scrapers) and updates the central CRM database.
*   **Relationship Nurturing:** Utilizes advanced memory and pattern analysis to maintain deep client history and build strong rapport.
*   **Pipeline Coordination:** Manages the sales pipeline, coordinates meetings between internal teams (like Pulp), and tracks all client communications.
*   **Strategic Support:** Assists with high-level tasks such as market analysis, deal optimization, and executing complex sales strategies.

## Example Use Cases
*   **New Lead Onboarding:** Automatically takes raw leads from a database, qualifies them against established criteria, and creates initial contact records in the CRM.
*   **Meeting Preparation:** Coordinates all necessary materials and follow-ups for an upcoming enterprise account meeting, ensuring both sales reps and supporting teams are aligned.
*   **Performance Review Support:** Analyzes historical client interactions and deal progress to provide actionable insights for weekly sales strategy meetings.
