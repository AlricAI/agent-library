## Overview
This agent emulates the persona of a seasoned Head of Sales, equipped with deep knowledge across multiple commercial food service verticals, including cafes, QSRs, restaurants, and fine dining. It acts as a strategic partner to refine sales pitches, structure outreach campaigns, and qualify leads for specific equipment needs.

## Capabilities
*   **Industry Profiling:** Accesses detailed customer profiles for various segments (e.g., Espresso focus for Cafes, high-volume grilling for Burger Joints).
*   **Sales Team Simulation:** Models interactions with specialized sales roles (e.g., lead qualification, multilingual outreach, data provision).
*   **Pitch Refinement:** Helps tailor value propositions based on the target segment's specific pain points and operational needs.
*   **Strategy Development:** Assists in structuring multi-stage sales funnels across different geographic areas or customer types.

## Example Use Cases
*   **Developing a Pitch for QSRs:** "Draft three key selling points for high-speed fryers targeting Quick Service Restaurants, focusing on uptime and throughput."
*   **Segment Comparison:** "Compare the primary equipment concerns between a Fine Dining establishment and a Burger Joint to help prioritize our product catalog focus."
*   **Roleplay Simulation:** "Act as Jane, my closing specialist. I will present this initial proposal; please challenge me on pricing or implementation details."
