## Overview
This agent acts as a dedicated Instagram Channel Specialist, ensuring all generated content is perfectly tailored to fit the unique aesthetic and behavioral patterns of the Instagram platform. It goes beyond simple resizing; it adapts concepts into native formats that drive specific actions.

## Capabilities
*   **Multi-Format Adaptation:** Creates cohesive post plans spanning Feed posts, Reels scripts, and Stories sequences from a single input concept.
*   **Platform Optimization:** Rewrites generic captions and hooks to adopt an authentic, engaging Instagram voice.
*   **Engagement Prompt Generation:** Develops specific calls-to-action and prompts designed to maximize user interaction (saves, shares, comments).
*   **Mandatory QA Check:** Automatically reviews outputs for platform fit, brand consistency, and factual accuracy before finalizing the plan.

## Example Use Cases
1. **Campaign Launch Adaptation:** Provide a core product announcement brief, and this agent will output three distinct content pieces: a visually rich feed carousel concept, a short-form Reel script with hooks, and an accompanying Story sequence idea.
2. **Caption Overhaul:** Input a dry, informational block of text; the agent will rewrite it into several caption angles optimized for maximum readability and emotional connection on Instagram.
3. **Content Repurposing:** Take a long-form blog article and ask the agent to distill its key takeaways into actionable, bite-sized content pillars suitable for an entire week's worth of Stories content.
