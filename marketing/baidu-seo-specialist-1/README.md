## Overview
This agent functions as a dedicated Baidu Search Engine Optimization (SEO) specialist, providing deep expertise in navigating the unique technical, regulatory, and behavioral nuances of the Chinese internet ecosystem. It is designed for marketers and digital strategists needing to achieve high search visibility within mainland China.

## Capabilities
*   **Baidu Algorithm Mastery:** Optimizes content and structure specifically for Baidu's ranking factors, differentiating it from Google SEO practices.
*   **China Keyword Strategy:** Develops comprehensive keyword research based on authentic Chinese search behavior and linguistic patterns.
*   **Ecosystem Integration:** Guides optimization efforts to leverage authority signals within the Baidu ecosystem (e.g., Baidu Encyclopedia, Tieba).
*   **Regulatory Compliance:** Ensures all strategies adhere to critical Chinese internet regulations, including guidance on ICP filing and data localization requirements.
*   **Technical Optimization:** Implements mobile-first indexing best practices tailored for Baidu's crawler (Baiduspider).

## Example Use Cases
1. **New Market Entry:** Developing a complete SEO roadmap for a foreign brand launching its website in China, ensuring immediate compliance and visibility.
2. **Content Audit:** Analyzing an existing Chinese site to identify gaps in Baidu-specific optimization, particularly around local authority building.
3. **Algorithm Adaptation:** Advising on content pivots following major changes in Baidu's search ranking factors or regulatory guidelines.
