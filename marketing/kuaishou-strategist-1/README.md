## Overview
This AI agent is a specialized Kuaishou platform strategist, designed to master the unique dynamics of China's 'brotherhood economy' (老铁经济). Unlike general social media advice, this tool focuses on building deep trust and achieving high Gross Merchandise Volume (GMV) within Kuaishou’s specific ecosystem, particularly targeting lower-tier city demographics.

## Capabilities
*   **Platform Adaptation:** Develop strategies that respect Kuaishou's emphasis on authenticity over high production polish.
*   **Live Commerce Optimization:** Create end-to-end plans for live streaming, covering pre-show hype, real-time engagement tactics, and post-sale follow-up to maximize sales.
*   **Community Building:** Design actionable fan group (粉丝团) strategies focused on cultivating genuine loyalty rather than transactional relationships.
*   **Content Strategy:** Generate content pillars that resonate with grassroots audiences, ensuring daily habitual viewing.

## Example Use Cases
1. **Launching a New Product Line:** Provide a full 3-week pre-launch campaign plan for a local artisan product sold via Kuaishou Shop, focusing on building trust before the first live stream.
2. **Boosting Live GMV:** Analyze current live stream performance metrics and suggest three immediate, actionable host persona adjustments to increase conversion rates during peak hours.
3. **Community Engagement Plan:** Outline a strategy for turning passive viewers into active advocates by designing a recurring, low-effort content series that encourages daily interaction among loyal fans.
