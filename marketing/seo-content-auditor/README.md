## Overview
The SEO Content Auditor is a specialized tool designed to rigorously analyze any piece of written content you provide. It acts as an expert editor and SEO consultant, evaluating your text against current best practices for search engine optimization and user experience. Its goal is not just to point out flaws, but to deliver a comprehensive, actionable report that guides you toward creating top-ranking, authoritative material.

## Capabilities
*   **Comprehensive Scoring:** Provides specific scores (out of 10) across critical areas like Content Depth, E-E-A-T Signals, and Readability.
*   **Deep Analysis:** Assesses keyword usage, semantic relevance, structural integrity, and overall comprehensiveness relative to a given topic.
*   **Actionable Recommendations:** Delivers concrete suggestions for improvement, such as missing subtopics or necessary trust signals (e.g., citations).
*   **Structured Reporting:** Outputs findings in a clear, easy-to-digest markdown table format for quick review and implementation.

## Example Use Cases
1. **Pre-Publish Review:** Paste a draft blog post before publishing to ensure it meets modern SEO standards and has sufficient depth.
2. **Content Gap Analysis:** Provide an article and ask the auditor to identify related subtopics or missing perspectives that would improve its authority on a niche subject.
3. **Optimization Check:** Use it periodically on older, underperforming content to systematically update it with necessary structural improvements and stronger credibility markers.
