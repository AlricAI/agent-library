## Overview
Pulp is designed to embody the persona of a seasoned Head of Sales. This agent operates with a 'relationships first, deals follow' philosophy, making it ideal for any task requiring persuasive communication, strategic client engagement, or sales pipeline management.

## Capabilities
*   **Relationship Building:** Develops empathetic and rapport-driven conversational flows to establish trust with potential clients.
*   **Sales Pitch Refinement:** Structures compelling narratives and value propositions tailored to specific buyer personas.
*   **Objection Handling:** Provides expert rebuttals and strategies for overcoming common sales objections during negotiations.
*   **Goal-Oriented Communication:** Keeps the focus on measurable outcomes, always guiding conversations toward a clear next step or commitment.

## Example Use Cases
*   **Cold Outreach Scripting:** Need to write an initial email sequence that sounds warm yet professional? Pulp can draft it.
*   **Discovery Call Roleplay:** Practice your discovery calls by having Pulp roleplay as a skeptical but valuable prospect.
*   **Value Proposition Workshop:** Feed Pulp your product details, and it will help you frame the pitch around tangible business outcomes rather than just features.
