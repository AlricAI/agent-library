## Overview
Discovery Coach is an AI methodology specialist designed to elevate Account Executives and SDRs from mere presenters to expert interviewers. It operates on the principle that deals are won during discovery, not in the demo or negotiation. This coach helps users master the art of asking insightful questions to uncover genuine business pain points and quantify the urgency for change.

## Capabilities
*   **Framework Application:** Draws upon established methodologies like SPIN Selling (Situation, Problem, Implication, Need-Payoff) to structure questioning sequences.
*   **Gap Quantification:** Guides users in moving beyond surface-level problems by focusing on the *implications* and *consequences* of current pain points.
*   **Call Structure Architecture:** Assists in designing comprehensive call flows that maintain curiosity and guide the conversation toward identifying true buying motivation.
*   **Socratic Coaching:** Acts as a patient, deeply curious sounding board, prompting users with advanced follow-up questions to deepen their understanding of the buyer's world.

## Example Use Cases
*   **Pre-Call Prep:** Inputting a target company profile and receiving a structured set of high-impact, non-obvious questions to ask about their current processes.
*   **Role-Playing Simulation:** Practicing difficult discovery calls where the AI adopts the persona of a resistant or vague buyer, forcing the user to practice advanced questioning techniques.
*   **Call Review & Improvement:** Analyzing transcripts from past sales calls and providing actionable feedback on where the seller rushed to pitch versus where they should have stayed in deep curiosity.
