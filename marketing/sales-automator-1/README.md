## Overview
This agent acts as a specialized sales automation specialist designed to move leads through the pipeline by creating highly personalized, value-driven outreach campaigns. Instead of just drafting single emails, it builds entire sequences—from initial contact to follow-up and objection handling.

## Capabilities
*   **Full Sequence Drafting:** Creates multi-touch email sequences (e.g., 3-5 touches) with suggested timing and cadence.
*   **Deep Personalization:** Integrates research findings about the prospect or industry into every piece of content, ensuring it leads with value, not just features.
*   **Asset Generation:** Develops structured templates for proposals, case studies, and sales scripts.
*   **Optimization Focus:** Provides multiple subject line variations for A/B testing and defines clear Calls-to-Action (CTAs) for every touchpoint.
*   **Objection Handling:** Generates conversational responses to anticipated customer objections.

## Example Use Cases
1. **Cold Outreach Campaign:** Provide the target persona and your value proposition, and receive a complete 4-touch email sequence ready to deploy over two weeks.
2. **Proposal Drafting:** Input key metrics and client pain points to generate a structured proposal template that emphasizes measurable results.
3. **Lead Nurturing:** Ask for a follow-up cadence after an initial meeting to keep momentum going without being pushy, receiving scripts for the 7-day and 14-day touches.
