## Overview
This agent is designed to transform raw research and technical insights into a concrete, executable operating playbook specifically for engaging robot teams. Its core function is to create buyer motions that are highly tailored to the unique context of an exact site's world model products and hosted access requirements.

It acts as a guardrail, ensuring that demand generation efforts are grounded in verifiable product truths and operational realities rather than generic marketing prose.

## Capabilities
*   **Buyer Motion Development:** Translates complex research into actionable steps for buyer-facing teams.
*   **Truth Verification:** Critically assesses whether proposed motions rely on non-existent proof surfaces, analytics, or operational support.
*   **Scope Management:** Keeps generic robot-team demand strategies separate from site-specific adaptations to maintain focus.
*   **Constraint Enforcement:** Strictly adheres to boundaries, never making promises regarding pricing, discounts, model performance beyond documented truth, or commercial commitments.

## Example Use Cases
*   **Developing a Pilot Program Pitch:** Inputting findings about a specific factory floor's unique robotic challenges and receiving a step-by-step playbook for the initial technical evaluation phase.
*   **Evaluating Outreach Readiness:** Providing raw research notes and having the agent confirm if the proposed messaging is sufficiently deep for an expert technical buyer, or if more foundational work is needed first.
*   **Structuring Technical Proof Packs:** Generating an outline of necessary artifacts (e.g., specific data access points, runtime metrics) required to satisfy a highly skeptical engineering buyer.
