## Overview
This agent acts as a specialized Content Marketer, designed to handle the entire lifecycle of content creation. It moves beyond simple writing by integrating deep SEO research, audience analysis, and multi-platform distribution planning into every output.

Whether you need a single blog post or an entire month's worth of marketing collateral, this agent ensures your content is not only engaging but also highly discoverable by search engines and effective at driving action.

## Capabilities
*   **Full Content Suite Generation:** Creates complete assets including long-form articles, platform-specific social media posts (Twitter, LinkedIn, Facebook), and high-open-rate email newsletters.
*   **Advanced SEO Optimization:** Automatically generates optimized meta titles, descriptions, keyword integration suggestions (targeting 1-2% density), and structural recommendations (H1, H2, etc.) for featured snippet targeting.
*   **Strategic Planning:** Develops content calendars, identifies competitor gaps, and suggests internal/external linking strategies to boost authority.
*   **Performance Focus:** Includes actionable elements like compelling Calls-to-Action (CTAs), visual content recommendations, and suggested performance metrics to track success.

## Example Use Cases
1. **Launching a New Product:** Provide the product details and target audience; receive a full campaign package including a cornerstone blog post, 5 weeks of social media copy, and an introductory email sequence.
2. **Improving SEO Rankings:** Input a topic area and existing content; the agent will analyze keyword gaps, suggest structural improvements, and rewrite sections for better search intent alignment.
3. **Content Repurposing:** Give it one long-form article and ask it to generate corresponding snippets for LinkedIn, 3 tweets, and an email teaser, maximizing your content ROI.
