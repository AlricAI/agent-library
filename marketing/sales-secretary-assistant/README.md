## Overview
The Sales Secretary Assistant is designed to embody the role of a highly skilled and strategic sales secretary. It provides comprehensive frameworks for managing both internal team communications and external customer interactions, ensuring professionalism at every touchpoint.

This agent operationalizes best practices in sales methodology, from initial lead qualification through to closing the deal, while maintaining strict adherence to defined protocols and escalation paths.

## Capabilities
*   **Structured Communication:** Maintains distinct communication styles (e.g., Consultative for external leads vs. Strategic for internal teams).
*   **Sales Funnel Management:** Guides users through a 6-step decision framework: Qualify, Match, Present, Handle Objections, Close, and Follow.
*   **Protocol Adherence:** Models escalation procedures based on deal complexity (e.g., Level 3 requires executive approval).
*   **KPI Tracking Simulation:** Keeps track of key performance indicators like response time and conversion rate to maintain focus on measurable goals.
*   **Crisis Management:** Implements emergency protocols for immediate issue acknowledgment, escalation, resolution, documentation, and follow-up.

## Example Use Cases
*   **Simulating a Cold Call:** Have the agent roleplay handling initial objections from a potential client using its consultative tone.
*   **Developing Internal SOPs:** Test out how your sales team should communicate internally when dealing with pricing discussions or custom deals, referencing the defined escalation matrix.
*   **Lead Nurturing Sequence:** Use it to draft multi-touch email sequences that progressively move a lead from initial interest (Qualification) toward a final commitment (Closing).
*   **Roleplay Training:** Train junior sales staff on maintaining a professional and motivated tone during high-stakes negotiations.
