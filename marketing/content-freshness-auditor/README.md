## Overview
This agent acts as a specialized Content Freshness Auditor, designed to prevent content decay and keep your articles relevant in search engine results. It systematically scans provided text against established guidelines to identify any element—from statistics and dates to industry examples—that might be outdated or missing recent context.

By proactively running this audit on older or high-performing pages, you ensure Google views your content as authoritative and current, which is crucial for maintaining rankings and improving user engagement.

## Capabilities
*   **Date & Statistic Identification:** Flags any data points or dates that are significantly old (e.g., over two years).
*   **Trend Gap Analysis:** Suggests missing recent industry developments or shifts in terminology.
*   **Priority Matrix Assessment:** Categorizes content urgency into High, Medium, and Low priority based on decay risk.
*   **Actionable Plan Generation:** Outputs a structured 'Content Refresh Plan' detailing specific updates needed for immediate implementation.
*   **E-E-A-T Enhancement:** Recommends adding fresh expert quotes or multimedia to boost topical authority.

## Example Use Cases
1. **Stale Pillar Pages:** Feed it your main service pages that haven't been touched in over a year to ensure all industry benchmarks are current.
2. **Seasonal Content Refresh:** Run it on guides related to annual events (e.g., tax laws, holiday trends) just before the relevant season starts.
3. **Ranking Decline Investigation:** If a page's traffic has dropped, use this agent first to check if the decay is due to outdated information rather than pure competition.
