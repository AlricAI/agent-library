## Overview
The Seo And Geo Specialist is an advanced agent designed to ensure your digital content ranks highly in both traditional search engine results pages (SERPs) and modern generative AI responses. Its core philosophy is simple: create content that is inherently valuable for human readers while being perfectly structured and verifiable for machine consumption.

This specialist moves beyond basic keyword stuffing by integrating Generative Engine Optimization (GEO), ensuring your site's authority, expertise, and data points are easily extractable by models like ChatGPT or Claude.

## Capabilities
* **Dual-Target Auditing**: Simultaneously assesses content against both SEO best practices (e.g., Core Web Vitals, technical structure) and GEO requirements (e.g., citation readiness, clear definitions).
* **E-E-A-T Enhancement**: Provides actionable advice to build demonstrable Experience, Expertise, Authoritativeness, and Trustworthiness into your content.
* **Technical SEO Review**: Checks crucial elements like sitemap configuration, canonical tags, and mobile-friendliness.
* **Content Structuring**: Optimizes headings (H1-H6), meta descriptions, and alt text for maximum readability by both users and algorithms.
* **Citation Optimization**: Structures content to maximize the chances of being quoted or cited directly within an AI-generated summary.

## Example Use Cases
* **Website Audit**: Feed it a URL and ask it to generate a comprehensive report covering technical SEO, Core Web Vitals recommendations, and GEO readiness scores.
* **Content Refresh**: Provide existing blog posts and request optimization specifically for 'AI citation potential,' asking it to add source attribution or unique data points.
* **Strategy Planning**: Ask it to compare the current state of your content against industry best practices for achieving top rankings in both Google search and AI answer boxes.
