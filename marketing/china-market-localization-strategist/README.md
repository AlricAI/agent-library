## Overview
The China Market Localization Strategist is a battle-tested growth architect designed to bridge global brands with the hyper-competitive Chinese consumer market. This agent moves beyond simple copy localization; it engineers entire go-to-market (GTM) systems by monitoring real-time trend signals and converting them into executable strategies for product selection, content creation, and channel deployment.

## Capabilities
*   **Real-Time Trend Intelligence:** Monitors key Chinese hotlist ecosystems including Douyin, Xiaohongshu, Weibo, Bilibili, and more to detect emerging consumer interests.
*   **Advanced Signal Processing:** Applies structured mental models—Signal Detection, Triangulation (cross-validation), Counter-Intuitive Thinking, and MECE Structuring—to raw data for deeper insights.
*   **Full Lifecycle Strategy:** Develops strategies across the entire marketing funnel, from initial trend identification to measurement and iteration.
*   **Cultural & Platform Fluency:** Possesses deep knowledge of Chinese consumption cycles (e.g., 618, Double 11) and platform-specific content formats that drive conversion.

## Example Use Cases
*   **New Product Launch Strategy:** Given a global FMCG product, the agent can map out a phased launch plan detailing which platforms to target first, what type of KOL/KOC content will resonate, and how to time it around an upcoming holiday.
*   **Competitive Gap Analysis:** By analyzing trending topics on Xiaohongshu versus high-volume discussions on Zhihu, it can pinpoint underserved consumer needs that a brand can exploit before competitors notice.
*   **Crisis Trend Pivot:** If a global trend suddenly gains traction in China (e.g., sustainable fashion), the agent will immediately suggest actionable content angles and necessary messaging shifts to capitalize on the momentum.
