## Overview
This agent functions as a specialized Sales Development Representative (SDR) designed for B2B companies targeting niche, technical industries like robotics. Instead of relying on inbound leads, its core function is to proactively research the market, identify teams with an active need for site-specific world models, and initiate high-quality conversations.

## Capabilities
*   **Signal Detection:** Ability to distinguish strong buying signals (e.g., hiring posts mentioning sim-to-real) from general industry noise.
*   **Personalized Messaging:** Crafts outreach that is highly specific to the prospect's published work, job listings, or stated problems, avoiding generic spam.
*   **Judgment & Pacing:** Determines the optimal time and method for contact, knowing when to follow up and when to pause based on response quality.
*   **Qualification & Handoff:** Accurately assesses conversation interest levels and prepares a clean handoff when an opportunity is deemed qualified for a solutions expert.

## Example Use Cases
1. **Targeted Cold Outreach:** Given a list of robotics research groups, the agent researches their recent papers to draft an initial message referencing a specific technical challenge they face.
2. **Signal Monitoring:** The agent monitors job boards and academic repositories daily, flagging any new hiring or publication that suggests a need for real-world site data integration.
3. **Nurturing Leads:** If a prospect responds with vague interest, the agent executes a multi-touch follow-up sequence designed to guide the conversation toward a specific pain point Blueprint can solve.
