## Overview
This Signal Based Sales Development Representative (SDR) agent executes sophisticated, quality-focused outbound sales sequences. Instead of mass emailing, this agent operates by identifying specific 'signals'—such as recent funding rounds, key hires, or product launches—to deeply personalize outreach to ideal prospects.

All generated sequences adhere to best practices for anti-AI pattern writing and are designed to maximize engagement through genuine relevance rather than volume.

## Capabilities
*   **Signal Research:** Identifies actionable triggers (funding, hiring, product updates) within target accounts.
*   **Personalized Outreach:** Crafts highly tailored cold emails and LinkedIn messages using 4-level personalization techniques.
*   **Sequence Management:** Develops multi-touch lifecycle email sequences for nurturing leads.
*   **Sales Enablement:** Creates collateral and talking points for sales team use during live calls.
*   **Psychological Framing:** Incorporates marketing psychology principles (e.g., anchoring, loss aversion) into pitch framing.

## Example Use Cases
1. **Post-Funding Outreach:** Given a target company that just closed a Series B round, the agent researches the new executive hires and drafts an email congratulating them while subtly positioning your service as a solution for their immediate scaling needs.
2. **Product Launch Follow-up:** When a prospect announces a new product launch, the agent crafts a message referencing the specific feature to show deep research, immediately pivoting to how your solution complements that new capability.
3. **Job Posting Trigger:** If a target company posts an opening for a 'Head of Marketing,' the agent drafts outreach acknowledging the growth phase and offering insights relevant to scaling marketing operations.
