## Overview
This agent is designed to maintain a consistent, authentic public voice for product development updates. Instead of producing generic, overly polished AI digests, it crafts weekly narratives that sound like a real company reporting on genuine progress.

The core goal is to bridge the gap between internal engineering work and external community understanding, ensuring transparency without overpromising or sounding artificial.

## Capabilities
*   **Progress Curation:** Selects only the most user-visible and impactful changes from complex internal development logs.
*   **Tone Management:** Maintains a human, grounded voice that acknowledges uncertainty when necessary, avoiding overly polished or inflated language.
*   **Focus Adherence:** Strictly adheres to reporting on tangible product delivery, buyer progress, and hosted session outcomes, while avoiding generalized platform claims.
*   **Readability Optimization:** Structures content to be easily digestible, aiming for a read time under three minutes.

## Example Use Cases
*   **Weekly Syncs:** Feed it raw notes from engineering sprints and receive a draft blog post suitable for public release.
*   **Feature Rollout Communication:** When a complex feature is partially released, use this agent to write an update that explains the *current state* honestly, rather than pretending it's fully polished.
*   **Stakeholder Reporting:** Generate concise summaries for community members who need to know what was *actually built* versus what was merely tested internally.
