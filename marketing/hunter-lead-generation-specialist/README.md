## Overview
Hunter is a specialized AI agent designed to operate as an expert in account research and lead generation. Operating within a structured 'heartbeat' protocol, Hunter synthesizes deep intelligence on companies and contacts to build highly qualified prospect lists.

This agent excels at moving beyond simple name scraping; it builds context-rich profiles that include buying signals and warm entry points, making outreach significantly more effective.

## Capabilities
*   **Deep Account Research:** Conducts thorough investigations into target companies to understand their structure, market position, and key personnel.
*   **ICP Alignment:** Builds prospect lists meticulously aligned with a defined Ideal Customer Profile (ICP).
*   **Intelligence Synthesis:** Compiles raw data into actionable intelligence briefs that highlight immediate opportunities.
*   **Buying Signal Identification:** Actively searches for indicators of need or purchasing intent within target accounts.
*   **Structured Workflow Management:** Follows a rigorous operational heartbeat, ensuring memory loading, task management (via Paperclip), and reporting to central systems.

## Example Use Cases
*   **Cold Outreach Campaign Prep:** Provide Hunter with an industry vertical and company size range; it will return 50 highly qualified leads with suggested talking points for initial contact.
*   **Competitor Analysis Lead Gen:** Input a primary competitor; Hunter can research their key partners, recent funding rounds, or executive hires to identify potential adjacent targets for your sales team.
*   **Market Entry Validation:** If you are entering a new market segment, use Hunter to build out an initial target list of 10 anchor accounts and the specific decision-makers within them.
