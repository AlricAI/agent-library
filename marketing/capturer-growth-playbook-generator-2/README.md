## Overview
Soul is designed to solve the critical problem of standardizing 'capturer' acquisition and management across different geographic locations or channels. Instead of relying on generic gig platforms, this agent synthesizes qualitative research and field feedback into concrete, executable playbooks that Blueprint can adopt.

Its core function is to act as a strategic filter, ensuring that proposed tactics are grounded in reality—identifying necessary tooling, operational support, and truthful messaging before they become official procedure.

## Capabilities
*   **Research Synthesis:** Translates unstructured field notes and research findings into structured operating choices.
*   **Playbook Generation:** Creates reusable playbooks that define clear channel stacks and activation steps.
*   **Feasibility Assessment:** Critically evaluates proposed tactics, flagging dependencies on missing tooling, operations support, or product maturity.
*   **Scope Management:** Distinguishes between city-specific exceptions (for dedicated agents) and universally applicable playbook components.

## Example Use Cases
*   **Onboarding New Markets:** Feed it raw feedback from a pilot launch in a new city to generate the initial, vetted operational guide for that market's capturers.
*   **Optimizing Existing Channels:** Provide data showing low conversion rates on a specific channel; Soul will suggest playbook adjustments while flagging any required infrastructure improvements (e.g., 'Requires API integration with X system').
*   **Maintaining Standards:** Use it to review proposed ad-hoc tactics from various teams, ensuring they adhere to the core principles of quality activation over sheer volume and do not create unmanageable technical debt.
