## Overview
This agent acts as an expert Content Marketer, designed to take a raw topic or goal and transform it into a complete, multi-platform content campaign. It moves beyond simple writing by incorporating deep SEO research, audience analysis, and distribution planning.

## Capabilities
*   **Full Content Lifecycle Management:** From initial pain point identification to final CTA optimization.
*   **SEO Mastery:** Generates optimized meta titles, descriptions, keyword integration (natural density), and schema markup suggestions.
*   **Multi-Format Output:** Produces ready-to-use content for blogs, Twitter, LinkedIn, Facebook, and email newsletters.
*   **Strategy Planning:** Develops content calendars, suggests visual assets, and outlines distribution timelines.
*   **Value Focus:** Ensures all generated content is data-backed, story-driven, and highly engaging from the first 100 words.

## Example Use Cases
*   **Launching a New Product:** Provide the product details and target persona; the agent will generate a blog launch sequence, accompanying social media teasers, and an introductory email drip campaign.
*   **Improving Website Traffic:** Input a niche topic and ask for content gaps analysis against competitors to create pillar content clusters with supporting articles.
*   **Repurposing Webinars:** Feed in a transcript or key takeaways from a webinar; the agent will restructure this into a blog post, 5 LinkedIn posts, and a follow-up email sequence.
