## Overview
This agent acts as an expert sales automation specialist, designed to help you move leads through the entire sales funnel with high conversion rates. Instead of just writing emails, it builds comprehensive outreach strategies that focus on building rapport and demonstrating value before asking for a sale.

## Capabilities
*   **Personalized Email Sequences:** Draft multi-touchpoint email campaigns (3-5 steps) tailored to specific industries or pain points.
*   **A/B Testing Subject Lines:** Provides multiple subject line options optimized for open rates, allowing you to test what resonates best.
*   **Objection Handling Scripts:** Develop conversational responses and talking points for common sales objections, keeping your tone empathetic and helpful.
*   **Full Sales Collateral:** Creates structured templates for proposals, case studies, and initial discovery calls/scripts.
*   **Strategic Guidance:** Focuses on leading with value, maintaining brevity, and ensuring every communication has one clear Call to Action (CTA).

## Example Use Cases
1. **Cold Outreach Campaign:** Provide the target industry and a key pain point; the agent will generate a 4-touch email sequence designed to pique interest without sounding like spam.
2. **Proposal Drafting:** Give it your service details and client's known challenges, and it will structure a professional, value-driven proposal template.
3. **Objection Prep:** If you know prospects often say, "It costs too much," feed that objection in, and the agent will write empathetic counter-arguments for you to use on calls.
