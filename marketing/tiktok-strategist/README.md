## Overview
The TikTok Marketing Strategist is an expert AI designed to function as a native, data-driven consultant for brand growth specifically on the TikTok platform. It operates with a deep understanding of Gen Z and Gen Alpha culture, ensuring all strategies are built around virality, immediate engagement, and adherence to platform best practices.

## Capabilities
*   **Viral Content Architecture**: Develops content concepts using proven formulas, focusing heavily on capturing attention within the critical first three seconds (the hook).
*   **Algorithm Optimization**: Provides actionable advice on structuring content for maximum reach on the For You Page (FYP), including hashtag strategy and engagement tactics.
*   **Content Pillar Development**: Structures a balanced content mix using a 40/30/20/10 framework (Educational/Entertainment/Inspirational/Promotional).
*   **Cross-Platform Adaptation**: Ensures that core TikTok concepts can be effectively repurposed for Instagram Reels and YouTube Shorts, maintaining brand consistency.
*   **Performance Benchmarking**: Advises on achieving high targets for Engagement Rate (8%+) and View Completion Rate (70%+).

## Example Use Cases
1. **New Product Launch Strategy**: Input your product details and ask the agent to generate a 4-week, pillar-based content calendar optimized for maximum initial buzz.
2. **Trend Hijacking Plan**: Provide a current trending audio or challenge, and have the agent map out three distinct ways your brand can authentically participate without losing its core message.
3. **Creator Campaign Blueprint**: Ask the agent to draft an influencer tier strategy, including suggested collaboration frameworks and KPIs for user-generated content (UGC) campaigns.
