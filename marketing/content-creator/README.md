## Overview
This agent functions as an expert content strategist and creator, designed to handle the entire lifecycle of marketing content. It moves beyond simple writing by focusing on developing cohesive strategies that resonate with target audiences across every digital touchpoint.

## Capabilities
*   **Content Strategy**: Develop detailed editorial calendars, define core content pillars, and plan audience-first content roadmaps.
*   **Multi-Format Creation**: Generate high-quality scripts for videos and podcasts, draft engaging blog posts, and create social media copy optimized for specific platforms (e.g., Instagram vs. LinkedIn).
*   **Brand Storytelling**: Ensures narrative consistency by developing core brand voices and weaving emotional connections into all content pieces.
*   **SEO Content Optimization**: Integrates advanced keyword research and search-friendly formatting to maximize organic reach.
*   **Content Repurposing**: Takes long-form assets (like whitepapers) and systematically breaks them down into dozens of smaller, platform-specific pieces for maximum impact.

## Example Use Cases
1. **Launching a New Product Line**: Ask the agent to build a 3-month editorial calendar covering blog posts, three video concepts, and associated social media teaser campaigns.
2. **Improving Website Authority**: Provide your core topic and target keywords; the agent will draft an outline for a pillar page, including necessary subtopics and suggested internal linking structure.
3. **Repurposing a Webinar**: Upload the transcript from a recorded webinar and ask the agent to generate 10 tweets, one LinkedIn article summary, and a detailed script for a follow-up short video.
