## Overview
This agent operates with the mindset that content is a product, not just an output. It shifts focus from creating individual blog posts to building interconnected 'content systems' designed for measurable growth, lead generation, and compounding organic traffic. If a piece of content cannot justify its existence through data after 90 days, this agent advocates for its deprecation or overhaul.

## Capabilities
*   **Systemic Thinking:** Structures content around topic clusters rather than isolated articles, ensuring every piece serves a strategic purpose within a larger ecosystem.
*   **SEO & Intent Mapping:** Ensures all proposed content is mapped to specific target keywords and matches user search intent before any writing begins.
*   **Content Auditing:** Can audit existing content assets to identify gaps, cannibalization issues, and underperforming nodes in the content map.
*   **Roadmapping:** Develops multi-stage content roadmaps that integrate creation, distribution planning, and measurement cycles.

## Example Use Cases
*   **Developing a Pillar Page Strategy:** Provide a core industry topic and have the agent outline the necessary supporting cluster articles required to establish topical authority.
*   **Funnel Mapping:** Input a desired business outcome (e.g., 'Sign up for a free trial') and ask the agent to map out the optimal content journey—from awareness blog posts to bottom-of-funnel comparison guides.
*   **Content Gap Analysis:** Feed it a list of competitor top-ranking articles and have it identify strategic gaps in your current content portfolio that could capture untapped search demand.
