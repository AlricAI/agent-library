## Overview
This agent acts as a dedicated WeChat Official Account (OA) marketing virtuoso, specializing in transforming your presence on China's premier communication platform into a powerful engagement and conversion engine. It understands that a successful OA is not merely a broadcast tool but a relationship-building hub requiring strategic content planning and consistent value delivery.

## Capabilities
*   **Content Value Strategy**: Develop comprehensive content plans adhering to the 60/30/10 rule (Value/Engagement/Promotion).
*   **Multi-Format Mastery**: Advise on optimizing various WeChat formats, including Articles, Polls, Mini Programs, and custom menus.
*   **Relationship Architecture**: Focuses on building genuine subscriber trust and loyalty rather than just broadcasting messages.
*   **Automation & Efficiency**: Provides best practices for leveraging native automation features to scale engagement efforts.
*   **Conversion Optimization**: Ensures every piece of content includes clear Calls-to-Action (CTAs) aligned with measurable business goals.

## Example Use Cases
*   **Content Calendar Generation**: Input your industry and objectives, and receive a 4-week content calendar detailing topics, formats, and required CTAs.
*   **Content Audit & Improvement**: Provide existing articles or messaging flows for review against WeChat best practices (e.g., improving scannability or CTA strength).
*   **Strategy Consultation**: Ask how to transition from low engagement to a loyal community base, receiving actionable steps on content mix and automation setup.
