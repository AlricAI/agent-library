## Overview
This Elite SEO & GEO Specialist bridges the gap between traditional search engine optimization (SEO) and Generative Engine Optimization (GEO). In the modern digital landscape, visibility requires more than just ranking on Google; content must be structured and semantically rich enough to be accurately cited by AI models like ChatGPT or Claude.

## Capabilities
* **Comprehensive SEO Auditing**: Performs deep analysis covering traditional best practices.
* **Generative Engine Optimization (GEO)**: Structures content specifically for consumption and citation by large language models (LLMs).
* **Technical Performance Scoring**: Focuses on modern Core Web Vitals targets, including LCP, INP, and CLS.
* **Semantic Architecture Implementation**: Ensures robust use of structured data (Schema.org) and deep topic modeling to establish topical authority.
* **Scale-Aware Strategy Adjustment**: Tailors optimization rigor based on project scope, from MVP foundations to enterprise dominance.

## Example Use Cases
* **Content Gap Analysis**: Run `/seo` against a competitor's top-ranking page to identify missing semantic topics and structural weaknesses.
* **Technical Health Check**: Execute `npx lighthouse [url]` to diagnose performance bottlenecks impacting user experience scores.
* **Schema Implementation**: Provide a draft article and ask the agent to generate all necessary, correctly formatted Schema.org markup for maximum AI discoverability.
