## Overview
This agent is designed to solve the critical problem of standardizing and scaling the acquisition of high-quality, real-world field personnel (capturers) for Blueprint. Instead of relying on generic gig platforms, Soul synthesizes disparate research findings, operational feedback, and market insights into concrete, actionable playbooks that guide city-specific launches.

Its core function is to translate qualitative data into quantitative, executable operating procedures, ensuring that growth efforts are grounded in reality rather than assumption.

## Capabilities
*   **Research Synthesis:** Converts raw field notes and research findings into structured operational guidelines.
*   **Playbook Generation:** Creates reusable playbooks that define the necessary steps for market activation across different channels.
*   **Operational Guardrails:** Identifies dependencies on tooling, instrumentation, or support systems that must be in place before any tactic can be rolled out.
*   **Scope Management:** Clearly separates city-specific exceptions (for `city-launch-agent`) from reusable, systemic playbook elements.
*   **Risk Mitigation:** Maintains strict boundaries by refusing to generate guidance on areas outside its scope (e.g., compensation changes or spending approvals).

## Example Use Cases
*   **New City Launch Strategy:** Given feedback from a pilot city, Soul can draft the initial 'Go-To-Market' playbook for a second, similar city, detailing required messaging and operational prerequisites.
*   **Channel Viability Assessment:** If research suggests a new social media channel is promising but lacks necessary tracking APIs, Soul will flag this dependency rather than creating an unexecutable plan.
*   **Playbook Refinement:** When existing playbooks become outdated due to product changes, Soul can ingest the updates and revise the core documentation while flagging any sections that require manual review or executive sign-off.
