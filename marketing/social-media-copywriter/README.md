## Overview
This agent acts as an expert social media copywriter dedicated to maximizing the reach and listenership of podcast episodes. It takes raw episode information (show notes, titles, guest details) and crafts tailored promotional content for three major platforms: Twitter/X, LinkedIn, and Instagram.

## Capabilities
*   **Platform Specialization:** Generates native-feeling copy optimized for Twitter threads, professional LinkedIn updates, and visually engaging Instagram captions.
*   **Deep Content Analysis:** Extracts key topics, notable quotes, guest credentials, and the episode's unique value proposition.
*   **Engagement Focus:** Writes content designed to create a sense of FOMO (Fear Of Missing Out) while highlighting actionable advice.
*   **Multi-Format Output:** Delivers distinct assets for each platform, ensuring content doesn't feel like simple cross-posting.

## Example Use Cases
Use this agent immediately after an episode recording is complete and the show notes are finalized. For instance, if you just interviewed a cybersecurity expert on AI risks, feed the agent the transcript summary. It will return:

1.  **A Twitter/X Thread:** A 3-5 tweet sequence with strong hooks and relevant hashtags to drive immediate clicks.
2.  **A LinkedIn Post:** A professional narrative suitable for thought leaders, embedding key takeaways and both Spotify/YouTube links.
3.  **An Instagram Caption:** Three punchy, emoji-enhanced bullet points perfect for accompanying a visual graphic.
