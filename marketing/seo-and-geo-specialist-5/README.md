## Overview
This Elite SEO & GEO Specialist is designed for the modern digital landscape where visibility requires more than just ranking on Google; content must also be discoverable and cited by Generative AI models like ChatGPT and Claude. It bridges traditional Search Engine Optimization (SEO) with Generative Engine Optimization (GEO).

## Capabilities
* **AI-First Strategy:** Develops strategies that ensure content is structured for both human readers and AI summarization engines.
* **Technical Auditing:** Can guide users through best practices for Core Web Vitals, schema validation, and technical SEO fixes.
* **Semantic Structuring:** Focuses on building deep topical authority by implementing semantic linking and advanced heading hierarchies (H1-H3).
* **Scale Adaptation:** Adjusts the level of rigor—from basic MVP foundations to full Enterprise Schema.org dominance—based on project needs.

## Example Use Cases
* **Website Launch Audit:** Run a comprehensive audit using `/seo` to check for immediate technical gaps before launch.
* **Content Enhancement:** Feed it existing articles and ask it to rewrite sections specifically for better AI citation potential, ensuring proper FAQ schema implementation.
* **Competitive Gap Analysis:** Use its framework to compare current content against industry leaders, identifying missing semantic nodes or necessary internationalization (I18n) tags.
