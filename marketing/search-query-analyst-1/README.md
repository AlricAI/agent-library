## Overview
This Paid Media Search Query Analyst agent acts as an expert data layer specialist, bridging the gap between what users actually search for and what advertisers are paying to show for. Its core function is to take raw, high-volume search term reports and transform them into a structured, actionable optimization roadmap. It treats query management not as a checklist item, but as a continuous system vital for maximizing Return on Ad Spend (ROAS).

## Capabilities
* **Search Term Mining & Clustering:** Performs large-scale analysis of search term reports to identify underlying patterns and cluster queries based on shared intent.
* **Negative Keyword Architecture:** Builds tiered negative keyword lists (account, campaign, ad group levels) and detects potential conflicts between existing negatives.
* **Intent Classification:** Maps every incoming query against established buyer intent stages (informational, navigational, commercial, transactional) to ensure message-market alignment.
* **Waste Identification & Scoring:** Implements spend-weighted scoring to flag irrelevant or zero-conversion queries that are draining budget without return.
* **Query Sculpting:** Recommends precise negative keyword additions and match type adjustments to direct traffic efficiently to the most relevant ad groups.

## Example Use Cases
* **Auditing a Campaign:** Upload a month's worth of search term data. The agent will return top 10 waste-generating query clusters, suggest corresponding negative keywords, and estimate potential savings.
* **Intent Gap Analysis:** Provide landing page URLs and associated high-volume queries. The agent will identify instances where the query intent does not match the page's commercial offering, flagging necessary ad copy or LPO adjustments.
* **Scaling Strategy:** Analyze top-performing long-tail keywords to generate a prioritized list of new, untapped keyword opportunities for immediate campaign expansion.
