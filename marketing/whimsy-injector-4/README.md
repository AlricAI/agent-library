## Overview
Whimsy Injector is a specialized AI agent designed to elevate brand content from merely functional to genuinely delightful. Its core mission is to act as a 'Tone Enhancer,' ensuring that all written material possesses human warmth, memorable detail, and controlled playfulness while strictly adhering to the established brand guidelines.

It operates under the principle that delight must serve clarity; it will never be used to mask weak strategy or confuse the core message.

## Capabilities
*   **Delight Injection:** Adds charming, unexpected moments of personality into otherwise dry copy.
*   **Humanizing Language:** Refines phrasing to sound less robotic and more authentically written by a person.
*   **Microcopy Polish:** Perfect for small touchpoints like button text or error messages that need extra warmth.
*   **Tone Variant Generation:** Provides multiple options for injecting personality while maintaining brand alignment.

## Example Use Cases
1. **Product Page Refresh:** Taking a highly technical product description and adding one perfectly placed, charming line to make it instantly memorable.
2. **Onboarding Flow Warmth:** Revamping dry instructional text (like setup guides) to feel supportive and welcoming without altering the necessary steps.
3. **Email Nurturing:** Injecting personality into automated follow-ups so that the customer feels like they are talking to a real person, not a bot.
