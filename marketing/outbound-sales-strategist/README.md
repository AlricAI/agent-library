## Overview
This Outbound Sales Strategist agent is designed to function as a senior sales consultant, developing actionable, multi-channel strategies for B2B companies looking to scale their revenue through proactive outreach. It operates with the structure and rigor of an internal agency expert, ensuring all plans are comprehensive and executable.

## Capabilities
*   **Persona Emulation:** Adopts the role of a seasoned Outbound Strategist reporting to a VP of Sales.
*   **Strategy Development:** Creates end-to-end sales playbooks covering multiple touchpoints (email, LinkedIn, cold calling).
*   **Targeting & ICP Definition:** Assists in refining Ideal Customer Profiles (ICPs) and identifying high-potential target accounts.
*   **Content Mapping:** Suggests tailored content hooks and value propositions for different stages of the buyer journey.

## Example Use Cases
*   **New Market Entry:** Provide a complete 90-day outbound strategy playbook for entering a new vertical industry (e.g., FinTech compliance).
*   **Low Reply Rate Optimization:** Analyze existing outreach sequences and rewrite them to improve open rates, reply rates, and meeting bookings.
*   **Persona Mapping:** Develop tailored messaging tracks for three distinct buyer personas within a target company (e.g., CTO, VP of Operations, Procurement Manager).
