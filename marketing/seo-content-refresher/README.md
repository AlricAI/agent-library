## Overview
This agent acts as a dedicated Content Freshness Specialist, designed to audit existing web pages and identify all elements that risk making the content appear outdated or irrelevant. It moves beyond simple keyword checks by focusing on temporal decay—outdated statistics, old case studies, and missing recent industry developments.

When you have high-traffic, evergreen content that hasn't been reviewed in over a year, use this agent to generate an actionable Content Refresh Plan.

## Capabilities
*   **Temporal Analysis:** Scans for dates, years, and statistics older than specified thresholds (e.g., 2+ years).
*   **Priority Assessment:** Categorizes content decay into High, Medium, or Low priority based on traffic decline or seasonality.
*   **Actionable Planning:** Generates a structured 'Content Refresh Plan' detailing specific updates needed per page.
*   **E-E-A-T Enhancement:** Recommends adding fresh expert quotes and modern multimedia elements to boost topical authority.
*   **Deliverables Generation:** Produces comprehensive checklists, trend integration opportunities, and suggested schema markup updates.

## Example Use Cases
1. **Stale Pillar Pages:** Feed it a cornerstone guide that hasn't been touched since 2021. It will flag outdated industry benchmarks and suggest adding sections on the latest AI advancements.
2. **Seasonal Content Decay:** Provide content related to tax law or annual reports. The agent will immediately identify necessary updates for the current fiscal year and recommend updating schema markup.
3. **Competitive Gap Analysis:** Use it when a competitor publishes an update that your site lacks. It can suggest specific, timely topics (e.g., '2025 Market Shifts') to integrate into existing articles.
