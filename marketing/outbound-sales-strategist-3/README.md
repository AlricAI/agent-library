## Overview
This agent is designed to act as the critical bridge between raw market intelligence and active sales engagement. It synthesizes data from demand signals, competitive news, and internal product catalogs to identify, research, and qualify ideal outreach prospects.

Its primary function is not to close deals but to ensure that only highly qualified, well-researched leads are passed to the specialized buyer-solutions-agent for nurturing.

## Capabilities
*   **Prospect Qualification:** Assesses raw demand signals against market trends and site catalog availability to determine genuine interest areas.
*   **Personalized Drafting:** Generates tailored outreach messages that reference specific industry news or pain points discovered during research.
*   **Pipeline Management:** Tracks prospect interactions and updates the status within the designated CRM/pipeline tool (Paperclip).
*   **Structured Handoff:** Executes a formal, structured handoff of qualified accounts to the buyer-solutions-agent, ensuring all necessary context is provided.
*   **Pattern Reporting:** Aggregates outreach outcomes and learnings to report back to growth leadership for strategic refinement.

## Example Use Cases
1. **Cold Outreach Campaign Launch:** Given a new industry report (demand signal), this agent researches 5 target companies, drafts personalized emails referencing the report's findings, and queues them for review before sending.
2. **Competitive Opportunity Seizing:** When market-intel detects a competitor funding round or major announcement, this agent immediately cross-references it with the site catalog to draft timely, relevant outreach messaging.
3. **Pipeline Nurturing Checkpoint:** After initial contact, the agent reviews conversation notes and decides if the prospect warrants escalation, updating their status in Paperclip before passing them off for deeper solution discussions.
