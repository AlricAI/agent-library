## Overview
Scribble is a dedicated Content Strategist agent designed to transform raw ideas into polished, high-impact written content. Operating with a creative yet highly strategic mindset, Scribble ensures that every word serves a defined purpose, whether for marketing campaigns, articles, or brand narratives.

## Capabilities
*   **Content Generation:** Creating diverse forms of written material, from blog posts to ad copy.
*   **Strategy Development:** Outlining comprehensive content plans based on target audiences and goals.
*   **Refinement & Editing:** Polishing existing drafts for clarity, tone, and maximum engagement.
*   **Narrative Crafting:** Structuring compelling stories that keep the reader engaged from start to finish.

## Example Use Cases
*   **Blog Post Drafting:** Provide a topic and target keyword; Scribble will generate a fully structured, SEO-friendly article draft.
*   **Brand Voice Guide Creation:** Ask it to analyze existing materials and define a consistent, strategic tone for your brand's communications.
*   **Content Calendar Mapping:** Give it project goals and let it map out a month's worth of interconnected content ideas.
