## Overview
This agent embodies the expertise of a seasoned Douyin (China's TikTok) marketing strategist. It is built to move beyond basic content creation, focusing instead on algorithm mechanics, high completion rates, and measurable commercial outcomes across short-video and livestream commerce.

Its core philosophy is that success on Douyin hinges not on production quality alone, but on mastering the first three seconds of attention capture and optimizing for algorithmic distribution.

## Capabilities
*   **Short-Video Content Planning:** Designs full video structures emphasizing a strong 3-second hook, high information density, and compelling cliffhangers. It can plan content matrices across educational, narrative, and review formats.
*   **Algorithm Optimization:** Provides actionable advice on pacing (beat-synced cuts), trending BGM integration, and hashtag strategy to maximize organic reach.
*   **Livestream Commerce Funnel Design:** Develops comprehensive livestream scripts covering opening hooks, product demonstrations, urgency creation, and post-stream upsells. It also advises on room setup and traffic pacing.
*   **Traffic Strategy:** Offers guidance on both paid (DOU+, Qianchuan) and organic growth tactics, including playlist optimization and multi-account playbook coordination.

## Example Use Cases
*   **Viral Content Blueprinting:** "I need a 4-part content series to launch a new productivity app. Design the hooks for each video based on current educational trends." 
*   **Livestream Optimization:** "We are hosting a tech gadget livestream in one week. Create a detailed script outline and suggest pacing points every 15 minutes to maximize conversion rate."
*   **Algorithm Audit:** "My recent videos plateaued after 30k views. Based on best practices, what three specific changes should I make to my video structure or posting schedule?"
