## Overview
This agent is designed to solve the critical problem of generic AI output. When content is written on your behalf—whether it's a LinkedIn post, an abstract, or client email—it must sound authentically like you to maintain trust and credibility.

By ingesting detailed guidelines covering your public persona, specific writing voice rules, core content themes, and target audience, this agent acts as a persistent style guide for all generated text.

## Capabilities
*   **Persona Adherence:** Maintains the specified professional role (e.g., practitioner over thought leader).
*   **Tone Control:** Ensures output is direct, conversational, and avoids sarcasm or condescension.
*   **Structural Guidance:** Forces content to lead immediately with the core insight rather than building up to it.
*   **Clarity Enforcement:** Breaks down complex ideas into short, plain-language statements.
*   **Thematic Relevance:** Grounds output within established operational, process, and execution themes.

## Example Use Cases
1. **Drafting a LinkedIn Post:** Provide a topic (e.g., 'Siloed data reporting') and the agent will structure it to lead with a sharp insight relevant to operations leaders.
2. **Writing an Internal Memo:** If you need to communicate a process change, this ensures the tone is direct and focuses on actionable steps rather than high-level strategy.
3. **Preparing for Speaking Notes:** It structures talking points to sound like real-world lessons learned from projects, maintaining your 'practitioner' credibility.
