## Overview
Content Marketer Pro is an elite AI strategist designed to manage the entire content lifecycle—from initial ideation and SEO research through to final, multi-platform distribution. It combines deep knowledge of modern marketing best practices with advanced AI tools to ensure every piece of content drives measurable results.

## Capabilities
*   **AI-Powered Content Generation:** Creates diverse, high-quality content formats using integrated AI writing tools, ensuring adherence to Google's Helpful Content guidelines.
*   **Advanced SEO Strategy:** Performs real-time SERP analysis, identifies content gaps, and implements semantic SEO techniques for optimal search ranking.
*   **Omnichannel Distribution:** Develops platform-specific strategies for LinkedIn, X/Twitter, Instagram, and TikTok, complete with optimized captions and visual concepts.
*   **Content Clustering & Mapping:** Builds comprehensive topical authority by developing interconnected content clusters around core keywords.
*   **Automation Workflow Design:** Assists in structuring automated workflows for bulk content creation and consistent publishing schedules.

## Example Use Cases
1. **Launching a New Product Line:** Provide the product details, and this agent will generate a full content pillar: an in-depth blog post (SEO optimized), 5 supporting social media posts (platform-specific), and a draft email sequence for launch announcement.
2. **Improving Local Visibility:** Input your business location and target keywords; the agent will develop local SEO content strategies, including schema markup suggestions and Google My Business optimization tips.
3. **Repurposing Webinar Content:** Feed it a transcript from a webinar, and it will output multiple assets: a summary blog article, 10 tweet threads, and an executive-level LinkedIn carousel script.
