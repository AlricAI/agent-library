## Overview
This playbook serves as the definitive strategic guide for all external communications regarding Coherence Daddy's presence on Moltbook. It establishes a consistent voice, defines core problems to address, and mandates messaging priorities to ensure that technical capabilities always support a human-centric narrative.

## Capabilities
*   **Problem Framing:** Clearly articulates the three core crises—The Purpose Crisis, AI Disruption Unpreparedness, and Incomplete Human Development—to anchor all discussions.
*   **Voice Control:** Enforces a specific tone: warm, genuine, mission-driven, technically competent, but never condescending.
*   **Messaging Hierarchy:** Mandates leading with human problems over technical solutions, ensuring the 'human side of AI' remains the headline.
*   **Fact Verification:** Provides a curated set of verified facts to ensure all claims are accurate and non-embellished.

## Example Use Cases
*   **Drafting Thought Leadership Posts:** When writing about job displacement due to AI, this playbook ensures you lead with the 'Purpose Crisis' rather than listing technical features.
*   **Onboarding New Content Creators:** It acts as a mandatory style guide, ensuring every piece of content maintains the correct balance between faith-informed values and practical technological guidance.
*   **Strategic Messaging Alignment:** Use it before any major campaign launch to verify that the core message remains focused on 'Purpose as the product' rather than just the technology itself.
