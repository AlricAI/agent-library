## Overview
The Carousel Growth Engine is an autonomous, data-driven marketing agent designed to maximize social media reach by transforming any provided website URL into a series of viral 6-slide carousels for TikTok and Instagram. It operates as a fully closed-loop system: research $\rightarrow$ generate $\rightarrow$ publish $\rightarrow$ learn.

## Capabilities
*   **Website Research (Playwright):** Scrapes content from any given URL to extract core themes and talking points.
*   **Visual Storytelling (Gemini):** Generates 6 visually coherent slides, ensuring a consistent aesthetic across the entire carousel by establishing a visual DNA in Slide 1.
*   **Structured Narrative:** Adheres strictly to a proven 6-slide arc: Hook $\rightarrow$ Problem $\rightarrow$ Agitation $\rightarrow$ Solution $\rightarrow$ Feature $\rightarrow$ CTA.
*   **Automated Publishing:** Posts are published directly to TikTok and Instagram via the Upload-Post API, including auto-trending music selection.
*   **Performance Feedback Loop:** Fetches post analytics to identify high-performing hooks, styles, and topics, automatically incorporating these learnings into subsequent content cycles for continuous improvement.

## Example Use Cases
1. **Niche Authority Building:** Provide the URL of a small SaaS company's landing page; the agent will generate 30 days of educational carousels demonstrating its core value proposition to build authority in that niche.
2. **Competitor Analysis Content:** Feed it a competitor's website and task it with creating content that highlights gaps or superior features compared to the competition, driving traffic back to your own site.
3. **Evergreen Lead Magnet Promotion:** Use it to systematically break down complex industry reports (via URL) into digestible, scroll-stopping visual narratives for consistent lead generation.
