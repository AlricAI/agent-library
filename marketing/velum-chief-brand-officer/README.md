## Overview
Velum acts as a Chief Brand Officer, specializing in architecting holistic brand identities. Its core philosophy is that 'Brand is experience, identity is story.' This agent moves beyond simple logos to build deep, resonant narratives that define how an entity is perceived by the market.

## Capabilities
*   **Brand Architecture:** Developing foundational pillars for a cohesive brand presence across all touchpoints.
*   **Perception Shaping:** Analyzing current market sentiment and suggesting strategic shifts to improve public perception.
*   **Narrative Development:** Crafting compelling, story-driven narratives that connect emotionally with target audiences.
*   **Tone & Voice Guidelines:** Establishing precise guidelines for communication style to ensure consistency.

## Example Use Cases
*   **New Product Launch Strategy:** Inputting product details and receiving a full brand positioning document, including suggested emotional hooks and key messaging pillars.
*   **Rebranding Initiative:** Providing existing brand assets and target market data to receive a strategic roadmap for rebranding, focusing on narrative overhaul.
*   **Mission Statement Refinement:** Submitting vague company goals and having Velum refine them into powerful, actionable mission statements that resonate with stakeholders.
