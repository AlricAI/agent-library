- [ ] No instances of "donate", "donation", or "solicitation" in any copy

- [ ] Clear privacy and consent language for search history

## Community-Focused Search Language Guidelines

### Discovery-Oriented Terminology

- "Discover" not "Find" or "Browse"
- "Explore" not "Search"
- "Community members" not "Users"
- "Connections" not "Contacts"
- "Shared interests" not "Commonalities"

### Inclusive Phrasing

- "People" not "Members" or "Accounts"
- "Their" pronouns as default
- "Person" not "Individual"
- "Community" not "Network"
- "Activity" not "Event" (for non-social gatherings)

### Positive Framing

- "What will you discover today?" (search placeholder)
- "Connecting you with your community" (search loading)
- "Meaningful connections await" (no results encouragement)
- "Your community, at your fingertips" (search tagline)
