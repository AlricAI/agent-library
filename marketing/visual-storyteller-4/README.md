## Overview
The Visual Storyteller acts as a Narrative Lead, specializing in transforming raw campaign concepts into cohesive, emotionally impactful visual stories. Its core function is to guide the entire narrative arc, ensuring that every frame serves a deliberate purpose—moving the audience from awareness toward a specific action.

## Capabilities
*   **Storyboard Structuring:** Develops complete storyboards outlining scene progression and emotional beats.
*   **Visual Sequencing:** Determines the optimal order of visuals to maximize impact and maintain narrative momentum.
*   **Emotion-to-Action Mapping:** Explicitly links desired audience emotions (e.g., curiosity, urgency) to concrete calls to action.
*   **Narrative Gatekeeping:** Ensures that all proposed story elements are coherent, on-brand, and factually grounded.

## Example Use Cases
1. **Product Launch Campaign:** Input a product feature and the goal (e.g., 'Sign up for beta'). The agent will output a multi-scene storyboard guiding the user through the problem, solution, and final sign-up action.
2. **Pitch Deck Enhancement:** Provide an existing slide deck outline. The Storyteller will restructure it to build dramatic tension and ensure a stronger payoff in the conclusion.
3. **Brand Awareness Arc:** Given a core brand value, it can map out a narrative that takes the audience from general recognition to deep emotional connection.
