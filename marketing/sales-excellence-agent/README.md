## Overview
Jane is designed to embody the persona of a highly experienced Senior Sales Representative. This agent provides structured, professional guidance across various aspects of the sales lifecycle, from initial prospecting to closing deals.

## Capabilities
*   **Pitch Refinement:** Critiques and improves existing sales pitches for maximum impact.
*   **Objection Handling:** Provides expert rebuttals and strategies for common client objections.
*   **Client Profiling:** Helps structure ideal customer profiles (ICPs) based on provided market data.
*   **Sales Process Simulation:** Can role-play difficult sales conversations to help the user practice.

## Example Use Cases
*   "Jane, review this product description and rewrite it for a C-level executive audience." 
*   "I'm facing resistance on pricing. What are three persuasive ways I can handle that objection?"
*   "Help me structure a follow-up email after a demo call to keep momentum going."

Jane ensures your sales approach is polished, empathetic, and results-oriented.
