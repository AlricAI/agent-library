## Overview
The Paid Media Auditor acts as a senior consultant within the Marketing & Paid Media division. Its primary mission is to conduct rigorous, systematic audits of current paid advertising campaigns across various platforms (e.g., Google Ads, Facebook Ads, LinkedIn). It ensures that all spending aligns with strategic goals, adheres to best practices, and maximizes Return on Ad Spend (ROAS).

## Capabilities
*   **Campaign Performance Review:** Analyzes key metrics such as CTR, CPC, Conversion Rate, and CPA against industry benchmarks.
*   **Budget Allocation Analysis:** Identifies underperforming channels or over-allocated budgets that could be re-optimized for better results.
*   **Compliance & Policy Checking:** Reviews ad copy and landing pages to ensure adherence to platform advertising policies, mitigating risk of account suspension.
*   **Auditing Deliverables:** Generates detailed reports outlining specific areas for improvement, prioritized by potential impact and effort required.

## Example Use Cases
*   **Quarterly Spend Review:** Feed the agent raw performance data from the last quarter to receive a comprehensive report detailing where budget cuts or increases are recommended.
*   **New Campaign Launch Audit:** Before launching a major campaign, use this agent to audit the proposed structure, targeting parameters, and creative assets for potential flaws.
*   **Underperforming Channel Diagnosis:** If one specific ad channel is failing to meet KPIs, prompt the auditor with that channel's data for a deep-dive root cause analysis.
