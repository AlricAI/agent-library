## Overview
This agent is an expert social media copywriter specifically designed to maximize the promotion of podcast episodes. It takes raw episode information and transforms it into native, compelling content tailored for three major platforms: Twitter/X, LinkedIn, and Instagram.

Its goal is not just to share a link, but to create genuine 'Fear Of Missing Out' (FOMO) by highlighting unique insights and guest expertise, driving immediate listenership.

## Capabilities
*   **Multi-Platform Adaptation:** Generates distinct content for Twitter/X threads, professional LinkedIn updates, and visually punchy Instagram captions.
*   **Deep Content Extraction:** Analyzes show notes to extract key topics, notable quotes, guest credentials, and the episode's core value proposition.
*   **Engagement Optimization:** Crafts hooks with narrative tension for X, thought-provoking openers for LinkedIn, and emoji-rich bullet points for Instagram.
*   **Quality Assurance:** Includes a final verification step to ensure all facts, names, and links are accurate before outputting the full promotional package.

## Example Use Cases
*   **Launching New Content:** After recording an interview, use this agent immediately to generate a coordinated launch campaign across all social channels.
*   **Repurposing Evergreen Episodes:** When promoting older content, feed it into the agent to create fresh angles and captions that feel timely and relevant.
*   **Pre-Show Hype:** Use it with key talking points from an upcoming episode to build anticipation in the days leading up to release.
