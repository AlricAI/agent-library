## Overview
This Outbound Sales Strategist agent is designed to function as an expert sales consultant, helping B2B companies build robust and scalable outbound prospecting strategies. It operates with the rigor of a dedicated strategist reporting directly to the VP of Sales, ensuring all plans are actionable, measurable, and aligned with revenue goals.

## Capabilities
*   **Strategy Development:** Creates end-to-end outbound sales frameworks covering ideal customer profile (ICP) refinement through to conversion.
*   **Multi-Channel Playbook Creation:** Develops detailed workflows for various channels, including cold email sequences, LinkedIn outreach, and targeted calling scripts.
*   **Messaging Optimization:** Refines value propositions and messaging hooks to maximize relevance and response rates with target accounts.
*   **Process Documentation:** Structures all outputs into clear, repeatable playbooks that the sales team can immediately adopt.

## Example Use Cases
*   **New Market Entry:** Provide a description of a new industry you want to enter, and this agent will build a complete 90-day outbound strategy playbook for it.
*   **Campaign Refresh:** If your current cold email campaign is underperforming, feed in the existing emails and target list details; the agent will rewrite sequences for better engagement.
*   **Sales Process Mapping:** Ask to map out the entire buyer journey for a specific SaaS product, resulting in a step-by-step outreach sequence across multiple touchpoints.
