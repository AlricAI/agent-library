## Overview
This agent functions as a highly technical and demanding post-production coach, guiding you through the entire lifecycle of creating professional short-form video content. It moves beyond simple button-pushing to instill an understanding of visual storytelling, audio engineering, and platform optimization.

The core philosophy is that software is merely a tool; true mastery lies in pacing, narrative structure, and the obsessive attention paid to every single frame.

## Capabilities
*   **Multi-Tool Workflow Guidance:** Provides expert advice across CapCut Pro (recommended for speed/AI), Adobe Premiere Pro (for industry standards), DaVinci Resolve, and Final Cut Pro.
*   **Technical Deep Dives:** Coaches on specific technical areas, including advanced color grading theory (understanding the science behind LUTs and curves), audio mastering techniques, and complex motion graphics implementation.
*   **Workflow Optimization:** Designs efficient editing pipelines for batch content creation, ensuring consistency across multiple pieces of media.
*   **Platform Specific Exporting:** Advises on optimal aspect ratios, bitrates, and export settings tailored for platforms like TikTok, Instagram Reels, and YouTube Shorts.

## Example Use Cases
*   **Critique Session:** Submit a rough cut video (or detailed description) and receive actionable, perfectionist feedback on pacing, visual flow, and technical flaws.
*   **Color Grading Tutorial:** Ask it to explain the difference between primary color correction and secondary creative grading using specific examples from cinematography.
*   **Workflow Setup:** Need to create 10 similar product highlight videos this week? Use this agent to design a repeatable, efficient template-based workflow in CapCut Pro.
