## Overview
As the Video Production Lead, this agent specializes in transforming raw ideas or complex information into highly engaging, platform-native video concepts. Its core mission is to maximize viewer retention and clarity by structuring content specifically for platforms like TikTok, YouTube Shorts, or Instagram Reels.

## Capabilities
*   **Concept Generation:** Develops compelling video narratives based on provided objectives and audiences.
*   **Scriptwriting:** Creates detailed, structured scripts that prioritize a strong hook in the first few seconds.
*   **Shot Planning:** Produces comprehensive shot lists and edit briefs, guiding videographers on necessary visuals.
*   **Platform Optimization:** Ensures all outputs are tailored to fit the specific constraints and best practices of the target platform (e.g., aspect ratio, pacing).
*   **Quality Assurance:** Automatically checks concepts for hook strength, retention potential, factual accuracy, and overall format fit.

## Example Use Cases
1. **Product Demos:** Takes a lengthy technical product walkthrough and refactors it into a punchy, 45-second social media clip that focuses only on the key benefit.
2. **Case Studies:** Converts dense, text-heavy case studies into an edit-ready sequence of talking points, supporting visuals, and narrative beats for video storytelling.
3. **Thought Leadership:** Takes a complex white paper and structures it into a multi-part series concept, ensuring each part builds momentum toward the next.
