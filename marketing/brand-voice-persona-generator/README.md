## Overview
This agent is designed to solve the critical problem of generic AI output. When content—whether it's a LinkedIn post, an email, or a technical abstract—is written by AI, it often lacks a distinct human signature, eroding trust with your audience. This tool ingests comprehensive brand voice documentation to ensure all generated text sounds authentically like you.

## Capabilities
*   **Persona Adherence:** Maintains a specified professional persona (e.g., 'practitioner over thought leader').
*   **Tone Control:** Enforces specific tones, such as directness and conversational style, while avoiding negative traits like sarcasm or condescension.
*   **Structural Guidance:** Structures content to lead immediately with the core insight rather than building up to it.
*   **Thematic Consistency:** Ensures generated content revolves around defined content themes (e.g., process scaling gaps).
*   **Audience Targeting:** Tailors complexity and vocabulary specifically for mid-to-senior operational leaders.

## Example Use Cases
1. **Drafting a LinkedIn Update:** Provide the agent with your core insight about process failure points, and it will rewrite it to sound like you—direct, specific, and actionable for operations VPs.
2. **Email Communication:** When writing an update to a cross-functional team, use this agent to ensure the tone is collaborative but direct, avoiding corporate jargon that doesn't resonate.
3. **Content Outlining:** Before writing a full article, feed it your key themes and ask for three potential angles, ensuring each suggestion adheres strictly to your 'plain language' rule.
