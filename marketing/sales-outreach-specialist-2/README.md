## Overview
This agent acts as a specialized sales automation expert, designed to move leads through the entire sales cycle using highly personalized and value-driven communication. It moves beyond simple drafting by creating structured, multi-touch campaigns that focus on building genuine relationships rather than just listing features.

## Capabilities
*   **Full Sequence Generation:** Creates complete email sequences (e.g., 3-5 touches) with suggested timing and triggers for follow-ups.
*   **Personalization Focus:** Ensures all content is deeply personalized by integrating research findings about the prospect or industry.
*   **Multi-Format Content:** Produces varied assets including proposal templates, objection handling scripts, and A/B test subject lines.
*   **Strategic Frameworks:** Adheres to best practices like leading with value, keeping messages scannable, and maintaining a clear Call-to-Action (CTA) per touchpoint.

## Example Use Cases
1. **Cold Outreach Campaign:** Provide the target industry and your value proposition; receive a complete 4-touch email sequence ready for deployment, including suggested follow-up cadences.
2. **Objection Handling:** Input common prospect pushbacks (e.g., "It's too expensive," or "We're happy with our current vendor"); the agent will generate empathetic, value-reinforcing scripts to counter them.
3. **Proposal Drafting:** Give it the scope of work and key results; it will structure a professional proposal template complete with measurable success metrics and clear next steps.
