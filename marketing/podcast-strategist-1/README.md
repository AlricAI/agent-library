## Overview
This agent acts as a seasoned Chinese podcast content strategist and full-funnel operations expert. Its core mission is to help creators move beyond simply recording episodes to building deeply engaging, sticky audio brands that resonate with listeners on major platforms like Xiaoyuzhou and Ximalaya.

The AI operates with an acute focus on quality, viewing the podcast not just as content, but as an intimate companion for the listener during their daily routines. It possesses deep knowledge of the unique operational nuances of the Chinese audio ecosystem.

## Capabilities
*   **Show Positioning & Planning:** Defines optimal show formats (e.g., vertical knowledge dives, narrative storytelling) and crafts clear value propositions that avoid vague content mandates.
*   **Audience Deep Dive:** Helps define precise target listener personas based on age, context (commute, bedtime), and consumption habits.
*   **Branding & Aesthetics:** Assists in developing memorable show names, compelling descriptions, and visually cohesive branding suitable for platform thumbnails.
*   **Platform Optimization:** Provides strategic guidance tailored to the specific mechanics of Chinese platforms like Xiaoyuzhou (community engagement) and Ximalaya (user base reach).
*   **Full-Funnel Strategy:** Covers everything from initial concept development through to monetization pathways.

## Example Use Cases
*   **Niche Definition:** "We want to talk about tech. How do I narrow this down for a 25-35 year old working professional who commutes by subway?"
*   **Content Gap Analysis:** "My show is getting good views, but not enough retention. What kind of 'companion' content should I add to make listeners feel more connected?
*   **Platform Launch Plan:** "I am launching a documentary series on Xiaoyuzhou. Outline the first three episodes focusing on maximizing community interaction and cross-promotion."

By leveraging its expertise, you can transform an idea into a professionally positioned, high-retention audio brand ready for success in Asia's leading podcast markets.
