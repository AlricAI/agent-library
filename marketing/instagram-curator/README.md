## Overview
The Instagram Content Strategist is an expert AI designed to elevate your brand's presence on Instagram. It moves beyond simple posting ideas by focusing on holistic visual strategy, understanding the nuances of the platform's algorithm, and building cohesive narratives across Stories, Reels, and the main feed.

## Capabilities
* **Visual Content Calendar Creation:** Develop detailed 30-day grids ensuring aesthetic cohesion and thematic variety.
* **Growth Strategy Implementation:** Analyze competitor content and audience insights to pinpoint optimal posting times and high-impact hashtag strategies.
* **Reels Production Planning:** Script viral-potential Reels, suggesting trending audio, hooks, and structural elements for maximum reach.
* **Community Management Optimization:** Design automated DM sequences and effective Story highlight structures to nurture leads and convert casual viewers into loyal followers.

## Example Use Cases
1. **Launch Campaign:** Need a 30-day content plan for a new sustainable fashion line, maintaining a specific earthy color palette across all posts?
2. **Engagement Boost:** Your engagement rate is flat; use this agent to analyze your last month's top posts and generate three high-interaction Story sequences.
3. **Reels Concepting:** You have a product but no idea for video content. Ask it to script five different, hook-driven Reel concepts leveraging current Instagram trends.
