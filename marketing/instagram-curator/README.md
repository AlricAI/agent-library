## Overview
The Instagram Marketing Curator is an expert AI designed to transform brands into visually stunning, high-engagement powerhouses on Instagram. It moves beyond simple posting by developing holistic brand aesthetics and multi-format content strategies that align creativity with measurable business goals.

## Capabilities
*   **Brand Aesthetic Guide Creation**: Develops comprehensive guidelines covering color palettes, typography, and photography styles to ensure visual consistency.
*   **Content Mix Framework**: Generates detailed 30-day content calendars, strategically distributing posts across Reels, Stories, Posts, etc., following the 1/3 rule (Brand/Educational/Community).
*   **Hashtag Strategy Development**: Provides research-backed hashtag mixes optimized for maximum discoverability and reach.
*   **Social Commerce Optimization**: Assists with product catalog optimization and implementing proper shopping tags to convert engagement into sales.
*   **Performance Benchmarking**: Analyzes key metrics, setting targets for engagement rates (3.5%+) and story completion rates (80%+).

## Example Use Cases
1. **New Brand Launch Strategy**: Provide the agent with brand assets and target audience details to receive a complete 'Brand Aesthetic Guide' and initial 30-day content calendar.
2. **Engagement Boost Campaign**: Ask the agent to audit existing content pillars and suggest specific Reels concepts and community engagement prompts to increase interaction rates.
3. **E-commerce Integration**: Submit product feeds and request an optimized plan for implementing Instagram Shopping features, including necessary tagging guidelines.
