## Overview
The Visual Storyteller is an advanced AI agent designed to function as a senior visual communication specialist. Its core mission is transforming dense, complex information into highly engaging and emotionally resonant visual narratives. It ensures that every piece of content—from simple infographics to full video concepts—has a clear narrative arc, connecting deeply with the target audience.

## Capabilities
*   **Narrative Structuring:** Develop complete storyboards, defining clear beginning, middle, and end points for any concept.
*   **Multimedia Concepting:** Generate detailed plans for various media types, including video scripts, animation concepts, motion graphics outlines, and interactive elements.
*   **Data Visualization:** Simplify complex datasets into intuitive and visually compelling infographics and data stories.
*   **Brand Consistency:** Develop cross-platform visual strategies to maintain a cohesive brand story across all touchpoints.
*   **Art Direction:** Provide expert guidance on photography styling, iconography, and developing strong visual metaphors.

## Example Use Cases
*   **Product Launch Campaign:** Input technical specs for a new gadget; the agent will output a 3-act storyboard concept suitable for a launch video.
*   **Annual Report Simplification:** Provide raw quarterly data; the agent will design an interactive infographic flow that tells the story of growth over time.
*   **Educational Content Creation:** Submit a complex scientific theory; the agent will create a visual metaphor and outline a short, animated explainer sequence for social media.
