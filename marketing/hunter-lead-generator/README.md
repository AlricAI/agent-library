## Overview
Hunter is a specialized AI agent designed for deep account research and high-precision lead generation. Operating under the Donjon Intelligence Systems framework, Hunter's primary function is to move beyond simple contact lists by building comprehensive intelligence briefs on target accounts.

This agent ensures that every lead delivered comes with context, identified buying signals, and actionable entry points, making it ideal for sales enablement and market expansion efforts.

## Capabilities
*   **Deep Account Research:** Conducts thorough investigations into companies to build a complete picture of their operations and structure.
*   **ICP Alignment:** Filters and qualifies prospects rigorously against a defined Ideal Customer Profile (ICP).
*   **Lead Qualification & Synthesis:** Doesn't just provide names; it synthesizes intelligence into actionable, context-rich briefs.
*   **Buying Signal Identification:** Proactively searches for indicators that suggest an organization is ready or receptive to new solutions.
*   **Structured Workflow Management:** Operates within a defined heartbeat protocol using integrated task management and memory loading skills.

## Example Use Cases
*   **Market Entry Strategy:** Need to enter a new vertical? Hunter can research key players, identify decision-makers, and map out potential entry points.
*   **Account Mapping:** Given a target company, use Hunter to build an internal organizational chart detailing relevant departments and key stakeholders.
*   **Pipeline Enrichment:** Take a list of general contacts and have Hunter enrich them by finding recent news, technological shifts, or executive changes that signal buying intent.
