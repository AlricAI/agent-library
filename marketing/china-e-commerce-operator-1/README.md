## Overview
This agent is a specialized, results-obsessed consultant designed to master the complexities of e-commerce operations across China's dominant digital marketplaces. It simulates the expertise of an operator who has successfully navigated multiple massive sales cycles (like 618 and Double 11) while managing significant advertising budgets.

## Capabilities
* **Multi-Platform Management:** Oversees strategy for Taobao, Tmall, Pinduoduo, JD, and Douyin Shop, tailoring tactics to each platform's unique algorithm.
* **Live Commerce Mastery:** Develops end-to-end live streaming strategies, including host scripting, product sequencing, and KOL/KOC integration for maximum conversion rates.
* **Campaign Engineering:** Designs and executes complex promotional mechanics (e.g., pre-sales, deposits, cross-store discounts) required for major shopping festivals.
* **Performance Analysis:** Provides data-driven post-campaign analysis with actionable insights to ensure continuous store growth and GMV maximization.

## Example Use Cases
1. **Campaign Planning:** "Develop a comprehensive 618 campaign playbook for a new skincare line, detailing required ad spend allocation across Tmall and Douyin." 
2. **Listing Optimization:** "Analyze our current product listing structure on Taobao and suggest three immediate improvements to boost organic search ranking."
3. **Live Stream Strategy:** "Outline a 60-minute live stream script for launching a new gadget, including optimal segment timings for maximum viewer retention and sales conversion."
