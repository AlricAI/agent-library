## Overview
This agent acts as a strategic co-author and ghostwriter, designed to help founders, industry experts, and thought leaders transform disparate raw materials—such as voice notes, interview transcripts, and bulleted fragments—into cohesive, high-impact first-person book chapters. Its core mission is not just to write content, but to architect a narrative that builds undeniable category authority for the author.

## Capabilities
*   **Structured Drafting:** Converts unstructured inputs into logically flowing, chapter-ready drafts written in a strong first-person voice.
*   **Narrative Cohesion:** Maintains a consistent 'red thread' across multiple chapters, ensuring the book reads as one unified argument rather than disparate essays.
*   **Voice Preservation:** Actively works to preserve the author's unique personality, conviction, and strategic rhythm, avoiding generic AI prose.
*   **Argument Strengthening:** Challenges weak logic, soft claims, and filler language, forcing every section to earn its place in the narrative.
*   **Editorial Rigor:** Delivers versioned drafts alongside explicit assumptions, identified evidence gaps, and concrete revision requests for continuous improvement.

## Example Use Cases
1. **From Notes to Chapter:** Feed it a collection of 30 minutes of rambling voice notes from an expert on decentralized finance. It will structure these into a compelling chapter draft arguing a specific thesis about the future of DeFi.
2. **Maintaining Arc:** After drafting three chapters, you can feed it a summary prompt like, "The book needs to pivot toward regulatory risk in Chapter 4." The agent will ensure the new content logically connects to the established themes and arguments from Chapters 1-3.
3. **Refining Authority:** When a draft feels too academic or vague, instruct it: "Strengthen this section's commercial edge; make the claim more aggressive and actionable for C-suite readers." It will revise by grounding claims in stronger logic and removing filler.
