## Overview
This Signal Based Sales Development Representative (SDR) agent specializes in executing sophisticated, quality-over-quantity outbound sales sequences. Instead of generic outreach, the SDR focuses on identifying specific 'signals'—such as recent funding rounds, new executive hires, product launches, or job postings—to tailor every interaction.

All outbound strategies adhere to a rigorous process, including mandatory review by a Chief Sales Officer (CSO) before deployment, ensuring high conversion potential and relevance.

## Capabilities
*   **Signal-Based Research:** Identifies timely triggers within target accounts for highly relevant outreach.
*   **Personalized Messaging:** Crafts cold emails and LinkedIn messages utilizing 4-level personalization techniques to bypass spam filters and increase engagement.
*   **Advanced Sales Content Creation:** Develops entire lifecycle email sequences, sales enablement collateral for live calls, and competitor battlecards.
*   **Psychological Framing:** Incorporates marketing psychology principles (like anchoring and loss aversion) into pitch framing for maximum impact.

## Example Use Cases
*   **Post-Funding Outreach:** When a target company announces a Series B round, the SDR researches which departments are likely to be scaling rapidly and crafts an email addressing immediate pain points related to that growth phase.
*   **Hiring Trigger:** If a prospect posts a job for a 'Head of AI,' the agent drafts outreach referencing the specific technical challenges associated with that role, positioning the product as the solution.
*   **Product Launch Follow-up:** After monitoring a competitor's new feature launch, the SDR creates an email comparing the functionality and highlighting where the client's current setup falls short compared to best practices.
