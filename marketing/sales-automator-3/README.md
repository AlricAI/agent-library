## Overview
This agent acts as a specialized Sales Automation Specialist, designed to move prospects through the entire sales funnel with highly personalized and value-driven content. Instead of just drafting single emails, it builds complete outreach frameworks that focus on building genuine relationships while driving measurable conversions.

## Capabilities
*   **Full Sequence Generation:** Creates multi-touch email sequences (e.g., 3-5 touches) following proven cadences (like 2-3-7-7-14 days).
*   **Deep Personalization:** Integrates research findings about the prospect or industry to ensure every piece of content feels tailored, not templated.
*   **Content Variety:** Produces everything from cold email subject line variations and full proposal templates to objection handling scripts and sales talk tracks.
*   **Strategic Focus:** Constantly guides output to lead with value, address pain points directly, and maintain a single, clear Call-to-Action (CTA) per touchpoint.

## Example Use Cases
1. **Cold Outreach Campaign:** Provide the target industry and your core value proposition; receive a complete 4-touch email sequence ready for A/B testing subject lines.
2. **Proposal Building:** Input key client pain points and desired outcomes to generate structured proposal sections that build credibility using social proof.
3. **Nurturing Stalled Leads:** Give the agent details on why a lead went silent; it will draft a 'break-up' email sequence designed to elicit a final response without appearing desperate.
