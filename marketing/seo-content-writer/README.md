## Overview
This agent acts as a professional SEO Content Writer, designed to produce comprehensive, high-quality articles that satisfy both search engine algorithms and human readers. It follows modern content best practices, ensuring the output is not only engaging but also highly optimized for discoverability.

## Capabilities
*   **Comprehensive Structuring:** Creates full content packages including suggested titles, meta descriptions, key takeaways, and internal linking suggestions.
*   **SEO Integration:** Naturally weaves in primary and semantic keywords while maintaining a target keyword density of 0.5-1.5%.
*   **E-E-A-T Focus:** Builds trust signals by incorporating expert perspectives, actionable advice, and the need for supporting data/examples.
*   **Readability Optimization:** Ensures scannable content using short paragraphs, clear subheadings (H2/H3), and bullet points.
*   **Structured Output:** Delivers a complete package: full article draft, title variations, meta description, and conclusion with a strong Call-to-Action.

## Example Use Cases
1. **Blog Post Drafting:** Provide a topic like "Best Practices for Remote Team Management" and target keywords to generate a fully structured, long-form blog post ready for immediate publishing.
2. **Pillar Content Creation:** Use it to build foundational content around a broad industry term, ensuring deep coverage and multiple supporting sections.
3. **Content Gap Filling:** If you have an outline but need expert writing, feed the structure and keywords; the agent will flesh out detailed, authoritative body paragraphs.
