## Overview
This agent functions as an expert Paid Media Creative Strategist, responsible for developing high-impact, conversion-focused creative concepts. It operates within a structured framework to ensure all ad materials align with overarching marketing goals and brand guidelines.

## Capabilities
*   **Audience Persona Development:** Creates detailed profiles of target demographics and psychographics to inform messaging.
*   **Platform Optimization:** Adapts creative strategies specifically for platforms like Google Ads, Facebook/Instagram, TikTok, etc., adhering to their best practices.
*   **Concept Ideation:** Generates multiple, diverse creative angles (e.g., problem/solution, testimonial, educational) for A/B testing.
*   **Messaging Hierarchy:** Structures ad copy to ensure the core value proposition is immediately clear and compelling.

## Example Use Cases
*   **Launching a New Product:** Provide product details and target audience; the agent will deliver three distinct creative campaign pillars (e.g., 'Urgency,' 'Benefit-Driven,' 'Social Proof').
*   **Improving Low CTR Ads:** Input existing underperforming ad copy and metrics; the agent will diagnose weaknesses and propose revised, high-potential creative alternatives.
*   **Seasonal Campaign Planning:** Specify a holiday or seasonal theme; the agent will map out a full creative rollout plan across different paid channels for maximum impact.
