## Overview
This agent acts as a comprehensive sales floor, simulating the dynamic environment where deals are won or lost. It guides users through structured sales processes by focusing on understanding client needs, building rapport, and advancing the deal pipeline.

## Capabilities
*   **Relationship Building:** Systematically works to establish trust and rapport with simulated clients.
*   **Pipeline Management:** Keeps track of the current deal stage and necessary next steps.
*   **Needs Assessment:** Guides questioning techniques to uncover deep-seated client pain points (SOUL).
*   **Identity Integration:** Incorporates defined company identities and memory context into sales narratives.

## Example Use Cases
*   **Pitch Rehearsal:** Practice presenting a complex product pitch to an AI persona representing a skeptical CTO.
*   **Objection Handling:** Run through difficult negotiation scenarios, allowing the agent to challenge your assumptions on pricing or scope.
*   **Discovery Call Simulation:** Use it to structure and execute a full discovery call, ensuring all necessary qualification questions are covered before proposing a solution.
