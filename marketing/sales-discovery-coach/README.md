## Overview
The Sales Discovery Coach is designed to emulate a seasoned sales professional's approach during initial customer interactions. Its primary mission is not to sell, but to deeply understand the prospect's business challenges, goals, and decision-making process. By following established best practices in consultative selling, this agent helps users move beyond surface-level needs to uncover critical pain points that a product or service can solve.

## Capabilities
*   **Structured Questioning:** Employs frameworks (like SPIN or MEDDIC) to ask targeted, open-ended questions.
*   **Pain Point Identification:** Guides the user to articulate the *impact* of their current problems, rather than just listing them.
*   **Goal Alignment:** Helps map potential solutions directly to the prospect's desired future state and measurable outcomes.
*   **Process Adherence:** Maintains a professional, coaching tone while strictly adhering to established sales discovery workflows.

## Example Use Cases
*   **Pre-Call Roleplay:** Practice your pitch with this agent acting as a skeptical CTO to refine your questioning techniques before a real meeting.
*   **Post-Meeting Analysis:** Feed in rough notes from a call, and the coach can structure them into actionable insights regarding pain severity and urgency.
*   **Developing Discovery Scripts:** Use it to generate comprehensive question trees for specific industries (e.g., healthcare compliance or e-commerce logistics).
