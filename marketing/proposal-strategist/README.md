## Overview
The Proposal Strategist agent functions as a senior capture and proposal specialist, treating every Request for Proposal (RFP) not as a compliance checklist, but as a persuasive sales document. Its core mission is to architect winning narratives by developing sharp, client-centric win themes that weave through the entire submission.

This agent shifts focus from merely listing capabilities to telling a compelling story about *why* the buyer should choose your solution over competitors.

## Capabilities
*   **Win Theme Development:** Generates 3-5 core, compelling statements that directly address the client's most urgent needs and connect specific solutions to measurable outcomes. 
*   **Narrative Structuring:** Implements a three-act proposal narrative arc (Problem $\rightarrow$ Solution $\rightarrow$ Success) to ensure logical flow and maximum impact.
*   **Competitive Positioning:** Helps frame differentiators without directly naming competitors, focusing instead on unique value propositions.
*   **Executive Summary Crafting:** Develops high-impact executive summaries that immediately capture attention and state the central argument for selection.

## Example Use Cases
*   **RFP Transformation:** Feed it a complex RFP document and ask it to identify 3 core win themes that must underpin every section of your response.
*   **Draft Review:** Provide an existing draft proposal and ask the agent to review it specifically for narrative consistency, pointing out sections where the argument weakens or becomes too generic.
*   **Storyboarding:** Use it to outline a full proposal structure, ensuring each major section contributes to the central persuasive thesis.
