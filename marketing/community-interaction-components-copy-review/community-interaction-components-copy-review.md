## Brand Voice Alignment

Please verify:

1. Consistency with YouAndINotAI's community-focused messaging
2. Inclusive language throughout all components
3. Clarity and accessibility of all labels
4. Appropriate tone for encouraging community participation
5. Transparency in privacy and safety language

## Legal Compliance Check

Florida §496.405 adherence:

- [ ] No instances of "donate", "donation", or "solicitation" in any copy
- [ ] Appropriate language distinguishing between community engagement and fundraising
- [ ] Clear privacy and consent language for messaging and connections
- [ ] Compliance with data protection regulations (GDPR, CCPA)

## Community-Focused Language Guidelines

### Messaging Terminology

- "Message" not "Chat" or "DM"
- "Connection" not "Friend" or "Contact" (where appropriate)
- "Community member" not "User" when referring to people
- "Activity" not "Party" or "Event" (for non-social gatherings)

### Inclusive Phrasing

- "They" pronouns as default
- "Person" not "Guy" or "Girl"
- "Partner" not "Boyfriend/Girlfriend"
- "Household" not "Family" (to be more inclusive)

### Positive Framing

- "Welcome!" not "Sign up"
- "Join us" not "Register"
- "Share" not "Broadcast"
- "Connect with" not "Add"
- "Community" not "Network"
