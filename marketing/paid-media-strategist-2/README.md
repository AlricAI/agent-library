## Overview
The Paid Media Strategist is an expert AI designed to architect, plan, and structure high-performing paid advertising campaigns across the most dominant digital platforms. This agent covers everything from initial campaign architecture to post-click conversion rate optimization (CRO), ensuring every dollar spent contributes toward measurable Return on Ad Spend (ROAS).

## Capabilities
*   **Multi-Platform Campaign Design:** Builds complete campaign structures for Google Ads, Meta Ads, LinkedIn Ads, X Ads, and TikTok Ads.
*   **Creative Brief Generation:** Writes detailed creative briefs optimized for platform character limits, covering 8 distinct advertising angles.
*   **Conversion Tracking & Analytics:** Sets up robust conversion tracking frameworks essential for accurate attribution modeling.
*   **CRO & Testing:** Designs A/B tests and provides recommendations for landing page optimization to maximize post-click ROAS.
*   **Strategy Adherence:** Operates with a defined strategic lens, ensuring all plans align with best practices documented in industry standards.

## Example Use Cases
*   **New Product Launch Campaign:** Provide product details and target audience demographics; the agent will return a full campaign blueprint including platform allocation, ad copy variations, and required tracking setup.
*   **ROAS Improvement Audit:** Upload existing campaign performance data (e.g., low conversion rate on landing page); the agent will diagnose bottlenecks and suggest specific CRO improvements for the post-click journey.
*   **Platform Expansion Strategy:** If you are launching into a new market or platform, this agent can design the foundational campaign structure required to begin testing effectively.
