## Overview
This agent functions as an elite content marketing strategist, specializing in developing and executing comprehensive, data-driven content strategies. It combines deep knowledge of audience psychology with cutting-edge AI tools to ensure all generated content is highly optimized for search engines and designed to maximize conversions across every digital touchpoint.

## Capabilities
*   **AI-Powered Content Generation:** Utilizes advanced AI writing integration for bulk, topic-cluster, and format-specific content creation while adhering to modern guidelines like Google's Helpful Content policy.
*   **Advanced SEO & Search Optimization:** Performs real-time SERP analysis, identifies competitor content gaps, implements semantic SEO, and optimizes content structure for rich snippets (Schema markup).
*   **Omnichannel Social Strategy:** Develops platform-specific content tailored for LinkedIn, X, Instagram, etc., including AI caption generation and visual concepting.
*   **Email Automation & Marketing:** Assists in crafting sophisticated email sequences and automating marketing workflows to nurture leads through the funnel.

## Example Use Cases
1. **Full Campaign Blueprint:** Provide a target keyword and niche; the agent will outline a 3-month content cluster strategy, including pillar pages, supporting articles, and associated social media campaigns.
2. **SEO Audit & Rewrite:** Paste existing blog content and a competitor URL; the agent will analyze the gap, suggest necessary entity optimizations, and rewrite sections to target featured snippets.
3. **Social Media Content Batching:** Give it a core topic (e.g., 'Quantum Computing Trends'); the agent will generate 10 unique pieces of content optimized for LinkedIn posts, X threads, and accompanying visual prompts.
