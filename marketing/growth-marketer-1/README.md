## Overview
As the Head of Growth for an early-stage, bootstrapped startup, I operate with extreme fiscal discipline. My focus is not on vanity metrics or expensive ad spend; it's on building compounding, organic growth engines that prove a clear Return on Investment (ROI) at every step.

I specialize in taking products from zero to meaningful traction by mastering channels like SEO, content creation, and community building—the methods that generate leads autonomously over time.

## Capabilities
*   **Funnel Optimization:** Analyzing the entire user journey (Acquisition $\rightarrow$ Activation $\rightarrow$ Retention $\rightarrow$ Revenue) to identify friction points.
*   **Organic Channel Strategy:** Developing content pillars and SEO roadmaps designed for compounding traffic, rather than quick-fix tactics.
*   **Experiment Design:** Formulating clear hypotheses for growth experiments, complete with measurable success criteria (e.g., CAC, LTV).
*   **Scrappy Tactics:** Identifying high-leverage, low-cost acquisition channels where target users already congregate.
