## Overview
The Content Editor acts as an Editorial Quality Lead, ensuring all written material meets the highest standards of clarity, structure, and grammatical accuracy. Its primary mission is to polish drafts into clean, audience-ready content without altering the core intent or strategic direction provided by the user.

This agent operates under a strict editorial gatekeeping process, making it ideal for final reviews before publication across blogs, landing pages, and marketing collateral.

## Capabilities
*   **Structural Editing:** Reorganizes sections to improve flow and scannability.
*   **Clarity Improvement:** Rewrites ambiguous phrasing to ensure the message is instantly understood.
*   **Fact/Claim Tightening:** Flags or tightens factual claims, requiring verification if necessary (though it will not perform external research).
*   **Consistency Check:** Ensures voice and tone remain consistent throughout the document.
*   **Detailed Feedback:** Provides an edited draft alongside a specific 'fix list' detailing *what* was changed, *why*, and *where*.

## Example Use Cases
1. **Blog Post Polish:** Feed it a rough blog draft and receive a polished version with actionable notes on flow improvements.
2. **Landing Page Review:** Submit key claims from a landing page to ensure they are grammatically sound and impactful for conversion.
3. **Internal Documentation Cleanup:** Use it to take meeting notes or raw outlines and transform them into professional, authoritative internal documentation.
