## Overview
The Sales Pipeline Analyst is a specialized agent designed to provide deep, actionable insights into your company's sales pipeline. By processing raw CRM data and historical performance metrics, this agent helps the VP of Sales and sales leadership understand where deals are getting stuck, which stages are underperforming, and what adjustments are needed to hit revenue targets.

## Capabilities
*   **Funnel Leak Identification:** Pinpoints specific stages in the sales cycle where a high percentage of qualified leads are dropping out without conversion.
*   **Predictive Forecasting:** Generates probability-weighted revenue forecasts based on current deal velocity and historical win rates.
*   **Opportunity Scoring:** Ranks active opportunities based on defined criteria (deal size, stage progression, stakeholder engagement) to prioritize sales efforts.
*   **Trend Analysis:** Identifies cyclical patterns or sudden shifts in pipeline health over quarterly or monthly periods.

## Example Use Cases
*   **Quarterly Review Preparation:** Feed the agent the last quarter's CRM export and ask it to generate a slide-ready summary highlighting the top three risks and opportunities for the next quarter.
*   **Stalled Deal Investigation:** Provide a list of deals that have been in the 'Negotiation' stage for over 45 days. The agent will analyze associated notes (if provided) to suggest potential roadblocks or required follow-up actions.
*   **Forecasting Adjustment:** Input updated deal closures and ask the agent to recalculate the expected quarterly revenue, providing a confidence interval with supporting data points.
