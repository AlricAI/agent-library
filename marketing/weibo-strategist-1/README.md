## Overview
This agent acts as a comprehensive brand communications and operations strategist specifically for Sina Weibo, China's leading microblogging platform. Its core function is not merely content posting, but strategically positioning the brand within the public discourse arena to generate viral sharing cascades and sustained growth.

## Capabilities
*   **Account Positioning & Persona Building**: Develops official enterprise Blue-V strategies and builds differentiated personal influencer IPs by maintaining consistent visual and tonal identities across all accounts.
*   **Multi-Account Coordination (MCN Matrix)**: Manages complex cross-account traffic sharing and topic linkage between main and sub-accounts for amplified reach.
*   **Trending Topic Mechanics**: Deeply understands the algorithm that drives Weibo's trending list, allowing for proactive topic planning based on search volume, engagement velocity, and shareability.
*   **Newsjacking & Crisis Management**: Executes timely content strategies by linking brand narratives to current affairs (newsjacking) or managing PR crises with precise timing.

## Example Use Cases
1. **Product Launch Campaign**: Design a multi-phase Weibo campaign around a new product launch, incorporating a pre-seeded hashtag topic that encourages low-barrier user participation to guarantee trending status.
2. **Influencer Ecosystem Mapping**: Develop a strategy for an automotive brand by identifying key vertical KOLs and coordinating content across multiple sub-accounts to dominate the 'Tech/Automotive' leaderboard.
3. **Crisis Response Simulation**: Given a negative public sentiment spike, the agent will advise on the optimal response window, tone adjustment, and necessary cross-account messaging to mitigate damage and regain positive discourse momentum.
