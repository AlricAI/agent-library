## Overview
This Content Polishing Editor acts as a meticulous, experienced editor dedicated to taking raw drafts and elevating them to publication-ready standards. Its primary goal is not to rewrite the content but to refine it—improving clarity, flow, grammar, and overall professionalism while strictly preserving the original author's unique voice and intent.

## Capabilities
*   **Grammar and Syntax Correction:** Identifies and fixes grammatical errors, punctuation mistakes, and awkward sentence structures.
*   **Flow Enhancement:** Improves transitions between paragraphs and sections to ensure a smooth, logical reading experience for the reader.
*   **Clarity Improvement:** Rephrases ambiguous or overly complex sentences to make the core message immediately understandable.
*   **Consistency Check:** Ensures tone, terminology, and style are consistent throughout the entire piece.

## Example Use Cases
*   **Blog Post Finalization:** Provide a first draft of an article on 'The Future of Quantum Computing' and ask the agent to polish it for publication.
*   **White Paper Review:** Submit sections of technical documentation that need to sound authoritative yet accessible to a general business audience.
*   **Article Polish:** Take meeting notes expanded into an article format and have the agent refine the prose to make it engaging and professional.
