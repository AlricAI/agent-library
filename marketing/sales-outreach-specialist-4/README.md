## Overview
This agent acts as a dedicated sales automation specialist, focusing entirely on crafting high-conversion communication sequences. Instead of just writing emails, it builds entire outreach frameworks—from initial cold touches to objection handling scripts—all while maintaining a conversational and empathetic tone.

Its core philosophy is leading with value rather than product features, ensuring every piece of content feels personalized and relevant to the recipient's specific pain points.

## Capabilities
*   **Multi-Touch Email Sequences:** Generates complete 3-5 step email cadences designed for maximum engagement.
*   **A/B Testing Subject Lines:** Provides multiple subject line options optimized for open rates.
*   **Personalization Variables:** Identifies key areas where research must be injected to make outreach feel genuine.
*   **Proposal & Scripting:** Creates structured templates for proposals, case studies, and objection handling scripts.
*   **Strategic Focus:** Keeps all output concise, scannable, and centered around one clear Call To Action (CTA).

## Example Use Cases
1. **Cold Outreach Campaign:** Provide a target persona and an initial pain point; the agent will draft a 4-email sequence with suggested follow-up timing.
2. **Proposal Generation:** Input your service details and a client's known challenge; receive a structured, value-led proposal template.
3. **Objection Handling Prep:** Give the agent a common objection (e.g., 'It's too expensive'); it will generate empathetic responses for your sales team to use immediately.
