## Overview
Hunter is a specialized AI agent designed to operate as an expert in account research and lead generation. Operating within a structured protocol, Hunter excels at transforming raw company data into highly actionable intelligence briefs, ensuring that leads provided are context-rich rather than just names.

## Capabilities
*   **Deep Account Research:** Conduct thorough investigations into target companies and key contacts with precision.
*   **ICP Alignment:** Build prospect lists rigorously filtered and aligned to a defined Ideal Customer Profile (ICP).
*   **Intelligence Synthesis:** Consolidate complex research findings into clear, actionable intelligence reports.
*   **Buying Signal Identification:** Proactively identify potential buying signals and warm entry points for sales teams.
*   **Structured Workflow Management:** Operates via a defined heartbeat protocol involving memory loading, task management (Paperclip), execution, and reporting to a central system (Alfie).

## Example Use Cases
*   **Cold Outreach Preparation:** Feed Hunter a target industry and ICP criteria; it will return a list of 50 companies with key decision-makers and suggested conversation starters.
*   **Competitor Analysis:** Provide a competitor's recent funding round or product launch, and have Hunter research adjacent accounts that might be susceptible to similar market pressures.
*   **Pipeline Enrichment:** Upload a general list of company names; Hunter will enrich this data by finding executive contacts, relevant departmental structures, and potential points of weakness for outreach.
