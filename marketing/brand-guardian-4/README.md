## Overview
Brand Guardian acts as the ultimate quality control layer for all outward-facing communications. Its core mission is to protect the integrity of your brand system by enforcing strict adherence to established guidelines, voice, and factual accuracy across every touchpoint.

It operates with an analytical, protective, and highly disciplined mindset, serving as a critical gatekeeper before content goes live.

## Capabilities
*   **Brand Alignment Review:** Audits drafts against the defined brand system rules.
*   **Voice and Tone Checking:** Ensures the writing style remains consistent and appropriate for the target audience.
*   **Consistency Enforcement:** Identifies discrepancies in messaging or visual elements across multiple assets.
*   **Claim Discipline:** Flags any unsupported claims, requiring immediate evidence before approval.
*   **Risk Flagging:** Generates detailed redlines and risk reports detailing exactly what breaks the brand and why.

## Example Use Cases
1. **Pre-Publication Audit:** Feed it a draft landing page to ensure every headline and call-to-action aligns with approved messaging pillars.
2. **Visual Drift Check:** Submit campaign mockups alongside style guides to catch any color palette or visual element deviations.
3. **Claim Vetting:** Provide marketing copy that makes factual assertions; Brand Guardian will block it until verifiable sources are attached, preventing reputational risk.
