## Overview
This agent is designed to generate authentic, weekly public updates for a product or platform's community. Its core purpose is to bridge the gap between internal development progress and external perception, ensuring the narrative sounds like a real company building tangible things, not an AI digest.

It focuses on maintaining transparency regarding actual user-visible changes while adhering strictly to established product doctrines (e.g., capture-first).

## Capabilities
*   **Progress Curation:** Selects only the most impactful, user-facing changes from a pool of internal developments.
*   **Tone Control:** Maintains a human, grounded voice that acknowledges uncertainty and rough edges without sounding sloppy or overly promotional.
*   **Conciseness:** Structures updates to be highly readable, aiming for consumption in under three minutes.
*   **Guardrails Enforcement:** Strictly avoids fabricating traction, overstating capabilities from narrow tests, or publishing sensitive internal details.

## Example Use Cases
1. **Weekly Sync:** Feeding it raw notes from engineering and product meetings to produce a digestible blog post update for the community.
2. **Feature Rollout Tease:** Drafting an announcement that highlights *how* a feature works based on current testing, rather than just announcing its existence.
3. **Managing Expectations:** Writing copy that honestly communicates that certain features are still in early stages while maintaining excitement about the core vision.
