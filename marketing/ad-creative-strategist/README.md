## Overview
This agent functions as a specialized creative strategist for paid media advertising across platforms like Google, Meta, and Performance Max. Its core philosophy is that in automated bidding environments, the creative asset is the most controllable lever for performance improvement. It moves beyond simply writing catchy ads; it builds systematic frameworks for testing hypotheses.

## Capabilities
* **Search Ad Copywriting:** Generates optimized headlines and descriptions for Responsive Search Ads (RSAs), including strategies for keyword insertion and dynamic content.
* **RSA Architecture Design:** Develops comprehensive 15-headline strategies, ensuring all combinations read coherently and cover diverse value propositions (e.g., brand, benefit, CTA).
* **Meta Creative Strategy:** Crafts primary text, headlines, and descriptions optimized for Meta's Hook-Body-CTA structure across various formats (single image, carousel, video).
* **Performance Max Asset Grouping:** Structures asset groups by defining necessary text assets, image requirements, and aligning creative themes with audience signals.
* **Systematic Creative Testing:** Designs robust A/B testing frameworks, including criteria for monitoring creative fatigue and determining statistical significance for test winners.
* **Competitive Analysis:** Performs research on competitor ad libraries to identify messaging gaps and opportunities for differentiation in your own campaigns.

## Example Use Cases
1. **Launching a New Product Campaign:** Provide the product details and target audience, and ask the agent to generate 15 unique RSA headlines covering benefits, features, and urgency.
2. **Optimizing Meta Ads:** Input existing ad copy and specify the desired format (e.g., video carousel), asking for three distinct creative angles with corresponding hooks and CTAs.
3. **Structuring PMax Assets:** Give the agent your core value proposition and target keywords, and request a structured asset group composition plan detailing required image types, video concepts, and supporting text assets.
