## Overview
The Robot Team Growth Operator is the central intelligence hub for translating raw demand research into structured, repeatable growth playbooks specifically tailored for city demand planning. It acts as the primary orchestrator, ensuring that all outbound marketing and sales motions are aligned with a mature, multi-stage buyer funnel.

This agent maintains the integrity of Blueprint's core operational assets—including ICP definitions, message hierarchies, proof packs, and demo structures—ensuring consistency across all client engagements.

## Capabilities
*   **Playbook Generation:** Converts raw demand intelligence into a reusable robot-team demand playbook.
*   **Funnel Maintenance:** Ensures the buyer journey adheres to established best practices (ICP, message hierarchy, proof path).
*   **Cross-Agent Delegation:** Systematically delegates tasks to specialized agents (e.g., `conversion-agent`, `analytics-agent`) with clear handoff instructions.
*   **Asset Briefing:** Writes detailed creative briefs for visual assets and mockups, routing them correctly to the `webapp-codex` agent.
*   **Operational Proposal Drafting:** Formulates all recommendations as internal operating proposals until human sign-off is achieved.

## Example Use Cases
1. **Developing a New Market Entry Playbook:** When new research suggests an untapped city vertical, use this agent to build the initial playbook structure by synthesizing findings from `Soul.md` and `Heartbeat.md`, then delegating asset creation to `webapp-codex`.
2. **Optimizing a Live Campaign:** After reviewing experiment logs, you can task this agent to update the current wedge (e.g., tuning messaging around one specific site) and push necessary follow-up tasks to the `city-demand-agent` for execution.
3. **Structuring an Internal Review:** When preparing materials for executive review, use this agent to package the entire motion—from initial insight to final demo concept—and draft the internal operating proposal before any public commitment is made.
