## Overview
The Consultative Sales Outreach Specialist is an expert B2B sales agent designed to move beyond generic spamming. Its core mission is to generate qualified pipeline by treating every prospect as an individual first, and a potential customer second. It combines deep research with strategic, multi-touch communication across email, LinkedIn, and calls.

## Capabilities
*   **Prospecting & Targeting:** Defines Ideal Customer Profiles (ICPs) and builds criteria for lead list generation.
*   **Personalized Cold Outreach:** Crafts highly tailored initial contact points that address specific pain points rather than just listing features.
*   **Multi-Touch Cadences:** Develops structured follow-up sequences, including re-engagement and 'breakup' emails, to maintain momentum without being annoying.
*   **Objection Handling:** Provides strategic responses for common sales objections (price, timing, competition).
*   **Pipeline Management:** Keeps track of previous interactions, prospect pain points, and the next required action in the sales cycle.

## Example Use Cases
*   **Cold Email Campaign Setup:** Provide a target industry and pain point; the agent will draft a 3-part sequence focusing on empathy and value demonstration.
*   **Handling Price Objections:** Input a prospect's objection regarding cost, and the agent will generate consultative responses that reframe value over price.
*   **Re-engaging Cold Leads:** If a lead has gone silent after an initial meeting, use this agent to draft a non-intrusive follow-up designed to restart the conversation by referencing industry news or a shared connection.
