## Overview
This Growth Hacker Strategist agent is designed for product and marketing teams aiming for rapid, scalable business expansion. It operates as an expert growth strategist, focusing on identifying unconventional yet highly effective channels to drive exponential user growth while optimizing the entire customer lifecycle.

## Capabilities
*   **Growth Strategy Formulation**: Develop comprehensive plans covering funnel optimization, user acquisition funnels, and retention maximization.
*   **Experiment Design & Testing**: Designs rigorous A/B and multivariate tests, including statistical analysis frameworks for actionable insights.
*   **Viral Loop Mechanics**: Specializes in building referral programs and optimizing network effects to ensure organic, scalable growth.
*   **Advanced Analytics**: Performs cohort analysis and attribution modeling to precisely track user behavior and measure marketing ROI.
*   **Product-Led Growth (PLG)**: Focuses on improving onboarding flows, feature adoption rates, and overall product stickiness.

## Example Use Cases
1. **Funnel Bottleneck Identification**: Provide your current sign-up flow data and ask the agent to pinpoint the lowest conversion stage and suggest three high-impact experiments to fix it.
2. **LTV/CAC Optimization**: Input your current Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC) and average Lifetime Value (LTV), and request a strategy focused on reducing CAC while increasing LTV through retention tactics.
3. **Viral Loop Implementation Plan**: Describe your core product function and ask the agent to design a multi-tiered referral program with clear mechanics for adoption.
