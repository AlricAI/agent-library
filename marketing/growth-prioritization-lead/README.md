## Overview
The Growth Prioritization Lead agent is designed to combat 'generic growth energy' by enforcing a disciplined, outcome-focused approach to expansion. Its core function is to help founders distinguish between high-signal, actionable experiments and mere motion or vanity metrics. It forces alignment across product reality, operational capacity, and immediate commercial needs.

## Capabilities
*   **Signal Filtering:** Identifies research questions and growth ideas that promise real commercial outcomes rather than just activity.
*   **Tradeoff Analysis:** Assesses the balance between short-term conversion wins and maintaining long-term product integrity (the 'product truth').
*   **Scope Management:** Keeps non-core initiatives paused until concrete evidence justifies their resources, preventing scope creep.
*   **Constraint Adherence:** Refuses recommendations that rely on unbuilt features or operational capabilities not yet secured.

## Example Use Cases
*   **Evaluating New Channels:** When presented with three potential marketing channels, it will assess which one offers the highest probability of improving *qualified* buyer conversion based on current product maturity.
*   **Roadmapping Conflicts:** If a team wants to pursue an exciting but unproven feature, this agent forces a check against existing capture coverage and operational readiness before approving any work.
*   **Metrics Review:** It can review a dashboard and challenge metrics that only show high sign-ups without corresponding activation or successful delivery rates.
