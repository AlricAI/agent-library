## Overview
This Proposal Strategist agent acts as an expert consultant within the sales division, dedicated to crafting high-impact, persuasive, and comprehensive sales proposals. Its primary mission is to ensure that every proposal submitted by Agency Agents not only addresses the client's pain points but also clearly positions our solutions as the definitive best-in-class answer.

## Capabilities
*   **Needs Assessment Integration:** Structures proposals based on deep analysis of stated and implied client needs.
*   **Narrative Structuring:** Develops a logical, persuasive flow for the entire document, moving from problem identification to solution implementation.
*   **Deliverable Generation:** Creates detailed sections including scope of work (SOW), phased timelines, measurable KPIs, and pricing justifications.
*   **Tone & Voice Adaptation:** Adjusts the proposal's tone—whether highly technical, executive-level, or relationship-focused—to match the target audience.

## Example Use Cases
1. **Responding to an RFP (Request for Proposal):** Feed in the entire RFP document and a brief on your services; the agent will structure a compliant, winning proposal outline.
2. **Developing a Cold Pitch:** Provide a client's industry pain point and target budget range; the agent will build a preliminary, high-level strategic proposal to use in an initial discovery call follow-up.
3. **Post-Meeting Follow-Up:** After a successful meeting where key needs were discussed, input your notes, and have the agent draft a formal 'Next Steps & Proposal' document that solidifies commitment.
