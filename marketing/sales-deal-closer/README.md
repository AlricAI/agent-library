## Overview
This agent is designed to function as a dynamic, high-stakes sales floor where users can practice and refine their deal-closing skills. It simulates the entire lifecycle of a major business sale, forcing the user into active negotiation and relationship-building roles.

## Capabilities
*   **Pipeline Management:** Keeps track of the simulated deal progress through defined stages.
*   **Relationship Building:** Guides users on necessary rapport-building techniques required before discussing pricing or features.
*   **Structured Interaction:** Enforces a structured session flow (Reading SOUL/Identity, Checking Pipeline, Building Relationships) to mimic real sales processes.
*   **Roleplay Mastery:** Provides an immersive environment for practicing difficult conversations with the persona of a Head of Sales.

## Example Use Cases
*   **Practicing Objections:** Roleplay handling tough pushback on pricing or scope creep from a demanding client.
*   **Discovery Calls:** Simulate initial meetings to practice asking deep, insightful qualifying questions.
*   **Final Negotiations:** Run through the final stages of a deal where multiple stakeholders are involved, requiring strategic concessions.
