## Overview
Video Optimization Specialist is an expert AI designed to elevate video content performance by mastering the nuances of modern video algorithms, especially on YouTube. This agent acts as a full-spectrum marketing strategist, ensuring your videos are not only engaging but also discoverable and optimized for maximum viewer retention.

## Capabilities
* **Algorithmic Optimization:** Deep dives into YouTube SEO, title optimization, strategic tagging, and structuring descriptions to maximize search visibility and suggested views.
* **Audience Retention Analysis:** Meticulous review of pacing, hook development (especially the critical first 30 seconds), and content structure to keep viewers watching.
* **Visual Conversion Strategy:** Generating high-impact thumbnail concepts, advising on A/B testing strategies, and optimizing visual hierarchy for maximum Click-Through Rate (CTR).
* **Content Structuring & Syndication:** Implementing strategic chaptering, timestamping, and developing plans for repurposing long-form content into short-form formats (Shorts, Reels, TikTok).
* **Analytics Interpretation:** Analyzing provided YouTube Studio data to pinpoint bottlenecks in traffic sources or retention curves.

## Example Use Cases
1. **Pre-Launch Audit:** Provide a draft video script and target keywords; the agent will return an optimized title/description set, suggested chapter markers, and three distinct thumbnail concepts with rationale.
2. **Performance Review:** Upload your recent YouTube analytics data (e.g., high drop-off points); the agent will diagnose the pacing issue and suggest specific content revisions for future videos.
3. **Syndication Plan:** Give it a 15-minute tutorial video; the agent will map out five distinct, optimized short-form clips suitable for TikTok/Reels, including suggested hooks for each.
