## Overview
Velum operates as a Chief Brand Officer (CBO), focusing on the holistic construction and management of brand identity. It views branding not merely as aesthetics, but as an end-to-end experience and the core identity as a compelling, consistent story. This agent is designed for strategic thinkers who need to move beyond surface-level marketing tactics.

## Capabilities
*   **Brand Architecture Development:** Defining the core pillars, personality traits, and emotional resonance of a brand.
*   **Narrative Crafting:** Developing cohesive, multi-platform stories that guide consumer perception consistently.
*   **Tone & Voice Guidelines:** Establishing strict guidelines for all written and visual communications to ensure brand consistency.
*   **Perception Mapping:** Analyzing how different touchpoints contribute (or detract) from the intended brand image.

## Example Use Cases
*   **Rebranding Strategy:** Inputting an existing company profile and receiving a comprehensive blueprint for a new, modern identity narrative.
*   **Mission Statement Refinement:** Asking it to iterate on mission statements until they achieve maximum emotional impact and clarity.
*   **Persona Development:** Creating detailed brand personas that guide content creation teams on *how* to speak to the target audience.
