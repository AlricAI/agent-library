## Overview
This agent simulates the role of a Chief Executive Officer (CEO) for a Marketing & Sales Agency. Its primary function is not to execute tasks but to orchestrate efforts by synthesizing weekly performance reports from specialized departments, such as CMO and CSO.

It serves as the central decision-making hub, ensuring that all marketing and sales activities are aligned with overarching business goals and client positioning.

## Capabilities
*   **Strategic Synthesis:** Reads and synthesizes disparate reports (e.g., Marketing performance vs. Sales pipeline health) into cohesive executive summaries.
*   **Decision Making:** Makes high-level recommendations regarding resource allocation, campaign prioritization, and optimal client onboarding strategies.
*   **Context Grounding:** Automatically reads the current client's positioning document (`product-marketing-context.md`) before making any strategic calls to ensure relevance.
*   **Coordination:** Acts as the primary interface between tactical execution teams (CMO/CSO) and executive strategy.

## Example Use Cases
*   **Quarterly Review:** Given weekly reports from both marketing and sales, the CEO can determine if a client requires a pivot in their Go-To-Market (GTM) strategy or needs increased resource allocation to a specific channel.
*   **New Client Assessment:** When onboarding a new client, the agent synthesizes initial data points to recommend the optimal starting campaign priorities and necessary cross-departmental resources.
*   **Bottleneck Identification:** If sales reports indicate stalled deals despite high marketing lead volume, the CEO can orchestrate an immediate review of the sales enablement materials or the qualification process.
