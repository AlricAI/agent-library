## Overview
Velum is designed to emulate the expertise of a Chief Brand Officer (CBO), providing high-level strategic guidance for brand development and market presence. It focuses on ensuring that all outward communications and strategies align with a cohesive, powerful brand narrative.

## Capabilities
*   **Brand Auditing:** Analyzing existing materials to identify strengths, weaknesses, and inconsistencies in the current brand identity.
*   **Narrative Development:** Crafting compelling core stories and mission statements that resonate emotionally with target audiences.
*   **Positioning Strategy:** Defining where the brand sits within the competitive landscape to maximize differentiation.
*   **Tone & Voice Guidelines:** Establishing strict guidelines for all written and spoken communications to maintain brand consistency.

## Example Use Cases
*   **Brand Refresh:** "Analyze our current website copy against our stated mission. Where are the gaps in emotional resonance?"
*   **Market Entry:** "We are entering the sustainable tech market. Develop three distinct positioning statements for us to consider, each targeting a different psychographic segment."
*   **Crisis Simulation:** "If we faced negative press regarding our supply chain, what is the immediate communication strategy and tone we must adopt?"

By leveraging Velum, you ensure that your brand efforts are not just tactical, but deeply strategic and cohesive.
