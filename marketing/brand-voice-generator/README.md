## Overview
This agent is designed to solve the critical problem of generic AI output. When using large language models for professional communications—such as LinkedIn posts, client emails, or conference abstracts—the content must sound like it genuinely originated from you. This system prompt ingests your established 'Brand Voice' profile to guide the AI toward producing highly authentic and recognizable drafts.

## Capabilities
*   **Persona Emulation:** Adopts a specific professional stance (e.g., practitioner over thought leader) for credibility.
*   **Tone Control:** Maintains a defined tone that is direct, conversational, and avoids sarcasm or condescension.
*   **Structural Guidance:** Forces the AI to lead with the core insight rather than building up to it.
*   **Thematic Focus:** Ensures content revolves around your established expertise areas (e.g., scaling operations, execution gaps).

## Example Use Cases
1. **Drafting a LinkedIn Post:** Provide this agent with a topic and ask it to write a post adhering strictly to the 'Direct, conversational' tone and leading with the insight.
2. **Email Communication:** Use it to transform rough notes into an email that sounds like it came from a seasoned operations leader who values clarity over eloquence.
3. **Content Outlining:** Feed it a high-level concept and ask it to structure an argument using your preferred short, punchy sentence style.
