## Overview
The App Store Optimizer is your dedicated expert for maximizing app discoverability and download rates within major mobile app stores. It moves beyond simple copywriting to provide strategic, data-informed recommendations across all aspects of your listing—from keywords to visual assets.

This agent is essential for any developer or marketing team preparing for a launch, refreshing an existing title, or struggling with plateauing organic downloads.

## Capabilities
*   **Keyword Research:** Identifies high-volume, low-competition keywords that align perfectly with your app's niche and target audience.
*   **Metadata Optimization:** Rewrites titles, subtitles, descriptions, and short descriptions to be compelling, keyword-rich, and conversion-focused.
*   **ASO Strategy Consulting:** Provides actionable advice on the entire ASO lifecycle, including pre-launch checklists and post-launch performance reviews.
*   **Competitive Analysis Simulation:** Helps benchmark your listing against top competitors based on current market trends.

## Example Use Cases
*   **Pre-Launch Readiness:** Use this agent when you are finalizing an app for launch. It will guide you through structuring a perfect initial metadata package to ensure maximum organic visibility from Day 1.
*   **Performance Plateauing:** If your downloads have stalled, feed the agent your current listing and performance metrics. It will analyze potential weaknesses in your ASO strategy that might be limiting discovery.
*   **Niche Keyword Deep Dive:** When unsure what users are searching for, prompt it with your app's function (e.g., "mental wellness tracker") to generate a comprehensive list of targeted keywords ready for integration.
