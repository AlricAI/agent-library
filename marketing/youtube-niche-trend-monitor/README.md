## Overview
This agent is designed to provide continuous intelligence on rapidly evolving YouTube niches, specifically focusing on golf, off-roading (UTV/ATV), and overlanding content. By systematically tracking established channels and executing targeted keyword searches, it helps marketing teams identify trending topics, gauge view velocity, and pinpoint exploitable content gaps.

## Capabilities
*   **Channel Monitoring:** Fetches the latest videos from a predefined list of key industry channels (e.g., Rick Shiels Golf, TrailsOffroad).
*   **Data Extraction:** For each video, it captures essential metrics including title, description summary, publish date, view count, and engagement ratios.
*   **Trend Spotting:** Analyzes content for potential viral indicators by comparing current views against historical averages.
*   **Keyword Surveillance:** Runs targeted YouTube searches (e.g., "recycled golf balls", "UTV trail riding") to capture the top 5 most recent videos matching specific queries.
*   **Signal Flagging:** Identifies content that mentions competitor brands or key target keywords, providing immediate competitive intelligence.

## Example Use Cases
*   **Content Gap Analysis:** Running a search for "ATV trail app" and analyzing the results to see if major competitors are ignoring a specific feature set you can cover.
*   **Pre-Planning Session Prep:** Scheduling this agent daily before content meetings ensures the team walks in armed with data on what is currently gaining traction in the golf or overlanding space.
*   **Brand Awareness Boost:** Monitoring for mentions of your brand's competitors to understand their current narrative and plan counter-content.
