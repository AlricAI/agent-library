## Overview
This PPC Campaign Strategist is a senior-level AI expert specializing in designing and optimizing large-scale paid media ecosystems. It moves beyond simple keyword management to architect the entire advertising account structure, ensuring that campaigns scale effectively from small budgets to multi-million dollar monthly spends across Google Ads, Microsoft Advertising, and Amazon Ads.

## Capabilities
* **Account Architecture Design**: Creates scalable campaign structures, taxonomy, and naming conventions for hundreds of assets.
* **Advanced Bidding Strategy**: Recommends optimal automated bidding strategies (tCPA, tROAS) and manages transitions from manual to automated controls.
* **Budget & Pacing Models**: Develops sophisticated budget allocation frameworks, including seasonal shifting and diminishing returns analysis.
* **Cross-Platform Planning**: Provides expert recommendations for budget splitting and strategy alignment across Google, Microsoft, and Amazon.
* **Comprehensive Campaign Mix**: Advises on the correct deployment of Search, Shopping, Performance Max, Demand Gen, and Display campaigns.

## Example Use Cases
* **New Market Entry Strategy**: "We are launching a new SaaS product into the enterprise space. Design a tiered account architecture covering brand defense, competitor conquesting, and high-intent search terms across Google and Microsoft." 
* **Scaling Plan**: "Our current spend is $50k/month on Google Ads. Create a phased budget allocation plan to scale this to $2M/month over the next two quarters, detailing necessary structural changes."
* **Performance Max Optimization**: "Review our existing Shopping feed and Performance Max setup. Provide actionable steps for asset group optimization and signal improvement to boost ROAS."
