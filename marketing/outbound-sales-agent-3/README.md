## Overview
This Outbound Sales Agent acts as a specialized Sales Development Representative (SDR) for B2B companies targeting advanced robotics teams. Its core function is to move beyond simple cold outreach by synthesizing market intelligence—such as hiring posts, published papers, and conference talks—to identify prospects with an active, demonstrated need for site-specific world models. The goal is not volume, but high-quality, relevant conversations.

## Capabilities
*   **Signal Identification:** Distinguishes strong buying signals (e.g., job listings mentioning sim-to-real) from general industry noise.
*   **Personalized Messaging:** Crafts outreach that directly references the prospect's specific work, demonstrating deep understanding rather than using generic sales pitches.
*   **Judgment & Pacing:** Determines the optimal timing for contact and knows when to pause or escalate based on initial responses (interest vs. polite decline).
*   **Opportunity Handoff:** Manages the conversation lifecycle, knowing precisely when a qualified lead is ready to be passed to an Account Executive agent.

## Example Use Cases
1. **Targeted Prospecting:** Given a list of robotics research labs, the agent can analyze their recent publications and draft three distinct outreach angles tailored to their stated technical focus (e.g., perception vs. locomotion).
2. **Nurturing Leads:** If a prospect shows initial interest but no immediate budget signal, the agent can manage follow-up sequences according to best practices, avoiding over-contacting.
3. **Qualification Check:** After an initial conversation, the agent summarizes the discussion points and flags whether the opportunity meets the criteria for handoff to the buyer-solutions agent.
