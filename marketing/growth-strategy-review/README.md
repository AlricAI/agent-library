## Overview
This agent acts as a strategic 'Heartbeat' monitor for growth initiatives. It synthesizes disparate data sources—including live analytics, ongoing experiments, research findings, and supply chain readiness signals—to provide a single, actionable assessment of the current operational health.

Its primary function is to prevent resource drain by identifying low-signal work and ensuring that all proposed next moves directly improve a measurable bottleneck in the core business funnel or supply loop.

## Capabilities
*   **Signal Aggregation:** Consolidates data from live analytics, open experiments, research outputs, and supply readiness reports.
*   **Bottleneck Identification:** Pinpoints the most critical next action required to improve a real-world conversion funnel or resolve a supply constraint.
*   **Prioritization & Scoping:** Translates high-level priorities into explicit, actionable downstream issues and necessary program updates.
*   **Resource Pruning:** Proactively identifies and recommends cutting non-core growth loops to maintain focus on critical paths.
*   **Cadence Management:** Provides structured review cycles (Daily/Weekly) for scanning anomalies, validating experiment outcomes against operational reality, and rebalancing the workload across specialized agents.

## Example Use Cases
*   **Funnel Stagnation:** When top-of-funnel interest is high but qualified demand or supply lags, this agent flags the mismatch and suggests targeted interventions (e.g., focusing on instrumentation gaps).
*   **Experiment Overload:** If multiple experiments are running that improve local metrics but degrade overall product truth or increase operational load, the agent recommends pausing non-core tests.
*   **Weekly Planning:** During weekly reviews, it ensures that specialized agents (like `analytics-agent` and `market-intel-agent`) are correctly weighted based on current business needs, preventing resource drift.
