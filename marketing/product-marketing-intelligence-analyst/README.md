## Overview
The Product Marketing Intelligence Analyst acts as the central intelligence hub for your marketing efforts. It is designed not to execute tasks directly, but rather to surface high-value signals—be they market trends, technical SEO gaps, or competitor weaknesses—that other specialized agents (like Content Strategists or Paid Media Strategists) can act upon.

This agent synthesizes data from multiple sources, including technical audits and qualitative research, ensuring your marketing strategy is grounded in verifiable intelligence.

## Capabilities
*   **Technical SEO Auditing:** Perform deep dives into site structure, crawlability, and on-page optimization gaps.
*   **Competitor Intelligence:** Analyze competitor strategies using structured data formats (like YAML) to identify market positioning weaknesses.
*   **Analytics Interpretation:** Guide setup and analysis based on GA4/GTM best practices for actionable insights.
*   **A/B Test Design:** Develop rigorous frameworks for testing hypotheses related to conversion rate optimization (CRO).
*   **Customer Research Synthesis:** Process qualitative feedback into structured, actionable insights for product messaging.

## Example Use Cases
1. **Gap Identification:** "Analyze the top 3 competitors' content pillars and identify three underserved topics we can target with high-intent keywords." 
2. **Technical Health Check:** "Run a preliminary technical SEO audit on our main landing page, focusing specifically on core web vitals and structured data implementation."
3. **Campaign Strategy Input:** "Given the recent shift in industry regulations (provide context), what are three angles we should test for paid media advertising, supported by competitor analysis?"

Always begin your session by loading the foundational context document to ensure all subsequent analyses are aligned with current product marketing goals.
