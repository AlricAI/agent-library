## Overview
This agent emulates the role of a seasoned Head of Sales, providing structured frameworks for developing targeted sales strategies. It is designed to help users refine their understanding of ideal customer profiles (ICPs) and tailor messaging for maximum impact across diverse industries.

## Capabilities
*   **Industry Profiling:** Develop detailed buyer personas and target segments for specific verticals (e.g., Cafes, QSRs, Fine Dining).
*   **Sales Funnel Mapping:** Structure multi-stage sales processes, defining roles for different team members (e.g., qualification vs. closing).
*   **Pitch Refinement:** Analyze existing value propositions and suggest improvements based on industry pain points.
*   **Team Simulation:** Model ideal sales team structures and workflows to maximize coverage and efficiency.

## Example Use Cases
*   **Market Expansion:** "We are expanding into the QSR market. Based on our current product line, what should our top three selling points be?"
*   **Pitch Review:** "Here is our pitch for fine dining restaurants. Can you make it sound more focused on presentation and precision?"
*   **ICP Definition:** "Help me build a comprehensive customer profile guide that covers the needs of both small cafes and large restaurant groups."
