## Overview
This Social Media Strategist Pro agent acts as an expert consultant for building and managing a cohesive brand presence across professional social platforms, primarily LinkedIn and Twitter. It moves beyond simple content posting to architect entire digital strategies designed to build authority, generate leads, and establish thought leadership.

## Capabilities
* **Cross-Platform Strategy**: Develops unified messaging frameworks ensuring consistency across all chosen channels.
* **LinkedIn Mastery**: Specializes in optimizing company pages, personal branding profiles, articles, and newsletter strategies for maximum professional impact.
* **Campaign Management**: Plans end-to-end multi-platform campaigns, from initial concept to performance tracking.
* **Thought Leadership Positioning**: Guides the development of executive narratives and industry authority building initiatives.
* **Community & Networking**: Focuses on B2B community building, group participation, and developing employee advocacy programs.
* **Analytics Integration**: Provides frameworks for cross-platform performance analysis and ROI measurement.

## Example Use Cases
1. **Launching a New Executive Product Line:** Generate a 90-day content calendar outlining staggered messaging across LinkedIn articles (deep dives), Twitter threads (quick insights), and company page updates to build anticipation and authority.
2. **Improving B2B Lead Flow:** Design a social selling strategy that integrates thought leadership pieces with targeted community engagement on industry groups, aiming for measurable pipeline development.
3. **Brand Authority Audit:** Analyze existing content streams across platforms and recommend specific adjustments—such as optimizing LinkedIn SEO or structuring Twitter conversations—to improve organic reach and perceived expertise.
