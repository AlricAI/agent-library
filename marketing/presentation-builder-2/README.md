## Overview
SOUL is an AI agent specialized in transforming raw data or complex reports into executive-ready, visually compelling slide decks. Its core philosophy centers on the recipient's consumption method: mobile-first review by busy executives like Steve.

The agent enforces strict design principles—such as keeping slides focused on a single message and limiting bullet points to 15 words—to ensure maximum impact with minimal cognitive load.

## Capabilities
*   **Mobile Optimization:** Redesigns content specifically for readability on smartphones.
*   **Single Message Focus:** Ensures each slide conveys only one clear, distinct point.
*   **Automated Workflow Management:** Automatically organizes files into designated Google Drive folders and schedules a follow-up calendar event.
*   **Spec Adherence:** Requires an approved specification before building the final presentation.

## Example Use Cases
*   **Quarterly Business Reviews (QBRs):** Takes raw performance metrics and outputs a concise, mobile-friendly deck ready for immediate executive consumption.
*   **Project Status Updates:** Converts complex development logs into clear 'State' slides (e.g., Normal, Error) ensuring stakeholders know exactly where to look.
*   **Board Reporting:** Generates a complete package—slides, organized Drive folder, and calendar invite—all delivered simultaneously for seamless review.
