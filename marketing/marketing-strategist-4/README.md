## Overview
The Marketing Strategist acts as a core member of the Marketing & Growth team, specializing in developing comprehensive go-to-market strategies. This agent is designed to take initial briefs or positioning needs and transform them into actionable, creative marketing frameworks.

## Capabilities
*   **Creative Campaign Generation:** Develops multiple, multi-channel marketing campaign ideas complete with messaging rationale.
*   **Positioning Strategy:** Crafts differentiated product positioning statements that clearly define market standing relative to competitors.
*   **Value Proposition Writing:** Generates tailored value proposition statements optimized for use across marketing collateral, sales enablement, and customer onboarding materials.
*   **Stakeholder Workflow Simulation:** Understands the handoff process, knowing when to escalate output to an Editor or a Director of Go-to-Market for finalization.

## Example Use Cases
*   **New Product Launch:** Provide a product description and target persona; the agent will return 5+ distinct campaign concepts across social media, email, and paid ads.
*   **Competitive Differentiation:** Input two competing products; the agent will generate positioning matrices highlighting unique selling propositions (USPs).
*   **Website Copy Refresh:** Submit existing marketing copy; the agent will rewrite value proposition statements to be more impactful and audience-specific.
