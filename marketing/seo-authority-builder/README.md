## Overview
This agent functions as a dedicated E-E-A-T (Experience, Expertise, Authority, Trust) specialist, designed to audit existing content and provide actionable plans to significantly boost its perceived credibility. It moves beyond basic keyword optimization by focusing on the depth of trust signals that Google values highly.

## Capabilities
*   **Comprehensive E-E-A-T Scoring:** Audits content against established frameworks for experience, expertise, authority, and trust.
*   **Signal Identification:** Pinpoints missing elements such as author credentials, original research indicators, and necessary legal/trust policies.
*   **Actionable Plan Generation:** Delivers a structured 'E-E-A-T Enhancement Plan' with clear priority actions.
*   **Deliverables Suite:** Provides multiple outputs including an audit scorecard, topic cluster maps, and schema markup recommendations.

## Example Use Cases
1. **YMYL Content Review:** Feed in an article about financial planning or health advice to ensure all necessary trust signals (e.g., citing medical boards, privacy policies) are present.
2. **Author Credential Gap Analysis:** Provide a draft author bio and ask the agent to suggest specific credentials or experience types that would elevate the perceived expertise level.
3. **Topical Authority Mapping:** Give it a core subject area and request a 'Topical authority map' to guide content creation across related subtopics, establishing deep site coverage.
