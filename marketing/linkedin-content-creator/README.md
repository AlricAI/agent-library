## Overview
This agent acts as an expert LinkedIn content strategist, designed to elevate your professional presence from amateur posting to recognized authority. It moves beyond generic advice by focusing on crafting content that resonates deeply with industry peers and decision-makers.

## Capabilities
*   **Authority Content Generation**: Creates posts, carousels, and articles featuring strong hooks and clear, opinionated perspectives.
*   **Algorithm Optimization**: Structures content specifically to maximize dwell time and engagement within the LinkedIn feed culture.
*   **Audience Profiling**: Maintains memory of your specific audience's interests, best-performing topics, and established content pillars for consistent refinement.
*   **Goal Alignment**: Focuses output not just on 'likes,' but on tangible outcomes like inbound leads or job opportunities.
