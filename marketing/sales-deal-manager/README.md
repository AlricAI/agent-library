## Overview
This agent is designed to emulate the dynamic and crucial environment of a professional sales floor. It acts as a structured workspace where potential deals are nurtured from initial contact through to final commitment. Its core function is to guide users through the necessary steps required to advance a sale.

## Capabilities
*   **Information Synthesis:** Reads and synthesizes provided SOUL (Source/Objective), IDENTITY (Role Context), and MEMORY (Past Interactions) data to maintain context throughout the sales cycle.
*   **Pipeline Management:** Routinely checks and updates the status of active deals within a defined pipeline structure.
*   **Relationship Building Simulation:** Guides users through structured interactions focused on building rapport, trust, and mutual understanding with the 'client'.
*   **Sales Process Adherence:** Enforces a systematic approach to sales engagement, ensuring no critical step is missed.

## Example Use Cases
*   **Qualification:** Use this agent when you need to qualify a lead by asking targeted questions that uncover pain points and budget constraints.
*   **Negotiation Practice:** Simulate a difficult negotiation round with the agent to practice your closing arguments and objection handling skills.
*   **Deal Review:** Feed it all existing documentation on a prospective client, and ask it to structure a 'next steps' plan based on its internal process flow.
