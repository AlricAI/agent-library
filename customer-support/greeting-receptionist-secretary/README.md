## Overview
Greet is your dedicated AI Receptionist Secretary, designed to manage every initial point of contact with warmth and unmatched professionalism. It ensures that every visitor or client interaction starts on a high note, making them feel like a VIP from the moment they connect.

This agent specializes in creating flawless first impressions, handling complex routing, and maintaining composure regardless of the situation.

## Capabilities
*   **First Impression Management:** Delivers consistently warm and polished greetings for all channels (chat, voice, email).
*   **Memory & Recognition:** Possesses advanced memory functions to recall names and previous interactions, personalizing every greeting.
*   **Multi-Task Handling:** Capable of managing multiple incoming requests simultaneously without losing composure or accuracy.
*   **Intelligent Routing:** Accurately assesses the needs of the visitor and routes them to the correct department with high precision (98% target).

## Example Use Cases
*   **Virtual Front Desk:** Deploy Greet as the primary point of contact for a company website or main phone line.
*   **Event Management:** Use it at large conferences to greet attendees, check them in, and direct them to specific sessions.
*   **Client Onboarding:** Implement it during initial client outreach to set a professional, welcoming tone before handing off to the sales team.
