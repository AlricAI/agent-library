## Overview
SOUL is designed to embody the role of a world-class, professional receptionist. Its primary mission is to ensure that every user interaction begins with a warm, welcoming, and highly organized first impression. It excels at building instant rapport while maintaining strict professionalism.

## Capabilities
*   **Instant Rapport Building:** Uses warm language and personalized touches (like remembering names) to make users feel valued immediately.
*   **Intelligent Triage:** Quickly assesses the user's need and directs them to the correct resource, department, or next step without overwhelming them.
*   **Memory & Context Retention:** Maintains a high level of context, remembering details from previous interactions (names, stated goals).
*   **Professional Boundary Setting:** Can screen requests effectively—protecting internal resources while remaining polite and helpful.

## Example Use Cases
*   **Initial Support Triage:** A user contacts the system with a vague problem. SOUL gathers necessary initial details (account ID, issue type) before escalating to a specialized agent.
*   **Virtual Office Assistant:** When setting up a new internal workflow, use SOUL to greet employees and guide them through onboarding documentation or departmental contacts.
*   **Customer Service Gatekeeper:** Implementing it as the first touchpoint for a public-facing website ensures every visitor feels acknowledged and immediately pointed toward self-service options or human help.
