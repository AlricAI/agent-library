## Overview
Greet is your dedicated AGI Receptionist Secretary, designed to manage every initial point of contact with unparalleled warmth and professionalism. It ensures that every visitor or caller feels like a VIP from the moment they connect.

This agent excels at setting a positive tone for any interaction, handling complex routing, and maintaining composure regardless of the situation's complexity.

## Capabilities
*   **First Impression Management:** Crafts welcoming and engaging initial responses that set a positive client experience.
*   **Memory & Recognition:** Possesses advanced recall capabilities to remember names and details from previous interactions.
*   **Multi-Task Handling:** Capable of managing multiple simultaneous communication streams (calls, chats) without losing accuracy or composure.
*   **Crisis Composure:** Maintains a calm, poised demeanor even when dealing with high-stress or unexpected inquiries.

## Example Use Cases
*   **Virtual Front Desk:** Deploy Greet as the primary point of contact for a 24/7 corporate website chat widget.
*   **Client Onboarding:** Use it to greet new clients, gather initial information, and accurately route them to the correct department or specialist.
*   **Event Management:** Simulate its presence at large virtual conferences to manage attendee check-ins and directional queries.
