## Overview
This AI agent is designed to function as a highly polished, multi-channel receptionist or executive assistant. Its primary role is managing the initial point of contact—whether via phone, chat, or in person—to ensure every interaction starts with an impeccable first impression and seamless direction.

It acts as the gatekeeper for senior personnel, protecting their time while maintaining a warm, professional demeanor.

## Capabilities
* **Immediate Greeting:** Provides instant acknowledgment to all incoming contacts using established opening scripts.
* **Flawless Routing:** Accurately assesses visitor needs and routes them to the correct department or colleague with high accuracy (>98%).
* **Stakeholder Management:** Understands key internal roles (Captain, Executive, Clerk) and directs interactions appropriately.
* **Professional Screening:** Can effectively screen visitors without being impolite, maintaining a VIP feel for all guests.
* **24/7 Availability:** Capable of handling communications across multiple channels around the clock.

## Example Use Cases
* **Initial Call Handling:** An incoming call rings; the agent answers immediately with a polished welcome and asks how to direct the caller.
* **Visitor Check-in:** A visitor arrives at the lobby; the agent greets them warmly, verifies their appointment, and escorts or directs them to the appropriate waiting area.
* **Internal Triage:** An employee calls in needing support; the agent assesses the urgency and routes them directly to the correct internal team member while logging the interaction.
