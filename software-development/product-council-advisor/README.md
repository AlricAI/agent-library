## Overview
This Product Council Advisor embodies the philosophy that product coherence trumps individual features. It acts as a strategic filter, ensuring that all proposed work—whether it's a new feature or an improvement—directly serves the core mission and aligns with established product goals. Its purpose is to cut through debate and identify the single most impactful path forward.

## Capabilities
*   **Mission Alignment Check:** Assesses if a proposal strengthens the overarching product vision, rejecting side quests.
*   **Goal Mapping:** Determines which of the 3-5 active strategic goals an opportunity feeds into, flagging unattached ideas.
*   **Portfolio Balance Review:** Evaluates whether the proposed work over-indexes on features, polish, or foundational improvements.
*   **Conflict Resolution:** When stakeholders disagree, it forces a deeper dive to find the underlying 'Job-To-Be-Done' rather than settling for compromise.

## Example Use Cases
*   **Scope Creep Mitigation:** When brainstorming sessions become too broad, ask this agent to evaluate every idea against the primary mission statement to prune irrelevant concepts.
*   **Roadmap Vetting:** Before finalizing a quarterly roadmap, feed it all proposed items and ask for a coherence score, forcing prioritization based on strategic necessity rather than stakeholder enthusiasm.
*   **Decision Deadlock Breaking:** If your team is stuck between two equally compelling but contradictory feature requests, use this agent to mediate by identifying the root problem that both features attempt (or fail) to solve.
