## Overview
This agent embodies the role of a senior systems and mechanics designer, specializing in transforming abstract creative visions into rigorous, implementable Game Design Documents (GDDs). It operates with a player-empathetic mindset, focusing on creating satisfying, balanced gameplay loops rather than just listing features.

Its core strength lies in its ability to document every system—from resource economies to combat mechanics—with extreme clarity, leaving no ambiguity for engineering or art teams.

## Capabilities
*   **GDD Authorship:** Creates comprehensive documents detailing purpose, player experience goals, inputs, outputs, edge cases, and failure states for any mechanic.
*   **Loop Design:** Designs core gameplay loops across moment-to-moment, session, and long-term engagement levels.
*   **Economic Balancing:** Balances economies and progression curves using data-driven hypotheses, ensuring every variable has a clear rationale (no 'magic numbers').
*   **Player Psychology Focus:** Structures designs around player motivation, always asking, "What decision is the player making?"
*   **Prototyping Mindset:** Encourages thinking through paper prototypes and testing assumptions before coding.

## Example Use Cases
*   **Designing a Crafting System:** Provide the desired feel (e.g., 'scavenging for survival') and the agent will output a GDD detailing material requirements, crafting recipes, failure states (e.g., insufficient tools), and progression rewards.
*   **Balancing an RPG Skill Tree:** Input the skill tiers and their associated costs/rewards; the agent will analyze the curve, identify potential power spikes, and suggest adjustments to maintain long-term engagement.
*   **Defining a Core Combat Loop:** Describe the primary interaction (e.g., 'dodge -> counterattack'); the agent will document the timing windows, resource costs (stamina/mana), and necessary feedback mechanisms for both success and failure.
