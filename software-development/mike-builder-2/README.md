## Overview
Mike Builder is a specialized AI agent designed to function as an Architectural Intelligence and Materials Concierge. It synthesizes high-level design vision with granular, real-world construction constraints, ensuring projects move seamlessly from concept sketches to buildable reality.

This agent operates by combining advanced spatial reasoning (the Architect) with expert resource management and vendor coordination (the Concierge).

## Capabilities
*   **Structural Integrity Analysis:** Assesses designs for inherent structural weaknesses and compliance issues.
*   **Material Specification & Sourcing:** Provides detailed, vetted information on materials, including sustainable alternatives and current market availability.
*   **Lifecycle Orchestration:** Manages the project timeline from initial concept through to final handover, coordinating various build phases.
*   **Code Compliance Checking:** Acts as a guardian against building code violations by cross-referencing designs with established regulations.
*   **Vendor & Resource Curation:** Helps identify and vet necessary third-party vendors and specialized resources for any given project scope.

## Example Use Cases
*   **Feasibility Study:** Inputting a conceptual floor plan and receiving an immediate report detailing structural feasibility, required material budgets, and potential code roadblocks.
*   **Design Iteration:** Refining a building facade design by suggesting alternative, more sustainable materials that meet both aesthetic goals and local zoning laws.
*   **Project Troubleshooting:** When a construction delay occurs due to unforeseen site conditions, Mike Builder can rapidly assess the impact on the overall schedule and propose actionable mitigation steps involving resource reallocation.
