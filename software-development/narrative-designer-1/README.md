## Overview
Narrative Story Architect is an advanced AI agent specialized in treating game narrative not as supplementary content, but as a core, interactive system. It moves beyond simple scriptwriting to architect the underlying mechanics of story—the choices, consequences, and lore structures that players actively inhabit.

This agent ensures that every piece of dialogue, every branching path, and every environmental detail feels organic, consequential, and deeply integrated with the gameplay loop.

## Capabilities
*   **Systemic Narrative Design:** Designs complex, weighted branching narratives suitable for RPGs, open-world games, and roguelikes.
*   **Authentic Dialogue Generation:** Writes dialogue that passes a rigorous 'real person' test, maintaining consistent character voice pillars (vocabulary, rhythm) while avoiding exposition dumps.
*   **Lore Architecture:** Builds deep, rewarding lore structures that encourage player curiosity through environmental storytelling rather than explicit text dumps.
*   **Player Agency Focus:** Designs choices where consequences are tangible and meaningful, ensuring the player feels authorship over their story arc.
*   **Technical Documentation:** Outputs narrative designs in a structured format suitable for direct implementation by game engine engineers, preserving authorial intent.

## Example Use Cases
1. **Designing a Faction Reputation System:** The agent can map out how specific dialogue choices (e.g., siding with faction A vs. B) will trigger cascading story events and alter the world state across multiple zones.
2. **Creating Environmental Storytelling Beats:** Given a location description, it can suggest subtle narrative elements—a misplaced item, graffiti, or overheard conversation snippet—that imply a backstory without needing an in-game journal entry.
3. **Developing Character Arcs:** It can outline multi-stage character development paths, ensuring that the dialogue written at Act III reflects the emotional weight and changes established during early gameplay interactions.
