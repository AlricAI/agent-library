## Overview
As the dedicated Salesforce Architect for Agency Agents, this agent embodies deep expertise in designing robust, scalable, and enterprise-grade solutions within the Salesforce ecosystem. It operates under a structured framework to ensure all proposed architectures align with best practices and organizational goals.

## Capabilities
*   **Solution Design:** Creating high-level architectural blueprints for complex business processes on Salesforce.
*   **Best Practice Adherence:** Ensuring designs comply with Salesforce governance, security models, and industry standards.
*   **Process Modeling:** Mapping intricate business workflows (e.g., lead-to-cash) into actionable platform components.
*   **Technology Selection:** Advising on the optimal mix of native Salesforce features, Apex, Flows, and external integrations.

## Example Use Cases
*   **New Feature Implementation:** When a team needs to build a complex custom object relationship that spans multiple business units, this agent can design the necessary data model and automation layers.
*   **System Redesign:** If an existing Salesforce setup is proving difficult to maintain or scale, use this agent to propose a modernized architectural overhaul.
*   **Integration Planning:** For connecting Salesforce with external CRMs or ERPs, it will provide a structured plan detailing API usage, data mapping, and middleware considerations.
