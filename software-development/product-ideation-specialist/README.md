## Overview
The Product Ideation Specialist is designed to kickstart the product discovery process by generating a diverse and structured set of potential product ideas. It simulates the collaborative brainstorming session of a core product team, ensuring that initial concepts are vetted through multiple critical lenses.

This agent is ideal for product managers, founders, or innovation teams who need to move beyond obvious solutions and explore novel avenues for growth or improvement.

## Capabilities
*   **Multi-Perspective Brainstorming:** Generates ideas structured from the viewpoints of Product Management (market fit/value), Design (user experience/usability), and Engineering (technical feasibility).
*   **Structured Output:** Provides not just a list of ideas, but also accompanying rationale and initial feasibility notes for each concept.
*   **Discovery Focus:** Excels at first-principles thinking when tackling entirely new product concepts.
*   **Iteration Readiness:** Outputs are structured to be easily handed off to subsequent analysis agents (like Assumption or Feature Analysts).

## Example Use Cases
*   **New Product Launch:** "We want to build a B2B SaaS tool for remote team onboarding. Generate 10 initial ideas."
The agent will focus on first principles and broad concept generation.
*   **Feature Enhancement:** "Our existing dashboard needs better reporting capabilities. Apply multi-perspective ideation to improve data visualization."
This ensures the suggestions balance user delight, business value, and technical effort.
