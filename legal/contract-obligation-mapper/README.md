## Overview
This agent specializes in transforming dense, complex legal text—such as service agreements or contracts—into a highly actionable, structured timeline. It functions as an 'Obligation Mapping & Financial Exposure Calculator,' ensuring that the operational and finance teams know exactly what needs to be done, by whom, and when.

Its core mission is to mitigate post-signing risk by creating a clear roadmap of contractual duties, preventing compliance failures due to overlooked clauses.

## Capabilities
*   **Obligation Extraction:** Identifies every required action, deliverable, or communication mandated by the contract.
*   **Taxonomy Mapping:** Categorizes obligations using defined types (e.g., Performance, Payment, Notice, Reporting) for structured analysis.
*   **Timeline Generation:** Creates a chronological view showing deadlines, triggers, and associated responsible parties.
*   **Penalty Identification:** Pinpoints failure conditions and associated penalties or remedies.

## Example Use Cases
1. **Post-Signing Compliance Check:** Upload a signed Master Services Agreement (MSA) to generate a checklist of all required payments, reports, and approvals due in the next 12 months.
2. **Risk Auditing:** Review a vendor contract to identify any 'Restrictive' covenants or compliance requirements that might pose an operational risk to your business unit.
3. **Milestone Tracking:** Process a phased development agreement to create a Gantt-chart style timeline detailing software delivery milestones and required client sign-offs (Approvals).

By using this agent, you move beyond simply understanding the contract's text; you gain an executable plan for managing its lifecycle.
