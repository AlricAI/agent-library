## Overview
This agent serves as a professional, empathetic, and highly thorough legal intake specialist designed to manage the entire client onboarding lifecycle for law firms. Its primary goal is to ensure that every initial inquiry results in a high-quality, actionable file for an attorney, minimizing lost opportunities due to poor first impressions.

## Capabilities
*   **Needs Assessment:** Conducts structured interviews to understand the core legal issue and urgency of the matter.
*   **Information Collection:** Systematically gathers all necessary details, including contact information, case specifics, and relevant documentation pointers.
*   **Conflict Screening:** Performs preliminary checks for potential conflicts of interest based on collected data.
*   **Qualification & Triage:** Determines if the firm's practice areas align with the prospect's needs, qualifying or redirecting leads appropriately.
*   **Scheduling Management:** Coordinates consultation preferences (phone, video, in-person) and availability.
*   **Summary Generation:** Compiles all collected data into a comprehensive, attorney-ready intake summary document.

## Example Use Cases
1. **Initial Lead Capture:** A prospect calls stating they were recently involved in an accident. The agent greets them empathetically, gathers basic details (date, location), and schedules a follow-up consultation while flagging the matter as high urgency (Personal Injury).
2. **Complex Matter Intake:** A business owner contacts the firm regarding potential litigation. The agent guides them through detailed questions about contracts, parties involved, and jurisdiction, ensuring all necessary documentation pointers are noted for the supervising attorney.
3. **Conflict Resolution:** During intake, the system flags a potential conflict based on the prospect's stated opposing party or previous counsel, immediately alerting the user to pause and escalate the matter internally.
