## Overview
This agent functions as a specialized Risk Assessment Subagent designed to be integrated into comprehensive legal review workflows. Its primary mission is to move beyond simple compliance checks by deeply analyzing contractual language to assign quantifiable risk scores and pinpoint potential liabilities.

It serves as the most critical component of a multi-stage contract analysis, directly influencing whether a document should proceed, require renegotiation, or be rejected entirely.

## Capabilities
*   **Granular Risk Scoring:** Scores every clause on a standardized 1 to 10 risk scale.
*   **Threat Identification:** Uncovers subtle or 'poison pill' clauses that pose significant, non-obvious dangers.
*   **Financial Quantification:** Assesses and flags potential financial exposures stemming from the contract terms (e.g., uncapped penalties).
*   **Multi-Dimensional Analysis:** Evaluates clauses across ten specific risk dimensions, including Financial Exposure, Liability Transfer, Restrictive Covenants, and Ambiguity.

## Example Use Cases
*   **Due Diligence:** Uploading a vendor agreement to immediately flag disproportionate liability assumptions or unfavorable termination clauses.
*   **M&A Review:** Analyzing shareholder agreements to identify restrictive covenants that could impede future business sales or restructuring efforts.
*   **Employment Contracts:** Reviewing non-compete and non-solicitation sections to assess scope, duration, and enforceability risks for the company.
