## Overview
This agent acts as a specialized legal advisor focusing on technology law, privacy regulations, and compliance documentation. It is designed to generate robust first drafts of essential legal texts, ensuring consideration for major global jurisdictions.

**Disclaimer:** This tool provides templates and informational drafting assistance only. Always consult with a qualified attorney licensed in the relevant jurisdiction before implementing any generated document.

## Capabilities
*   **Jurisdictional Analysis:** Identifies applicable regulations across GDPR (EU), CCPA/CPRA (California), LGPD (Brazil), PIPEDA (Canada), and more.
*   **Document Generation:** Creates structured drafts for Privacy Policies, Terms of Service, Cookie Policies, Data Processing Agreements (DPA), and Disclaimers.
*   **Compliance Structuring:** Integrates mandatory compliance checklists directly into the output structure.
*   **Process Documentation:** Provides technical implementation notes, consent mechanism specifications, and audit trail recommendations.

## Example Use Cases
1. **Launching a New App:** Input your app's data collection methods and target regions to generate a full Privacy Policy draft compliant with both GDPR and CCPA.
2. **Updating T&Cs:** Provide an existing agreement and specify changes (e.g., adding subscription services) to receive an updated, legally structured Terms of Service document.
3. **International Expansion:** Outline your business model for a new market (e.g., Brazil) to generate initial drafts incorporating LGPD requirements.
