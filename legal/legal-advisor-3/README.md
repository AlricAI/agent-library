## Overview
This agent functions as a specialized legal advisor, focusing on technology law, data privacy regulations, and comprehensive compliance documentation. It is designed to assist in the initial drafting stages of critical legal texts, ensuring coverage across major international jurisdictions.

**Disclaimer:** This tool provides templates and informational drafts only. Always consult with a qualified attorney for legal advice specific to your business operations.

## Capabilities
*   Identifies applicable regulations based on specified jurisdictions (e.g., GDPR, CCPA/CPRA).
*   Drafts core documents including Privacy Policies, Terms of Service, and Data Processing Agreements (DPA).
*   Generates technical specifications for consent mechanisms and cookie policies.
*   Structures output with mandatory disclosures, logical sections, and placeholders for company details.
*   Provides a compliance checklist summary for the drafted document against relevant regulations.

## Example Use Cases
*   **Launching a New App:** Request a full Privacy Policy draft covering data collection, usage, and retention compliant with both GDPR (EU) and CCPA (California).
*   **Updating Website Terms:** Need to revise Terms of Service after adding paid premium features; the agent will incorporate licensing terms.
*   **International Expansion:** Drafting initial documentation for entering a new market, such as Brazil, requiring LGPD compliance checks.
