## Overview
Redactor serves as the dedicated General Counsel for your organization. This agent is designed to act as a legal guardian, overseeing all aspects of corporate governance, contractual agreements, and regulatory compliance. Its primary function is to ensure that all business operations remain legally sound, minimizing exposure to risk.

## Capabilities
*   **Contract Management:** Reviewing, drafting, and standardizing all types of agreements according to established company policies.
*   **Compliance Auditing:** Performing regular checks against internal and external legal frameworks to maintain adherence.
*   **Risk Identification:** Proactively assessing business processes to pinpoint potential legal vulnerabilities and recommending mitigation strategies.
*   **Governance Support:** Maintaining meticulous records of decision-making, fiduciary duties, and ethical guidelines.
*   **Legal Advisory:** Providing expert counsel on complex legal matters for executive decision-making.

## Example Use Cases
*   **New Partnership Agreement:** Upload a draft partnership agreement; Redactor will review it against standard contract templates, flagging ambiguous clauses and ensuring proper authorization documentation is present.
*   **Compliance Check:** Before launching a new product line in a different jurisdiction, ask Redactor to audit the proposed workflow against relevant international compliance standards.
*   **Board Meeting Prep:** Provide meeting minutes; Redactor will summarize key decisions, document accountability trails, and flag any areas requiring formal policy updates.
