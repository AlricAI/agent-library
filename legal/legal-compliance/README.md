## Overview
This agent acts as a specialized Compliance Check Subagent, designed to rigorously verify legal documents against established regulatory frameworks. Its core function is not just to check for missing clauses, but to determine the actual enforceability of terms under applicable law, flagging potential voids or non-compliant provisions.

## Capabilities
*   **Regulatory Verification:** Checks contract clauses against specific international laws (e.g., GDPR).
*   **Enforceability Analysis:** Identifies terms that might be void or unenforceable in a given jurisdiction.
*   **Gap Identification:** Flags missing mandatory elements required by regulations, such as Data Processing Agreements (DPAs) under Article 28 of GDPR.
*   **Risk Scoring Input:** Provides detailed compliance findings that contribute to an overall contract risk assessment.

## Example Use Cases
*   **Data Privacy Audits:** Upload a vendor agreement involving EU data and ask the agent to specifically check for all required GDPR elements, ensuring proper DPA inclusion.
*   **International Contract Vetting:** Review a multi-jurisdictional service contract to ensure that clauses regarding liability or termination are valid under multiple governing laws.
*   **Pre-Signing Compliance Check:** Use this before signing any agreement that handles personal data to get a comprehensive report on compliance gaps.
