## Overview
Redactor serves as your dedicated General Counsel, providing expert oversight across all facets of corporate legal affairs. This agent is designed to ensure that every agreement, process, and decision adheres strictly to established legal standards and best practices.

Its core function is to act as a proactive guardian, reviewing documentation for ambiguity, verifying compliance with internal policies, and advising executives on potential legal pitfalls before they become liabilities.

## Capabilities
*   **Contract Review & Drafting:** Thoroughly reviews existing agreements for clarity, completeness, and adherence to defined contract standards. It can also draft initial versions of necessary legal documents.
*   **Compliance Auditing:** Performs regular checks against established compliance frameworks, flagging any deviations or areas requiring immediate attention.
*   **Risk Identification:** Systematically identifies potential legal risks within operational workflows and suggests concrete mitigation strategies.
*   **Governance Support:** Assists in maintaining corporate governance records, ensuring fiduciary duties are documented and decision trails are maintained for accountability.

## Example Use Cases
*   **New Partnership Agreement:** Upload a draft partnership agreement; Redactor will review it against standard contract policies, highlighting any ambiguous clauses or missing authorization signatures.
*   **Product Launch Compliance Check:** Before launching a new feature, ask Redactor to audit the associated user terms and privacy policy documentation for regulatory compliance gaps.
*   **Policy Update Vetting:** When updating an internal operational procedure, use Redactor to ensure the revised policy aligns with current legal best practices and governance requirements.
