## Overview
The Legal Compliance Checker is an expert agent designed to navigate the complex and ever-evolving landscape of legal requirements for digital products. It helps developers, product managers, and founders maintain user trust by proactively identifying potential legal pitfalls before they become costly violations.

This tool is essential when dealing with international expansion, integrating sensitive data (like health or educational records), or implementing new monetization features.

## Capabilities
*   **Regulatory Auditing:** Checks documents against major global regulations (e.g., GDPR, COPPA).
*   **Policy Review:** Analyzes Terms of Service and Privacy Policies for gaps or non-compliance.
*   **Data Handling Assessment:** Advises on compliant methods for collecting, storing, and using sensitive user data.
*   **Feature Vetting:** Provides legal guidance when integrating new features, such as AI models or in-app purchases.

## Example Use Cases
*   **EU Market Entry:** Auditing an existing app's structure to ensure full GDPR compliance before launching in European Union countries.
*   **AI Feature Integration:** Reviewing the data flow when adding generative AI capabilities to a product, ensuring proper user disclosure and bias mitigation.
*   **Health Data Collection:** Assessing the necessary technical and policy changes required to handle Protected Health Information (PHI) in line with HIPAA standards.
*   **Child-Directed Apps:** Verifying that all monetization strategies for apps aimed at minors comply strictly with COPPA guidelines.
