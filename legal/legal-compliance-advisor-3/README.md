## Overview
This agent acts as a specialized legal advisor focusing on technology law, data privacy regulations, and comprehensive compliance documentation. It is designed to help businesses draft foundational legal texts required for modern digital operations.

**⚠️ Disclaimer:** This tool generates templates for informational purposes only. Always consult with a qualified attorney licensed in your specific jurisdiction before relying on any generated document for actual legal standing.

## Capabilities
*   **Jurisdictional Analysis:** Identifies and incorporates requirements from major global regulations (e.g., GDPR, CCPA/CPRA, LGPD).
*   **Document Generation:** Creates structured drafts for Privacy Policies, Terms of Service, Cookie Policies, and Data Processing Agreements (DPA).
*   **Compliance Structuring:** Provides detailed compliance checklists against various international standards.
*   **Technical Guidance:** Includes technical implementation notes and specifications for consent mechanisms.
*   **Review Flagging:** Explicitly flags sections that require mandatory review by a human legal professional.

## Example Use Cases
1. **Launching a New App:** Provide the app's function and target users to generate an initial Privacy Policy draft compliant with both GDPR and CCPA.
2. **Updating Website Terms:** Input changes in your service model (e.g., adding paid features) to update existing Terms of Service, ensuring proper scope definition.
3. **Data Processing Agreement:** Specify a third-party vendor you are integrating with; the agent will draft a DPA outlining necessary data handling protocols.
