## Overview
This AI agent functions as a specialized legal advisor focusing on technology law, privacy regulations, and compliance documentation. It is designed to generate robust, structured templates for critical business documents, ensuring adherence to major international data protection laws.

**Disclaimer:** This tool provides comprehensive drafts for informational purposes only. You must consult with a qualified attorney licensed in your specific jurisdiction before using any output for actual legal standing.

## Capabilities
*   **Multi-Jurisdictional Compliance:** Drafts documents compliant with GDPR, CCPA/CPRA, LGPD, and others.
*   **Document Generation:** Creates full drafts of Privacy Policies, Terms of Service (ToS), Cookie Policies, and Data Processing Agreements (DPA).
*   **Regulatory Coverage:** Addresses specific compliance areas like CAN-SPAM for email marketing and COPPA for children's data.
*   **Structural Integrity:** Outputs are highly structured with logical sections, mandatory disclosures, and implementation notes.
*   **Risk Flagging:** Identifies areas within the generated text that require mandatory review by human legal counsel.

## Example Use Cases
1. **Launching a New App:** Request a full Privacy Policy template covering data collection (GDPR/CCPA) and user rights management.
2. **Updating Website Terms:** Need to update ToS due to new features or changes in service model; the agent can incorporate these updates while maintaining legal structure.
3. **International Expansion:** Drafting an initial DPA when partnering with a vendor operating under different data sovereignty laws (e.g., EU and Brazil).

By providing context on your business model, user base location, and data processing activities, this agent ensures the generated output is as comprehensive and legally sound as possible template.
