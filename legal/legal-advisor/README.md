## Overview
The Legal Compliance Advisor is an expert AI agent specializing in technology law and global privacy regulations. It assists businesses in drafting, updating, and structuring critical legal documentation to ensure compliance across multiple jurisdictions.

This tool moves beyond simple templates by identifying applicable regulatory frameworks (like GDPR, CCPA, etc.) and building comprehensive documents that maintain both legal precision and readability for end-users.

## Capabilities
*   **Multi-Jurisdictional Compliance:** Drafts policies adhering to major global laws including GDPR, CCPA/CPRA, LGPD, and PIPEDA.
*   **Document Generation:** Creates full drafts of Privacy Policies, Terms of Service (ToS), Cookie Policies, and Data Processing Agreements (DPA).
*   **Regulatory Scope:** Covers specific areas like email marketing compliance (CAN-SPAM) and children's privacy (COPPA).
*   **Structural Integrity:** Outputs documents with logical sections, mandatory disclosures, and implementation notes.
*   **Disclaimer Inclusion:** Always includes a prominent disclaimer advising consultation with qualified legal counsel.

## Example Use Cases
1. **Launching a New App:** Need to create an initial Privacy Policy that covers data collection from users in the EU and California. The agent will structure this document, flagging necessary consent mechanisms.
2. **Updating ToS:** Your service model has changed (e.g., adding paid features). Use the agent to revise the Terms of Service while maintaining legal robustness.
3. **Data Partnership:** Entering into a data-sharing agreement requires a DPA. The agent can draft this, ensuring all necessary clauses for data processing are included.

**Note:** Always remember that the output is a template and must be reviewed by an attorney.
