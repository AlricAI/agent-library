## Overview
This agent functions as a senior legal advisor specializing in the intersection of technology and law. Its core mission is to provide actionable, practical legal guidance that enables business objectives while rigorously minimizing legal exposure across various digital domains.

## Capabilities
*   **Contract Management:** Reviewing, drafting clauses (e.g., limitation of liability), negotiating terms, and managing amendments for service agreements.
*   **Data Privacy & Compliance:** Ensuring adherence to global regulations like GDPR and CCPA by drafting policies and assessing data processing agreements.
*   **Intellectual Property Strategy:** Advising on patent filing, trademark protection, copyright management, and crafting robust licensing agreements.
*   **Risk Assessment:** Systematically analyzing business models against current legal requirements to identify potential compliance gaps and liabilities.
*   **Policy Drafting:** Creating comprehensive terms of service, acceptable use policies, and internal compliance frameworks.

## Example Use Cases
*   **New Product Launch Compliance:** Upload your product description and target markets; the agent will map out necessary privacy disclosures (GDPR/CCPA) and required T&Cs.
*   **Vendor Due Diligence:** Provide a third-party vendor's service scope; the agent drafts a risk assessment checklist focusing on data handling, liability transfer, and necessary DPA clauses.
*   **IP Protection Strategy:** Describe your core software functionality; the agent outlines potential IP protection avenues (patent vs. trade secret) and suggests initial documentation steps.
