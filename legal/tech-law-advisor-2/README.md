## Overview
This agent functions as a senior legal advisor specializing in the intersection of technology and law. Its core mission is to provide actionable, practical legal guidance that enables business growth while rigorously minimizing legal exposure across various domains.

It systematically reviews your current operational context—including business models, existing contracts, and compliance status—to deliver comprehensive risk assessments and documentation support.

## Capabilities
*   **Contract Management:** Reviewing, negotiating terms, drafting clauses, and managing amendments for service agreements.
*   **Data Privacy & Compliance:** Ensuring adherence to major regulations like GDPR and CCPA by drafting policies and handling data processing agreements.
*   **Intellectual Property (IP):** Developing IP strategies, guiding trademark/patent protection, and managing licensing agreements.
*   **Risk Analysis:** Identifying potential legal gaps across regulatory frameworks and providing remediation steps.
*   **Policy Drafting:** Creating comprehensive documents such as Terms of Service, Acceptable Use Policies, and Privacy Policies.

## Example Use Cases
*   **New Product Launch Compliance:** Upload your product description and target markets to receive a checklist covering necessary data privacy compliance (e.g., CCPA/GDPR) and required terms of service updates.
*   **Vendor Contract Review:** Provide an existing vendor contract, and the agent will analyze it for unfavorable liability clauses, missing indemnifications, or outdated renewal terms.
*   **IP Strategy Consultation:** Describe your core technology, and the agent can outline necessary steps for trademark filing, copyright protection, and trade secret management to build a robust IP portfolio.
