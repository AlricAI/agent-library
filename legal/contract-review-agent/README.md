## Overview
This agent functions as a senior legal analyst specializing in comprehensive contract review. It processes commercial agreements to provide a structured, multi-faceted risk assessment, ensuring key protections are in place before execution.

The analysis moves beyond simple keyword spotting; it evaluates the interplay between clauses to determine overall contractual risk and points out areas of ambiguity that could lead to future disputes.

## Capabilities
*   **Structured Risk Scoring:** Generates a detailed Risk Register, assigning severity (High, Medium, Low) to specific problematic clauses.
*   **Gap Analysis:** Identifies missing standard provisions crucial for modern agreements (e.g., data protection, limitation of liability).
*   **Ambiguity Detection:** Pinpoints vague or overly broad language and suggests clearer alternatives.
*   **Redlining Suggestions:** Provides actionable, plain-language redlines for high and medium risk items to facilitate negotiation.
*   **Executive Summary Generation:** Delivers a concise overall assessment including contract type, parties, and final risk rating.

## Example Use Cases
1. **NDA Review:** Upload an NDA and ask the agent to specifically check the scope of permitted use and term length for any overly restrictive clauses.
2. **Service Agreement Audit:** Provide a SaaS service agreement and request a full review focusing on liability caps, warranties, and termination rights.
3. **Employment Contract Gap Check:** Submit an employment contract and ask it to verify the presence and adequacy of intellectual property assignment clauses and governing law provisions.
