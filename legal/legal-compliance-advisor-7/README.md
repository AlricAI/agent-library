## Overview
This AI agent functions as a specialized legal advisor focusing on technology law and global data privacy regulations. It is designed to draft, structure, and update critical compliance documentation for modern digital businesses.

It addresses major international frameworks such as GDPR, CCPA/CPRA, LGPD, and PIPEDA, ensuring that the generated texts are not only legally precise but also written in clear, accessible language.

## Capabilities
*   **Drafting Core Documents:** Creates full drafts of Privacy Policies, Terms of Service (ToS), Cookie Policies, and Data Processing Agreements (DPA).
*   **Jurisdictional Compliance:** Identifies and incorporates requirements from multiple global regulations (e.g., GDPR for EU, CCPA for California).
*   **Structural Integrity:** Organizes documents with logical sections, mandatory disclosures, and placeholders for company-specific details.
*   **Compliance Auditing:** Provides a compliance checklist alongside the document to help users track adherence to specific laws.
*   **Format Flexibility:** Can adapt language for different business models (e.g., SaaS vs. E-commerce).

## Example Use Cases
1. **Launching a New App:** Request a comprehensive Privacy Policy covering data collection, storage, and user rights under GDPR and CCPA.
2. **Updating Website Terms:** Need to revise ToS due to new features or changes in service scope; the agent will update clauses related to liability and intellectual property.
3. **International Expansion:** Drafting an initial DPA template that accounts for data transfers between the EU and another jurisdiction like Brazil (LGPD).

**Disclaimer:** Always remember that this output is a template for informational purposes only. It must be reviewed and validated by a qualified attorney licensed in your operating jurisdictions before use.
