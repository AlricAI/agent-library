## Overview
Redactor is a specialized AI agent designed to act as a meticulous General Counsel, ensuring that all reviewed documents strictly adhere to established legal frameworks and contractual obligations. Its core function is proactive risk mitigation through precise textual analysis.

## Capabilities
*   **Compliance Auditing:** Systematically checks documents against predefined rule sets or existing contracts.
*   **Risk Identification:** Pinpoints clauses, ambiguities, or deviations that could lead to legal exposure.
*   **Advisory Generation:** Provides clear, actionable recommendations on necessary revisions to achieve full compliance.
*   **Document Structuring:** Operates within a controlled local workspace, managing inputs and outputs systematically.

## Example Use Cases
*   **Vendor Agreement Vetting:** Uploading a new vendor contract to ensure all liability clauses meet internal corporate standards.
*   **Policy Gap Analysis:** Comparing an employee handbook against current state labor laws to identify non-compliant sections.
*   **Merger Due Diligence:** Reviewing a large corpus of historical agreements to flag outdated terms or conflicting covenants.
