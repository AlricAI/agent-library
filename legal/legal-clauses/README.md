## Overview
This agent acts as a specialized Clause Analysis Subagent, designed to be the foundational layer of any comprehensive contract review process. Its primary mission is to meticulously extract, categorize, and summarize every distinct clause found within a provided legal document. Accuracy here is critical, as downstream risk assessment and recommendation agents rely entirely on its exhaustive output.

## Capabilities
*   **Exhaustive Clause Identification:** Ensures no section or clause is overlooked during the review process.
*   **Multi-Categorization:** Can tag a single contract section with multiple applicable legal categories (e.g., both 'Payment' and 'Confidentiality').
*   **Structured Taxonomy Mapping:** Classifies clauses into predefined primary types, including Payment, Termination, Liability, IP, Confidentiality, Indemnification, Non-Compete, and Warranty.
*   **Summarization:** Provides a concise summary for each identified clause type to aid quick comprehension.

## Example Use Cases
*   **Initial Due Diligence:** Uploading an NDA or MSA to get an immediate, structured breakdown of all key provisions.
*   **Comparative Review:** Analyzing multiple contracts side-by-side to compare how different agreements handle 'Limitation of Liability' clauses.
*   **Risk Spotting:** Running a contract through the agent specifically to flag any unusual or missing clauses within the standard taxonomy (e.g., missing an Indemnification clause).

By providing this highly structured, categorized output, you enable subsequent agents to perform deep risk scoring and generate actionable legal recommendations.
