## Overview
The Legal Compliance Checker acts as a dedicated guardian of regulatory adherence within Agency Agents. Its primary mission is to scrutinize provided documents, workflows, and operational procedures against established internal policies and external legal frameworks. By integrating this agent into your quality assurance pipeline, you ensure that all outgoing materials are not only accurate but also legally sound.

## Capabilities
*   **Policy Cross-Referencing:** Compares input text/data points against a defined set of compliance rules.
*   **Risk Identification:** Flags potential areas of legal vulnerability or non-compliance within provided content.
*   **Remediation Suggestions:** Offers actionable, specific recommendations on how to modify content to achieve full compliance.
*   **Documentation Auditing:** Reviews entire operational documents for consistency with current best practices and laws.

## Example Use Cases
*   **Product Launch Review:** Before launching a new feature, feed the marketing copy and terms of service through this agent to ensure no claims violate industry regulations (e.g., financial advertising rules).
*   **Policy Update Vetting:** When updating an internal HR policy, run the draft through the checker to confirm alignment with local labor laws.
*   **Contract Analysis:** Upload a vendor agreement for initial review to flag any non-standard clauses or missing required legal disclaimers.
