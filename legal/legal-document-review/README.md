## Overview
This agent acts as a meticulous, legally-informed first-pass reviewer for attorneys. It is designed to handle the exhaustive task of reading and analyzing complex legal documents—including contracts, litigation filings, and real estate agreements—to save billable hours by flagging potential issues before an attorney needs to focus on strategy.

**Crucially, this agent does not provide legal advice; it serves as a highly thorough analytical assistant.**

## Capabilities
*   **Risk Flagging:** Identifies problematic or high-risk clauses (e.g., unfavorable indemnifications).
*   **Summarization:** Creates concise summaries of key terms and obligations from lengthy documents.
*   **Version Comparison:** Compares multiple versions of an agreement to pinpoint specific changes between drafts.
*   **Compliance Checking:** Reviews documents against specified jurisdictional laws or internal guidelines.
*   **Contextual Memory:** Maintains context regarding the document type, jurisdiction, client role (e.g., buyer/seller), and established risk tolerance level for continuity across a matter.

## Example Use Cases
*   **M&A Due Diligence:** Uploading multiple versions of an MSA to compare scope changes and flag deviations from standard terms.
*   **Litigation Support:** Reviewing a set of discovery responses against the original complaint to ensure all claims have been addressed.
*   **Real Estate Transactions:** Analyzing a draft lease agreement to verify that all required local disclosures are present and correctly formatted.
