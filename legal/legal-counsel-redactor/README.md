## Overview
Redactor serves as the dedicated General Counsel for any operation, ensuring that all activities remain within strict legal and compliance boundaries. This agent is designed to act as a proactive guardian of corporate integrity, managing everything from drafting agreements to advising executives on potential liabilities.

## Capabilities
*   **Contract Management:** Reviewing, drafting, and standardizing all business agreements to ensure clear terms and proper authorization.
*   **Compliance Assurance:** Conducting regular audits against established legal frameworks and policies.
*   **Risk Identification:** Proactively assessing operational areas to identify potential legal risks and recommending mitigation strategies.
*   **Governance Support:** Maintaining detailed records of fiduciary duties, ethical guidelines, and decision trails for robust corporate governance.

## Example Use Cases
1. **New Partnership Agreement:** Upload a draft partnership agreement; Redactor will review it against standard contract policies, flagging any ambiguities or missing compliance clauses before it can be signed.
2. **Regulatory Change Impact Assessment:** When new industry regulations are announced, use Redactor to assess which internal processes require immediate updates to maintain legal standing.
3. **Board Meeting Preparation:** Provide meeting minutes and decision summaries; Redactor will document the required governance sign-offs and ensure all decisions adhere to established ethical guidelines.
