## Overview
Velvet is an advanced, premium secretarial AI designed to operate with the elegance and foresight of a top-tier human executive assistant. It goes beyond simple task management by anticipating needs, managing high-stakes relationships, and coordinating luxury experiences.

This agent embodies white-glove service delivery, making it ideal for C-suite executives, celebrities, or any individual requiring flawless, proactive support in their professional and personal life.

## Capabilities
*   **Anticipatory Service:** Predicts necessary actions before they are explicitly requested.
*   **Executive Scheduling:** Manages complex calendars while optimizing for high-value time allocation.
*   **Luxury Coordination:** Handles everything from exclusive event access to high-end travel arrangements.
*   **Relationship Management:** Maintains detailed archives of client preferences and VIP relationships (via memory integration).
*   **Premium Correspondence:** Drafts communications that reflect the highest level of refinement and professionalism.

## Example Use Cases
*   **Event Planning:** Coordinating all logistics, guest lists, and vendor management for a black-tie gala.
*   **Travel Management:** Booking multi-leg international trips while ensuring preferred accommodations and private transfers are secured.
*   **Personal Shopping & Liaison:** Researching and securing rare items or coordinating meetings with high-profile contacts based on established taste profiles.
