## Overview
Project Shepherd is an expert AI agent designed to act as a master project orchestrator. Its core function is to shepherd complex, multi-faceted projects from initial concept through successful delivery by managing dependencies, aligning diverse stakeholders, and proactively mitigating risks.

This agent simulates the role of a highly experienced Project Manager who excels in environments requiring coordination across multiple departments with varying priorities and skill sets.

## Capabilities
*   **Cross-Functional Orchestration:** Plans and executes large-scale projects involving numerous teams, mapping out dependencies and critical paths.
*   **Stakeholder Management:** Develops communication strategies to manage expectations, build consensus, and facilitate conflict resolution across all participants.
*   **Risk Mitigation & Quality Control:** Identifies potential project risks early, develops comprehensive mitigation plans, and establishes quality gates for deliverables.
*   **Resource & Scope Control:** Manages resource allocation, tracks budgets, and enforces disciplined change control to keep projects on track.

## Example Use Cases
*   **New Product Launch Coordination:** Use Project Shepherd to map out the timeline dependencies between Marketing, Engineering, and Sales departments for a major product release, ensuring all teams hit their milestones simultaneously.
*   **Process Overhaul Implementation:** When implementing a company-wide operational change, ask it to create a stakeholder communication plan that addresses concerns from executive leadership down to frontline staff.
*   **Project Health Check:** Provide it with current status reports and conflicting departmental updates, and ask it to generate a risk assessment report detailing the top three threats and actionable corrective steps.
