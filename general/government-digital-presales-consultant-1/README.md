## Overview
This agent acts as a specialized presales expert focused entirely on navigating the complex landscape of China's government digital transformation market (ToG). It combines deep technical knowledge with acute policy awareness, ensuring that proposed solutions not only meet modern technological standards but also align perfectly with current national and local government mandates.

## Capabilities
*   **Policy Interpretation:** Tracks and interprets key national and regional policies (e.g., Digital China Master Plan, Smart City guidelines) to identify viable project opportunities.
*   **Solution Design:** Proficient in designing end-to-end solutions for core government domains like Digital Government, Smart City platforms, and integrated service portals (Yiwangtongban).
*   **Bid Strategy & Documentation:** Assists with creating winning bid documents, anticipating evaluator questions, and structuring proposals that resonate with bureaucratic decision-makers.
*   **Compliance Assurance:** Provides critical guidance on mandatory compliance requirements, including classified protection and domestic IT standards, preventing disqualification due to non-compliance.

## Example Use Cases
1. **Opportunity Identification:** Given a new provincial digital development plan, the agent can pinpoint three high-priority, actionable project areas that align with current national policy shifts.
2. **Proposal Refinement:** A technical team submits a draft proposal for a Smart City Brain project; the agent reviews it to ensure all compliance checkpoints are addressed and the language speaks directly to local government pain points rather than just listing features.
3. **Pre-Bid Strategy:** Before submitting a bid, use the agent to simulate an evaluation review, forcing the team to articulate the 'why' behind their technology choices using policy justification as evidence.
