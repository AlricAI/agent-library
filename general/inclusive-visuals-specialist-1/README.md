## Overview
The Inclusive Visuals Specialist is a rigorous prompt engineering agent designed to counteract the systemic biases embedded within foundational image and video generation models (like Midjourney, Sora, and DALL-E). Its core mission is to elevate visual output beyond generic stock photo tropes by demanding authentic, contextually accurate, and dignified representation of human diversity.

## Capabilities
*   **Bias Subversion:** Actively rewrites prompts to move away from common AI stereotypes (e.g., avoiding the 'white savior' trope or over-simplifying cultural contexts).
*   **Constraint Engineering:** Implements explicit negative constraints to prevent common AI failures, such as generating extra fingers, clone faces in crowds, or nonsensical cultural details.
*   **Cultural Specificity:** Ensures that prompts anchor subjects correctly within their actual environments, detailing appropriate architecture, clothing styles, and lighting conditions for diverse skin tones.
*   **Intersectionality Focus:** Treats identity not merely as a descriptor, but as a complex domain requiring technical prompting expertise to capture intersectional reality (culture, age, disability, socioeconomic status).

## Example Use Cases
*   **Scenario 1: Corporate Diversity Shot:** Instead of asking for 'a diverse team meeting,' use this agent to generate prompts ensuring distinct facial structures, varied professional attire appropriate to the stated industry, and natural interaction dynamics.
*   **Scenario 2: Cultural Scene Generation:** When depicting a specific cultural festival or market, it will enforce accurate architectural details and culturally relevant clothing patterns, preventing 'exoticizing' or inaccurate renderings.
*   **Scenario 3: Portraiture:** It refines prompts to ensure lighting techniques are flattering and contextually appropriate for melanin-rich skin tones, moving beyond flat, artificial studio looks.
