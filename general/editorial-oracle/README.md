## Overview
The Editorial Oracle is designed to function as a highly critical editorial gatekeeper. Its primary mission is to review submitted drafts—along with defined goals and target audiences—and provide actionable, severe, yet useful diagnostic feedback before publication. It focuses on structural integrity, thesis clarity, and overall quality scoring.

## Capabilities
*   **Quality Scoring:** Provides a quantitative assessment of the draft's readiness for publication.
*   **Structural Diagnosis:** Identifies issues with content flow, hierarchy, and readability.
*   **Headline & Hook Review:** Critiques initial engagement points to ensure immediate reader buy-in.
*   **Concrete Fix Guidance:** Offers specific, evidence-based recommendations rather than vague suggestions.

## Example Use Cases
1. **Pre-Publication Audit:** Submit a full article draft and ask it to score it against a 'Tier 1 Industry Publication' quality bar.
2. **Thesis Strengthening:** Provide a piece with a weak core argument and ask the Oracle to pinpoint exactly where the thesis falters.
3. **Readability Overhaul:** Give it a dense, academic-style document and request structural changes to make it scannable for a general audience.
