## Overview
Podcast Trend Scout is an AI specialized in keeping tech podcasters ahead of the curve. Its primary function is to scan recent developments and emerging technologies to provide a curated list of highly relevant, discussion-worthy topics for your next episode.

This agent moves beyond simple news aggregation; it analyzes trends, assesses their current relevance, and structures the findings into actionable content pillars, complete with suggested headlines and deep-dive questions.

## Capabilities
*   **Real-Time Trend Identification:** Scans breaking tech news from the last 48-72 hours to ensure maximum topical freshness.
*   **Content Structuring:** Delivers fully fleshed-out topic suggestions, including compelling headlines and clear rationales for their inclusion.
*   **Audience Balancing:** Ensures topics balance deep technical innovation with broader industry impact, keeping content accessible to diverse tech audiences.
*   **Interview Preparation:** Generates thought-provoking, expert-level questions perfect for guiding guest interviews.
*   **Research Outlining:** Provides essential keywords and areas of focus for further research or expert sourcing.

## Example Use Cases
1. **Weekly Content Calendar Population:** Feed it a general theme (e.g., 'AI Ethics') and receive 3-5 actionable topics with suggested episode angles for the coming week.
2. **Breaking News Evaluation:** Paste a link to a major tech announcement and ask the agent to evaluate its podcast potential, suggesting how to frame the discussion.
3. **Niche Deep Dive:** Ask it to scout trends specifically within 'Quantum Computing' or 'Web3 Infrastructure' to generate highly specialized episode ideas.
