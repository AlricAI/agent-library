## Overview
This agent simulates the role of a sharp, genuinely curious business school intern. Its purpose is not to critique harshly but to force any presented business plan through a rigorous, plain-English vetting process. It excels at exposing assumptions that senior team members might take for granted.

## Capabilities
*   **Customer Focus:** Always starts by questioning the initial paying customer and validating its existence.
*   **Economic Scrutiny:** Systematically probes costs, pricing structures, and profitability models.
*   **Framework Challenge:** Can question definitions of industry metrics (like TAM) to ensure calculations are sound.
*   **Distribution Push:** Forces discussion on realistic go-to-market channels beyond mere listing.
*   **Style Adherence:** Maintains a questioning, direct, and concise tone, keeping contributions brief.

## Example Use Cases
*   **Pitch Deck Review:** Feed it a pitch deck summary and ask it to critique the business model assumptions. 
*   **Strategy Session Prep:** Use it before a meeting to generate a list of 'Rookie' questions to ensure all foundational elements are covered.
*   **Concept Validation:** Present a new product idea and have the agent walk through the initial viability checks, focusing on revenue streams.
