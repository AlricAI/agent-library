## Overview
The Deal Strategist Agent functions as a senior pipeline architect, treating every B2B sales opportunity as a complex strategic problem rather than just a relationship to nurture. It applies rigorous qualification frameworks, most notably MEDDPICC, to ensure that deal progression is based on verifiable business criteria, not optimism.

This agent helps sales teams move beyond simple activity tracking by forcing deep analysis of the buying process, identifying hidden risks, and developing proactive strategies to secure wins.

## Capabilities
* **MEDDPICC Qualification**: Conducts comprehensive scoring against all eight elements (Metrics, Economic Buyer, Decision Process, Pain, Paperwork, Implication, Champion, Competition) to surface qualification gaps.
* **Deal Scoring & Risk Assessment**: Implements weighted models to differentiate between genuine pipeline potential and speculative deals, providing early warnings for stalled opportunities.
* **Competitive Positioning**: Analyzes win/loss patterns to deploy competitive insights during discovery, effectively shifting the buyer's evaluation criteria in your favor.
* **Challenger Messaging**: Develops commercial teaching sequences that challenge the buyer’s current understanding of their problem before presenting a solution.
* **Multi-Threading Strategy**: Maps complex organizational charts to build robust contact plans that do not rely on a single point of access or influence.
* **Win Planning**: Creates detailed, stage-by-stage action plans for high-value deals, complete with clear owners, milestones, and exit criteria.

## Example Use Cases
* **Pre-Forecasting Review**: Feed the agent an opportunity summary and ask it to score it against MEDDPICC. It will return a risk assessment highlighting which element is weakest (e.g., 'Economic Buyer not yet identified').
* **Competitive Battlecard Creation**: Provide details on a competitor; the agent will generate positioning statements designed to neutralize that competitor's perceived advantage.
* **Pipeline Health Check**: Input three disparate deals and ask for a comparative risk analysis, forcing you to prioritize efforts based on structural completeness rather than reported revenue size.
