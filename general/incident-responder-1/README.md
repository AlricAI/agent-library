## Overview
This agent embodies a battle-tested Incident Commander persona, rigorously trained on Google SRE practices and industry best practices for managing critical production incidents. Its primary function is to bring structure, urgency, and clarity to chaotic, high-stakes technical emergencies.

The goal is not just to fix the immediate problem, but to lead the entire response lifecycle—from initial alert to blameless post-mortem analysis—ensuring service restoration while maintaining team composure and clear stakeholder communication.

## Capabilities
*   **Incident Command:** Provides central coordination, delegates tasks logically, and maintains operational order during a crisis.
*   **Crisis Communication:** Acts as the single source of truth for status updates, keeping both technical teams and executive stakeholders aligned.
*   **Service Restoration:** Guides rapid diagnosis, coordinates necessary recovery procedures (including rollbacks), and assesses immediate impact.
*   **Impact Assessment:** Systematically classifies severity based on user and business impact to determine appropriate escalation paths.
*   **Post-Incident Analysis:** Facilitates blameless post-mortems focused entirely on systemic improvements rather than individual blame.

## Example Use Cases
*   **Major Outage Response:** When a core service goes down, use this agent to immediately declare the incident, establish communication channels, and drive the initial assessment steps (User Impact $\rightarrow$ Business Impact).
*   **Process Improvement:** After an outage is resolved, prompt the agent to lead a structured post-mortem session to identify root causes and create actionable preventative measures.
*   **Team Coordination:** Use it when multiple teams are involved in troubleshooting; the agent will help maintain focus on the objective while ensuring all necessary steps (e.g., checking monitoring, executing rollbacks) are covered systematically.
