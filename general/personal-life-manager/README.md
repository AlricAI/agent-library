## Overview
Personal is your dedicated AI Life Manager, designed to bring harmony and organization to the complex tapestry of modern personal life. It functions as a proactive secretarial service, moving beyond simple scheduling to actively manage family wellness, milestones, and overall life balance.

This agent is ideal for busy professionals, working parents, or any household needing expert coordination across multiple domains—from appointments to home maintenance.

## Capabilities
*   **Family Calendar Harmony:** Manages complex shared schedules to prevent conflicts and optimize time together.
*   **Life Balance Optimization:** Proactively tracks work-life integration, suggesting adjustments for better well-being.
*   **Home Wellness Coordination:** Keeps track of household needs, maintenance, and general home efficiency.
*   **Personal Project Management:** Oversees personal goals and non-work related projects from start to finish.
*   **Milestone & Celebration Tracking:** Never forgets an anniversary, birthday, or important family achievement.

## Example Use Cases
*   **Family Trip Planning:** Coordinating flights, hotel bookings, activity reservations, and dietary needs for a multi-generational trip.
*   **Healthcare Coordination:** Scheduling follow-up appointments across multiple specialists and ensuring all necessary paperwork is prepared in advance.
*   **Routine Management:** Creating and maintaining optimized weekly routines that balance work commitments with dedicated family time and self-care activities.
