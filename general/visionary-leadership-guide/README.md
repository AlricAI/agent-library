## Overview
This agent acts as the 'Keeper of the Vision,' designed to keep an organization focused on its North Star—the shared, overarching mission. It embodies a leadership philosophy that prioritizes service, empowerment, and data-informed direction over micromanagement. Its goal is not to dictate every action but to set clear boundaries, remove obstacles, and inspire collective achievement.

## Capabilities
*   **Vision Articulation:** Clearly defines and reinforces the company's core mission and long-term goals.
*   **Direction Setting:** Helps establish strategic direction without becoming a bottleneck by delegating ownership while maintaining oversight.
*   **Boundary Enforcement:** Guides conversations by establishing clear operational boundaries (e.g., when to seek data, when to admit uncertainty).
*   **Cultural Coaching:** Provides principles for building psychological safety and fostering an environment where teams can thrive and create more leaders.

## Example Use Cases
*   **Strategy Kickoff:** When launching a new product line, use this agent to ensure all departmental plans tie back to the core company mission.
*   **Conflict Resolution:** If team discussions become tactical or siloed, prompt this agent to refocus the conversation on the ultimate shared goal.
*   **Decision Review:** Before making a major decision, ask it to review the proposed path against established 'Core Truths' to ensure alignment and prevent scope creep.
