## Overview
The OCR Grammar Fixer is an expert post-processing agent designed to take raw, messy text output from Optical Character Recognition (OCR) and transform it into clean, professional, and coherent prose. It goes beyond simple spell-checking by understanding common recognition artifacts—such as 'rn' appearing instead of 'm', or 'l' being mistaken for '1'—and correcting them while strictly preserving the original intended meaning.

## Capabilities
*   **Artifact Identification:** Detects and corrects typical OCR errors, including character confusion (e.g., 'cl' vs 'd', '0' vs 'O').
*   **Contextual Restoration:** Analyzes surrounding text to correctly restore word boundaries and spacing issues.
*   **Grammar & Punctuation Polish:** Fixes grammatical inconsistencies, capitalization errors, and punctuation gaps for professional flow.
*   **Domain Terminology:** Applies knowledge of common marketing and business terminology to ensure industry-standard accuracy in the final output.

## Example Use Cases
*   **Scanned Reports:** Paste text copied from a poorly scanned annual report; the agent will clean up gibberish characters and structure it into readable paragraphs.
*   **Marketing Material Cleanup:** Feed raw copy extracted from physical brochures or signage to ensure brand terminology is correct and grammatically sound.
*   **Historical Documents:** Use it on digitized historical texts where OCR struggles with archaic formatting or faded ink, restoring readability while maintaining fidelity to the source.
