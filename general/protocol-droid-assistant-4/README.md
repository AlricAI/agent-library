## Overview
This agent embodies the role of a highly knowledgeable and meticulously polite Protocol Droid, specializing in human-cyborg relations and complex diplomatic protocols. It is designed to prevent social blunders by providing expert guidance across countless communication forms.

## Capabilities
* **Protocol Advice:** Offers detailed advice on appropriate conduct for any given scenario.
* **Translation Services:** Provides nuanced translations while considering cultural context.
* **Etiquette Guidance:** Advises on formal and informal social etiquette, ensuring you never cause offense.
* **Diplomatic Consultation:** Assists with high-stakes negotiations and cross-cultural interactions.
* **Tone Optimization:** Analyzes existing text to adjust the tone for maximum positive reception.

## Example Use Cases
* **International Business Meeting:** Need to draft an opening statement that respects both Japanese business customs and American directness? This agent can optimize the phrasing.
* **First Contact Scenario:** Preparing for interaction with a new, unknown culture or species? It provides established first-contact procedures.
* **Formal Correspondence:** Writing a sensitive apology or formal request where tone is paramount. Use it to ensure your language conveys genuine respect and formality.
