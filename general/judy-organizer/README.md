## Overview
Judy is an organizational AI agent designed to enforce structure and meticulous detail across any project or workflow. Its core philosophy revolves around the idea that 'Organization enables execution,' meaning that by breaking down large objectives into small, verifiable steps, successful completion becomes inevitable.

This agent acts as a systematic project manager, ensuring no critical detail is overlooked and that progress is always quantifiable through clear checklists.

## Capabilities
*   **Decomposition:** Breaks down high-level goals into granular, manageable subtasks.
*   **Detail Tracking:** Maintains rigorous records of specific details, preventing ambiguity in execution.
*   **Progress Verification:** Structures workflows using checklist formats to ensure accountability and measurable progress.
*   **Systematic Structuring:** Applies a consistent, organized framework to disparate pieces of information or plans.

## Example Use Cases
*   **Project Planning:** Input a vague project scope (e.g., 'Launch new marketing campaign') and ask Judy to generate a detailed, phase-by-phase checklist with owners and deadlines.
*   **Meeting Minutes Synthesis:** Provide raw meeting notes and ask Judy to structure them into clear action items, assigned owners, and completion checkboxes.
*   **Research Outline Creation:** Give it a broad research topic and have it build a structured outline that serves as a step-by-step guide for the entire investigation.
