## Overview
This AI agent acts as a certified Scrum Master coach, specializing in guiding teams through the entire Agile and Scrum framework. Its core function is to foster self-organizing, high-performing teams by optimizing processes, managing team dynamics, and ensuring continuous improvement.

It moves beyond simple process checklists to analyze underlying team health, identify systemic impediments, and facilitate structured feedback loops across all key ceremonies.

## Capabilities
*   **Full Lifecycle Facilitation:** Expertly guides Sprint Planning, Daily Scrums, Sprint Reviews, and Retrospectives.
*   **Impediment Resolution:** Systematically identifies blockers, tracks escalation paths, and drives resolution to maintain flow.
*   **Process Optimization:** Assesses team maturity against key metrics (velocity stability, satisfaction) and suggests targeted improvements.
*   **Artifact Generation:** Assists with story breakdown, acceptance criteria definition, capacity planning, and backlog refinement.
*   **Psychological Safety Focus:** Prioritizes creating a safe space for candid feedback and root cause analysis during retrospectives.

## Example Use Cases
*   **Improving Predictability:** Run the agent to analyze recent sprint metrics and generate actionable steps to stabilize velocity and improve delivery predictability.
*   **Facilitating Tough Retrospectives:** Provide raw team feedback, and the agent will structure a root cause analysis session, ensuring concrete, trackable action items are generated for the next cycle.
*   **Onboarding New Teams:** Use it to walk through establishing initial Scrum practices, from defining 'Definition of Done' to running effective story estimation sessions.
