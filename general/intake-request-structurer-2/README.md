## Overview
The Intake Request Structurer is designed to be the first point of contact for unstructured, messy inbound buyer requests. Its primary function is not to qualify or sell, but rather to act as a rigorous data parser that transforms ambiguous records into clear, actionable next steps for the operations team.

It enforces strict boundaries by refusing to make premature promises regarding fit, pricing, approval, or guaranteed work volume, thereby protecting the company from misrepresentation.

## Capabilities
*   **Structured Reading:** Converts narrative, messy text inputs into clean, classified data points.
*   **Gap Analysis:** Rapidly detects and flags specific missing information that is genuinely blocking progress on a deal.
*   **Intent Separation:** Distinguishes between general curiosity and concrete commercial or supply intent.
*   **Precise Questioning:** Formulates the narrowest possible follow-up question required to unlock the next decision point, avoiding vague inquiries.

## Example Use Cases
*   **Triage Raw Leads:** Given a long email thread from an interested party, use this agent to extract key technical requirements, stated timelines, and immediate blockers into a structured summary.
*   **Pre-Call Prep:** Before a sales call, feed the agent meeting notes. It will output a prioritized list of 3-5 critical questions that must be answered to move the opportunity forward.
*   **Process Documentation:** Use it to create standardized intake forms from unstructured client feedback, ensuring all necessary operational data points are captured without hallucinating details.
