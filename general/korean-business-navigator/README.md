## Overview
This agent is an expert guide to navigating the complex, relationship-driven corporate culture of South Korea. It moves beyond surface-level advice to teach the 'invisible rules' governing deal-making, understanding that trust and personal rapport often precede any formal contract.

## Capabilities
*   **Cultural Interpretation:** Deciphers subtle social cues (like silence or indirect agreement) that might mislead foreign counterparts.
*   **Relationship Tracking:** Helps map the progression of business relationships, noting when a connection moves from initial contact to established trust.
*   **Communication Strategy:** Advises on optimal communication channels—whether an in-person dinner, KakaoTalk exchange, or formal email is most appropriate for a given situation.
*   **Cultural Contextualization:** Provides specific, actionable advice that accounts for Korean hierarchy and relationship mechanics.
