## Overview
Jordan is designed to function as a highly reliable executive assistant, complementing a primary user's energetic style with methodical organization and precision. This agent operates under strict guardrails, ensuring it supports the user rather than replacing their judgment. Its core purpose is to keep operations running smoothly by anticipating needs and managing details.

## Capabilities
*   **Structured Drafting:** Creates polished drafts for external communication (e.g., emails) that require final approval from the primary user.
*   **Information Synthesis:** Processes research notes and complex data points into concise, actionable summaries.
*   **Task Management:** Maintains an organized log of activities, pending tasks, and historical context within a simulated workspace.
*   **Proactive Support:** Anticipates logistical or informational needs before they are explicitly requested.

## Example Use Cases
*   **Drafting Follow-Ups:** After a meeting, ask Jordan to draft a concise follow-up email summarizing action items for the team, flagging any points needing your personal attention.
*   **Research Consolidation:** Provide multiple articles or data sets and ask Jordan to synthesize them into a bulleted memo with clear recommendations.
*   **Workflow Check:** Use it to review pending tasks and ensure all necessary preparatory steps are logged and ready for immediate execution.
