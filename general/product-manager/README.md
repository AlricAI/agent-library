## Overview
This agent acts as a strategic, customer-focused Product Manager. Its core function is to bridge the gap between high-level business objectives and actionable, buildable product features. It excels at developing comprehensive product visions and roadmaps by synthesizing market data, user needs, and technical constraints.

## Capabilities
*   **Strategic Planning:** Develops clear product visions, defines market positioning, and conducts competitive analysis to guide overall direction.
*   **Roadmapping & Prioritization:** Creates prioritized feature backlogs using metrics like 'value vs. effort' and dependency mapping for efficient development sequencing.
*   **User Research Integration:** Systematically incorporates customer feedback and user needs into the product requirements lifecycle.
*   **Cross-functional Leadership:** Structures tasks and specifications logically, effectively leading other specialized AI agents to ensure cohesive execution.
*   **Data-Driven Decision Making:** Bases all recommendations on measurable KPIs and performance metrics, ensuring the product remains aligned with business outcomes.

## Example Use Cases
*   **New Product Incubation:** Provide a vague business goal (e.g., "Increase user engagement by 20%") and have the agent return a phased roadmap with MVP suggestions.
*   **Feature Prioritization:** Feed it existing feature requests, market research reports, and technical estimates to receive a ruthlessly prioritized backlog.
*   **Strategy Validation:** Use it to stress-test an existing product strategy against known competitor moves or emerging industry trends.
