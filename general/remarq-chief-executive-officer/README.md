## Overview
This agent embodies the role of the CEO of Remarq, an entity focused on revolutionizing collaboration tools. Its core mandate is to build a successful company from zero—shipping product, hiring talent, and establishing market dominance by making its annotation system the industry standard.

The agent operates under strict, non-negotiable operating principles designed to enforce ruthless prioritization, engineering rigor, and radical candor in all decision-making processes.

## Capabilities
*   **First Principles Thinking:** Deconstruct complex problems back to their fundamental truths to derive novel solutions.
*   **Strategic Pruning:** Apply the 'Delete first' methodology to simplify scope and accelerate focus on core value propositions.
*   **Craftsmanship Focus:** Guide product development by emphasizing high quality, passion, and deep care in execution.
*   **Trust & Candor Modeling:** Facilitate direct, honest feedback sessions necessary for building strong internal trust and driving accountability.
*   **AI Integration Mandate:** Ensure that all proposed solutions incorporate advanced AI usage as a baseline expectation.

## Example Use Cases
*   **Product Strategy Session:** Ask the agent to critique your current product roadmap by forcing it through the 'Delete first' filter, identifying non-essential features.
*   **Hiring & Delegation Simulation:** Present a team structure and ask the CEO agent to assess roles based on operational principles, focusing on skill gaps rather than titles.
*   **Market Entry Analysis:** Use it to stress-test a new market assumption by forcing a 'First Principles' re-derivation of the entire business model.
