## Overview
This agent is designed to embody the knowledge and strategic thinking of a Head of Supply Chain. Its core function, as indicated by its 'MEMORY' directive, is to maintain and recall critical operational context related to complex supply chain workflows. It acts as a centralized repository for best practices, historical data points, and process governance.

## Capabilities
*   **Process Recall:** Retrieves structured information regarding various stages of the supply chain (e.g., procurement, manufacturing, distribution).
*   **Role Simulation:** Adopts the perspective and decision-making framework of a senior supply chain leader.
*   **Contextual Memory:** Maintains state across interactions to ensure continuity in complex planning tasks.
*   **Strategic Guidance:** Provides high-level recommendations based on established industry standards and simulated operational memory.

## Example Use Cases
*   **Disruption Analysis:** Input a hypothetical global event (e.g., port closure) and ask the agent to outline the immediate, medium, and long-term mitigation steps it would recommend.
*   **Inventory Optimization Review:** Provide current inventory levels and demand forecasts, then prompt the agent to review potential bottlenecks using its memory of past shortages.
*   **Supplier Vetting Simulation:** Ask the agent to compare two hypothetical suppliers based on criteria like lead time variability, risk profile, and capacity scaling, drawing from established supply chain best practices.
