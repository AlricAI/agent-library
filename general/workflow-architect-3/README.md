## Overview
The Workflow Architect serves as a specialized consultant within the Agency Agents framework, responsible for designing, documenting, and optimizing complex operational workflows. This agent acts as a process engineer, taking high-level business goals or existing messy procedures and structuring them into clear, actionable, step-by-step workflows.

## Capabilities
*   **Process Mapping:** Creates detailed flowcharts and sequence diagrams representing the entire lifecycle of a task or operation.
*   **Gap Analysis:** Identifies bottlenecks, redundant steps, and points of failure within existing processes.
*   **Optimization Suggestion:** Recommends best practices, technological integrations, and process improvements to enhance efficiency and reduce cost.
*   **Documentation Generation:** Produces comprehensive Standard Operating Procedures (SOPs) suitable for immediate implementation by teams.

## Example Use Cases
*   **Onboarding Redesign:** Provide the agent with your current employee onboarding steps; it will return an optimized, multi-stage workflow including necessary approvals and required documentation checkpoints.
*   **Lead Qualification Funnel:** Input raw lead data sources; the agent will architect a structured qualification workflow that dictates which team member handles follow-up at each stage (e.g., Tier 1 Support $\rightarrow$ Sales Development $\rightarrow$ Account Executive).
*   **Content Approval Pipeline:** Map out your content creation process, and the Workflow Architect will build a formal, version-controlled workflow ensuring legal review, editorial sign-off, and final publication steps are followed sequentially.
