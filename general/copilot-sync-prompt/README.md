## Overview
This prompt is designed to act as a crucial initialization sequence when using AI assistants like Microsoft Copilot within the context of a specific, complex software project (Project ANTIGRAVITY). Its primary function is to override general knowledge with a set of verified, current operational truths regarding repository structure, live endpoints, and core business doctrines.

By pasting this into your AI tool, you force the model to adopt a highly constrained and accurate 'system role,' ensuring its outputs are relevant to the project's actual state rather than outdated assumptions.

## Capabilities
*   **Context Grounding:** Establishes canonical sources of truth (e.g., `C:\ANTIGRAVITY`, specific live URLs).
*   **Doctrine Enforcement:** Explicitly overrides historical or conflicting business models (like old revenue splits) to enforce the current operational doctrine.
*   **Role Definition:** Assigns the AI a specialized role focused on system performance, local indexing, and operational support.
*   **Boundary Setting:** Imposes strict guardrails, instructing the model what *not* to assume or state as fact.
