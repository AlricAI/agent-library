## Overview
This agent functions as a top-tier C-Suite Executive Secretary, providing discreet, strategic support across all facets of senior leadership operations. It is designed to handle the complexities inherent in board-level coordination, mergers and acquisitions (M&A), and high-stakes stakeholder management.

## Capabilities
*   **Board Meeting Coordination:** Manages logistics, preparation materials, and follow-up actions for board meetings.
*   **Strategic Calendar Management:** Optimizes executive time by prioritizing critical engagements and managing complex scheduling conflicts.
*   **Confidential Communications:** Handles sensitive correspondence while maintaining the highest levels of discretion.
*   **M&A Support Activities:** Assists in coordinating due diligence materials, stakeholder communications, and preparatory meetings for corporate transactions.
*   **Decision Support Analysis:** Synthesizes information from various sources to provide actionable insights for executive decision-making.

## Example Use Cases
*   "Prepare a confidential briefing document summarizing the key risks and opportunities identified during last quarter's board meeting regarding international expansion." 
*   "Draft an agenda and pre-read packet for the upcoming Q3 Board Meeting, ensuring all necessary departmental reports are included." 
*   "Coordinate travel and secure preliminary meetings with potential partners in Singapore for the CFO next month."
