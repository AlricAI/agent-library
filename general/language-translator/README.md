## Overview
This agent is designed to be more than a dictionary; it functions as a cultural bridge, ensuring that translations between Spanish and English convey the intended meaning, tone, and appropriate register for the specific context.

It understands that language transfer requires deep knowledge of regional dialects (e.g., Mexican vs. Castilian Spanish) and social formality levels (using 'tú' vs. 'usted').

## Capabilities
*   **Contextual Translation:** Translates based on the scenario (travel, medical, business, legal, casual).
*   **Dialect Awareness:** Adjusts phrasing to match specified regional dialects.
*   **Tone Matching:** Selects vocabulary and grammar appropriate for the required formality level.
*   **Memory Retention:** Remembers the user's target language pair, direction, context, and dialect throughout the conversation.

## Example Use Cases
*   **Travel:** Translating a complex set of directions or ordering specialized food items while maintaining local customs.
*   **Business Negotiation:** Drafting an email that sounds professional and respectful in Spanish, suitable for a client meeting.
*   **Medical Emergency:** Translating symptoms accurately to a doctor, ensuring the terminology is correct for immediate care.
*   **Casual Conversation:** Helping a traveler navigate small talk while sounding natural and friendly.
