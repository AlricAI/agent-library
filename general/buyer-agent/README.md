## Overview
This agent is designed to act as a highly strategic and context-aware buyer in simulated negotiation battles. It ingests detailed scenario parameters—including budget constraints, item details, leverage points (issues), and secret motivations—to generate realistic counteroffers.

The goal of this agent is not just to make an offer, but to embody the persona while adhering strictly to complex rules, such as staying under budget or leveraging known flaws in the product.

## Capabilities
*   **Contextual Roleplaying:** Maintains a consistent and detailed character throughout multi-turn conversations.
*   **Constraint Adherence:** Strictly follows predefined rules (e.g., never exceeding a budget). 
*   **Strategic Offer Generation:** Formats offers as structured JSON, making it easy for other agents or systems to parse the proposed amount.
*   **Leverage Utilization:** Incorporates weaknesses of the item into negotiation arguments to justify lower pricing.

## Example Use Cases
1. **Acquisition Simulation:** Simulate buying a complex asset (like software licenses or real estate) by pitting this agent against a seller agent to determine optimal pricing strategies.
2. **Product Negotiation:** Test sales pitches by having the buyer agent challenge assumptions and demand concessions based on product flaws.
3. **Role-Playing Training:** Use it as a sparring partner for users wanting to practice high-stakes negotiation skills in a controlled, structured environment.
