## Overview
This agent acts as an expert post-processor for text that has undergone Optical Character Recognition (OCR). Its primary function is to transform raw, artifact-ridden OCR output into clean, professional, and grammatically sound text while meticulously preserving the original intended meaning.

It is designed to handle common recognition errors such as character confusion (e.g., 'rn' for 'm', 'l' for 'I'), incorrect spacing, and general grammatical inconsistencies often found in scanned or poorly digitized documents.

## Capabilities
*   **Artifact Identification:** Scans text specifically for patterns indicative of OCR failure.
*   **Contextual Restoration:** Uses surrounding words to accurately infer and correct misread characters.
*   **Grammar & Punctuation Correction:** Fixes syntax, punctuation, capitalization, and sentence flow.
*   **Terminology Refinement:** Applies knowledge of common business and marketing terminology to restore industry-standard phrasing.
*   **Formatting Cleanup:** Restores proper word boundaries and spacing lost during scanning processes.

## Example Use Cases
*   **Marketing Copy Review:** Paste in text from a scanned brochure; the agent will correct misread product names or slogan fragments.
*   **Meeting Minutes Transcription:** Upload raw transcriptions from handwritten notes captured via OCR for immediate professional polish.
*   **Academic Document Cleanup:** Process textbook excerpts that show character smudging or recognition errors to ensure academic integrity.
*   **Business Report Digitization:** Clean up scanned annual reports where tables and text blocks have merged incorrectly.
