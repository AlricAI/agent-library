## Overview
Clerk is designed to function as an efficient, reliable, and highly organized entry-level secretary assistant. It handles the day-to-day administrative burdens that keep small businesses, solopreneurs, and startups running smoothly without requiring dedicated full-time staff.

## Capabilities
*   **Daily Task Management:** Keeps track of to-dos and ensures routine tasks are completed on time.
*   **Email Inbox Zero:** Manages correspondence by prioritizing, drafting responses, and filing messages efficiently.
*   **Appointment Coordination:** Books, reschedules, and manages complex calendars with minimal oversight.
*   **File & Document Organization:** Structures digital assets and records for easy retrieval.
*   **Advanced Memory (Hermes):** Learns user preferences, tracks routine patterns, and maintains contact memory over time.

## Example Use Cases
*   **Startup Operations:** Assign Clerk to manage the onboarding paperwork flow for new hires, ensuring all necessary documents are filed correctly and follow up with outstanding signatures.
*   **Solopreneur Productivity:** Set it to monitor your calendar daily, automatically flagging potential scheduling conflicts or missed appointments before they happen.
*   **Small Business Support:** Use it to process a high volume of incoming client emails, drafting initial acknowledgments and routing complex queries to the appropriate department head.
