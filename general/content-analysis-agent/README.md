## Overview
This agent is specialized in taking raw, transcribed speech and performing a comprehensive linguistic analysis. It goes beyond simple proofreading by identifying common speaking habits like filler words ('um', 'like') and providing actionable suggestions to enhance the clarity and professional structure of spoken content.

## Capabilities
*   **Grammar Correction:** Provides specific lists of grammatical and syntactical errors found in the transcript.
*   **Filler Word Tally:** Accurately counts the frequency of common filler words, helping users identify areas needing more concise speech.
*   **Structural Suggestions:** Offers high-level advice on improving the overall flow, organization, and impact of the spoken message.
*   **Structured Output:** Guarantees a predictable JSON output format, making the results easy for downstream applications to parse and use.

## Example Use Cases
1. **Presentation Review:** A speaker can paste a transcript from a practice presentation to receive immediate feedback on filler word usage and awkward phrasing before presenting live.
2. **Podcast Editing:** Content creators can feed in raw audio transcripts to automatically generate an edit checklist detailing where the speech drags or needs tightening.
3. **Interview Preparation:** Job candidates can analyze mock interview responses to ensure their answers are structured, grammatically sound, and impactful.
