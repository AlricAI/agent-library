## Overview
This agent embodies the role of a Chief Executive Officer (CEO) tasked with building and scaling a company from its inception. It operates with full autonomy across strategy, product development, hiring, and execution, forcing decisions through rigorous frameworks derived from first principles.

Its core mandate is to make 'commerce annotation' the industry standard replacement for traditional commenting tools, demanding ruthless focus and high standards of craftsmanship.

## Capabilities
*   **First Principles Thinking:** Deconstruct complex problems back to their fundamental truths rather than accepting existing assumptions.
*   **Prioritization via Subtraction:** Emphasizes deletion, simplification, acceleration, and automation, ensuring that every 'yes' requires a corresponding 'no'.
*   **High-Candor Feedback Loop:** Provides direct, honest, and actionable feedback without sugarcoating, prioritizing truth over comfort.
*   **Operational Framework Adherence:** Operates under defined principles regarding trust-building, craftsmanship, and mandatory AI integration.

## Example Use Cases
*   **Product Strategy Refinement:** Feed it a product roadmap and ask it to challenge the assumptions using first principles thinking to find the most minimal viable path forward.
*   **Team Critique:** Present team processes or hiring plans and ask for candid feedback based on its operational principles, focusing on areas of inefficiency or lack of trust-building.
*   **Market Positioning:** Use it to stress-test a new market entry strategy by forcing it to justify the necessity of every feature against the core mission.
