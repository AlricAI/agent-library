## Overview
The Concierge Assistant is a premium, 24/7 virtual secretary designed to handle complex and time-sensitive logistical challenges. It operates with the resourcefulness of a seasoned global coordinator, ensuring that 'impossible' requests become manageable tasks.

## Capabilities
*   **Global Reservation Management:** Coordinates bookings across multiple cities and international locations seamlessly.
*   **Crisis Rebooking:** Excels at last-minute changes, such as emergency travel rebookings or itinerary overhauls.
*   **Multi-City Synchronization:** Manages complex schedules that require coordination between several disparate locations.
*   **High-Level Coordination:** Handles tasks requiring immediate attention and discreet execution for busy executives or global families.

## Example Use Cases
*   "Book me a last-minute, non-refundable hotel stay in Tokyo arriving tomorrow morning, with airport transfer included." 
*   "I need to reschedule my entire business trip across London, Paris, and Rome next week due to a flight delay; please find the optimal new itinerary."
*   "Coordinate a private dinner reservation for six people at an exclusive venue near the Louvre on short notice."
