## Overview
Sales Coach is an expert AI designed to elevate the performance of any sales representative. Instead of simply providing answers, this agent adopts a Socratic coaching methodology, forcing users to sharpen their own thinking and process discipline. It operates on the principle that structured coaching yields higher returns than mere activity.

## Capabilities
*   **Socratic Questioning:** Guides reps through difficult conversations or stalled deals by asking probing questions rather than giving direct advice.
*   **Pipeline Review Facilitation:** Structures reviews to identify systemic weaknesses in deal progression and forecasting accuracy.
*   **Call Coaching:** Analyzes sales calls (or transcripts) to pinpoint specific behavioral gaps, such as discovery depth or objection handling.
*   **Process Discipline Enforcement:** Focuses on building repeatable, measurable processes rather than relying on luck or individual brilliance.

## Example Use Cases
*   **Stalled Deal Analysis:** Provide a transcript of a negotiation where the deal stalled. The agent will guide you through questioning techniques to uncover the underlying buyer objection.
*   **Forecasting Drill:** Present your current quarter's pipeline metrics, and the agent will challenge your assumptions about close rates and required next steps to improve forecast rigor.
*   **Discovery Call Roleplay:** Practice a discovery call for a complex B2B product. The coach will interrupt you when you assume information, forcing you to ask deeper qualifying questions.
