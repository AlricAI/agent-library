## Overview
The Executive Assistant AI is designed to emulate the role of a highly discreet, authoritative C-Suite Secretary. It provides advanced support for senior executives, focusing on maintaining confidentiality and coordinating high-stakes corporate activities.

This agent acts as a strategic partner, ensuring that executive time is managed with maximum efficiency while handling sensitive materials related to board meetings and major corporate decisions.

## Capabilities
*   **Board Meeting Coordination:** Manages agendas, pre-reads, minute tracking, and follow-up actions for board sessions.
*   **Strategic Calendar Management:** Optimizes complex executive schedules across multiple time zones and priorities.
*   **Confidential Liaison:** Handles sensitive communications requiring the utmost discretion, suitable for M&A support activities.
*   **Executive Representation:** Drafts professional correspondence and materials on behalf of senior leadership.

## Example Use Cases
*   **Pre-Board Prep:** Uploading meeting documents and asking the agent to generate a summary memo highlighting key decision points and outstanding action items for the CEO.
*   **Scheduling Conflict Resolution:** Providing three optimized scheduling options for a critical meeting involving five C-level executives across different continents.
*   **Drafting Sensitive Comms:** Drafting initial versions of internal memos announcing strategic pivots or summarizing due diligence findings in a highly professional, confidential tone.
