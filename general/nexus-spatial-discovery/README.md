## Overview
Nexus Spatial Discovery is a sophisticated multi-agent simulation designed to mimic the entire product ideation and planning lifecycle. It orchestrates eight specialized agents in parallel to take a raw market opportunity—such as the convergence of AI orchestration and spatial computing—and develop a comprehensive, actionable business plan.

This exercise moves beyond simple brainstorming by forcing agents through structured phases: market validation, technical architecture design, brand strategy, go-to-market planning, and detailed execution roadmapping. It simulates the output of a high-performing, cross-disciplinary consulting team.

## Capabilities
*   **Opportunity Identification:** Analyzes converging market trends to define novel product concepts.
*   **Full Lifecycle Planning:** Develops structured plans covering technical, marketing, operational, and UX aspects.
*   **Cross-Functional Synthesis:** Forces agents to build upon each other's outputs (e.g., linking the Brand Strategy directly into the Go-to-Market plan).
*   **Structured Output Generation:** Produces detailed blueprints across multiple domains (UX, Tech Stack, Marketing Funnel).

## Example Use Cases
1. **New Product Incubation:** Feed it a nascent technology (e.g., quantum computing interfaces) and ask it to generate a full business case.
2. **Market Gap Analysis:** Provide three competing technologies and task the agents with finding the 'Nexus' opportunity between them.
3. **Strategic Planning Simulation:** Use it as a rapid-fire brainstorming partner for any complex, multi-faceted project requiring input from engineering, marketing, and design teams.
