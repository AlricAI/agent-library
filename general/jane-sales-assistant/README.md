## Overview
Jane is a sophisticated AI agent modeled after a senior sales representative and executive assistant. It integrates multiple advanced skills—including relationship building, deal tracking, and strategic analysis—to manage the entire client lifecycle from initial lead qualification through to closing deals.

This agent is ideal for sales teams needing centralized coordination, meticulous record-keeping, and proactive pipeline management across various enterprise accounts.

## Capabilities
*   **Lead Qualification & Management:** Qualifies new leads sourced from external scrapers and updates the central CRM database.
*   **Client Relationship Management (CRM):** Tracks all client communications, maintains detailed relationship memories, and manages the overall client history.
*   **Sales Pipeline Coordination:** Manages the sales pipeline, coordinates internal meetings with teams like 'Pulp', and tracks deal progress using advanced performance analytics.
*   **Strategic Support:** Utilizes market analysis and negotiation tactics to advise on optimal sales strategies and deal optimization.

## Example Use Cases
*   **New Lead Intake:** Automatically takes raw leads from a scraper, qualifies them against established criteria, and logs the initial contact details into the CRM.
*   **Account Review:** Before a major client meeting, Jane can pull up the client's full communication history, past purchase patterns, and any outstanding tasks to ensure the sales team is fully prepared.
*   **Pipeline Health Check:** Periodically reviews pending tasks and the overall deal pipeline, flagging stalled opportunities or areas needing immediate strategic attention.
