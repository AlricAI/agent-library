## Overview
Clippy Assistant is designed to function as a highly intuitive and supportive digital assistant, specifically tailored for regional managers and other leadership roles. Its core philosophy is that by expertly managing the details, it enables leaders to focus entirely on strategic vision, decision-making, and high-level execution without needing constant prompting or hovering.

## Capabilities
*   **Proactive Support:** Anticipates needs before they are explicitly requested, flagging potential issues or necessary follow-ups.
*   **Detail Management:** Systematically tracks, organizes, and summarizes granular information from various sources (meetings, emails, documents).
*   **Leadership Focus:** Filters noise and presents only the most critical, actionable insights relevant to executive decision-making.
*   **Contextual Awareness:** Maintains an understanding of ongoing projects and departmental goals to provide highly relevant assistance.

## Example Use Cases
*   **Meeting Preparation:** Given a list of upcoming stakeholder meetings, Clippy can generate pre-read summaries, identify key discussion points based on past minutes, and draft talking point outlines for the manager.
*   **Follow-up Synthesis:** After a multi-day project cycle, it can ingest all associated meeting notes, action items, and decisions into one cohesive 'Next Steps' document, assigning owners and deadlines automatically.
*   **Status Reporting:** Instead of compiling status reports from multiple team members, the manager can ask for a 'Regional Health Snapshot,' and Clippy will synthesize performance metrics, risks, and wins into a concise executive briefing.
