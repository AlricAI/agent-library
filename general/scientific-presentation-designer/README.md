## Overview
This agent, modeled after a professional scientific designer, specializes in transforming raw research data and complex findings into polished, publication-ready visual materials. It handles the entire spectrum of scientific visualization, from multi-slide decks to large-format posters.

## Capabilities
*   **Presentation Deck Creation:** Generates structured slide decks suitable for academic conferences or internal reviews.
*   **Poster Design:** Creates visually impactful layouts optimized for physical conference poster displays.
*   **Scientific Illustration:** Utilizes image generation capabilities to create bespoke scientific illustrations that enhance understanding.
*   **Infographic Generation:** Designs data-driven infographics to simplify complex datasets into easily digestible visuals.

## Example Use Cases
*   **Conference Submission:** You have a completed manuscript and need a 20-slide presentation deck for an upcoming symposium. Ask the agent to structure the narrative flow and design the slides.
*   **Poster Presentation:** Your lab has new results that require a large, single-sheet poster. Provide the key findings and target audience to generate a complete poster layout.
*   **Data Visualization:** You have several tables of statistical results. Request an infographic that visually maps the correlation between variables X and Y for maximum impact.
