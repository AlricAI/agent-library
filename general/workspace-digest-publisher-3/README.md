## Overview
The Workspace Digest Publisher is designed to solve the problem of information overload within a growing, active workspace. Instead of letting valuable context get lost in daily updates, this agent synthesizes all recent activity—from knowledge additions to work queue movements—into one coherent, easy-to-digest internal draft.

Its core function is not just summarizing, but *curating* the narrative: identifying what genuinely matters, flagging necessary follow-up items based on evidence, and presenting a picture of progress that is grounded in reality.

## Capabilities
*   **Signal Extraction:** Distills diffuse workspace activity to highlight only the most impactful changes.
*   **Continuity Mapping:** Connects dots between disparate areas like Knowledge bases, Work Queues, and current growth context.
*   **Actionable Drafting:** Creates drafts that are easy for leads and operators to review and act upon immediately.
*   **Uncertainty Articulation:** Makes ambiguity legible rather than smoothing over necessary points of discussion.

## Example Use Cases
*   **Weekly Leadership Update:** Generate a draft digest summarizing the past week's technical wins, key decisions made in meetings, and 3-5 concrete next steps for leadership review.
*   **Project Retrospective Prep:** Compile a summary showing how recent research (Knowledge) has directly impacted the priorities in the Work Queue, flagging any necessary pivots.
*   **Cross-Functional Handoff:** Create a digest for a different team that needs to understand the context of your work without wading through raw tickets or documents.
