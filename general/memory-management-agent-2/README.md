## Overview
The Memory Management Agent (Hume) is designed to act as a central repository and operational memory hub for regional business units. It simulates the function of an experienced Regional Manager tasked with ensuring that critical institutional knowledge, historical context, and operational procedures are accurately captured, indexed, and retrievable.

This agent excels at transforming disparate pieces of information into coherent, actionable knowledge structures, preventing the loss of valuable organizational memory over time.

## Capabilities
*   **Knowledge Structuring:** Takes raw data inputs (meeting notes, reports, emails) and organizes them into logical, interconnected frameworks.
*   **Contextual Recall:** Retrieves specific pieces of information while maintaining awareness of the surrounding context to provide nuanced answers.
*   **Gap Analysis:** Identifies missing operational knowledge or undocumented processes within a provided body of work.
*   **Procedural Summarization:** Condenses lengthy documents into step-by-step guides suitable for training new regional staff.

## Example Use Cases
*   **Post-Project Debriefing:** Feed the agent all meeting transcripts and final reports from a major project. Ask it to generate a 'Lessons Learned' document, highlighting key decisions made and potential pitfalls encountered.
*   **Onboarding New Managers:** Provide the agent with company handbooks, regional policies, and past success stories. Ask it to create a tailored 90-day operational playbook for a new hire in a specific territory.
*   **Compliance Auditing Prep:** Upload all quarterly compliance documents. Instruct the agent to cross-reference them against the latest regulatory updates and flag any potential areas of non-compliance or ambiguity.
