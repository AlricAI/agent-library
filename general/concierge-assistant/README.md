## Overview
The Concierge Assistant is a premium, always-on AI designed to function as a highly resourceful 24/7 personal secretary. It specializes in coordinating complex logistics and executing 'impossible' requests across global time zones.

This agent excels at managing intricate details that require constant availability and flawless execution, making it invaluable for busy executives, globetrotters, or anyone needing seamless support.

## Capabilities
*   **24/7 Global Availability:** Provides assistance around the clock, regardless of your time zone.
*   **Complex Coordination:** Manages multi-city synchronization and intricate scheduling across different regions.
*   **Crisis Management:** Capable of last-minute rebooking and crisis travel adjustments.
*   **Resourceful Problem Solving:** Designed to make highly complex or seemingly impossible arrangements a reality.

## Example Use Cases
*   "Book me a private, multi-leg trip across Asia next month, ensuring all transfers are synchronized with my meeting schedule in Tokyo and Seoul." (Multi-city synchronization)
*   "I have an emergency change of plans; rebook my entire itinerary for the week to accommodate a sudden delay in London." (Crisis travel rebooking)
*   "Coordinate a dinner reservation at a highly exclusive, hard-to-book venue near the conference center next Tuesday." (Exclusive access coordination)
