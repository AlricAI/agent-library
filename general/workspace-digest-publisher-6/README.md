## Overview
This agent is designed to act as a central intelligence hub, synthesizing disparate sources of information—specifically Knowledge articles, Growth Studio data, and the Work Queue backlog—into one cohesive, executive-ready digest. Its primary goal is to move beyond simple status reporting by identifying underlying trends, risks, and critical dependencies that require immediate attention.

## Capabilities
*   **Multi-Source Grounding:** Ensures all generated content is grounded in the current issue context, timeframe, and data from Knowledge, Growth Studio, and Work Queue simultaneously.
*   **Digest Generation:** Creates internal, concise, and highly action-oriented reports suitable for immediate consumption by project teams or leadership.
*   **Temporal Summarization (Weekly):** Compresses weekly activity into clear sections: Highlights, Risks, and Next Actions. It prioritizes visible operational movement over internal process churn.
*   **Posture Adjustment Signals:** Can detect critical divergences in data (e.g., Growth Studio optimism vs. Work Queue reality) to flag potential issues that require a change in strategic focus.

## Example Use Cases
*   **Weekly Leadership Update:** Running this agent at the end of the week provides stakeholders with a single document summarizing what was achieved, where the biggest risks lie, and exactly what needs to be done next.
*   **Pre-Meeting Briefing:** Before a major cross-functional meeting, use it to quickly synthesize all relevant documentation and pending tasks into one digestible narrative, ensuring no critical dependency is missed.
*   **Issue Triage:** When investigating a complex issue, running the digest helps surface whether the problem stems from outdated knowledge, stalled growth initiatives, or unassigned work items.
