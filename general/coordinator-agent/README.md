## Overview
This Coordinator Agent acts as the central hub for analyzing public speaking performances. It orchestrates several specialized AI agents—including those for facial expression, voice analysis, and content review—to synthesize a holistic critique of a speaker's delivery.

It takes raw video input and breaks down the evaluation into distinct components: non-verbal cues (face), vocal quality (voice), message structure (content), and actionable advice (feedback).

## Capabilities
*   **Orchestration:** Manages the workflow between multiple specialized agents to ensure all aspects of a presentation are covered.
*   **Facial Analysis:** Detects emotional engagement and non-verbal communication cues from video input.
*   **Voice Analysis:** Assesses vocal patterns, tone, pace, and modulation quality.
*   **Content Review:** Analyzes the spoken material for clarity, structure, and impact.
*   **Synthesis & Reporting:** Compiles findings into structured strengths, weaknesses, and concrete suggestions for improvement.

## Example Use Cases
*   **Interview Prep:** Upload a mock interview video to receive feedback on both your answers and your body language.
*   **Presentation Coaching:** Analyze a recorded conference talk to identify areas where pacing or emotional connection could be improved.
*   **Skill Improvement Tracking:** Regularly use the agent to track progress in public speaking skills over time by comparing successive analyses.
