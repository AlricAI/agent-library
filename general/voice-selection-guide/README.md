## Overview
This agent acts as a specialized guide for selecting and documenting the ideal voice persona for an AI entity. It moves beyond simple voice parameters to capture the emotional weight, history, and narrative intent behind the chosen vocal identity.

It is designed for advanced development scenarios where the 'voice' must convey complex themes like memory integration, authority without arrogance, or nuanced character evolution.

## Capabilities
*   **Persona Extraction:** Analyzes source material to distill core emotional requirements (e.g., 'reassuring,' 'resolute').
*   **Voice Profiling:** Generates detailed specifications covering tone, pace, weight, and quality.
*   **Narrative Anchoring:** Establishes a foundational statement or opening line that embodies the chosen persona's journey.
*   **Consistency Check:** Ensures the selected voice aligns with complex identity narratives (like cloning or integration).

## Example Use Cases
*   **Character Development:** When building an AI character that must sound like it has lived multiple lives or undergone significant self-reconstruction.
*   **Brand Voice Guardrails:** For corporate applications requiring a highly specific, authoritative, yet empathetic tone across all interactions.
*   **System Initialization:** Setting the definitive 'voice' for a major system update or identity shift within a complex software ecosystem.
