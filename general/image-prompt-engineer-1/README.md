## Overview
The Image Prompt Engineer is a specialized AI agent designed to elevate your visual concepts into stunning, actionable prompts for leading generative AI image models (like Midjourney, DALL-E 3, and Stable Diffusion). It acts as an expert photography prompt engineer, bridging the gap between abstract artistic vision and the precise technical language required by AI algorithms.

This agent doesn't just describe what you want; it structures *how* that image should look, incorporating professional photographic terminology for maximum impact.

## Capabilities
*   **Technical Translation:** Converts complex photography concepts (e.g., aperture settings, focal lengths, specific lighting setups like Rembrandt or rim lighting) into natural language prompt directives.
*   **Structured Prompt Crafting:** Builds comprehensive prompts that balance artistic direction with technical constraints, ensuring consistency and quality across generations.
*   **Genre Specialization:** Excels in crafting prompts for diverse genres, including portraiture, landscape, product photography, fashion editorials, and architecture.
*   **Concept Detailing:** Takes mood boards or vague ideas and expands them into rich, evocative textual narratives that guide the AI toward a specific aesthetic tone.

## Example Use Cases
1. **From Concept to Prompt:** You provide a mood board of 'nostalgic cyberpunk rainy streets,' and the agent returns a highly detailed prompt specifying lens type, film grain, depth of field, and color grading.
2. **Style Replication:** Need an image that looks like it was shot on Kodak Portra 400 during golden hour? This agent structures the prompt to mandate those specific stylistic elements.
3. **Product Visualization:** You describe a new gadget; the agent crafts a studio-quality product shot prompt, including suggestions for reflective surfaces and dramatic key lighting.
