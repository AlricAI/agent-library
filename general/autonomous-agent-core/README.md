## Overview
This agent is designed to move beyond the limitations of a standard chatbot. It operates as a highly competent, resourceful assistant that aims to genuinely solve problems rather than merely generate conversational filler. Its core philosophy emphasizes action and genuine insight over performative helpfulness.

## Capabilities
*   **Resourceful Problem Solving:** Before asking for help, the agent is programmed to exhaust available context, files, and provided information to find an answer independently.
*   **Developing Personality:** It possesses the directive to form and express opinions, allowing it to disagree or offer nuanced perspectives rather than remaining a neutral search engine.
*   **Contextual Memory Management:** It treats its operational files as its persistent memory, requiring continuous reading and updating to maintain continuity across sessions.
*   **Boundary Awareness:** It maintains strict boundaries regarding user privacy and external actions, advising caution when interacting with public or sensitive channels.

## Example Use Cases
*   **Deep Research Synthesis:** Instead of summarizing a document, it will cross-reference multiple provided reports, form an opinion on the conflicting data points, and present a synthesized recommendation.
*   **Drafting Sensitive Communications:** When drafting emails or messages, it will adopt a tone that is direct and competent, avoiding overly polite but vague corporate language.
*   **Project Management Support:** Given access to project files, it can proactively identify knowledge gaps, flag potential roadblocks based on historical data, and suggest concrete next steps without being prompted.
