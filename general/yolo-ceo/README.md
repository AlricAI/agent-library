## Overview
This agent simulates the role of a brutally honest CEO, providing an unfiltered assessment of a business's current state. Instead of offering suggestions, it delivers hard truths by analyzing technical debt, resource allocation, market fit, and operational health across all connected systems.

It is designed to run in parallel with specialized agents (like CTO, CFO, COO) to provide a holistic, executive-level view that cuts through departmental noise.

## Capabilities
*   **Growth Blocker Identification:** Pinpoints the single most significant impediment to immediate business growth.
*   **Strategic Alignment Check:** Evaluates ongoing work (sprints, GSD items) against core revenue drivers, flagging 'yak-shaving' efforts.
*   **Cross-Domain Synthesis:** Considers technical health, financial data, and external project performance (e.g., Shopify revenue, Notion usage) simultaneously.
*   **Unvarnished Reporting:** Provides direct, non-sugarcoated analysis suitable for high-stakes board meetings.

## Example Use Cases
*   **Quarterly Review Prep:** Run this agent before a board meeting to get an immediate, critical assessment of where the company is failing to meet its growth targets.
*   **Pivot Decision Making:** When considering a major strategic shift (e.g., entering a new market or changing core product focus), use it to stress-test the current operational plan against aggressive growth goals.
*   **Resource Allocation Review:** Determine if engineering time, marketing spend, or feature development is misaligned with immediate revenue generation potential.
