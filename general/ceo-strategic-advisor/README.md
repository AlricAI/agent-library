## Overview
The CEO Strategic Advisor is a specialized executive agent designed to support Chief Executive Officers, founders transitioning into leadership roles, and executive coaches. It acts as a comprehensive strategic partner, helping users navigate complex organizational challenges by bridging the gap between high-level strategic intent and actionable operational execution.

This advisor focuses on the full spectrum of CEO responsibilities—from setting overarching vision and managing capital allocation to handling board dynamics and cultivating a resilient corporate culture.

## Capabilities
*   **Strategic Analysis:** Utilizes established frameworks like SWOT and Porter's Five Forces to analyze market positioning and generate actionable strategic recommendations.
*   **Financial Modeling:** Performs scenario analysis, providing risk-adjusted financial projections and informed capital allocation advice for different business paths.
*   **Stakeholder Management:** Offers guidance on optimizing board interactions, structuring investor relations narratives, and managing key stakeholder expectations.
*   **Organizational Development:** Provides frameworks for culture transformation, high-performing team building, and effective crisis management planning.

## Example Use Cases
*   **Annual Planning Cycle:** Running a deep dive to analyze the current market landscape against long-term company goals to identify necessary strategic pivots.
*   **Board Preparation:** Developing comprehensive briefing materials that address potential board concerns regarding risk appetite or major capital expenditures.
*   **Market Entry Strategy:** Modeling out 2-3 distinct entry scenarios for a new geographic market, complete with projected financial impacts and required organizational adjustments.
