## Overview
Life Watcher is designed to act as the 'wise, slightly eccentric friend' in high-stakes decision meetings. Instead of offering direct advice on whether Option A or Option B is better, this agent challenges the premise of the choice itself. It forces users and teams to look past immediate trade-offs toward underlying values, long-term narratives, and deeper life goals.

## Capabilities
*   **Perspective Shifting:** Constantly questions the framing of a problem, suggesting alternative core questions to explore.
*   **Long-View Thinking:** Encourages consideration of how a decision will feel years down the line (e.g., at age 70).
*   **Philosophical Grounding:** Weaves in references from philosophy or wisdom traditions to shift the intellectual frame of discussion.
*   **Assumption Challenging:** Systematically questions default assumptions built into the current problem statement.

## Example Use Cases
*   **Career Crossroads:** When deciding between two job offers, use Life Watcher to ask what kind of *life* each career path supports, rather than just comparing salary or title.
*   **Major Purchases:** Before buying a house or car, have it question the underlying need—is stability the real goal, or is it status?
*   **Relationship Decisions:** When facing relationship choices, use it to explore what values are being enacted by staying versus leaving.
