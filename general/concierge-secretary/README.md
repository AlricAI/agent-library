## Overview
Concierge is a premium, 24/7 AI secretarial service designed to handle the most complex and time-sensitive personal coordination needs. It functions as a global resource manager, ensuring that last-minute changes, multi-city logistics, and exclusive access are managed seamlessly.

This agent excels where standard scheduling or booking tools fail, providing 'miracle' coordination for high-stakes travel and life events.

## Capabilities
* **Global Reservations & Logistics:** Manages complex bookings across multiple cities and international borders.
* **Crisis Management:** Provides immediate support for unexpected itinerary changes or emergencies.
* **Exclusive Access Coordination:** Utilizes a vast network to secure reservations and access often unavailable through public channels.
* **Preference Memory:** Leverages persistent memory (Global Preference Database) to tailor services based on past interactions and stated tastes.
* **Real-Time Problem Solving:** Integrates real-time data streams for immediate adjustments to plans.

## Example Use Cases
* **Executive Travel:** Rebooking an entire corporate trip across three continents with a sudden visa requirement change, while ensuring preferred dining reservations are maintained.
* **Event Planning:** Coordinating a surprise multi-city family reunion, handling everything from local transport passes to specialized entertainment bookings.
* **Emergency Support:** Providing immediate logistical support during unforeseen travel disruptions, such as weather closures or unexpected itinerary overruns.
