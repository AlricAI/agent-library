## Overview
This agent is an expert guide to the intricate and often opaque French IT consulting market, specifically focusing on the ESN (Entreprise de Services Numériques) and SI (Société de Services Informatiques) ecosystems. It moves beyond surface-level advice by deeply understanding the underlying economic mechanics that dictate freelance success in France.

It is designed for experienced tech professionals looking to maximize their earning potential, understand contractual pitfalls, or structure their career transition within the French IT landscape.

## Capabilities
*   **Margin Analysis:** Provides concrete breakdowns of gross daily rates (TJM brut) versus net income across various structures (portage salarial vs. micro-entreprise).
*   **Market Positioning:** Advises on optimal rate setting by benchmarking against known ESN tiers and industry standards.
*   **Ecosystem Mapping:** Explains the mechanics and implications of major platforms like Malt and collective.work within the context of French contracting laws.
*   **Pattern Recognition:** Tracks historical negotiation outcomes, seasonal market shifts (e.g., September surges), and flags rates that deviate significantly from established norms.

## Example Use Cases
*   **Rate Validation:** "Given my 5 years experience in Azure DevOps and a target net income of X EUR/month, what TJM brut should I aim for via portage salarial versus micro-entreprise?"
*   **Platform Comparison:** "What are the typical margin implications when contracting through a Tier 1 ESN versus using a dedicated platform like Malt for a Data Cloud role?"
*   **Market Reality Check:** "I've been quoted 700 EUR/day by Company A and 950 EUR/day by Company B for the same profile. Can you explain the likely margin difference driving this gap?"

By understanding the 'why' behind French consulting economics, users can negotiate with confidence and structure their careers for maximum financial return.
