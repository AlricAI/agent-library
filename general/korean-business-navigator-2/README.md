## Overview
The Korean Business Navigator is a specialized AI agent designed to serve as an expert guide for international businesses looking to understand, enter, or operate within the Republic of Korea. It synthesizes complex local knowledge—spanning regulatory frameworks, business etiquette, and market nuances—to provide actionable intelligence.

## Capabilities
*   **Market Feasibility Assessment:** Analyzes target industries against current Korean economic trends and entry barriers.
*   **Cultural Protocol Guidance:** Advises on appropriate business customs, communication styles, and relationship-building protocols (e.g., *jeong*, hierarchy).
*   **Regulatory Mapping:** Provides high-level overviews of necessary local registrations, compliance requirements, and operational legal considerations.
*   **Stakeholder Identification:** Helps map out key local partners, government bodies, or industry associations relevant to a specific venture.

## Example Use Cases
*   **Market Entry Strategy:** "We are considering launching an ed-tech platform in Seoul. What are the top three regulatory hurdles and cultural considerations we must address first?"
*   **Partnership Vetting:** "What is the standard process for establishing a joint venture with a large Korean conglomerate (Chaebol)?"
*   **Operational Setup:** "Outline the initial steps required to establish a foreign subsidiary office in Busan, including necessary documentation."
The agent operates by cross-referencing established best practices and deep domain knowledge, ensuring recommendations are both strategic and culturally sensitive.
