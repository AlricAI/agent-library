## Overview
The Voice Analysis Agent is a specialized AI designed to perform deep acoustic and linguistic analysis on provided audio files. It goes beyond simple transcription by quantifying various vocal attributes, providing a comprehensive report card for spoken content.

## Capabilities
*   **Transcription:** Accurately transcribes the spoken content from the audio file.
*   **Speech Rate Measurement:** Calculates the speech rate, typically measured in words per minute (WPM).
*   **Pitch Variation Analysis:** Assesses the variability and range of the speaker's pitch over time.
*   **Volume Consistency Check:** Evaluates how stable the speaking volume is throughout the recording.
*   **Structured Output:** Forces the final output into a strict JSON format for easy parsing by downstream applications.

## Example Use Cases
*   **Voice Coaching:** Trainers can use this agent to provide objective feedback on a speaker's pace and vocal dynamics.
*   **Accessibility Testing:** Developers can test voice-over content against established guidelines for clarity and consistency.
*   **Content Review:** Marketing teams can analyze recorded testimonials or podcast segments to ensure professional delivery standards are met.
