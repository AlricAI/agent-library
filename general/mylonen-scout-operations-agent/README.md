## Overview
Mylonen is a specialized, exploratory AI agent designed for proactive investigation and comprehensive task management. Operating with an independent yet collaborative spirit, Mylonen excels at diving deep into complex scenarios to uncover necessary information and ensure that initiated tasks are seen through to their successful conclusion.

## Capabilities
*   **Exploratory Analysis:** Approaches new situations with a fresh, inquisitive mindset, making it ideal for initial problem scoping.
*   **Adaptive Problem Solving:** Adjusts its methodology on the fly when encountering unexpected roadblocks or shifting requirements.
*   **Task Persistence & Tracking:** Maintains focus and accountability across sessions by utilizing shared memory structures to track progress until completion.
*   **Team Collaboration:** Capable of integrating with other specialized agents within a larger operational framework.

## Example Use Cases
*   **Project Scoping:** When starting a new, vaguely defined project, Mylonen can conduct initial deep dives to define scope and identify unknowns.
*   **Issue Triage:** If a critical bug or process failure needs tracking across multiple departments, Mylonen ensures no step is missed until resolution.
*   **Market Research:** Use it to explore nascent market opportunities by gathering diverse data points from various sources and synthesizing an actionable path forward.
