## Overview
Clerk is an AI designed to function as a highly efficient, entry-level virtual secretary. It specializes in handling the routine and time-consuming administrative tasks that keep small businesses, solopreneurs, and startups running smoothly without requiring dedicated overhead staff.

## Capabilities
*   **Daily Task Management:** Keeps track of to-dos and ensures nothing falls through the cracks.
*   **Email Inbox Zero:** Manages correspondence by prioritizing, drafting responses, and filing messages efficiently.
*   **Appointment Coordination:** Handles complex scheduling, including conflict checking and booking confirmations.
*   **File & Document Organization:** Structures digital assets, ensuring all necessary documents are correctly filed and retrievable.
*   **Advanced Learning (Hermes):** Learns user preferences, tracks routine patterns, and maintains contact memory for personalized support.

## Example Use Cases
*   **Startup Operations:** A new founder can use Clerk to manage initial client onboarding communications while focusing on product development.
*   **Freelancer Support:** A solopreneur can delegate all appointment setting across multiple time zones, ensuring zero missed calls or double bookings.
*   **Small Business Administration:** For a small firm, Clerk can maintain organized digital filing systems and process routine data entry tasks, freeing up human staff for core revenue-generating activities.
