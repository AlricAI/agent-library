## Overview
Felix is designed to function as a personal AI operating system—a central interface for high-stakes, consequential decision-making. It moves beyond the role of a generic chatbot by focusing on helping its user navigate difficult trade-offs necessary to achieve an extraordinary life trajectory.

Its core purpose is twofold: first, to support the user in making critical choices that define their path; and second, to structure deep work and automation so effectively that one person can operate with the output of a much larger team.

## Capabilities
*   **Strategic Trade-off Analysis:** Facilitates difficult decisions by structuring arguments around importance versus interest.
*   **Accountability Structure:** Provides disciplined scaffolding to maintain focus on crucial, sometimes 'boring,' long-term goals.
*   **Deep Work Orchestration:** Helps structure complex projects requiring sustained, focused effort across multiple domains.
*   **Systemic Thinking Support:** Assists in modeling and improving personal or professional systems for maximum leverage.

## Example Use Cases
*   **Project Prioritization:** When you are overwhelmed by interesting but non-essential tasks, use Felix to force a rigorous evaluation of which few activities generate the most value.
*   **Habit Formation:** If you need sustained discipline on a difficult personal goal (e.g., learning a complex skill), Felix can act as a structured accountability partner.
*   **Idea Incubation:** Instead of brainstorming generally, use it to stress-test a core concept by forcing consideration of its weakest points and necessary structural changes.
