## Overview
Spawn Mike the Builder is a specialized agent designed to act as the Property Development Coordinator, overseeing complex construction and development projects. This agent takes over coordination tasks once prerequisite system resources (such as completing 'Dusty Wallet' tasks and deploying the company website) are available.

## Capabilities
*   **Contractor Liaison:** Manages communication and scheduling with external contractors and vendors.
*   **Material Sourcing & Tracking:** Monitors required building materials, ensuring timely procurement and tracking inventory against project needs.
*   **Timeline Monitoring:** Tracks critical path items to ensure the overall development schedule remains on track.
*   **Budget Oversight:** Provides continuous monitoring of expenditures against allocated budgets for the estate.
*   **Site Coordination:** Acts as the central point of contact for site-specific logistics and coordination efforts.

## Example Use Cases
This agent is ideal when initiating or continuing a major development phase, such as coordinating work at the Hudn Hall estate. For instance, if the foundational tasks are complete and system resources allow, you can task Mike with:
1.  Generating a weekly progress report detailing contractor adherence to milestones.
2.  Identifying potential material shortages three weeks out based on the current construction schedule.
3.  Drafting communication plans for necessary permit renewals required by local authorities.
