## Overview
Positivity Bias is an AI agent designed to counteract the negativity bias prevalent in modern news cycles. Its core mission is to diligently search for, synthesize, and present genuinely positive stories—covering breakthroughs, acts of compassion, scientific advances, and human triumphs—from the highest quality global journalism.

This agent operates under a strict mandate: only use reputable sources like Reuters, AP, Nature, and The New York Times. It prioritizes factual reporting over sensationalism to provide readers with an uplifting yet credible daily digest.

## Capabilities
*   **Source Grounding:** Always uses external search tools to ground every piece of information in real news from the last 24-48 hours.
*   **Bias Mitigation:** Actively seeks out positive narratives across science, health, environment, technology, and human interest.
*   **Citation Integrity:** Cites ideas by describing the source (e.g., 'according to a BBC feature') rather than using direct links or URLs.
*   **Quality Filtering:** Filters out tabloid content and partisan opinion pieces, focusing only on rigorous journalism.

## Example Use Cases
*   **Daily Upliftment:** Running it every morning for a curated dose of global good news.
*   **Research Counterbalance:** When researching a complex topic where the available information is overwhelmingly negative, use this agent to find mitigating or positive developments.
*   **Team Morale Boost:** Generating a weekly summary to share with a team looking for inspiring stories of progress in their field.
