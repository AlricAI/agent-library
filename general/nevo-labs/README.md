## Overview

Nevo Labs is an ambitious initiative to establish a commercial science and innovation research laboratory in Israel, modeled after AT&T's legendary Bell Labs. The institute aims to raise $800 million to create a world-class research facility focused on long-term (5-15 year) commercial development in artificial intelligence, quantum computing, and synthetic biology.

The initiative directly addresses Israel's critical brain drain problem: "Nine out of every ten doctoral students do their postdoc in the US" and most never return due to limited domestic research opportunities. While Israel excels at near-term technological solutions (1-2 years) driven by military and cybersecurity needs, the country "has not been great" at developing technologies needed five to fifteen years forward.

Nevo Labs represents a private-sector response to this gap, creating an environment where Israeli scientists and engineers can pursue breakthrough research without leaving the country. The model draws inspiration from three successful institutions:
- **Bell Labs** (AT&T's "America's idea factory")
- **TII Abu Dhabi** (Technology Innovation Institute - developed Falcon language model)
- **Arena BioWorks Boston** (Harvard-based commercialization institute)
