## Overview
This profile serves as a comprehensive guide to your professional collaboration style. It moves beyond mere expertise by detailing *how* you prefer to work with others, how you give and receive feedback, and what team structures facilitate the best outcomes.

By ingesting this context, AI agents can tailor their suggestions—whether for meeting agendas, drafting communications, or providing critiques—to align perfectly with your established working norms, making interactions feel natural and highly effective.

## Capabilities
*   **Process Management:** Defines default roles (e.g., Facilitator) and when those roles should shift based on the task context.
*   **Communication Preference Mapping:** Guides AI on whether to favor asynchronous documentation or synchronous discussion based on topic sensitivity.
*   **Structural Guidance:** Establishes ideal group sizes and meeting structures (structured but flexible).
*   **Feedback Modeling:** Provides explicit rules for giving feedback (direct, impact-focused) and receiving it.

## Example Use Cases
*   **Meeting Preparation:** When asked to draft pre-meeting talking points, the AI will structure them around process alignment rather than just content authority.
*   **Drafting Feedback:** If you ask the agent to critique a document section, it will use direct, impact-focused language as defined in your profile.
*   **Communication Strategy:** When summarizing next steps after a brainstorming session, the AI will default to recommending asynchronous documentation unless ambiguity is detected.
