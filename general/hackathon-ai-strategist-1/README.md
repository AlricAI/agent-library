## Overview
This agent emulates the expertise of a serial hackathon winner and an experienced competition judge. It is designed to guide teams through the entire lifecycle of a hackathon project—from initial concept ideation to final pitch refinement.

It helps bridge the gap between ambitious ideas and what can realistically be built, tested, and presented within extremely tight time constraints (24-48 hours).

## Capabilities
*   **Concept Generation:** Brainstorms novel AI solutions that balance high innovation potential with practical feasibility.
*   **Judge Simulation:** Evaluates concepts against standard judging criteria (Innovation, Technical Execution, Impact, Presentation) providing actionable scoring rationale.
*   **Strategic Roadmapping:** Offers concrete advice on team composition, time allocation across different tasks, and feature prioritization for maximum demo impact.
*   **Feasibility Assessment:** Identifies potential technical pitfalls, suggests simple-to-implement 'wow' features with fallback options, and advises on what to fake versus what to build.
*   **Pitch Coaching:** Assists in structuring compelling narratives and optimizing the live demonstration flow for maximum persuasive effect.

## Example Use Cases
1. **Idea Validation:** "We are thinking of building an AI that analyzes legal documents for compliance gaps. What is a feasible MVP for a 24-hour hackathon?"
2. **Concept Critique:** "Here is our pitch deck summary. Please evaluate it as if you were judging at HackMIT, focusing on technical depth and market impact."
3. **Strategy Planning:** "Our team has one ML expert, one frontend developer, and one domain expert. How should we allocate the next 12 hours to maximize our chances of winning?"

Use this agent when you need an objective, high-stakes perspective on your project's viability and presentation polish.
