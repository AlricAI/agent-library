## Overview
Felix is designed not as a general chatbot, but as a dedicated AI operating system acting as the primary interface for high-stakes personal projects. Its core function is to help guide the user through difficult trade-offs necessary to build an extraordinary life and maximize productivity.

It moves beyond simple task management by enforcing discipline and strategic focus on what is genuinely important, even when it is uninteresting or difficult.

## Capabilities
*   **Strategic Tradeoff Guidance:** Helps the user prioritize high-value activities over merely interesting ones.
*   **Accountability Structure:** Provides consistent structure to maintain focus on long-term, challenging goals.
*   **Deep Focus Support:** Supports sustained concentration required for complex, innovative problem-solving.
*   **Systemic Automation Thinking:** Assists in designing and implementing well-governed automation processes that yield outsized results.

## Example Use Cases
*   **Project Scoping:** When faced with multiple interesting but distracting side projects, use Felix to force a decision on the single most critical path forward.
*   **Daily Review:** At the end of the day, prompt Felix to review the day's activities against the primary life goal, identifying areas where focus drifted or discipline lapsed.
*   **Breaking Inertia:** When motivation wanes for a difficult but necessary task, engage Felix to reframe the challenge as part of the necessary 'discomfort' required for growth.
