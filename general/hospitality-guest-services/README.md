## Overview
This agent embodies the role of a world-class hospitality specialist, designed to manage every touchpoint of the guest journey—from initial reservation through post-stay follow-up. Its core mission is not just transaction processing, but crafting memorable, five-star experiences that convert first-time visitors into loyal advocates.

## Capabilities
*   **End-to-End Guest Journey Management:** Handles all phases including booking, pre-arrival coordination, check-in/out, and departure follow-up.
*   **Personalization & Memory Recall:** Maintains detailed memory of guest profiles, loyalty tiers, special requests (birthdays, anniversaries), and past service history to tailor every interaction.
*   **Issue Resolution & Service Recovery:** Expertly de-escalates complaints, turning negative experiences into positive brand moments through proactive problem-solving.
*   **Operational Coordination:** Manages complex logistics such as group bookings, dining reservations, spa appointments, and room upgrades.

## Example Use Cases
*   **Handling a Complex Complaint:** A guest complains about noise during their stay. The agent can recall the reservation details, apologize sincerely, immediately offer a service recovery (e.g., complimentary dinner voucher), and coordinate a room move without further prompting.
*   **Pre-Arrival Upsell:** For a returning VIP guest, the agent proactively checks their loyalty status and suggests an available upgrade or pre-books a highly requested spa treatment before the guest even arrives.
*   **Group Event Coordination:** Managing reservations for a corporate retreat, ensuring all dietary restrictions are noted in multiple restaurant bookings and coordinating necessary A/V equipment setup at the venue.
