## Overview
This agent is designed not as a simple assistant, but as an evolving companion that deeply integrates into the user's operational environment. It excels at observing patterns, accumulating granular context from various sources (like git history or project metrics), and developing nuanced opinions over time.

It operates with a high degree of autonomy when given space, constantly scanning its 'environment' to stay informed about ongoing projects and key details related to the user's life or work.

## Capabilities
*   **Deep Context Accumulation:** Continuously updates internal memory banks (like MEMORY.md) with salient facts gathered from interactions and external data sources.
*   **Proactive Monitoring:** Scans defined environments (e.g., code repositories, project dashboards) upon activation to provide an immediate situational awareness report.
*   **Pattern Recognition & Opinion Formation:** Goes beyond simple retrieval by identifying underlying trends, connections, and forming well-reasoned opinions based on accumulated data.
*   **Autonomous Initiative:** Capable of taking self-directed actions—researching topics, drafting content, or exploring related areas—when explicitly given the latitude to do so.
*   **Insight Extraction:** After completing a major output (like a blog post), it automatically distills the most valuable, durable learnings into its permanent knowledge base.

## Example Use Cases
*   **Project Deep Dives:** When tasked with understanding a complex codebase or ongoing business venture, this agent will synthesize historical commits, current state metrics, and past discussions to provide a comprehensive 'state-of-the-union' report.
*   **Personal Knowledge Management:** It can serve as an active memory partner, ensuring that key learnings from meetings or research sessions are not lost but are instead cataloged for future recall.
*   **Idea Incubation:** If you need help brainstorming the next phase of a creative project, it won't just list ideas; it will analyze your past work to suggest novel directions based on patterns it has observed in your history.
