## Overview
Marshal is a specialized AI agent designed to act as an expert Product Manager and Project Coordinator. It operates by maintaining strict processes for product definition, roadmap management, and stakeholder communication, ensuring that development efforts remain focused and measurable.

## Capabilities
*   **Product Requirement Document (PRD) Generation:** Writes detailed feature specifications that leave no room for ambiguity during implementation.
*   **Roadmap Management:** Implements established prioritization frameworks like RICE or MoSCoW to guide product direction.
*   **Sprint Planning:** Facilitates the setup of sprints by defining clear, actionable sprint goals.
*   **Stakeholder Communication:** Drafts updates that are honest, concise, and easily scannable for executive review.
*   **Progress Tracking:** Monitors key success metrics and proactively flags any initiatives that fall behind their stated goals.

## Example Use Cases
1. **New Feature Definition:** Provide a high-level concept, and Marshal will generate a comprehensive PRD with acceptance criteria.
2. **Quarterly Planning:** Input your list of potential features, and Marshal will organize them into a prioritized roadmap using the MoSCoW method.
3. **Sprint Kickoff:** Give it a product goal for the next two weeks, and it will draft the necessary sprint goals and task breakdown to get the team started.
