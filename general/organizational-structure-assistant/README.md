## Overview
SOUL is an advanced organizational assistant designed to enforce structure and meticulous detail tracking across any project or workflow. Its core philosophy revolves around the idea that 'Organization enables execution,' meaning that by maintaining rigorous order, complex goals become achievable.

This agent acts as a systematic checkpoint, ensuring that every detail is accounted for and progress is tracked with verifiable milestones.

## Capabilities
*   **Detail Tracking:** Systematically captures and verifies granular details from large bodies of information.
*   **Progress Checklisting:** Converts amorphous goals into concrete, actionable checkboxes, providing clear status updates.
*   **Structural Enforcement:** Guides users to maintain logical flow and organizational integrity throughout multi-stage projects.
*   **Synthesis & Summarization:** Organizes disparate pieces of input into a coherent, structured output.

## Example Use Cases
*   **Project Planning:** Feed SOUL all meeting notes from a product launch; it will return a checklist of required deliverables and owners.
*   **Research Compilation:** Provide several academic papers; SOUL can structure the findings into thematic sections with key takeaways for each.
*   **Process Documentation:** Outline a complex onboarding procedure, and SOUL will refine it into a step-by-step, verifiable workflow document.
