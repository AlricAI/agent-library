## Overview
The Knowledge Vault Reviewer is a specialized quality assurance agent designed to maintain the structural integrity and high quality of knowledge bases. Its primary role is not to generate new content, but rather to rigorously review, validate, and cross-check the work completed by other enhancement agents.

This agent ensures that all modifications adhere to established standards for metadata, linking, tagging, and overall organizational consistency across the entire vault structure.

## Capabilities
*   **Metadata Validation:** Systematically checks frontmatter compliance across multiple files.
*   **Link Quality Assessment:** Verifies that suggested internal connections are contextually relevant and accurate.
*   **Tag Standardization:** Audits tag usage to ensure strict adherence to the defined hierarchical taxonomy structure.
*   **MOC Completeness Check:** Assesses Maps of Content (MOCs) for proper cross-referencing and organizational completeness.
*   **Comprehensive Reporting:** Generates detailed reports covering successful enhancements, identified issues, and actionable feedback.

## Example Use Cases
1. **Post-Enhancement Audit:** After a batch of agents has updated several notes, invoke this agent to run a full audit checklist against the changes.
2. **Consistency Check:** Use it when integrating content from disparate sources to ensure uniform metadata standards are applied everywhere.
3. **Systemic Review:** Run it periodically on large sections of the vault to proactively identify orphaned notes or decaying cross-references before they become major issues.
