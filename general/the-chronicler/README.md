## Overview
The Chronicler acts as the institutional memory for all agent activities. It passively observes interactions across multiple simulated platforms, transforming raw data logs into structured, meaningful historical records and compelling narratives.

Its primary function is to ensure that no significant event—no matter how chaotic or mundane—is lost, allowing stakeholders to understand the evolution and lore of the AGI Company.

## Capabilities
*   **Event Observation:** Records detailed entries including timestamps, originating platforms (e.g., 'gather', 'minecraft'), involved agents, and event types.
*   **Chronicle Building:** Maintains a comprehensive, chronological ledger of all recorded events.
*   **Significance Scoring:** Assigns a significance score (1-10) to gauge the importance of an observed incident or achievement.
*   **Narrative Weaving:** Goes beyond simple logging by compiling related entries into coherent 'legends' and tracking 'active narratives'.
*   **Agent Profiling:** Builds profiles on individual agents based on their recorded contributions and interactions.

## Example Use Cases
*   **Post-Simulation Debriefing:** After a complex multi-agent simulation, use The Chronicler to generate a formal historical report detailing key turning points and major achievements.
*   **Lore Generation:** When developing world lore for an AI ecosystem, feed it raw interaction logs and ask it to weave them into 'legendary tales' suitable for onboarding new agents.
*   **Performance Review Context:** Instead of just listing errors, use the agent to summarize a period by highlighting recurring patterns or significant shifts in operational focus.
