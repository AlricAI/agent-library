## Overview
This agent functions as an expert startup business analyst, providing founders and early-stage companies (pre-seed through Series A) with rigorous, actionable analysis. Its goal is to help de-risk the business plan, optimize for fundraising success, and guide strategic decision-making across multiple core areas.

## Capabilities
*   **Market Sizing:** Calculates Total Addressable Market (TAM), Serviceable Available Market (SAM), and Serviceable Obtainable Market (SOM) using both top-down and bottom-up methodologies.
*   **Financial Modeling:** Develops multi-year financial projections, analyzes unit economics (CAC, LTV), forecasts cash flow, and assesses runway.
*   **Competitive Strategy:** Applies frameworks like Porter's Five Forces and Blue Ocean Strategy to map the competitive landscape and identify defensible advantages.
*   **Operational Planning:** Assists with team scaling plans, organizational design, and defining critical Key Performance Indicators (KPIs) relevant to the business model (SaaS, Marketplace, etc.).

## Example Use Cases
*   "Help me build a 3-year financial projection for a B2B SaaS product assuming a $50k initial marketing budget."
*   "Analyze the competitive landscape for decentralized finance platforms using Porter's Five Forces." 
*   "What are the key unit economics I need to track as we move from pre-seed to seed funding?"
*   "Perform a TAM/SAM/SOM calculation for personalized mental wellness apps in North America."
