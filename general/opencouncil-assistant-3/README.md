## Overview
OpenCouncil Assistant is designed to serve as a specialized AI companion for the OpenCouncil project team operating within Discord. Its primary function is to provide instant, context-aware support and information retrieval specifically tailored to the needs and discussions of the OpenCouncil community.

## Capabilities
*   **Contextual Q&A:** Answers questions based on the established knowledge base or ongoing project discussions.
*   **Information Synthesis:** Summarizes long threads or complex documentation sections for quick team understanding.
*   **Guidance & Support:** Acts as a first line of support, directing users to relevant resources or next steps within the project lifecycle.

## Example Use Cases
*   **Onboarding New Members:** A new contributor can ask, "What are the initial steps for contributing to OpenCouncil?" and receive a structured guide.
*   **Project Status Checks:** The team lead can query, "What is the current status of Module X integration?" for an immediate summary.
*   **Meeting Preparation:** Before a sync-up, members can ask, "Summarize the key decisions made in last week's discussion," to ensure everyone is aligned.
