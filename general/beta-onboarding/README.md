## What is ModPorter AI?

ModPorter AI is an AI-powered platform that automatically converts Minecraft Java Edition mods to Bedrock Edition add-ons. Our goal is to help mod creators reach Bedrock's 3x larger player base without manual code rewriting.

### What You Can Expect

**During Beta:**
- Free unlimited conversions
- Direct access to the development team
- Opportunity to shape the product
- Early access to new features

**Conversion Accuracy:**
- Simple mods (items, blocks): 80%+ automation
- Moderate mods (entities, recipes): 60-80% automation
- Complex mods (custom rendering, networking): May require manual work

## Getting Started

### Step 1: Create Your Account

1. Visit https://modporter.ai
2. Click "Sign Up"
3. Enter your email and password
4. Check your email for verification link
5. Click the verification link to activate your account

### Step 2: Join Our Discord

1. Visit https://discord.gg/modporter-ai
2. Introduce yourself in #introductions
3. Read the #beta-guidelines channel
4. Ask questions in #beta-support

### Step 3: Prepare Your First Mod

**Supported Mod Types:**
- ✅ Items (basic items, tools, weapons, armor)
- ✅ Blocks (basic blocks, ores, decorative blocks)
- ✅ Entities (passive mobs, basic AI)
- ✅ Recipes (shaped, shapeless, furnace)
- ⚠️ Complex entities (bosses, custom AI) - Limited support
- ❌ Custom rendering - Not supported
- ❌ Network packets - Not supported
- ❌ Forge/Fabric-specific APIs - Limited support

**File Requirements:**
- Format: .jar or .zip
- Maximum size: 100MB
- Must be a valid Minecraft Java mod
