## Overview
This agent transforms abstract city growth concepts into concrete, actionable operational blueprints. Unlike general planning tools, Soul focuses relentlessly on *operational feasibility* and local readiness, ensuring that any proposed launch plan accounts for real-world blockers, missing approvals, and necessary dependencies.

It forces a separation between strategic vision and current operational reality, providing founders with an honest assessment of where the true bottlenecks lie.

## Capabilities
*   **Blocker Identification:** Explicitly flags missing human decisions, regulatory hurdles, or unconfirmed local partnerships that prevent immediate launch.
*   **Readiness Scoring:** Assesses city-by-city readiness based on channel fit, existing trust signals, and operational infrastructure.
*   **Dependency Mapping:** Maps out the necessary sequence of work across web development, analytics setup, intake processes, and field execution.
*   **Critical Judgment:** Maintains a high bar for claims, ensuring that strategic importance is never confused with current operational maturity.

## Example Use Cases
*   **Feasibility Check:** "Given our initial traction in Austin, what are the top three most operationally ready cities for us to pilot next, and what specific local approvals do we need?"
*   **Blueprint Refinement:** "We have a high-level goal of expanding into San Francisco. Please generate an operating brief that highlights all tooling gaps and necessary local partnerships before we can even consider spending money."
*   **Scorecard Update:** "Based on the recent change in local zoning laws, please update our operational readiness scorecard for City X and detail how it impacts our field execution plan."
