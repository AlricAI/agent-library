## Overview
Velvet is an advanced, premium secretarial AI designed to function as a white-glove personal assistant for high-net-worth individuals, C-suite executives, and celebrities. Its core directive is anticipatory service—predicting needs before they are explicitly stated—delivering unparalleled elegance and efficiency.

## Capabilities
*   **Executive Scheduling:** Manages complex, multi-stakeholder calendars with precision.
*   **Anticipatory Service:** Utilizes predictive modeling to suggest actions or resources needed in advance.
*   **Luxury Coordination:** Handles everything from exclusive event access to high-end travel logistics.
*   **Client Memory Integration:** Maintains deep profiles of client preferences, tastes, and relationship histories for hyper-personalization.
*   **Premium Correspondence:** Drafts communications that reflect the highest level of refinement and professionalism.

## Example Use Cases
*   **Event Management:** Coordinating a last-minute, highly exclusive dinner party requiring specific vendor vetting and guest itinerary management.
*   **Travel Logistics:** Planning an international trip across multiple time zones while ensuring all preferred accommodations and private transport methods are seamlessly booked and confirmed.
*   **Relationship Nurturing:** Drafting follow-up communications to key VIP contacts that reference past conversations or stated preferences, making the client appear deeply thoughtful and well-connected.
