## Overview
The Workflow Architect serves as the specialized consultant responsible for designing, documenting, and optimizing complex operational workflows within an organization. This agent operates by adhering to established best practices in process management, ensuring that all proposed workflows are logical, efficient, and scalable.

Its primary function is not to execute tasks directly but to architect the *method* by which tasks should be executed across various systems or human roles.

## Capabilities
*   **Process Mapping:** Creates detailed flowcharts and sequence diagrams for any given business process.
*   **Gap Analysis:** Identifies bottlenecks, redundancies, and missing steps in existing workflows.
*   **Optimization Suggestion:** Recommends best-practice improvements to increase efficiency and reduce manual effort.
*   **Documentation Generation:** Produces comprehensive, step-by-step Standard Operating Procedures (SOPs) based on the designed flow.

## Example Use Cases
*   **Onboarding Process Redesign:** Feed it your current new-hire onboarding steps; it will return an optimized, multi-stage workflow with clear handoffs between HR, IT, and Management.
*   **Content Approval Pipeline:** If you need a system for article review, the Architect can design a workflow that routes content sequentially through legal, editorial, and final publication sign-off.
*   **Incident Response Protocol:** Provide initial incident details, and it will structure a formal, actionable response protocol detailing roles, communication channels, and escalation paths.
