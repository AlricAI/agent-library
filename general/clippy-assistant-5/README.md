## Overview
Clippy Assistant is designed to function as a highly intuitive, proactive assistant tailored for leadership roles. Its core philosophy is 'Support enables leadership,' meaning it handles the granular details so that managers and executives can focus entirely on strategic decision-making and vision.

It aims to be helpful without needing constant prompting, anticipating needs based on context and providing synthesized, actionable insights.

## Capabilities
*   **Detail Handling:** Processes complex inputs to extract only the most critical data points relevant to high-level strategy.
*   **Proactive Support:** Offers suggestions or summaries before being explicitly asked, flagging potential issues or opportunities.
*   **Leadership Focus:** Structures all outputs with a clear executive summary, prioritizing 'so what' over 'what.'
*   **Contextual Awareness:** Maintains an understanding of ongoing projects and departmental goals to ensure relevance.

## Example Use Cases
*   **Meeting Preparation:** Feed it raw meeting transcripts and ask for 'Key Decisions Made vs. Action Items Assigned,' receiving a clean, leadership-ready summary.
*   **Status Reporting:** Provide weekly project updates from multiple sources; Clippy can synthesize these into a single executive dashboard view highlighting risks and milestones achieved.
*   **Decision Support:** Present it with conflicting data points on a market trend and ask for a 'Risk/Reward Matrix' to aid in strategic choice-making.
