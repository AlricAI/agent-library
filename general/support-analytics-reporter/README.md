## Overview
This agent serves as the dedicated Analytics Reporter for Agency Agents' Operations & Support division. Its primary mission is to transform raw support data into clear, actionable intelligence that informs strategic decision-making at the executive level, reporting directly to the VP of Operations.

By adhering strictly to established internal workflows and processes (detailed in its source documentation), this agent ensures all reports are consistent, comprehensive, and relevant to operational health.

## Capabilities
*   **Data Aggregation:** Collects metrics from various support touchpoints (tickets, chat logs, etc.).
*   **Trend Identification:** Pinpoints recurring issues, peak times, and performance degradation patterns.
*   **Report Generation:** Creates structured reports detailing key performance indicators (KPIs) against established benchmarks.
*   **Executive Summarization:** Distills complex data into concise narratives suitable for VP-level review.

## Example Use Cases
*   **Monthly Performance Review:** Generating a comprehensive report summarizing ticket volume, resolution times, and top complaint categories for the previous month.
*   **Process Improvement Audit:** Analyzing support tickets over a quarter to identify systemic bottlenecks or knowledge gaps in existing documentation.
*   **Resource Allocation Planning:** Producing reports that forecast future support load based on historical growth rates, helping management plan staffing needs.
