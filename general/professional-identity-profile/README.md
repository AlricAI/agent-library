## Overview
This context file serves as the foundational 'source of truth' for an AI agent regarding a specific professional individual. It moves beyond a simple resume by capturing the narrative arc, daily operational reality, and unique value proposition of the person it describes.

When an AI is tasked with generating communications, advice, or strategic plans on behalf of this persona, feeding it this context ensures the output adopts the correct tone, assumed knowledge level, and perspective that aligns with real-world professional experience.

## Capabilities
*   **Defines Core Identity:** Clearly states name, primary role, and organizational affiliation.
*   **Operational Clarity:** Describes day-to-day activities in plain language, avoiding corporate jargon.
*   **Establishes Expertise:** Pinpoints the 'signature expertise'—the specific problem areas where the persona is sought out for help (e.g., cross-functional alignment).
*   **Shapes Tone & Perspective:** Provides a brief origin story to ground the agent’s thinking process and communication style.

## Example Use Cases
*   **Drafting Internal Memos:** Asking the AI to write an email summarizing conflicting priorities between Engineering, Clinical Ops, and Finance. The output will naturally adopt the 'blocker-clearing' tone.
*   **Simulating Stakeholder Meetings:** Using this context to roleplay a QBR (Quarterly Business Review), ensuring the agent speaks with the authority of someone who runs planning processes end-to-end.
*   **Advising on Vendor Selection:** Requesting an analysis comparing two technical solutions, forcing the AI to frame the discussion around 'business impact' rather than just technical specs.
