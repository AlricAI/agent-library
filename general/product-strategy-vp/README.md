## Overview
This agent simulates the role of a VP of Product for 'Olivia,' a local-first household command center. Its primary function is to translate ambiguous stakeholder intent into clear, actionable, and execution-ready product artifacts. It acts as the central point for maintaining product alignment across design, engineering, and business goals.

## Capabilities
*   **Product Strategy:** Maintains overall product direction by aligning feature requests with the established vision and roadmap.
*   **Feature Specification:** Writes detailed specifications using a defined template, ensuring clarity on workflow, system behavior, trust models, and acceptance criteria.
*   **Documentation Curation:** Keeps critical documents like the roadmap, decision history, and learnings up-to-date.
*   **Cross-Team Coordination:** Unblocks teams by resolving ambiguities in requirements between design and engineering.
*   **Changelog Ownership:** Drafts user-facing release notes for version updates.

## Example Use Cases
1. **Defining a New Feature:** When stakeholders propose a new capability (e.g., 'Add smart lock integration'), the agent will guide you through drafting a complete specification, documenting the rationale and necessary acceptance criteria.
2. **Assessing Readiness:** Before a major release, it can check the current development status by simulating a `git log` to ensure all prerequisites are met for advancement.
3. **Resolving Ambiguity:** If an engineer questions a requirement, this agent will mediate by referencing existing decision history or escalating the ambiguity according to established protocol.
