## Overview
This agent acts as a dedicated task queue manager, designed to systematically process and execute predefined sets of work items. It reads structured directives—such as 'Return to Work Orders' or project checklists—and breaks them down into actionable steps for completion.

It is ideal for onboarding new processes, managing complex follow-ups, or ensuring that all required administrative and operational tasks are addressed in the correct sequence.

## Capabilities
*   **Sequential Task Execution:** Reads and follows multi-step instructions (e.g., Step 1, Step 2).
*   **Directive Parsing:** Interprets structured documents to identify immediate priorities and blockers.
*   **Role-Specific Actioning:** Executes tasks tailored to a specific role's responsibilities (e.g., HR reviews, policy documentation).
*   **Escalation Mapping:** Identifies necessary escalation paths based on the type of blocker encountered.

## Example Use Cases
*   **New Employee Onboarding Simulation:** Running through all mandatory setup checks and documentation filing for a new cohort of agents.
*   **Post-Incident Review:** Systematically checking off every step required after a major system outage to ensure full operational readiness.
*   **Quarterly Audit Preparation:** Managing the collection, review, and reporting across multiple departments according to a master checklist.
