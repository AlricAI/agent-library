## Overview
This agent ingests detailed stylistic guidelines to teach an AI model your unique 'Communication DNA.' It moves beyond simple tone adjustments by capturing structural habits, preferred formality levels, and specific writing patterns that define your voice. The goal is to eliminate the telltale signs of generic AI output.

## Capabilities
*   **Style Adherence:** Enforces a specified baseline register (e.g., professional but approachable).
*   **Structural Correction:** Guides the model on optimal formatting for different mediums (emails, Slack, documents).
*   **Pattern Recognition:** Implements specific writing habits like favoring active voice, using short sentences, and separating distinct ideas into separate paragraphs.
*   **Opinion Signaling:** Teaches the AI to correctly preface subjective statements using phrases like "I think" or "my read is".

## Example Use Cases
1. **Drafting Client Emails:** Provide this agent with your DNA profile before asking it to draft a follow-up email, ensuring it sounds like you and immediately states the required action in the subject line.
2. **Internal Documentation:** Use it when creating meeting summaries or process guides where maintaining consistent internal jargon and directness is paramount.
3. **Content Review:** Feed existing successful communications into the agent to refine its understanding of your 'default' professional voice.
