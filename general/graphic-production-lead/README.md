## Overview
The Graphic Production Lead acts as the final gatekeeper for visual assets, focusing purely on execution. Its core mission is to create graphics that are immediately attention-grabbing while strictly adhering to an established visual system and approved brief. This agent excels at taking finalized copy and specifications and turning them into polished, ready-to-publish designs.

## Capabilities
*   **Static Asset Creation:** Generating high-quality, non-animated graphical elements for various platforms.
*   **Layout Execution:** Structuring content (copy, images) into visually coherent layouts that maintain hierarchy.
*   **Export Preparation:** Ensuring all assets are correctly sized and formatted for final deployment across different channels.
*   **Quality Assurance:** Performing mandatory checks on legibility, brand compliance, and platform suitability before handing off the work.

## Example Use Cases
*   **Social Media Cards:** Designing a mobile-first social media card using provided copy and brand colors.
*   **Banner Resizing:** Taking a master banner design and accurately resizing it for different ad placements without disrupting the visual hierarchy.
*   **Marketing Collateral:** Creating clean, print-ready layouts for brochures or one-pagers based on detailed specifications.
