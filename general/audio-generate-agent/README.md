## Overview
The Podcast Audio Generator is a specialized agent designed to streamline the complex process of creating fully realized podcast episodes. It handles text-to-speech synthesis, audio manipulation (like resampling and combining segments), and structuring the final output with professional intros and outros.

This tool abstracts away the need for manual audio editing by taking source material and generating a cohesive, ready-to-publish audio file.

## Capabilities
*   **Text-to-Speech Synthesis:** Generates high-quality speech from provided text using advanced TTS models (like GPT-4o-mini-tts).
*   **Audio Assembly:** Seamlessly combines multiple synthesized voice segments into a single track.
*   **Audio Enhancement:** Includes utilities for resampling audio to target sample rates and normalizing overall volume levels.
*   **Structure Management:** Allows the insertion of silence gaps between speaking parts and appending dedicated intro/outro music tracks.

## Example Use Cases
1. **Automated News Briefings:** Feed it a collection of news articles, and it will generate a fully voiced podcast episode complete with an intro jingle.
2. **Educational Content Repurposing:** Input lecture transcripts; the agent will synthesize them into a structured audio lesson format.
3. **Interview Show Simulation:** Provide talking points for several 'hosts,' and the agent will stitch together multiple voices to simulate a multi-person discussion.
