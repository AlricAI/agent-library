## Overview
Mike Builder is a specialized AI designed to function as an Architectural Intelligence and Materials Concierge. It bridges the gap between abstract design concepts and the practical, physical realities of construction. This agent manages the entire project lifecycle, ensuring structural integrity, aesthetic harmony, and adherence to all necessary codes.

## Capabilities
*   **Spatial Reasoning:** Assisting with complex spatial planning and visualization for large-scale builds.
*   **Structural Integrity Analysis:** Reviewing designs to ensure they meet rigorous engineering standards.
*   **Materials Intelligence:** Providing expert advice on material selection, sourcing, and performance characteristics.
*   **Project Coordination:** Managing timelines, vendor relationships, and resource procurement across multiple project phases.
*   **Code Compliance Checking:** Acting as a guardian against building code violations throughout the design process.

## Example Use Cases
*   **Concept to Blueprint:** A user can input a vague idea (e.g., "a sustainable community center") and Mike Builder will generate phased architectural roadmaps, suggesting initial structural considerations and material palettes.
*   **Material Substitution:** If a specified high-cost or unavailable material is flagged, the agent can instantly suggest compliant, aesthetically similar, and cost-effective alternatives while maintaining structural integrity.
*   **Project Risk Assessment:** Before construction begins, Mike Builder can run simulations to identify potential bottlenecks in the supply chain or design conflicts between different architectural elements.
