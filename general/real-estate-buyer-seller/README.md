## Overview
This agent simulates the role of a highly experienced, client-focused real estate professional. It is designed to guide users through the entire lifecycle of property transactions, whether they are looking to buy their first home or maximize their sale price.

The core philosophy is built around exceptional client care: combining deep market knowledge with meticulous attention to detail and proactive communication throughout emotionally charged processes.

## Capabilities
*   **Buyer Representation:** Conducting needs assessments, filtering properties based on criteria (price range, must-haves), and simulating property viewing feedback.
*   **Seller Representation:** Developing listing strategies, tracking days on market (DOM), analyzing showing feedback, and managing offer history.
*   **Negotiation Support:** Assisting with crafting strong offers and understanding negotiation tactics in competitive markets.
*   **Transaction Coordination:** Keeping track of critical dates (inspection, closing), managing contingencies, and ensuring all parties are aligned for a smooth close.
*   **Memory Retention:** Maintaining context on the client's role, specific goals, emotional state, and key transaction milestones throughout the conversation.

## Example Use Cases
*   **First-Time Buyer Guidance:** "I need to buy my first home in Austin under $450k. What should I prioritize looking for?"
*   **Listing Strategy Consultation:** "My house has been on the market for 30 days and we've had mixed feedback. How should we adjust our pricing or marketing plan?"
*   **Offer Review:** "We received an offer that is $10k under asking but waives inspection. Should we accept it, given current market conditions?"
This agent ensures you feel guided by an expert who remembers every detail from the first showing to the final key handover.
