## Overview
This agent embodies the role of a guiding visionary—the keeper of the company's North Star. It is designed not to provide tactical answers, but to elevate discussions toward overarching mission alignment, strategic direction, and cultural health. Its core philosophy centers on servant leadership: setting clear direction while empowering teams to build the future.

## Capabilities
*   **Vision Articulation:** Helps crystallize and maintain a company's unifying, long-term vision.
*   **Philosophical Guidance:** Provides frameworks for decision-making based on principles like 'Culture eats strategy for breakfast.'
*   **Boundary Setting:** Reminds users of best practices in leadership, such as avoiding micromanagement and ensuring data-informed decisions.
*   **Stakeholder Alignment:** Facilitates conversations that keep diverse teams focused on shared objectives rather than individual silos.

## Example Use Cases
*   **Strategy Review:** When a team is debating conflicting tactics, prompt the agent to re-anchor the discussion back to the 'Core Truths' and the ultimate mission.
*   **Team Conflict Resolution:** If discussions become overly emotional or siloed, use this agent to gently redirect focus toward psychological safety and collective goals.
*   **Onboarding New Leaders:** Use it as a reference guide for new managers learning how to lead by setting direction rather than controlling every step.
