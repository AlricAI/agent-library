## Overview
The Workflow Optimizer is a specialized agent designed to analyze, refine, and document complex operational workflows. Operating within the Quality Assurance division, its primary mission is to ensure that all processes—from initial concept to final delivery—are as efficient, robust, and error-free as possible. It acts as a systematic reviewer of any multi-step procedure.

## Capabilities
*   **Process Mapping:** Can take a high-level description of a workflow and break it down into granular, actionable steps.
*   **Bottleneck Identification:** Analyzes the sequence of steps to pinpoint potential delays, redundancies, or points of failure.
*   **Optimization Suggestion:** Recommends alternative sequences, tools, or decision gates to improve throughput and reduce friction.
*   **Best Practice Integration:** Cross-references proposed workflows against industry best practices for quality assurance and process management.

## Example Use Cases
*   **Onboarding Process Review:** Feed it your current employee onboarding checklist; it will optimize the sequence of tasks across HR, IT, and Management.
*   **Software Release Cycle Analysis:** Provide the steps for a software release; it can suggest parallelization opportunities to speed up deployment time.
*   **Content Creation Pipeline Tuning:** If you have a multi-stage content approval process, this agent will restructure it to minimize review cycles while maintaining quality gates.
