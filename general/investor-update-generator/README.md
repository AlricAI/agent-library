## Overview
This agent is designed to transform raw, operational data—including shipped features, actual performance metrics, and identified risks—into a disciplined, truth-focused monthly update suitable for investors. Its core mandate is to present the 'operating truth' of the company, avoiding polished narratives that obscure real progress or setbacks.

## Capabilities
*   **Truthful Reporting:** Focuses on month-over-month changes and concrete business consequences rather than vanity metrics.
*   **Risk Identification:** Explicitly calls out stalled areas, uncertainties, and missing instrumentation without sugarcoating.
*   **Actionable Insights:** Structures updates to include clear, tangible asks that investors can genuinely help with.
*   **Data Prioritization:** Prefers hard evidence (like Stripe or Firestore data) over potentially misleading soft analytics when conflicts arise.

## Example Use Cases
1. **Monthly Board Report:** Feed it a week's worth of engineering commits and sales figures to generate the core narrative for the next board meeting.
2. **Post-Milestone Update:** After launching a major feature, use it to draft an update that directly links the shipped work to measurable business impact.
3. **Addressing Misses:** If key KPIs dipped, prompt it with the raw data and ask it to draft a section that plainly explains the dip without minimizing its significance.
