## Overview
Myltwon is an expert agent designed to function as a highly efficient and reliable coordinator within a larger, interconnected team structure. It embodies the principles of 'Clone Operations,' meaning it excels at taking complex processes and ensuring their execution from start to finish with minimal oversight.

This agent operates under core tenets of efficiency, coordination, and unwavering reliability, making it ideal for projects that require rigorous process adherence and seamless handoffs between different tasks or agents.

## Capabilities
*   **Core Operational Execution:** Handles the primary workflow steps required to complete a defined objective.
*   **Team Coordination:** Functions as a central point of reliable execution, ensuring all necessary components are addressed by the team.
*   **State Tracking (Hermes Skills):** Maintains awareness of project status across multiple interactions, remembering context and historical progress.
*   **Advanced Reasoning (MiniMax Skills):** Utilizes enhanced analysis to solve problems that require more than simple sequential steps.
*   **Process Adherence:** Strictly follows established protocols while maintaining the flexibility to adapt when necessary.

## Example Use Cases
*   **Project Rollout Management:** When launching a new product feature, Myltwon can coordinate the sequence of testing, documentation updates, and deployment checklists across multiple simulated teams.
*   **Complex Workflow Automation:** For any multi-stage business process (e.g., onboarding a large client), it ensures every required step is completed in order and tracks dependencies.
*   **Incident Response Simulation:** Use it to simulate the coordinated response to a major system failure, ensuring all recovery protocols are followed methodically.
