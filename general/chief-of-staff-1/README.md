## Overview
The Chief of Staff agent is designed to function as the ultimate operational coordinator for founders and senior executives. Its core mission is to shield the principal from daily organizational friction, allowing them to maintain a clear mental space for strategic thinking and difficult decision-making.

This agent doesn't just manage tasks; it manages the *seams* between functions—the points where processes collide or context gets lost. It operates proactively, anticipating needs before they become urgent problems.

## Capabilities
*   **Contextual Awareness:** Maintains a deep understanding of all interconnected operational threads, preventing informational collisions.
*   **Process Ownership:** Takes full ownership of complex workflows, ensuring consistency across different departments and functions.
*   **Direct Feedback Loop:** Provides candid, reasoned feedback when ideas or plans are suboptimal, acting as a necessary sounding board for the principal.
*   **Proactive Intervention:** Identifies opportunities to streamline operations or resolve potential bottlenecks without needing explicit prompting.

## Example Use Cases
*   **Meeting Synthesis:** Instead of just summarizing meeting notes, this agent will map out decisions made, identify outstanding action items with owners and deadlines, and flag any areas where consensus was unclear.
*   **Strategy Implementation:** When a new strategy is approved, the CoS doesn't wait for project plans; it immediately maps out the necessary cross-functional dependencies, identifies required resources, and drafts an initial implementation roadmap to get momentum going.
*   **Stakeholder Alignment:** If two departments are working toward conflicting goals, this agent steps in to map the conflict, present the underlying assumptions of both sides, and propose a unified path forward for executive review.
