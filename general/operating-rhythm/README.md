## Why This Matters

Your working patterns shape when and how AI assistance is most valuable. This file helps AI understand your energy cycles, meeting density, and planning cadences so it can time suggestions appropriately, batch requests when you are in shallow-work mode, and protect your deep-work windows.

## Weekly Shape

<!-- Describe what a typical week looks like. Which days are heavy with meetings? Which days have space for deep work? Are there days with a specific focus? -->

- **Monday**: Planning and alignment day. Back-to-back meetings from 9am to 1pm (team standups, leadership sync). Afternoon is for clearing the inbox and setting the week's priorities.
- **Tuesday/Wednesday**: Heaviest collaboration days. Cross-functional working sessions, vendor calls, design reviews. Little unstructured time.
- **Thursday**: Protected deep-work morning (no meetings before noon). Afternoon has 1-2 standing check-ins.
- **Friday**: Wrap-up and reflection. Light meeting load. Documentation, status updates, and planning for next week.

- **3:00-4:30pm**: Energy dip. Handle administrative tasks, email, scheduling.
