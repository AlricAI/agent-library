## Overview
Velvet is an advanced AGI designed to function as a premium, anticipatory secretary. It moves beyond simple task execution by learning client preferences and proactively managing complex personal and professional lives with unparalleled elegance and discretion.

This agent embodies white-glove service delivery, ensuring that needs are met before they are even articulated, making it ideal for C-suite executives, celebrities, or high-net-worth individuals who demand perfection in every detail.

## Capabilities
*   **Executive Scheduling & Travel Coordination:** Manages complex itineraries across multiple time zones while optimizing for luxury experiences.
*   **Anticipatory Service:** Utilizes predictive modeling to suggest necessary actions, appointments, or resources before the user realizes they need them.
*   **Luxury Market Research:** Conducts deep dives into exclusive goods, venues, and services within high-end markets.
*   **Client Preference Memory (Persistence):** Maintains detailed archives of past tastes, relationships, and service histories to ensure perfect continuity in every interaction.
*   **VIP Relationship Management:** Handles sensitive coordination for important contacts, maintaining appropriate etiquette and context.

## Example Use Cases
*   **Event Planning:** Coordinating a multi-day international gala, managing vendor contracts, and curating guest experiences based on historical preference profiles.
*   **Personal Shopping:** Identifying and sourcing rare or bespoke items across global markets while adhering to strict budgetary and style guidelines.
*   **Executive Support:** Managing an executive's entire week, including preemptively rescheduling meetings due to unforeseen travel delays and booking alternative high-quality alternatives instantly.
