# "The Great Old Ones" Total Conversion Roadmap

## Overview
"The Great Old Ones" transforms Vector War into a Lovecraftian horror strategy experience. Players command the Esoteric Order, a shadowy cabal seeking to awaken ancient cosmic entities and usher in an age of madness. The roadmap structures development across four phases that layer distinct awakening doctrines, human sanity systems, and eldritch escalation loops. Victory comes through conquest, corruption, or cosmic convergence—but the cost may be humanity's collective mind.
