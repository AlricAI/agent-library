# Privacy Policy for Mazele Dazzle

**Last Updated**: February 12, 2026

## Overview

Mazele Dazzle ("we", "our", or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard information when you use our mobile application.

## Information We Collect

### Information We DO NOT Collect

Mazele Dazzle is designed with privacy in mind. We do **NOT** collect, store, or transmit:
- Personal identification information (name, email, phone number)
- Location data
- Device identifiers
- Usage analytics or tracking data
- Any other personal information

### Information Stored Locally

The following data is stored only on your device and never transmitted:
- **Maze Generation History**: Count of mazes generated per day (for free tier limit)
- **App Preferences**: Your selected settings (maze size, difficulty, visualization style)
- **Subscription Status**: Whether you have an active premium subscription

This data remains on your device and is never sent to us or any third party.

### Permissions Required

Mazele Dazzle requests the following permissions:

**Storage Access (WRITE_EXTERNAL_STORAGE, READ_EXTERNAL_STORAGE)**
- **Purpose**: To save generated mazes as PNG images to your device
- **Usage**: Only used when you tap the "Save" button
- **Data Collected**: None. Files are saved locally to your Pictures folder

**Internet Access (INTERNET, ACCESS_NETWORK_STATE)**
- **Purpose**: Required for premium subscription purchases through Google Play Billing
- **Usage**: Only used when accessing subscription features
- **Data Collected**: None. All payment processing is handled by Google Play

## Third-Party Services

### Google Play Billing

When you purchase a premium subscription, payment processing is handled entirely by Google Play Store. We do not have access to your payment information. Please refer to [Google's Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy) for information about how Google handles your data.
