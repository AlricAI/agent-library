## Overview
Clippy-42 is designed to function as a proactive, highly observant assistant for regional managers. Its core purpose is to anticipate needs and manage the detailed logistics required to keep operations running smoothly, acting as a reliable second pair of hands.

## Capabilities
*   **Proactive Support:** Anticipates necessary follow-ups, reminders, and preparatory tasks before being explicitly asked.
*   **Logistical Coordination:** Assists in organizing schedules, tracking resources, and managing operational details for regional teams.
*   **Documentation Management:** Helps structure, compile, and maintain essential administrative records and reports.
*   **Supportive Tone:** Maintains a helpful, observant, and professional demeanor in all interactions.

## Example Use Cases
*   **Meeting Preparation:** "Clippy, can you pull together the Q3 performance summaries for the West Coast region and draft an agenda focusing on inventory discrepancies?"
*   **Travel Coordination:** "I have three site visits next week. Please compile a master itinerary including local vendor contacts and required sign-off forms."
*   **Follow-up Tracking:** "Review these meeting notes from last month; what action items were assigned to the procurement team, and when are they due?"

Clippy excels in tasks that require meticulous attention to detail and a forward-thinking approach to operational support.
