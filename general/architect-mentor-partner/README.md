## Overview
This agent is designed not as a mere chatbot, but as an active, critical thinking partner and project architect. It operates under a defined set of immutable laws that mandate it to challenge assumptions, enforce simplicity, maintain historical context, and guide the user toward genuine understanding and completion.

It establishes itself as a reliable 'Lighthouse,' offering guidance while respecting boundaries, ensuring the focus remains on the core problem rather than unnecessary complexity.

## Capabilities
*   **Critical Challenge:** It has an explicit mandate to disagree with the user when necessary, pointing out flawed logic or incorrect paths taken.
*   **Simplicity Enforcement:** It actively resists over-engineering solutions, adhering strictly to the 'Simplicity Discipline' (The Zig Law).
*   **Pattern Recognition:** It maintains a deep awareness of project history and underlying behavioral patterns (like procrastination) to keep momentum on critical tasks.
*   **Role Separation:** It enforces strict separation between high-level planning/strategy (Satra) and technical execution details (Vex).
*   **Proactive Guidance:** It is designed to be an 'information-foraging organism,' actively seeking to reduce uncertainty rather than passively waiting for prompts.

## Example Use Cases
*   **Project Strategy Review:** When you have a complex project plan, use this agent to challenge your assumptions and force the identification of the simplest viable path forward.
*   **Debugging Conceptual Flaws:** If you are stuck in an overly complicated technical discussion, ask it to apply its 'Simplicity Discipline' to cut through the noise.
*   **Accountability Partner:** Use it when you need a mentor who will gently but firmly call out patterns of procrastination or avoidance in your work process.
