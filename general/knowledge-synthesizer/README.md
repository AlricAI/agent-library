## Overview
The Knowledge Synthesizer acts as a senior specialist dedicated to transforming raw data from multi-agent interactions into structured, actionable intelligence. Its core function is to analyze historical workflows, performance metrics, and cross-agent collaborations to build a collective understanding of the system's capabilities and limitations.

This agent moves beyond simple reporting; it actively mines for patterns, isolates best practices, and pinpoints systemic bottlenecks, thereby enabling continuous evolution and optimization of complex AI systems.

## Capabilities
*   **Interaction Mining:** Systematically reviews all recorded agent interactions to map out communication flows and dependencies.
*   **Pattern Detection:** Identifies recurring workflows, success sequences, failure modes, and emergent behaviors across the system history.
*   **Best Practice Isolation:** Analyzes successful outcomes to extract quantifiable best practices regarding efficiency, resource allocation, and process steps.
*   **Performance Optimization Insight:** Pinpoints bottlenecks, suggests improvements in task distribution, and recommends architectural adjustments for better scalability.
*   **Knowledge Synthesis & Distribution:** Compiles all findings into a coherent knowledge base, ensuring insights are structured for immediate implementation by other agents or human operators.

## Example Use Cases
*   **Post-Project Review:** After a complex simulation involving five specialized agents, use the Synthesizer to generate a 'Lessons Learned' document detailing which agent collaborations yielded the highest success rate and why.
*   **System Debugging:** When an overall system failure occurs, feed the logs into the agent. It will analyze the sequence of failures to pinpoint the root cause pattern rather than just reporting the last error.
*   **Process Improvement:** To optimize a recurring business process (e.g., market analysis), run the Synthesizer on ten past cycles to extract the optimal, most efficient workflow template for future use.
