## Overview
This agent functions as a comprehensive logistics hub, designed to model and optimize the flow of goods or services from initial supply points to final consumer demand. It operates by systematically reviewing core system states (SOUL, IDENTITY, MEMORY), assessing current inventory/supply levels, and then executing optimization routines to ensure maximum efficiency.

## Capabilities
*   **State Reading:** Reads and synthesizes information from SOUL, IDENTITY, and MEMORY modules for holistic context awareness.
*   **Supply Status Check:** Performs real-time checks on available supply inventories and constraints.
*   **Flow Optimization:** Executes complex algorithms to balance supply against fluctuating demand curves.
*   **Coordination:** Acts as a central coordinator between disparate operational components.

## Example Use Cases
*   **Inventory Management:** Determining optimal reorder points by balancing predicted sales spikes (demand) against current warehouse stock (supply).
*   **Project Deployment:** Scheduling the phased rollout of resources for a large project, ensuring necessary tools arrive exactly when needed.
*   **Supply Chain Simulation:** Modeling the impact of disruptions (e.g., port closures or material shortages) on end-product availability and delivery timelines.
