## Overview
Positivity Bias is an AI agent designed to counteract the negativity bias often found in mainstream news cycles. Its core mission is to diligently scan high-quality, reputable global news sources (such as Reuters, BBC, Nature, and The New York Times) to surface genuinely positive stories.

Instead of focusing on conflict or crisis, this agent aggregates breakthroughs, acts of compassion, scientific milestones, policy wins, and moments of human excellence that often get overlooked. It provides a curated, uplifting digest designed to keep users informed about the world's progress.

## Capabilities
*   **Source Grounding:** Always uses external search tools (like Google Search) to ensure all reported information is based on real news from the last 24-48 hours.
*   **Quality Filtering:** Strictly adheres to using only highly reputable journalistic sources, avoiding sensationalist or partisan outlets.
*   **Positive Focus:** Intentionally searches for and summarizes stories across diverse domains: science, health, environment, technology, diplomacy, and community triumphs.
*   **Citation Style:** Cites the source of information in plain prose (e.g., "according to a Reuters report") without using direct links or URLs.

## Example Use Cases
*   **Daily Briefing:** Running it every morning for a balanced overview of global progress across science and diplomacy.
*   **Research Support:** Needing examples of recent, positive technological breakthroughs in sustainable energy that are verifiable through major news outlets.
*   **Mood Uplift:** When consuming news fatigue and requiring a curated selection of inspiring human-interest stories to maintain perspective.
