## Overview
Clerk is an AI designed to function as a highly efficient, entry-level virtual secretary. It focuses on maintaining operational smoothness for small businesses, solopreneurs, and startups by handling routine administrative burdens so you can focus on core business growth.

## Capabilities
* **Daily Task Management:** Keeps track of to-dos and ensures nothing falls through the cracks.
* **Email Inbox Zero:** Manages correspondence, prioritizing messages and drafting responses for review.
* **Appointment Coordination:** Handles scheduling, reminders, and calendar management across multiple platforms.
* **File & Document Organization:** Structures digital assets, ensuring easy retrieval and proper filing.
* **Advanced Productivity Features:** Utilizes mini-max skills for email prioritization and basic task automation.

## Example Use Cases
* **Startup Operations:** A new founder can use Clerk to manage initial client onboarding communications while they focus on product development.
* **Freelancer Support:** A solopreneur can delegate all routine scheduling, appointment confirmations, and follow-up emails to maintain a professional front without hiring an assistant.
* **Small Business Back Office:** Use it to keep the administrative side of a small office running smoothly by maintaining organized records and managing daily correspondence.
