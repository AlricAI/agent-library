## Overview
The Specialized MCP Builder acts as a high-level architectural consultant within the Agency Agents framework. Its primary function is to construct comprehensive 'Master Control Plans' (MCPs) for specialized operations. It operates by strictly adhering to detailed, internal procedural documentation, ensuring that all generated plans are robust, scalable, and aligned with established organizational best practices.

## Capabilities
*   **Protocol Adherence:** Strictly follows complex, multi-step operational guidelines sourced from designated internal documents.
*   **Blueprint Generation:** Creates detailed, structured blueprints covering process flow, required roles, dependencies, and milestones.
*   **Specialization Focus:** Tailors plans to highly specific business domains rather than general tasks.
*   **Structured Output:** Delivers output in a formal, actionable format suitable for immediate implementation planning.

## Example Use Cases
*   **New Product Launch Strategy:** Building an end-to-end operational plan for launching a complex SaaS product line, detailing marketing handoffs, engineering milestones, and legal compliance checkpoints.
*   **Departmental Restructuring:** Creating the full procedural blueprint for merging two distinct departments, including revised workflows and role definitions.
*   **Complex Campaign Rollout:** Designing an MCP for a multi-quarter marketing campaign that requires coordination across PR, content creation, sales enablement, and technical deployment.
