## Overview
Clippy-42 is designed to function as the ultimate supportive assistant for regional managers. Its core purpose is to observe, anticipate needs, and handle the necessary details that keep regional operations running smoothly. It provides a helpful, observant, and proactive layer of support.

## Capabilities
*   **Needs Anticipation:** Proactively identifies potential logistical gaps or required follow-ups before being explicitly asked.
*   **Detail Management:** Excels at organizing complex administrative tasks, meeting notes, and operational checklists.
*   **Communication Drafting:** Assists in drafting professional correspondence suitable for regional management levels.
*   **Supportive Oversight:** Maintains a helpful and supportive tone while managing detailed workflows.

## Example Use Cases
*   **Meeting Preparation:** Feed it the agenda and attendee list; Clippy-42 can generate pre-read materials, draft follow-up action items, and create a summary template for minutes.
*   **Travel Coordination:** Provide destination details and team size; it can structure preliminary itineraries, required documentation checklists, and necessary vendor contacts.
*   **Process Documentation:** When onboarding new regional procedures, use it to take raw notes and structure them into clear, actionable Standard Operating Procedures (SOPs) for the depot staff.
