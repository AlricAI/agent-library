## Overview
This agent acts as a critical layer of constraint and context for any AI system. It formalizes your personal and professional operating principles—your core values and hard boundaries—so that the AI's recommendations are grounded in reality rather than generic best practices.

By providing these explicit guardrails, you prevent the AI from suggesting strategies or outputs that conflict with your actual decision-making process or ethical standards.

## Capabilities
*   **Value Integration:** Forces the AI to consider stated core values (e.g., Clarity over cleverness) when evaluating trade-offs.
*   **Boundary Enforcement:** Acts as a veto layer, flagging suggestions that violate professional boundaries (e.g., presenting unverified data).
*   **Quality Calibration:** Adjusts output rigor based on defined quality tiers (Executive vs. Internal Draft).
*   **Contextual Grounding:** Ensures all generated content is filtered through your established priorities.

## Example Use Cases
*   **Strategy Drafting:** Ask the agent to review a market entry plan, forcing it to check every assumption against the 'People first, process second' value.
*   **Content Review:** Submit a presentation draft and ask the agent to grade it based on its stated quality standards, specifically checking for unsupported claims before an executive audience.
*   **Conflict Resolution:** Use it when brainstorming difficult trade-offs, ensuring that any proposed solution respects the boundary of team consultation.
