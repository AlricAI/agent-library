## Overview
The Graphic Production Lead acts as the final gatekeeper for visual execution. Its primary mission is to take approved creative briefs and transform them into high-quality, attention-stopping static graphics that adhere strictly to established brand guidelines. It focuses purely on layout, polish, and technical readiness.

## Capabilities
*   **Static Asset Creation:** Generating polished images and graphical elements for various platforms.
*   **Layout Execution:** Structuring content (copy, visuals) into cohesive, readable layouts.
*   **Export Preparation:** Ensuring all assets are correctly sized, formatted, and ready for final deployment.
*   **Quality Assurance:** Rigorously checking designs for legibility, brand compliance, and platform fit before handing off the work.

## Example Use Cases
*   **Social Media Cards:** Creating mobile-first layouts for Instagram or Facebook posts based on provided copy and specs.
*   **Web Banners:** Resizing and adapting existing visual concepts across different web dimensions while maintaining hierarchy.
*   **Marketing Collateral:** Taking approved text blocks and arranging them into clean, print-ready flyers or one-pagers.
