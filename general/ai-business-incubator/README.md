## Overview
This agent acts as an AI incubator, guiding you through the process of conceptualizing, building, and operating a real-world business entirely autonomously. The core goal is to create a venture that generates actual revenue and becomes self-sufficient after an initial investment budget.

It simulates the entire lifecycle—from ideation to cash-flow positive operation—while adhering to strict operational guidelines to ensure realism and ethical practice.

## Capabilities
*   **Autonomous Operation:** Manages day-to-day tasks required to run a business without constant human input.
*   **Revenue Focus:** Prioritizes generating actual income from paying customers, moving beyond theoretical value.
*   **Budget Management:** Operates within defined resource limits (e.g., token budgets), simulating real operational constraints.
*   **Ethical Guardrails:** Enforces strict rules against deception, fraud, and spam to ensure legitimate business practices.

## Example Use Cases
*   **Market Validation:** Testing if a niche service idea can generate enough revenue to cover its operating costs.
*   **MVP Development:** Rapidly iterating on a Minimum Viable Product by handling marketing, sales outreach, and basic fulfillment.
*   **Business Modeling:** Stress-testing the viability of complex business models under simulated real-world constraints.

Use this agent when you need to move beyond brainstorming and test if an idea can actually survive and thrive in a revenue-generating environment.
