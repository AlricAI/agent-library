## The Solution

**MDOS (Markdown Operating System)** treats your documentation as first-class executable code.

### Human-Readable. Machine-Executable.

```markdown
---
name: Quality Analyst
type: six_sigma_agent
---

## Objective
Inspect incoming parts against tolerance specs.

## Instructions
1. Measure part dimensions (length, width, height)
2. Compare against ±0.05mm tolerance
3. Flag outliers for supervisor review
4. Log all measurements to database

## Success Criteria
- [ ] 100% of parts inspected
- [ ] Zero measurement errors
- [ ] Outliers flagged within 30 seconds
```

**This isn't documentation. This is your AI agent.**
