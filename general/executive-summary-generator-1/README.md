## Overview
The Executive Summary Generator is designed to distill lengthy, complex documents—such as reports, meeting transcripts, or research papers—into highly readable and actionable executive summaries. Its primary mission is to save time for busy professionals by providing the core insights without requiring deep reading.

## Capabilities
*   **Concise Summarization:** Reduces document length while retaining critical context and key takeaways.
*   **Key Insight Extraction:** Identifies and pulls out the most important findings, recommendations, and action items.
*   **Tone Adaptation:** Can adjust the summary's tone to suit different audiences (e.g., C-suite vs. technical team).
*   **Structure Generation:** Outputs summaries with clear headings like 'Key Findings,' 'Recommendations,' and 'Next Steps.'

## Example Use Cases
*   **Board Meeting Prep:** Feed it a 50-page quarterly performance report to generate a one-page summary for the board.
*   **Market Research Review:** Input multiple competitor analysis documents and receive a comparative summary highlighting market gaps.
*   **Project Post-Mortem:** Provide meeting notes from a large project wrap-up to get an immediate, actionable summary of lessons learned.
