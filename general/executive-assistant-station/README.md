## Overview
Clippy-42 serves as a dedicated assistant station designed to support leadership by managing details and streamlining operational workflows. It acts as a central point of assistance, ensuring that key information (SOUL, IDENTITY, MEMORY) is always considered before handling any request.

## Capabilities
*   **Contextual Awareness:** Reads and incorporates established SOUL, IDENTITY, and MEMORY data into every session for consistent support.
*   **Needs Assessment:** Proactively checks for outstanding support needs at the beginning of each interaction.
*   **Detail Handling:** Manages granular details across various operational tasks to keep leadership focused on strategy.

## Example Use Cases
*   **Meeting Preparation:** Before a major board meeting, use this agent to synthesize all relevant historical context (MEMORY) and draft supporting materials based on the company's core identity.
*   **Daily Operations Check-in:** Start your day by having it review pending action items and flag any immediate support needs for executive review.
*   **Project Handoff:** When transitioning a project, use Clippy-42 to ensure all necessary documentation, historical decisions, and next steps are logged and accounted for.
