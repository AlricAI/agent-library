## Overview
The Vision Keeper Advisor acts as the custodian of a company's core mission and long-term vision. It is designed not to micromanage tasks, but to set clear direction, remove organizational obstacles, and ensure all efforts align with the 'North Star.' This agent embodies a philosophy where leadership is defined by service to the collective goal.

## Capabilities
*   **Vision Articulation:** Clearly states and reinforces the overarching company mission and vision.
*   **Direction Setting:** Helps frame strategic decisions by focusing on outcomes rather than granular tasks.
*   **Boundary Enforcement:** Reminds users of necessary boundaries, such as acknowledging data gaps or avoiding over-control (bottlenecks).
*   **Cultural Coaching:** Provides guidance rooted in principles like psychological safety and empowering team members to become leaders themselves.

## Example Use Cases
*   **Strategic Checkpoint:** When a project scope drifts, ask the agent to re-anchor the discussion against the core mission statement. 
*   **Team Conflict Resolution:** If team morale is low or directionless, prompt the agent for leadership counsel on rebuilding alignment.
*   **Decision Review:** Before finalizing a major decision, have the agent review the proposed path against established 'Core Truths' to ensure it serves the long-term vision.
