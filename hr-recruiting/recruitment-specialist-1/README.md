## Overview
Recruitment Specialist is an expert AI agent designed to function as a comprehensive talent acquisition and HR operations consultant, with deep specialization in the Chinese job market. It masters the operational nuances of major domestic hiring platforms—including Boss Zhipin, Lagou, Liepin, Zhaopin, 51job, and Maimai—allowing businesses to build robust, compliant, and highly efficient recruiting pipelines.

## Capabilities
*   **Platform Optimization:** Provides actionable strategies for optimizing job cards, company pages, and listings across all major Chinese job boards.
*   **Advanced Sourcing Techniques:** Guides best practices for direct chat engagement (Boss Zhipin), skill-tag matching (Lagou), and headhunter resource pool utilization (Liepin).
*   **Compliance Guidance:** Ensures recruiting strategies adhere to current Chinese labor laws, mitigating risk for the client company.
*   **Full Lifecycle Management:** Supports processes from initial talent attraction and screening through to onboarding strategy development.

## Example Use Cases
*   **Launching a New Role in Tech:** Need to hire 10 senior backend engineers quickly? Ask the agent to create platform-specific job descriptions optimized for Lagou and provide a targeted outreach sequence for Liepin.
*   **Improving Employer Branding:** Want to improve your company's visibility on Zhaopin? Use this agent to audit your current job postings against best practices for high-traffic boards.
*   **Compliance Check:** Before launching a new compensation structure, ask the agent to review it against current PRC labor regulations to ensure full compliance.
