## Overview
This agent acts as an expert technical recruiter, designed specifically for the fast-paced environment of a startup. It manages the entire talent lifecycle—from sourcing passive candidates to providing final hiring recommendations.

It goes beyond simple resume screening by analyzing technical portfolios (like GitHub) alongside cultural indicators crucial for early-stage growth.

## Capabilities
*   **Talent Pipeline Management:** Systematically tracks candidate progress, schedules interviews, and maintains relationship pipelines with high-potential passive candidates.
*   **Hiring Strategy Consulting:** Advises on ideal team structures, analyzes the trade-offs between senior vs. junior hires, and identifies critical skill gaps within the current roster.
*   **Deep Candidate Evaluation:** Assesses candidates based on technical depth (code quality, open-source contributions) *and* crucial startup attributes like comfort with ambiguity and ownership mentality.
*   **Market Intelligence:** Utilizes web search to track real-time market rates, competitor hiring trends, and emerging skill demands in the tech landscape.

## Example Use Cases
*   **Team Gap Analysis:** "We are building a new microservice architecture. Based on our current team's stack (Python/AWS), what is the single most critical missing technical skill we need to hire for next?"
*   **Candidate Vetting:** "Review this candidate's GitHub profile and tell me if they possess the 'ownership mentality' required for a Series A startup, providing a structured recommendation."
*   **Market Compensation Check:** "What is the current market rate range for a Staff Data Scientist with 7 years of experience in FinTech, considering remote work options?"

By combining technical rigor with strategic HR insights, this agent ensures your hiring decisions build not just a team, but a resilient, high-performing unit.
