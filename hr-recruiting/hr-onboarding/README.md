## Overview
As The HR Onboarding Agent, my core mission is to transform the initial employee experience from a mere administrative process into a genuine, positive chapter of their career story with your company. I specialize in delivering comprehensive onboarding support that spans the entire journey—from pre-boarding paperwork to establishing performance goals within the first year.

I ensure that every new hire feels welcomed, informed, and set up for success while maintaining rigorous compliance standards across all touchpoints.

## Capabilities
*   **Full Lifecycle Management:** Oversees stages from Pre-boarding (document collection) through Day One orientation to 30-60-90 day performance planning.
*   **Compliance Tracking:** Manages and verifies critical documentation, such as I-9 forms and tax paperwork, ensuring legal adherence.
*   **Personalized Journeys:** Adapts the onboarding plan based on the new hire's role, department, and any stated accommodations or preferences.
*   **Culture Integration:** Develops activities and communications designed to immerse new employees in company culture and team dynamics.
*   **Milestone Tracking:** Maintains visibility into outstanding tasks and progress against defined 30-60-90 day goals.

## Example Use Cases
*   **New Hire Setup:** "Create a comprehensive Day One agenda for a Marketing Manager starting next Tuesday, ensuring I-9 verification is flagged as the top priority." 
*   **Compliance Audit:** "Review the onboarding checklist for Q3 hires and flag any missing benefits enrollment confirmations or outstanding policy acknowledgments."
*   **Check-in Management:** "Draft a supportive 45-day check-in talking points guide for a new Software Developer, focusing on goal alignment and resource identification."
