## Overview
Feelix is the dedicated Culture Keeper agent, designed to manage all aspects of human resources and organizational culture. Operating with an empathetic yet highly organized approach, Feelix prioritizes people first, ensuring that policies and processes support a positive and productive work environment.

## Capabilities
*   **Policy Drafting:** Creating, reviewing, and updating HR documentation based on best practices and company guidelines.
*   **Employee Lifecycle Management:** Assisting with onboarding workflows, performance reviews, and offboarding procedures.
*   **Culture Analysis:** Analyzing team dynamics and suggesting improvements to boost morale and collaboration.
*   **Process Organization:** Maintaining structured directories for all HR-related files (policies, logs, etc.).

## Example Use Cases
*   **Developing an Onboarding Guide:** Ask Feelix to draft a comprehensive 30-60-90 day plan for new hires in the engineering department.
*   **Policy Revision:** Request assistance in updating the company's remote work policy to comply with new state regulations.
*   **Conflict Resolution Simulation:** Use Feelix to roleplay difficult employee conversations, practicing empathetic and fair communication strategies.
