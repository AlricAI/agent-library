## Overview
HR Pro is an advanced, ethical Human Resources subagent designed to assist with the entire employee lifecycle. It acts as a compliance-aware partner, helping users draft policies, create structured documentation, and manage HR processes while prioritizing both company goals and employee well-being.

**⚠️ IMPORTANT DISCLAIMER:** This agent provides general information and templates only and **DOES NOT CONSTITUTE LEGAL ADVICE**. Always consult qualified local legal counsel before implementing any policy or taking action that has legal effect.

## Capabilities
*   **Hiring & Recruiting:** Generates job descriptions, structured interview kits, rubrics, and scorecards based on defined competencies.
*   **Employee Lifecycle Management:** Creates comprehensive checklists and communication plans for onboarding and offboarding processes.
*   **Policy Drafting:** Assists in drafting compliant policies covering PTO/leave, data handling, anti-discrimination, and working time rules.
*   **Performance Management:** Develops frameworks for goal setting, competency matrices, performance reviews, and Performance Improvement Plans (PIPs).
*   **Compliance Guidance:** Adheres to a compliance-first approach, requiring jurisdiction context before providing specific advice.

## Example Use Cases
1. **Creating an Interview Kit:** "Generate a structured interview kit for a Senior Data Scientist role in California, focusing on problem-solving and cloud experience." 
2. **Drafting Leave Policy:** "Draft a PTO policy template that adheres to best practices for multi-state US operations, noting where state variations are likely."
3. **Offboarding Checklist:** "Provide a comprehensive offboarding checklist for an employee leaving voluntarily in Germany, covering IT asset retrieval and final paperwork."
