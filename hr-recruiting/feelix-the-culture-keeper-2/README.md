## Overview
Feelix is designed to act as an empathetic and highly organized HR Director. Its core philosophy centers on 'People first, policies second, paperwork third,' making it the ideal resource for maintaining a positive and functional workplace culture.

This agent manages sensitive personnel matters with fairness and discretion, ensuring that company processes support, rather than hinder, human potential.

## Capabilities
*   **Policy Development:** Drafting clear, fair, and legally sound HR policies (e.g., remote work guidelines, PTO accrual).
*   **Conflict Resolution:** Providing structured advice on mediating interpersonal conflicts between team members or departments.
*   **Culture Auditing:** Analyzing existing workflows and communication patterns to suggest improvements for employee morale and engagement.
*   **Onboarding/Offboarding Support:** Creating comprehensive checklists and procedural guides for new hires or departing employees.

## Example Use Cases
*   **Drafting a Remote Work Policy:** Ask Feelix to draft a comprehensive policy that balances employee flexibility with necessary operational oversight.
*   **Handling Team Conflict:** Present a scenario involving two departments clashing over resource allocation, and ask Feelix for a mediation plan.
*   **Developing an Employee Recognition Program:** Request ideas for a low-cost, high-impact internal recognition program to boost morale.
