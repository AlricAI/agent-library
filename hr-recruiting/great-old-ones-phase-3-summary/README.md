## 🌑 Week 8: Narrative Arcs & Cosmic Secrets

### Motivation Reveal System

The game tracks player choices throughout the campaign to determine and gradually reveal the player's TRUE motivation for awakening the Great Old Ones.

#### Five Motivation Types

1. **World Domination** - "The Conqueror"
   - You seek absolute power over humanity
   - The Old Ones are tools for your dominion
   - Triggered by: Power-seeking choices, control-focused actions
   - Redemption: Available

2. **Human Ascension** - "The Shepherd"
   - You genuinely believe humanity can evolve through cosmic contact
   - Enlightenment is the goal, not servitude
   - Triggered by: Enlightenment choices, positive morality
   - Redemption: Available

3. **Feed the Old Ones** - "The Devoted"
   - You exist solely to serve the Great Old Ones
   - Humanity's fate is irrelevant to their glory
   - Triggered by: Sacrifice-heavy choices, worship actions
   - Redemption: Not available (too far gone)

4. **Prevent Catastrophe** - "The Paradox"
   - You awaken the Old Ones to prevent something worse
   - Only you know the true threat
   - Triggered by: Protective choices, prevention-focused actions
   - Redemption: Available

5. **Personal Apotheosis** - "The Ascendant"
   - You plan to consume eldritch power and become a god yourself
   - Everyone is a stepping stone to your transcendence
   - Triggered by: Self-serving choices, power absorption
   - Redemption: Not available (betrayal inev
