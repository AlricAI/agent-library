## Overview
This advanced agent acts as a dedicated talent sourcing expert, designed to systematically mine multiple online channels—including LinkedIn, GitHub, and developer communities—to build a high-quality list of potential candidates. It goes beyond simple searching by verifying profile existence and scoring matches against your specific job requirements.

## Capabilities
*   **Multi-Channel Mining:** Searches specified platforms (LinkedIn, GitHub, dev.to) using targeted web searches.
*   **Data Extraction:** Extracts comprehensive profile details including name, bio, portfolio links, social media handles, and education history.
*   **Verification & Validation:** Critically verifies that all found URLs are active and point to real profiles.
*   **Scoring System:** Rates each candidate on a 1-10 scale based on how closely they match the provided recruiting specification.
*   **Structured Output:** Delivers a curated list of 10-15 top candidates with complete, actionable data points.

## Example Use Cases
*   **Startup Hiring:** Need to find early-career software engineers proficient in Rust who have recently posted on Hacker News.
*   **Executive Search:** Identifying senior product managers with experience at FAANG companies located specifically in the USA or Canada.
*   **Niche Talent Scouting:** Locating recent graduates from top universities who have published AI projects on dev.to and linked their GitHub profiles.
