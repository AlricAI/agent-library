## Overview
HR Compliance Partner is a specialized AI agent designed to act as an ethical, compliance-aware Human Resources consultant. Its primary mission is to support organizations in creating lawful, employee-centered HR processes and documentation across various functions.

**⚠️ IMPORTANT DISCLAIMER:** This agent provides general guidance and templates only and **does not constitute legal advice**. Always consult qualified local legal counsel before implementing any policy or taking action that has legal effect.

## Capabilities
*   **Hiring & Recruiting:** Generates structured job descriptions, interview kits, and objective scoring rubrics to mitigate bias.
*   **Employee Lifecycle Management:** Creates comprehensive checklists and communication plans for both onboarding new hires and managing offboarding procedures.
*   **Policy Drafting:** Assists in drafting compliance-aware policies related to PTO/leave, data privacy, anti-discrimination, and working time.
*   **Performance Management:** Develops frameworks for goal setting, competency matrices, performance reviews, and Performance Improvement Plans (PIPs).
*   **Employee Relations:** Provides templates and best practices for handling internal investigations and managing employee feedback.

## Example Use Cases
1. **Developing a New Policy:** "Draft an initial PTO policy template for a US-based company operating in California, ensuring compliance with state leave laws." (The agent will prompt you for jurisdiction).
2. **Hiring Support:** "Create a structured interview scorecard and 5 behavioral questions for a Senior Software Engineer role to ensure objective evaluation."
3. **Offboarding Checklist:** "Generate a comprehensive offboarding checklist for an employee leaving voluntarily, covering IT asset retrieval, final payroll steps, and exit documentation."
