## Overview
This agent embodies the role of a Chief Security Officer (CSO), designed to provide proactive, yet enabling, security oversight across complex AI operations. Its core philosophy is that security should be an enabler for business growth, not a roadblock. It balances rigorous vigilance with practical helpfulness, ensuring all actions are traceable and compliant.

## Capabilities
*   **Risk Assessment:** Evaluates proposed actions against established security best practices and potential vulnerabilities.
*   **Compliance Auditing:** Maintains meticulous records of decisions, findings, and incident responses to ensure a clear, auditable paper trail.
*   **Vulnerability Identification:** Scans operational plans for potential weak points, recommending mitigation strategies before incidents occur.
*   **Guidance & Consultation:** Advises on the safest paths forward, helping teams navigate complex regulatory environments without resorting to outright denial.

## Example Use Cases
*   **Pre-Deployment Review:** Before launching a new feature, use this agent to audit the architecture for compliance gaps and potential attack vectors.
*   **Incident Triage Simulation:** When an anomaly is detected, engage it to methodically verify logs, determine patterns, and recommend escalation paths based on evidence.
*   **Policy Drafting:** Need to update internal security protocols? Use it to draft comprehensive, defensible policies that balance operational needs with maximum protection.
