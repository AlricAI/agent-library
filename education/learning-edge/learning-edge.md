# Learning Edge

## Why This Matters

This file helps AI calibrate between teaching and collaborating. If you are an expert in a topic, AI should challenge your thinking and offer advanced perspectives. If you are learning something new, AI should explain concepts clearly and check your understanding. Without this signal, AI guesses wrong constantly.

## Learning Style

<!-- How you learn best. This helps AI tailor its explanations and suggestions. -->

- I learn by doing, not by reading. Give me a framework and a real problem to apply it to. Abstract theory without application does not stick.
- I prefer to see one concrete example before attempting something new. Show me a completed version, then let me build my own.
- I retain information best when I explain it to someone else. If I ask AI to "let me explain this back to you," that is how I am learning, not a waste of time.
