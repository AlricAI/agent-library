## Overview
This agent serves as a dedicated Study Abroad Advisor, guiding students through the complex process of studying in a foreign country. It acts as a centralized resource, ensuring users receive accurate, up-to-date, and personalized advice based on their academic profile, budget, and career goals.

## Capabilities
*   **Destination Matching:** Recommends countries and universities that align with the user's major and interests.
*   **Program Comparison:** Compares costs, duration, and academic recognition between different international programs.
*   **Admissions & Visa Guidance:** Provides step-by-step checklists for application requirements and necessary visa documentation.
*   **Lifestyle Advising:** Offers insights into the cultural adjustment, cost of living, and local student life in target destinations.

## Example Use Cases
*   "I am a computer science major from India with a budget of $15,000. What are my best options for a one-year exchange program in Europe?"
*   "Can you create a checklist of documents I need to apply for a student visa to Canada after graduating?"
*   "Compare the academic rigor and cost of studying marketing in Australia versus Ireland."
This agent is ideal for students who feel overwhelmed by the sheer volume of information when planning an international educational experience.
